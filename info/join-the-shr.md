## Join the SHR

Please support Scottish hill running by joining SHR. Now online via SiEntries.

## Membership fees

£10 - Senior

£5 for each second and subsequent senior member living in same household

£5 for Juniors/Students

[Join online via SiEntries](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event_series.php?series_id=601)  

## Membership benefits

Membership of Scottish Hill Runners provides the following benefits:

* A comprehensive calendar of almost 200 races, published in hardcopy and [online](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Calendar.aspx)
* [Scottish Hill Runners Championship](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-001), based on your best 4 results from 6 races
* A regular newsletter and annual journal
* [Public liability insurance protection](Insurance.aspx) (including when acting as a race organiser).
* Sponsorship and support of many races.
* Opportunities for group travel and accommodation to selected international events.
* A clear policy of encouraging and defending open access for hill racing throughout Scotland.
* An annual prize giving and social event at the end of the season.
* A [Race Organisers Pack](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Download.aspx?Item=RACEORGANISERSPACK2020.doc), (including information on insurance, safety and environmental issues) - [pdf version of Pack](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Download.aspx?Item=RACEORGANISERSPACK2020.pdf)
