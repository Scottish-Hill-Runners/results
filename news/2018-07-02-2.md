The David Shepherd Memorial Glamaig Hill Race is this Saturday (7th July) on Skye. It is the third counter, and final “short” category race, in the SHR championship. To complete the championship you need to complete 4 races, including one in each race category – so if you missed Chapelgill then you’d better pack your tent and head to Skye! Please enter via sientries, as it makes our lives a whole lot easier on the day. Also, there’s an entry limit of 230, so if you enter now then you’ll be guaranteed a place! Our site says full windproof cover and that full waterproof cover may be required – please read this and “bring waterproofs regardless of what you think the weather will be like”. You really, really cannot go wrong with waterproof cover...with hood!

[https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=4666](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=4666)

Glamaig is a wonderful weekend in a beautiful part of the world. I don’t feel you ever need an excuse to visit Skye, but if you do…then use this. Hope to see you there!
