Andy Fallas (Carnethy) won the first SHR Championship long counter in 01:57:33 ahead of Al Anthony (Ochils) and Joe Symonds (Shettleston) in 2nd and 3rd.

Jasmin Paris (Carnethy) won the female race with Helen Bonsor (Carnethy) 2nd and Clare Whitehead (Deeside) 3rd.
