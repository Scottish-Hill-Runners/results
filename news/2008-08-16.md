---
title: "Scotland's team for the World Mountain Running Trophy announced"
date: 2008-08-16
excerpt: "The team consists of six senior men, four senior women, four junior men and three junior women..."
---

Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC), who has won medals in the World Mountain Running Trophy in 1989, 1992 and 1995, is back in Scotland's team for this year's event, which is being held in Switzerland next month. The team is:

**Senior men**  
Joe Symonds (Kendal AC)  
Murray Strain (Hunters Bog Trotters)  
Al Anthony (Ochil Hill Runners)  
Jethro Lennox (Shettleson Harriers)  
Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC)  
Grant Stewart (Edinburgh AC)

**Senior women**  
Lyn Wilson (Carnethy HRC)  
Elke Schmidt (Bellahouston)  
Claire Gordon (HBT)  
Sue Ridley (Edinburgh AC)

**Under-20 men**  
Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven)  
Scott McDonald (Central AC)  
Alex Hendry (Central AC)  
Matthew Gillespie (Unattached)

**Under-20 women**  
Mhairi Inglis (Lothian RC)  
Jessica Martin (Kilbarchan)  
Christina Rankin (Kilbarchan)

More details on the **scottish**athletics [website](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=3685)
