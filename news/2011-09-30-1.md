A final reminder about the key details for Ben Venue on Saturday:

\* Registration, which is at the Ben Venue Car Park, is ONLY open from 11.30 to 12.30, dont be late!

\* Entry on the day costs £6, or £8 if you are not a SA member

\* Entrants must be over 18 (on 1 Jan 2011)

\* Toilets are not available here, the nearest are at Loch Katrine pier. Strictly no going in the woods!

\* You will be required to carry a map, compass, whistle, full waterproof body cover, hat and gloves.

For more details, including course description, go [here]( http://www.bellahoustonroadrunners.co.uk/events/benvenuechallenge)

And of course, see you at the SHR Dinner and presentation afterwards !
