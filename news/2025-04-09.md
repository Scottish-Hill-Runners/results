Traditionally Scotland has a strong representation in the GB team at the World Mountain and Trail Running Championships. For 2025 UKA won’t be funding the team and the athletes have to pay to represent GB & NI. We’ve set up a crowdfunding page to raise some money for the team. If you’d like to support the hill runners hoping to represent Britian please have a look at the link below. And thanks to anyone who makes a donation. [Click here to support](https://www.crowdfunder.co.uk/p/help-fund-gb-world-mountain-trail-champs-team)
