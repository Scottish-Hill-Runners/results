---
title: "Reinigeadal Race results and report"
date: 2014-09-15
excerpt: "The Harris Mountain Festival Race (Reinigeadal Race 'The Postal Track') was held on Saturday 13th September"
---

Perfect conditions for this years Harris Mountain Festival Race on what’s referred to as 'The Postal Track' between the villages of Urgha and Reinigeadal.

All that was missing was Brian Brennan and a Bog Trotter(s)!

A hard fought race between young local whizz kid Duncan McKellar and his island hill running nemesis Anesti Davenhill. Duncan eventually started to open up what would turn out to be a convincing lead at the start of the final 900ft climb. This makes it a McKellar family double for the 2 Harris hill races this year as younger sister Peigi enjoyed a very convincing win at this year's 'Up The Clisham.' Third place went to Lachlan Calder of Aberdeen Uni A.C. First lady to Kay Kershaw from Calbost Hill Runners.

A big thanks to Matt the Ranger for North Harris and the Langley family of Reinigeadal for their support. Without them this event would probably not take place.
