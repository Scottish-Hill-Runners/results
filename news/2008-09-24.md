The Junior Hill Running League, organised by **scottish**athletics, this year consisted of three races: Ben Lomond in May, Scolty in June and last week's Trials at Bonaly.

Halina Rees (Fife AC) and Rachel Armitage (Aberdeen AAC) were clear winners of the Girls U14 and U16 titles respectively. The U19 event was much closer, Jessica Martin (Kilbarchan AAC) taking it by a single point from Catriona Buchanan (Central AC).

Jamie Crowe (Dundee HH) just beat Adam Harris (Fife AC) in the Boys U14, whilst in the U16s the two bonus points earned by James Waldie (Cornwall AC) took him clear of Jamie Stevenson (Ochil HR). Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven AC) was the U19 Champion.
