With 5 races now counting we can see many of the final podium positions, or at least who they are likely to be, and also see who is in line to get a valuable SHR mug. For those who didn't run at Strathpeffer, you will need to complete the Devil's Beeftub in order to get your short race in. But it is worth running the Beeftub anyway - the only race in Scotland where the gun goes and you start walking !

The championship listings are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2017 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2017 - Female.xls) for women,  
or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.
