**_Saturday 3rd November 2012 at The Forth Inn, Aberfoyle_**

After last year’s successful event, this year we are again holding the annual “do” at the [Forth Inn](http://www.forthinn.com), Aberfoyle, in their superb “Gathering” function room. For those wanting to combine it with a race, the nearby Dumyat Dash is taking place earlier that day. Alternatively, there’s plenty of scope for a run of your own choosing in the Trossachs!

Prize giving for the Run 4 It SHR Championship will take place at around 6.30pm. Run 4 It will again be awarding a range of superb trophies to category winners in this year’s Senior Championship, and the unique pictorial mugs will again be available to all members who have completed the series. Prizes will also be awarded for the SHR Long Classics series, and this year for the first time mementos will be awarded to all members who have completed the series.

Prize giving will be followed by the dinner at around 7.30pm. There will be a three course meal from the Inn’s award winning kitchen, to include soup and crusty bread, a range of casseroles, a vegetarian option, couscous, potatoes, salad, plus desserts. Drinks will be available to purchase from the bar, including a selection of real ales.

Music will be provided by the brilliant [Darloch Ceilidh Band](http://www.darloch.co.uk), guaranteed to have everyone on the dance floor!

This year we hope to arrange bed and breakfast with local B&B’s e.g. [Mayfield](http://www.mayfield-aberfoyle.co.uk), typical cost around £30 per person per night. Please make your requirements clear on the booking form.

The cost for the do is just £20. Please complete a booking form as soon as possible to be sure of your place!

Further details from Angela at angela.mudge@btinternet.com or Dave at dcbscott@tiscali.co.uk.
