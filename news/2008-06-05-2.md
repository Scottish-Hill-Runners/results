Dougal Ross (Bellahouston RR) has consolidated his lead in the [Bog and Burn Series](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-003) with 4th place at the Kilpatricks Hill Race, and is one of 7 runners to have completed the first 4 races.

The leading runner with 3 races is Bob Wiseman (Ochil HR), and Jethro Lennox (Shettleston) leads the runners with 2 races, having won at both the Kilpatricks and Dumyat.
