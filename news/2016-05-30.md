---
title: "Cort-ma Law Hill Race – Instructions from Race Organiser"
date: 2016-05-30
excerpt: "Cort-ma Law Hill Race – Wednesday 1st June 7.30 pm"
---

The organiser has asked us to remind runners that, as usual, you will be required to carry the following standard safety equipment:

• water and wind-proof whole body cover, plus other body cover appropriate for the weather conditions  
• a map of the route, a compass and a whistle.

Your kit will be checked at the start of the race.
