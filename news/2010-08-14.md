This year the World Mountain Running Championships are being held in Slovenia on 5 September and are uphill-only.

The GB team contains three Scots:  
Prasad Prasad (Clydesdale), Senior Men  
Catriona Buchanan (Ochils), Junior Women  
Alex Hendry (Central), Junior Men.

In addition Sula Young (HBT) is the reserve for the Senior Women and Jamie Stevenson (Ochils) for the Junior Men.

There's more information on **scottish**athletics' web site [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=5032).

Congratulations and good luck to all.
