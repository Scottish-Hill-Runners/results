In-keeping with the informal nature of the race it is entry on-the-day. No advance entries. Adults £5; U18 £2. All entrants MUST complete an entry form at Race Registration. You can save time by printing the entry form from www.moorfootrunners.com and have it completed in advance to hand in at registration with the correct fee.
