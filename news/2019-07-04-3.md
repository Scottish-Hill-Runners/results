The Lochaber AC Triple Hirple races were run over the weekend.  
There were individual wins for Callum Fraser and Lisette Walter (both Lochaber) at Meall an t-Suidhe on Saturday 29th June.

On Sunday, the Half Ben Nevis race was won by Andrew Gilmore (Highland Hill Runners) and Caroline Marwick (Inverness Harriers)

And on Monday evening, the Cow Hill Race was won by Finlay Wild and Isla MacKay (both Lochaber).

Andrew Gilmore's win at the Half Ben and 3rd place finishes at both Meall an t-Suidhe and Cow Hill gave him the overall win in the Triple Hirple.
