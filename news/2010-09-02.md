---

The Achmony Hill Race is part of the Glenurquhart Highland Gathering. It was won by Manny Gorman (Westies), ahead of Ross Bannerman (Highland) and Alan Smith (Deeside). Ali Thomas (Eryri) was the first woman, as she had been at Wideford ten days earlier.
