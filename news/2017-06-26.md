---
title: "Eagle Crag Hill Race Results and Report"
date: 2017-06-26
excerpt: "Wins for Richard Macleod (Lochaber) and Diana Macdonald (Highland Hill Runners) at the Eagle Crag Hill Race on Saturday 24th June"
---

We had good conditions for the Eagle Crag race with light showers to stop overheating and enough of a breeze to keep the midges at bay.

35 runners took part with the field mainly represented by Lochaber, Highland, and Deeside. Stephen Traill was very strong on the first climb and built a strong lead over the rest of the field, but a bad line of descent down to the West Highland Way cost him his lead. Richard Macleod took full advantage of this and lead the field strongly to the finish - look forward to few battles between these two this season.

Alan Smith had another cracking race, first of the fifties and fifth overall. I want some of what he is taking. Enough of the old and bring in the new with Diana Macdonald running her first Eagle Crag and breaking the ladies record by 55 seconds. This could stand for a while, but looking at the smile on Diana's face at the end of the race I think she may be back next year , and now she knows the course you never know…….

Jim Hamilton
