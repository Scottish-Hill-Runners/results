Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) and Grant Stewart (City of Edinburgh) warmed up for the Knockdhu International next weekend by coming first and second in the Hunter's Bog Trot. Simon Peachey (Carnethy) was third and the early leader Oleg Chepelin (EUH&H) fourth. Jamie Thin (HBT), also in the team for Knockdhu, was the leading vet.

Veronique Oldham (Norham) won the ladies' race (and was first F40 as well), with Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT) the runner-up and Miranda Grant (also HBT) third.
