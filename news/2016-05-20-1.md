Wins for Rob Sinclair and Steph Provan at Pressendye on Thu 19th May. They both beat the old course record, and both second place runners (Hamish Battle and Anna McFadyen) also beat the old record. Cold and misty conditions meant a good evening for racing but a bad one for the marshals.
