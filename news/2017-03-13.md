This race is a lovely scenic hill running relay race based in the Ochil Hills above the town of Alva, Clackmannanshire. The race will comprise 3 legs each of which will be run by a separate competitor. _Teams can comprise runners from one or more hill running club and do not need to be solely from one club only._ £18 per team fully comprising members of Scottish Athletics (plus an additional £2 per runner must be added for each non-member of Scottish Athletics).

The hillsides are primarily grassy hills interspersed with a mixture of whin, gorse, ferns, heather, bracken, blaeberry and rocky outcrops. Athletes will also encounter steep hillsides, burn crossings, narrow gorge paths, animal burrows and rocky outcrops. It should also be noted there will be sections of steep climbing together with skilled, fast descents.

**Leg 1** - 439m ascent/descent over 6km (Highest altitude: 449m)

**Leg 2** - 740m ascent/descent over 7.5km (Highest altitude: 530m)

**Leg 3** - 428m ascent/descent over 4.75km (Highest altitude: 438m)

Last year, the race raised £800 for the MacMillan Cancer Support and Ochil Mountain Rescue Team and in 2017 the hope is to build on that.

If interested, please see full information with entry form available on the website -

[http://www.ochils8relay.co.uk/](http://www.ochils8relay.co.uk/)
