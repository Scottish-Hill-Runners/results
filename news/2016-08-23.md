**Tap o Noth Hill Race 2016**

Weather was dry and warm after a night of heavy rain.

The record was broken this year by James Espie, Deeside Runners in a time of 33:18 (previous record holder was Kyle Greig, Forress Harries, 33:26 in 2013). 1st female was Rachel Armitage, Aberdeen AAC in a time of 41:22.

Special thanks to brilliant time keepers - Rick and Claire Newman, Bob Sheridan and Joe Brown. The goodie bag crew Joy Murray and Caitlin Jallit. Route Marshalls Mathew Orr, Harris Williamson, Lewis Blockley, Summit Marshal Les Forbes from COTAG and cane sweepers Rod Campbell and Dennis MacDonald

And thank you for the annual support from land owner/framer Graeme Beverly for moving the cattle and using his fields and to John Mackie for cutting the grass and using the upper section of hill.

Jason Williamson
