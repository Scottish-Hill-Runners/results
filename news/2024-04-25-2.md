date: 2024-04-25
excerpt: "Wins for Alistair Thornton (Ochils) and Joanne Thom (Unattached) at the Beinn Dubh race on Wednesday 24th April. This was the second of this year's SHR Bog & Burn Championship races."
---

The drier weather in the past week vastly improved the running conditions at this year’s Beinn Dubh Hill Race. Sunshine along with a cool gentle breeze made for ideal racing conditions along a slightly different route to avoid the worst areas of footpath erosion. Alistair Thornton and Joanne Thom made best use of the conditions to claim first male and female respectively in a field of 97 runners.

Damien Theaker (Race Organiser)
