The fastest 5 descenders at Ben Lomond were Robbie Simpson, Prasad Prasad, Brian Marshall, Cameron Watson and Simon Peachey. The fastest 5 female descenders were Catriona Buchanan, Andrea Priestley, Iona Robertson, Eilidh Wardlaw and Nicola Meekin.
