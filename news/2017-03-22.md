This event uses ballot lists to allocate entries. _To get a ballot entry just enter as normal. No payment is required at this stage as there is **no guarantee of being offered a place**. If the Event Organiser is able to offer you a place they will send you an email inviting you to enter. You will then be asked to come back to this site and make your payment to confirm your entry. For more details please see our [**Ballot Help**](https://www.sientries.co.uk/help.php#BallotEntry)_ .

**Shettleston Harriers invites you to enter the Goat Fell Hill Race on Saturday 20th May 2017**

Incorporated as a 2017 SHR Championships Race (medium counter)

**Entry Process:**

*   Mar 22rd - Mar 30th - register to be part of the ballot via SiEntries and they will send you an email by way of acknowledgement (check spam filters etc); no limit on numbers; no payment details taken.
*   Mar 30th - race committee selects 250 runners from the ballot and these people receive emails inviting them to secure their place and pay via SiEntries (check spam filter etc).
*   Mar 31st - Apr 5th - runners secure their place and make £11/13 payment online (if you will not be around at this time then ask a friend to enter for you). You will receive a confirmation email (check spam filters etc), plus you will be listed on the race start list on SiEntries, if neither of these are present then you DO NOT have a place.
*   Apr 6th onwards - if any of the 250 places remain unfilled then these will be emailed out to a second round of invitees, with a new payment deadline, and the process will repeat in the following days until all 250 spaces are full. The aim is by Apr 5th all places will be allocated, confirmed and paid.
