title: "Devil's Burdens Race"
date: 2025-11-11
excerpt: "No Devil's Burdens Race for 2026"
---

The Devil's Burdens Relay will not be going ahead this year.

The weather (it has had to be cancelled three times recently) and the car parking situation has made it too difficult to go ahead with the race in its current format.

Fife AC are exploring some different options for the race for the future.
