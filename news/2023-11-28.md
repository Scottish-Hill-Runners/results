Mountaineering Scotland are collecting data on all incidents in the mountains to better understand what is causing accidents in the mountains and hope to provide better education resources to ultimately reduce them. If you have had an accident/incident in the Scottish Mountains where you self-evacuated, or needed help from someone or an organisation Mountaineering Scotland would like to hear from you. They are working in conjunction with SHR and other organisations that access the hills so please take some time to complete the survey below.

Mountain Incident survey went live on 27th November.

[Mountain Incidents Survey (surveymonkey.co.uk)](https://www.surveymonkey.co.uk/r/mountain-incidents)
