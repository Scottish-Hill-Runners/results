_From Stuart Simpson_

Perfect conditions for the second coming of the Harris Mountain Festival Hill Race on whats know as 'the postal track' between the villages of Reinegeadal and Urga. New course records for Craig Mattocks and Kate Jenkins respectively. Craig has a comfortable win although he was pushed on the first descent by young Herrach known as Duncan and a big name from the past who now lives in a Faroese outpost known as 'Ness' at the Butt of Lewis. Kate found time to stop and take lots of pictures. Lower turnout than last year due to a clash with a few other local events. My thanks for the Langley family of Reinigeadal and Matt Watt, Ranger for the North Harris Trust for their support and enthusiasm with this event. Probably going to be a 5pm start next year(which will still gives plenty of time for the evening ferry back to the wrong side of the Minch, sorry meant to say Skye)
