Stewart Whitlie continues to lead the [Scottish Long Classics Series](http://www.shr.uk.com/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006), and is the only runner to have completed all 7 races so far. Dave Eiser has moved into 2nd place, with Alan Smith 3rd. Alayne Finlay is currently leading the women's competition.

With 3 races remaining (Two Breweries, Morebattle Monster and Pentland Skyline), the clear favourite for the series has to be Finlay Wild with 3 wins out of 4 races, assuming he turns up to one more race. Likewise in the women's competition Angela Mudge and Jasmin Paris are both strongly placed on 3 races, so could still complete the series.
