SiEntries - [https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=2032](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=2032)

Registration from 9am at the Glencoe National Trust Visitor Centre. Registration will close at 10:45 am. Buses will transport you to the start and will leave at 11:15 am. There is limited car parking at the National Trust Visitor Centre please follow instructions on where to park. For additional parking space the National Trust have kindly given us sole use of the car park at An Torr for the day. This is approximately 2.8 miles south of the National Trust Centre on the A82 heading towards Glasgow.

Please be aware that there have been changes to the end of the race route to improve safety for runners given the anticipated increase in numbers taking part. The finish line has been changed to the Glencoe National Trust Visitor Centre thereby avoiding runners having to cross the A82. The last part of the route passes through an environmentally sensitive area and will be marked. All runners must comply with the route marking and marshals instructions.

**Start** : (**NM 973 447**)

Flags leading you onto the open hill.  
**Check point 1** : Beinn Churalain .549 (**NM 989 462**)

**Check point 6** :Gate at head of farm track (**NN 106 546**)

From check point 6 follow the farm track, there will be a marked diversion to route the race round some private property please follow this. At the end of the diversion you will be back on the farm track continue to follow it until directed by a marshal through the field towards the finish line at the National Trust Visitors Centre.

**Finish:** **Glencoe National Trust Visitor Centre** [**http://www.nts.org.uk/property/glencoe-and-dalness/**](http://www.nts.org.uk/property/glencoe-and-dalness/)

**Checkpoints**Marshals at each of the check points will mark your race number. You should note that the checkpoint grid references are approximate, read from an OS map not calculated by GPS.

**Retiring**Should you decide to retire from the race please report to the next marshal and also at the finish so that we know you are safely off the hill.

**Equipment**You are required to carry full body cover, a map, whistle and compass as well as a small amount of food. The organisers reserve the right to change the requirements to ensure compliance with the SHR guidance and best practice. You will be notified of any change.

**Rules**The race will be held under the rules of Scottish Hill Runners (SHR) a copy of which can be found on their web-site. [**www.****shr****.uk.com**](http://www.shr.uk.com/Safety.aspx)

**Finally**Good luck to all racing, we hope you enjoy the route, I have tried to improve the last section! We would also like to thank the various landowners who have given permission for us to race through their ground.
