The World Masters Mountain Running Championships visit the UK in 2015 after Betws-y-Coed was chosen as host location by the World Mountain Running Association (WMRA).

The stunning surroundings and challenging terrain of the Snowdonia National Park will play host to a week-long festival of mountain running this September as the renowned World Mountain Running Championships descend on Betws-y-Coed, North Wales, from Saturday 12-Sunday 20 September.

Taking place on the 19th of September, the World Mountain Running Championships will include senior men, senior women, junior men and junior women races as well as team events.

These races are the culmination of a week-long festival of mountain running which will also see the World Masters Championships take place on Saturday 12 September alongside a midweek programme of open races, school events, seminars, film screenings and more as part of an accompanying festival of sport and running in Betws-y-Coed and Snowdonia.
