As the busy racing season approaches, we thought we'd remind all race organisers that SHR have produced a free Race Organiser's Pack, with useful advice on all aspects of putting on a hill race. You can download the pack from this website, just click on this [link](http://shr.uk.com.95.seekdotnet.com/Download.aspx?Item=RACEORGANISERSPACK09.doc).

Meanwhile, The Cairngorm National Park Authority have recently published a "Guidance for Organised Outdoor Access Events" document - designed to help event organisers in the planning and management of organised outdoor events in the National Park. It follows on from an extended consultation phase to which SHR made a formal contribution.

This document is well presented and clearly written and any hill race organiser of an event taking place within the Cairngorms NP should be aware of the key recommendations. It would also be a very useful reference for race organisers in other areas and is available [here](http://www.cairngorms.co.uk/parkauthority/publications/results.php?publicationID=185).
