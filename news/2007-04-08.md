Provisional results for Norman's Law are on the Fife AC web site [here](http://uk.geocities.com/fife_ac/normanslaw.htm).

The first three runners were the same as last year, but this time Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) just pipped Chris Russell (Fife AC). Junior Alex Jones (Fife AC) was again third. Claire Gordon (HBT) was first lady, followed by Fife AC's Sarah Legge and HBT's Eilidh Wardlaw.

The results of the Emily Hill Junior Race (1.6km, 64m) were:

1 Suart Brooks (Alford Academy) 7.51, 2 Alasdair Mudie (Fife AC) 7.56, 3 Luke Foss (Law & Dist.) 8.00, 4 Logan Rees (Fife AC) 8.25, 5 James Pennycook (Fife AC) 9.31, 6 Callum Mitchell (Fife AC) 10.27, 7 George Rees (Fife AC) 10.35, 8 Euan Mitchell (Fife AC) 12.06, 9 Matthew Legge (Unatt.) 12.17.

Girls

1 Megan Gray (Law & Dist.) 8.54, 2 Stephanie Pennycook (Fife AC) 9.49, 3 Caitlyn Thomson (Fife AC) 12.06, 4 Katie Legge (Unatt.) 13.46, 5 Kate Biggar (Fife AC) 13.47.
