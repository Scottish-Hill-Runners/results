The tables have now been updated for the first two races. Holly Page (Carnethy) is so far dominating the Senior Women, while the Senior Men's looks very open. It's virtually all well-kent faces leading the veteran categories, i.e. Andy Fallas (O/40), Angela Mudge (O/40 & O/50), Kenny Richmond (O/50), Veronique Oldham (O/55), Colin Donnelly (O/55 & O/60), Hilary Ritchie (O/60), John Tullie (O/65), Murdo MacLeod (O/70). the only new name being Anne Craig (Carnethy) leading the Women's O/65.

In the Senior Men's team, it's a Carnethy v Shettleston battle once again, though Carnethy are keeping in front of a close chasing group in the Men's O/40 team. They are also leading the Senior Women's team, but it is Ochil's who are dominating the Women's O/40 team.
