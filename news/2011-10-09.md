Al Anthony (Ochil) won the Pentland Skyline in 2:38 from two Over 40s. Mark Harris (Fife) who just got the better of Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) for second place. First Over 50 was Gordon Pryde (Lomond) and first Over 60 Ray Murdoch (Lochaber).

Jasmin Paris (Carnethy) continued her great form in long races to easily win the women's race in 2:54. Second was Sharon Bird from Belfast in 3:01 and 3rd Charlotte Morgan 3:04. All brilliant times in the very windy conditions, which make it hard to stand on the tops at times, let alone move forward.

Carnethy won both men's and women's team contest.

There was a record field of 185, perhaps because it was the last counter in the SHR Long Classics Series.
