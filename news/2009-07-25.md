title: "Andy Symonds breaks Ben Rinnes record, 25 July"
date: 2009-07-25
excerpt: "In heavy underfoot conditions, Andy Symonds has broken the Ben Rinnes course record by 5 seconds, winning by over 5 minutes from Simon Peachey in 1:56:55.  '"
---

In heavy underfoot conditions but comfortable temperatures, Andy Symonds has broken the Ben Rinnes course record by 5 seconds, winning by over 5 minutes from Simon Peachey in 1:56:55. The previous course of 1:57:00 set by Jon Duncan had stood since the year 2000.

Simon Peachey finished 2nd in 2:02:09, a time which would normally have won the race, and former winner Stewart Whitlie, who got within 3 seconds of the record in 2005, finished 3rd in 2:03:34.

Megan Mowbray was first lady in 2:33:09, followed by Ellen Homewood in 2:35:27 and Veronique Oldham in 2:38:28.
