**Report**

**The Field, Dunkeld, was once again the venue for the Newtyle Hill Race on a cold spring night. The very low key informality of the race is quite pleasant, but a good number of runners turned out, total 49. Ever more locals appear on the scene and we can only encourage everyone to come and run in these beautiful surroundings. The route through Craigmad wood, around Fungarth walk and up Newtyle has become established. Thanks to Davie Duncan for marking the course with me and the landowners for their support and of course Dunkeld and Birnam Growers for their support and use of the facilities. . [https://communitysupportedagriculture.org.uk/csa/dunkeld-and-birnam-community-growing-the-field/](https://communitysupportedagriculture.org.uk/csa/dunkeld-and-birnam-community-growing-the-field/)**

**John Hammond led home the race in 31.38 with the first lady Marie Entwistle in 41.33.**

****Maybe we'll leave it until July, when its warmer next year!****
