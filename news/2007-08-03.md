---
title: "Mowbrays rule on the Law, 1 August"
date: 2007-08-03
excerpt: "Phil and Megan Mowbray were the first man and first woman in the North Berwick Law Race.  '"
---

A glorious evening attracted nearly 200 runners to the race from North Berwick's harbour up to the top of the Law and back.

Phil Mowbray (HBT) won in 19:54 from team-mate Dan Gay (20:04), with Carnethy's Garry MacInnes third and first vet (20:55). HBT took the team title, their third counter being Billy Minto.

Megan Mowbray (HBT) made it a family double by winning the women's race in 23:35; the vets' prize went to Dunbar's Rhona Anderson (26:30).
