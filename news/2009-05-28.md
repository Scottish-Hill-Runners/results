---
title: "Iida ninth in opening WMRA Grand Prix race, 21 May"
date: 2009-05-28
excerpt: "Edinburgh University's Yujiro Iida was ninth in the first of this year's WMRA Grand Prix races...  '"
---

The first round in this year's WMRA Grand Prix was La Montee du Grand Ballon in France on 21 May. Jonathan Wyatt (New Zealand) won the men's race with Edinburgh University's Yujiro Iida coming in a very creditable ninth of the nearly 500 finishers.

Robbie Simpson was an excellent 4th in the junior race.

Switzerland's Maya Chollet was first in the women's race with Great Britain's Candice Taylor third.
