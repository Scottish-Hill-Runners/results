---
title: "Fallon wins the battle of the vets at Merrick, 19 September"
date: 2010-09-20
excerpt: "Steven Fallon (Carnethy) wins from two M50s...  '"
---

Carnethy's M40 Steven Fallon got the better of two M50s, Peter Simpson (Carnegie) and Alan Smith (Deeside), with another Carnethy M40, Adam Anderson, in fourth.

The top three women were Fabienne Thompson (Carnegie), Shirley Singh (Unattached) and Julia Connor (Moorfoots).
