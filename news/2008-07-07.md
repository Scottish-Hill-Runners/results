**SAL championship hill race + U23 championship**

_Report from race organiser, Alex King:_

"The race started off in fine weather, but it soon started to rain. Fortunately the tops remained clear of cloud, making navigation a lot easier.

Jethro Lennox of Shettleston was first back in a time of 1:13:59 after leading the race from the start, but he was closely followed by Alasdair Anthony of Ochil finishing only 17 seconds later. Third was Murray Strain (HBT) in 1:15:54. Fourth and first vet was Colin Donnelly of Lochaber in 1:17:34.

Dan Halliday of Hinkley RC was the first under 23 in 1:22:22, first male super vet was Martin Laing (Fife AC) in 1:28:42 and first over 60 was Douglas Milligan, also of Solway, in 1:35:14.

The first Lady home was Jill Mykura of Carnethy in 1:30:18 followed 20 seconds later by Dawn Scott of Lochaber. The first Vet home was Louise Burt of Fife AC in 1:45:05 and the first under 23 was Rowena Bell-Scott of Edinburgh University in 1:45:19 and first Supervet was Alison West of Carnegie in 1:58:07.

Thanks to all the marshals for enduring the wet and windy weather, and those who helped out at the start /finish with the tea and cakes and thanks to Megan for the baking."
