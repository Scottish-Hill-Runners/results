---
title: "Tinto Hill Race Results"
date: 2016-11-13
excerpt: "Wins for Ewan Brown (Corstorphine) and Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) at Tinto on Saturday 12th November"
---

Thankfully the forecast rain never materialised and conditions were generally good if a little damp underfoot for this years edition of the Run And Become Tinto Hill Race. 216 runners finished with Ewan Brown of Corstorphine AAC taking first place for the men and Carnethy's Charlotte Morgan taking first ladies place. I'd like to thank the helpers from Carnethy HRC and Tinto Hill Runners, and also Adrian Stott at Run And Become for supporting the event.  
  

Cameron Scott, Race Director.
