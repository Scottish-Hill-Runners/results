It is with great sadness that we report the tragic death of Lisa MacDermid of Strathearn Harriers whilst negotiating the Aonach Eagach recently...

The accident happened on Saturday, 20th September. Lisa was running alone when she fell from the ridge.

Lisa had been a well known face on the hill running scene for many years. She regularly joined Ochil Hill Runners for club weekends away and social runs with her partner Bob Hughes. She was always a staunch supporter of long distance challenges by club members such as the Bob Graham Round and herself completed the Joss Naylor Challenge in recent years.

Lisa will be very sadly missed by us all and our thoughts go out to Bob, and Bob and Lisa’s families, at this time.

Lisa's funeral, a private, family occasion, will be held this week. However, a Book of Condolences will be available for signing on Monday 6th October between around 1100 and 1300 at Comrie Parish Church. A Minister will be in attendance and Bob Hughes will be there.

Dave Scott
