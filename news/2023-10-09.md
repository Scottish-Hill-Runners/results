title: "Morven - Race Report & Results"
date: 2023-10-09
excerpt: "Wins for Joe Wright and CJ Irvine (both Deeside Runners) at the Morven Hill Race on Saturday 7th October"
---

Wet day for a Hill Race, what fun! With the conditions, the race was shortened - the turning point was at the top of the second ascent section - about two thirds of the full race course (Poor visibility and low temperature), so no course records this year. However some very fast running, with Joe Wright winning overall in 32' 20", and also first to the turning point. First Lady home was C-J Irvine in 42' 29". First Junior on the Half Morven was Harris Comber - well done to Harris for completing his first hill race in such poor weather.

Warren Burgess (Race Organiser)
