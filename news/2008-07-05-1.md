Friday was a beautiful evening for the Whitetops Hill Race near Dundee which attracted 45 runners. The conditions underfoot were ideal compared to last year when the course was a mud bath!

The quality of the men's field was pretty high, and the winner was Dan Whitehead from Cosmics in a time of 34:17.

1st Lady home was Helen McKevitt (unattached ) in a time of 46:50.
