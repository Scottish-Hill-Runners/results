‘We are working hard to hold the Cairn William hill race on September the 19th. However, there are still some details to be finalised and a decision will be made by 31st August. Assuming we do go ahead online entries will open at 6pm on the 31st at [https://www.webscorer.com/register?raceid=218625](https://www.webscorer.com/register?raceid=218625) Entries will be strictly online advanced only, no one will be permitted to enter on the day.’

Christopher Hill (Race Organiser)
