---

Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) beat the two main contenders for the Bog and Burn Series, Murray Strain (HBT) and Andrew Wright (Carnegie), to win the Caerketton Hill Race. Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT) was the first woman, just ahead of Emma Birnie (Bellahouston) and Claire Gordon (HBT).

Cameron Law (Dunbar) triumphed in the junior race for the third time in a row, on this occasion from Anthony Addison (Corstorphine) and Robert Patterson (Shettleston). Hannah Addison (Corstorphine) was the leading girl, followed by Niamh Jarvis (HBT).
