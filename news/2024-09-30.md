The full results for the  races at Kincraig and Kingussie are on the SHR results tab. The winners at the Suidhe Hill Race on Saturday 21st September were John Yells (Highland HR) and Anna Meek (Inverness Harriers).

And on Sunday 22nd September, the winners at Creag Bheag were Rowan Taylor (Lasswade) and Jenny Singh (DDJC).
