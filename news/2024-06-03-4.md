#### **Race Day Information:**

*   **Dunkeld and Birnam Guide Hall,** School Lane, Little Dunkeld, Dunkeld PH8 0RQ
    *   From 9:30am
    *   registration hall is approximately 1 mile from the start time.
    *   [https://w3w.co/slap.inspected.dimes](https://w3w.co/slap.inspected.dimes)
    *   Bags can be left at the guide hall, left at own risk.
    *   Limited bag drop at start/finish line.
*   Prize giving will take place at the finishline.
*   Water & juice at start/finish line.
*   Public transport available.
    *   Train : Dunkeld & Birnam Station - [ScotRail link](https://www.scotrail.co.uk/plan-your-journey/stations-and-facilities/dkd)
    *   Bus : 23 Bus Route [Stagecoach link](https://www.stagecoachbus.com/routes/east-scotland/23/perth-city-centre-aberfeldy-or-pitlochry/xhao023.o)
*   The race will take place on trail routes in the beautiful and rugged Birnam woods, trail shoes are recommended for this race.
*   No changing facilities at the start/finish line.
*   Public toilets - [click here for latest information](https://www.pkc.gov.uk/article/19426/Birnam-Dunkeld-public-toilets)
*   Follow marshals instructions at all times.
*   Headphones and ear bubs not to be worn during the race.
*   Race number swopping between athletes is not allowed.
*   Wait list will be activated when entry limit reached.
