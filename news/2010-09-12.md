In 1685 the Covenanter John Hunter was shot dead whilst climbing out of the Devil's Beeftub in an attempt to escape from the dragoons who were chasing him. (There's a monument to him at the top of the climb, by the main road.)

In 2010 Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) was more successful, leaving his pursuers - notably Dan Gay (HBT) and Alan Smith (Deeside) - well behind to win the Beeftub Race by over a minute. Eve McCurrich (Tinto) was the first woman to finish, 15 seconds ahead of Julia Connor (Moorfoots).
