There's another chance to hear newly crowned British hill running champion Angela Mudge talk about her exploits on the Everest Marathon. She will be in Fort William next Wednesday, 12th November. She's giving her talk at the Ben Nevis Inn, Achintee, starting at 7:30pm. The entry is £5.00, payable at the door, with all proceeds going to a Highland cancer care charity.
