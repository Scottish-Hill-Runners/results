date: 2014-09-06
excerpt: "Iain Whiteside and Mary Lye (both Carnethy) win at the JBF Memorial Devils Beef Tub Race"
---

Iain Whiteside of Carnethy won the 2 mile JBF Memorial Devils Beef Tub Race in 24.34 ahead of Graham Nash, also of Carnethy and 1st in the MV40 category.

Terry McCloskey of Dumfries finished in 3rd place. The MV50, 60 & 70 prizes were won by John Robson (Annan), Bob Waterhouse (Carnethy) and Keith Burns (Carnethy)

Mary Lye was 1st Lady in 30.52. Category wins went to Janet Priestley (Annan) and Cali Ingham (Carnethy) for the FV50s & FV60s respectively
