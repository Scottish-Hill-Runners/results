New HHRC member Adam Park missed out on the course record by 2 seconds, and first lady [Alison Banford](https://www.facebook.com/alison.banford.71?fref=gs&__tn__=%2CdK-R-R&eid=ARD7fZ759IlRfZ7t32LvFMo7JzhGUKB93lkuR2Y2KCs2E5TB1MLO94hvntGKIrMzytr7GQW9ShHy8It-&dti=552275165282337&hc_location=group "Alison Banford") missed out taking Rosie's course record by 14 seconds. We had another record turn out of runners and it was great to see [Jean Robson](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100008144924144&fref=gs&__tn__=%2CdK-R-R&eid=ARCPEsSPntoFSO37y8Yo-Q3UWJc1uBE4h7cQ03mrGu7Er8F8EuYruWBGTZqVTdLBSN1jm43cERl0T1C7&dti=552275165282337&hc_location=group "Jean Robson") back running with us after 2 years out with injury.

A big thank you to our sponsors Moffat- [Moffat Outdoors](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100008921163961&fref=gs&__tn__=%2CdK-R-R&eid=ARCf8IT0REr9WdbuN_MXF7mNvcLF0JQk7Poop132WKA4kSA-rxcKHqF9glK-HNMbF-fCZfFU_5zFjN9B&dti=552275165282337&hc_location=group "Moffat Outdoors") and Ambacare for the prizes, the marshals, time keepers, those who took photos, land owners, [Nigel Priestley](https://www.facebook.com/nigel.priestley.7?fref=gs&__tn__=%2CdK-R-R&eid=ARBOIdFkTH5NtoTTm5farSYw51n77F2W5-I1YqS6c30WXT6QfJfuHYSVS3ep0rvUGpH3OwrS085NCaGa&dti=552275165282337&hc_location=group "Nigel Priestley") , [Jim Buchanan](https://www.facebook.com/jim.buchanan.98892?fref=gs&__tn__=%2CdK-R-R&eid=ARDLM8XlEMoWhqiarDbieFzptBLORvNN-6Ae5FzKBX84q_WEweZSEbjMAPPKTNnaKboH587Gq3EDyJQS&dti=552275165282337&hc_location=group "Jim Buchanan") , [Jean Robson](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100008144924144&fref=gs&__tn__=%2CdK-R-R&eid=ARBs5s2srBTv76J8GZXyMDeB3Ty4aT2Ef_MTZwyjahOxFl1LMSKAvFYZHB_6rWSmWFwEB2NvPX0DvrTT&dti=552275165282337&hc_location=group "Jean Robson") for contributing towards the cost of the after race refreshments and the Balmoral Hotel for stepping in at last minute with providing the said food.
