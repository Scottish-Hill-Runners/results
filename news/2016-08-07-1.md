Gordon Pryde and Neil Gilmore will complete their ‘terms in office’ as SHR Convenor and Secretary in October.

We are looking for volunteers to take over these roles at the AGM. If you are interested or would like a little more info then please contact Neil or Gordon at their SHR email addresses - [secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk) & [convenor@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:convenor@scottishhillrunners.uk)

Or contact any of the SHR committee that you already know.

We are also looking for a membership secretary to eventually take over from Tony Stapley who is keen to provide a hand-over period whereby the new person can shadow what Tony does for a year or so.
