Chris Hill (Cosmics) won the Cairn William race in a time of 49.21. It was the fourth win in a row for Chris!

First female was Rachel Armitage (Edinburgh Uni) in 57.04

The Pitfichie Junior Hill Race was won by Clyde Williamson of Huntly NSC in 29.41
