---
title: "Naylor turns tables on Whitehead at Kinnoull, 20 May"
date: 2009-05-25
excerpt: "Don Naylor (HBT) and Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy) win the main prizes at Kinnoull.  '"
---

Don Naylor (HBT) reversed last year's 1-2 with Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) and thereby won Kinnoull for the third time. Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy) was the first woman (and first FV40), ahead of former winner Sarah Blake (Penicuik).
