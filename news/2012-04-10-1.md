New course records for Joe Symonds and Catriona Buchanan

_Fron organiser Rob Brookes_

Fantastic turnout! 215 runners well and truly broke in the new Clachnaben race route Very fast times posted by men and women considering the recent snow melt Weather cool and clear, ideal for racing. Some great jostling on the ups and downs The new trophies were taken home by Joe Symonds and Catriona Buchanan Carnethy stormed to male team victory, HBT women dominated the female team category Corrected results will be published here shortly, apologies to those effected.

Huge thanks to the marshals, timers, cake bakers and all other helpers. Thanks to Fasque Estate for permission to hold the event, to Deeside Brewery for the beer and to The Running Shop for the generous prizes. Hope all the runners had a good day and enjoyed the route.
