Wins for Scott Fraser and Jill Stephen at the Baddingill Round Race on Sunday 28th August.

A new course record was set by Scott Fraser, taking 4 minutes of the last record, and the first person to have gone sub 90 minutes! Andy Nixon in 2nd and James Espie in 3rd place also broke the old record.
