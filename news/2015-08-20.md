**Kit requirements**

There will be a kit check at registration.

You will need to carry:

*   Full waterproof body cover
*   Hat and Gloves
*   Whistle and Compass
*   Map - one is supplied but we would advise carrying a more detailed one with you.

**Route changes**

A new forest has been planted in Menstrie Glen - between Blairdennon and Dumyat. See the map at [http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/infoFiles%5C130815063404.jpg](http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/infoFiles%5C130815063404.jpg) for further details.

Because of the planting the ground in this area has been dug up making many old routes almost impassable. Much of the planting is surrounded by a deer fence. Entry into this area **must** be through one of two gates at either NGR NS843998 or NS848993. Both of these gates will have kites and punches so you can prove you have used one of them.

The recommended exit from the fenced area is through the gate at NS838990 below Jerah.

Details of the crossing points are marked on the map supplied at registration.

For further updates, keep an eye on the OHR website at [http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/home.aspx](http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/home.aspx)
