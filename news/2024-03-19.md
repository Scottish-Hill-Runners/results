**Krunce 2024:** New Route (Figure of Eight).

**Start time:**

*   1900hrs (**September is 1830hrs due to darker nights**)

**Payment:**

*   £2 per race payable on the day (cash only)

**Race Limit:**

*   99 (waiting list available) (Event Licence is via Aberdeen City Council and capped at 99)

**Organisers:** 

*   [Cosmic Hillbashers (CHB)](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/www.cosmics.org.uk/__;!!O7V3aRRsHkZJLA!BaRT36NNM84IlGsSLpi_zCLkzPNOJzK85cnkYHX3q1CKYi2zv5F-9BaPn56M_DHDzt2KZAaYPLVXQqbxE5GrJZuO1y0X$)
*   [Insch Trail Running Club](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/inschtrailrunningclub.co.uk/__;!!O7V3aRRsHkZJLA!BaRT36NNM84IlGsSLpi_zCLkzPNOJzK85cnkYHX3q1CKYi2zv5F-9BaPn56M_DHDzt2KZAaYPLVXQqbxE5GrJXtRRyVx$)(ITRC)
*   [Garioch Road Runners](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/www.gariochroadrunners.com/__;!!O7V3aRRsHkZJLA!BaRT36NNM84IlGsSLpi_zCLkzPNOJzK85cnkYHX3q1CKYi2zv5F-9BaPn56M_DHDzt2KZAaYPLVXQqbxE5GrJQVwdotg$)(GRR)

**Series Dates:**

1.  1.  2nd April 2024: CHB
    2.  7th May 2024: CHB
    3.  4th June 2024: ITRC
    4.  2nd July 2024: GRR
    5.  6th August 2024: GRR
    6.  3rd September 2024: ITRC

**Parking:** 

*   Craibstone Golf Course Car Park (please use overflow parking - like the January Elbrimick Race)
*   Please be considerate to the Craibstone Golf Course Members and their carpark.
*   Please note the Golf Course may be hosting tournaments in April and May and do expect possibly a higher use of the car park should the tournaments continue into the evening.

**April to August:**

*   Registration and Payment:  We are not using the Brimmond Cafe. We are using the changing rooms. 
*   Please note: 2024 the Krunce organiser are paying the Craibstone Golf Course a nominal fee for each month to use the facilities.
*   Please remove muddy shoes before entering the facilities, please.

**September:**

*   Registration and Payment
*   Brimmind Cafe full access
*   Prizegiving: Brimmond Cafe

**Permissions:**

*   This race would not be possible without the generous permissions from Aberdeen City Council and Craibstone Golf Course. Thank you.
