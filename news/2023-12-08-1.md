The inaugural Gight Castle 8 race was held on Saturday the 18th of November at Methlick.

88 Runners competed the race on a dreich day. 

Wins for Euan Wilson 56:05 (Insch Trail Runners) and Emma Murray 1:05:55 (JSK)

The race was a sell out and proved very popular, lots of challenging terrain, plenty of climb, speedy descents and mud, with a Highland Games style lap of the village cricket to compete.

A get together in the Beaton Hall with soup, Butteries and Rocky road plus a unique medal made by race volunteers from a whiskey barrel ?? added to the buzz of the day.

Keep an eye out for the date for 2024!!!  (Sarurday 16th November)

Hugh Robertson (Race Organizer)
