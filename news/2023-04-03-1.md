**_Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay - Sunday 3rd September 2023_**

_Strathearn Harriers are happy to confirm that this year's race will take place on Sunday 3rd September and will, as always, feature 4 legs totalling 42km of stunning Perthshire scenery, varied terrain to suit hill runners and trail-runners alike and post-race refreshments including our legendary barbecue and home baking._

_This year, especially given the postponement of the Devil's Burdens relay in January, we expect to reach our race limit of 35 teams and so will be accepting a maximum of 4 teams (of 4 runners) per club. All proceeds from the race will again be donated to NHS Tayside Endowment Trust to support their research into pancreatic cancer._

_So save the date in your club calendars and start planning your teams!_
