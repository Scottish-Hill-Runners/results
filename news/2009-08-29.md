A reminder that the end season prize giving and social SHR 'do' will be held at Badaguish over the weekend of 30th and 31st October. 4 of the self catering lodges (sleeping max 14) have already been block booked by various groups and so there is only now very limited accomdation left in the remaining 1 lodge available. Any member(s) wishing to book accommodation at Badaguish (@ £40pp for 2 nights) for the weekend should book their place ASAP via the Treasurer, Louise Burt, contact details below.

Places for the actual prize giving, dinner and follow up (Ceilidh) entertainment for the night of sat 31st are also filling up and are limited to a maximum of 60 spaces. Again, to avoid disappointment, any member wishing to attend the 'do', whether staying at Badaguish or not, should book a place by contacting Louise Burt in the first instance. Inclusive price for the evening is £26pp.

Contact details for Louise are:

Louise Burt, 14 Wallace Avenue, St Andrews, Fife, KY10 8AP.

Please remember when booking to include a contact email address if possible, so Louise can confirm your place quickly and with minimum effort.
