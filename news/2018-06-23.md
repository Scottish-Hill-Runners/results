So the aim of the challenge is to accumulate the same climb in height as going from sea level to the top of Mount Everest in less than 72 hours. I will do this by climbing a little hill called Dumgoyne which is not too far from where I live. It's not the biggest of hills at just 427m but for those who have ventured to the top will know its no walk in the park. Each climb is approximately 360 m so 24 should get me almost to the height of Mount Everest at 8848m. Those with calculators will have worked out that I'll be 200m short but I aim to make this up with a few route variations.  

The challenge will begin on Thursday the 21st of June at 7pm and I'll aim to be finished in time for tea on the Sunday. I'll probably aim to climb 3 or 4 at any one time so if you fancy coming along to experience the best little hill in Scotland the support would certainly be appreciated. I'll update my progress and start times in the news section.

Gordon Bulloch
