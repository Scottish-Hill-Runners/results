---

The Senior race turned into a battle between two previous winners, Brian Marshall of Haddington and Moorfoot’s Darin Dougal with Brian taking the honours. Ann Aitken of Teviotdale was first lady.

In the junior race, Teviotdale Harriers’ Fraser Clyne and Maisie Ballantyne were the winners.

Full results for the senior race on the SHR Results tab.
