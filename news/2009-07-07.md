_From Chairman of Selectors Mark Johnston:_

**Information about the selection procedure for the 6th WMRA Long Distance Mountain Running Challenge (Tour de Tyrol – Kaisermarathon) 42km 1700m**

**Race date**: 10 October 2009; **Selection date**: 31 August 2009

**Size of team**: Up to 5 males and 5 females (team managers: TBA)

**Pre-selections**: Accepted - Jethro Lennox (Shettleston), Declined – Angela Mudge (Carnethy)

**Selection Notes**: From form in appropriate long mountain/hill races up to the selection date. Selectors will consider form from Alpine-style long mountain races similar in nature to the Kaisermarathon. The recommended Scottish races in which to show form are Ben Rinnes (25 July; 23km 1500m), Great Wilderness Challenge Long Course (22 August; 40km 900m) or Ochils 2000 (29 August; 28km 1500m). Those with a strong record in long races may also be considered without having done one of the above races, but must show evidence of general hill form by the selection date.

**Funding**: Those selected who compete in the event will receive a subsidy from the event organizer and **scottish**athletics towards travel and accommodation. The level of subsidy is to be confirmed.

The event's web site is [here](http://www.tourdetirol.com/english/tour_de_tirol/tour_de_tirol/kaisermarathon.html) and there's more information on **scottish**athletics' web site [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=4337).
