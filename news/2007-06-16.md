---
title: "Stevenson the Younger and Mudge triumph at Ben Sheann, 13 June"
date: 2007-06-16
excerpt: "Jamie Stevenson (Ochils) beat all his elders and Angela Mudge (Carnethy) smashed the course record she set in 1996.  '"
---

Last year’s race was run over a modified course, thanks to storm damage. This year the traditional course was open again, and Angela Mudge (Carnethy) took the opportunity to lop one-and-a-half minutes (5%) off the record she set eleven years ago,

Equally impressive was the feat of Jamie Stevenson (Ochils), last year’s British U14 Champion, who must surely now be the youngest person to have won a Bog and Burn race? In doing so he presented Race Organiser Archie Bennie with a problem, for the victor’s prize was whisky. Fortunately Stevenson the Elder (fifth overall and second M40) was on hand to solve it for him.
