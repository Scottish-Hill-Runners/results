---
title: "Scottish Mountaineering guidelines on the return to hill running"
date: 2020-05-30
excerpt: "Please run within your local area and do not travel to rural communities to access different or bigger hills"
---

Sportscotland have asked the hill running community to follow Scottish Mountaineering guidelines on the return to hill running. Please run within your local area and do not travel to rural communities to access different or bigger hills.

Link to Mountaineering Phase 1 Guidance [https://www.mountaineering.scot/coronavirus](https://www.mountaineering.scot/coronavirus)
