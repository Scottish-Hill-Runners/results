Bwystfil y Bryn (Beast of Bryn) Race – 30th May 2015 – Port Talbot

The Bwystfil y Bryn consists of 2 races - 6.8 and 15 mile alpine trail races taking in spectacular views. Post race and pre race entertainment with 5 live bands, marquee with licensed bar, children's rides and loads more!

Full details here - [www.bwystfilybryn.btck.co.uk](http://www.bwystfilybryn.btck.co.uk/)
