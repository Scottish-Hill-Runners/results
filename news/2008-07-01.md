Following Red Moss, the 7th race in the 2008 Bog and Burn Series, the only significant move is that Dougal Ross has leap-frogged Steven Fallon back into 2nd place.

Bob Wiseman continues to lead the series with his 4 good runs at Whangie, Kinnoull, Kilpatricks and Cort-ma Law.

Jethro, Al Anthony and Donald Naylor are all lurking in the wings on 2 races, and any of these guys could still win the series with 3 races remaining.

In the women's competition, Angela Mudge has essentially already won the series. Ellen Homewood is lying in 2nd position, but could still get overtaken by Elke or Iona from Bellahouston, both of whom have only completed 2 races so far.
