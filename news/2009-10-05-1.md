_From race organiser Steve Waites:_

After the howling gales of the Saturday, which made marking out the course quite interesting at times, Sunday dawned calm, dry, and while not exactly tropical, pleasant enough to entice 124 starters to the twentieth edition of this popular event, which is also the concluding race in the Aberdeenshire Hill Race Series.

The conditions certainly suited Chris Smith who travelled up from London to set a new course record of 53:51, finishing over four-and-a-half minutes ahead of second-placed Dan Gay (HBT).

The ladies’ race was won by Sula Young (HBT) in 1:04:30, who also had a comfortable lead over Veronique Oldham (Cosmics), second in 1:08:19.

The junior race went to Sam Burgess (Deeside) in 20.57, with Micaela Brown (Pitreavie) taking the girls' prize in 25.58.

Winners of the **Aberdeenshire Hill Race Series** were Dennis McDonald (Cosmics) in a combined time of 2:50:09 and Ruth MacKenzie (Deeside) in a combined time of 3:09:27. The full results are [here](Download.aspx?Item=AberdeenshireSeries2009.pdf).
