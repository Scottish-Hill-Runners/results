Due to bad weather conditions the Aonach Mor Gondola and the top and bottom restaurants were all hit by a power cut and so the race was changed to a 5 mile trail run - mainly in the forest, which helped give some protection to the runners from the heavy freezing rain.

A large field of around 130 took part and the race was dominated from the start by Dave Rodgers (Lochaber) who led all the way. In second place and closing in on Dave towards the end was Andy Fallas (Carnethy). Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) had another age defying run to finish 4th and first Over 50.
