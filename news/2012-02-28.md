The UKA Inter Counties Fell & Hill Running Championship takes place at the Shining Tor Fell Race in the Peak District, Derbyshire on Saturday 19 May, over a course of 12km with 533m climb. Full details about selection of Scottish District teams can be found [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=6083)

Note that this year’s event also incorporates a junior championship
