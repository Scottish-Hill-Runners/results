title: "Whangie Whizz Cancelled"
date: 2012-04-15
excerpt: "Please note that the Whangie Whizz Race scheduled for 25 April 2012 has been '*cancelled'*."
---

Please note that the Whangie Whizz Race scheduled for 25 April 2012 has been \*cancelled\*. The reason is we have been unable to find suitable car parking facilities. In previous years we have run a bus from car parks at the the nearby scout camp and the fishery, however these locations are no longer available to us. Therefore we have no option but to cancel the race.
