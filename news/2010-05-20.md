---

Mike McGovern from the host Club Moorfoot won the Gypsy Glen Race at Peebles (and was also the first M40). Portobello's Gareth Green was second and another local, Darin Dougal, third.

Course record-holder Susan Ridley (Edinburgh AC) was the first woman (and first F40), 20 seconds ahead of Kate Jenkins (HBT). Rachael Fagan (Gala) was third and Scout Adkin (Moorfoot), who earlier this month won the **scottish**athletics U19 Championship, was fourth.
