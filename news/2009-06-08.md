Adam Ward (Carnethy) won this race, which is unique in passing from Scotland into England and back again. He beat local boy, Damon Rodwell, with Darin Dougal (Moorfoot) in 3rd. Darin's clubmate Julia Connor was first woman.

_Organiser Les Turnbull comments:_

"For once the weather was ideal for both runners and marshals. Following last year's poor display of navigation, competitors were given another opportunity to find their way round in a anti-clockwise direction Most managed to stay reasonably close to the route although one Norham runner who has never missed this race was overheard asking a marshal where to go next.....never follow a local !!!!

No such problems for Adam Ward, home first in a new record time for this direction, a record also for V50 Russell Anderson with Moorfoot clubmate Julia Connor taking the ladies' race. Moorfoot also took the team prize.

Norham RC used the event as a fundraiser for the [Border Search & Rescue team](http://www.bordersar.org.uk/site/) who were in attendance as usual. V60 winner Keith Burns was their only customer of the day, falling over the finish line in his efforts to secure a highly-prized bottle of beer - although a fast-moving Dick Wall caused a major scare at the start of the race, almost taking out the whole field (he was in his car at the time).

Thanks to all who took part. "
