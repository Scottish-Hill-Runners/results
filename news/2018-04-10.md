---
title: "Change To Printed Calendar - No Craigengower Race"
date: 2018-04-10
excerpt: "The Craignengower race which is the printed calendar as 25 Aug TBC will not take place this year."
---

A decision has been taken not to hold Straiton Fun Day this year. As a result there will be no Craigengower Hill Race in 2018 and unlikely to be one in subsequent years.
