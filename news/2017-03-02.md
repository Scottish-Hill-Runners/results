title: "Bishop Hill Race - Sunday 5th March - 2pm"
date: 2017-03-02
excerpt: "Some reminders about race registration, parking and correct kit."
---

Here are some reminders about the Bishop Hill Race on Sunday. Registration is from 12-30 at the Portmoak Village Hall, Scotlandwell. The race start is 5 minutes away, up the hill. Please arrive early as parking is tight in the village. Once the church car park is full it may be necessary to park in Kinnesswood and jog/walk to registration, a distance of about a mile. Please don’t block in other vehicles as the area is popular with a variety of outdoor people and they may not be involved in the race. Remember to bring body cover. The forecast is quite good but there could be rain and the temperatures are to be cool. 10 to 15 year olds can do the junior race to half way and back. Look forward to seeing you.

Richard Gatehouse
