Organiser Stuart Simpson sends this report:

"Another fine Easter Sunday evening for this race and the later start time did make a difference to the numbers with many entrants having already done Normans Law in the afternoon. Dan Whitehead had a comfortable third win here, followed by HBTs Ray Ward, with third place going to the only runner under 30, Jamie Crowe. The first lady was Emma O'Shea of Deeside Runners. I haven't seen the Normans Law results, so I don't know was best in the combined event - thats something to consider for next year. As always, thanks to the marshalling team."
