_From Organiser Richard Gatehouse_

If you're coming to the Bishop Hill Race on Sunday please arrive early. Parking is can be tricky and we wish to avoid causing inconvenience to the village of Scotlandwell. There is street parking a mile to the north at Kinnesswood but allow time to register and get up to the start from there. The church car park in Scotlandwell is available once the congregation has vacated the spaces. Please do not block in other vehicles or park on the main road. The area is popular with outdoor enthusiasts and not every vehicle will be involved in the race. In the village hall there will be a 'bucket collection' for the Emergency Helicopter based at Scone which run as a charity. The weather forecast is favourable and we hope you have a good run.
