The Highlander 2015 will take place in the wonderful hills around the village of Elphin in Sutherland on weekend of the 25th & 26 th May. Entry places are still available for each of the courses; A-D Class as well as Score Class. More information on the Hands On Events website ([www.handsonevents.co.uk](http://www.handsonevents.co.uk/)) and to enter visit [https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=1726](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=1726)
