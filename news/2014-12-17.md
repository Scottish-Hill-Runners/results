Entry forms are now available for the Devil’s Burdens relay race on 24 January 2015. Please note that there are changes to leg 3 (now a paired leg) and leg 4 (solo runner). There are team categories for seniors, V40, V50 and V60 for men and women and a mixed category. All details (including maps) at  
[http://fifeac.org/events/fife-ac-events/devils-burdens/740-devil-s-burdens-2015.html](http://fifeac.org/events/fife-ac-events/devils-burdens/740-devil-s-burdens-2015.html)
