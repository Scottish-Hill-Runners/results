It’s a little under three weeks to the Durisdeer race on Saturday 2nd July This is the fourth race in the 2022 SHR Championship series and also the Scottish counter in the British Championship this year.

Online entry is available via Protay - [Durisdeer Hill Race 2022 (protay.co.uk)](https://protay.co.uk/durisdeer22/)

Note that there is no entry on the day this year, but anyone unable to enter online can contact the race organiser, Adam Anderson, and be added manually.
