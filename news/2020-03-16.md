_I've made the decision to postpone the Alex Brett Memorial Cioch Mhor. We do not yet have a new date for the race, and I don't expect we will be able to confirm one any time soon._

_If you had offered to volunteer we are very grateful you were prepared give your time to support this event. Without volunteers events like this would not exist._

_If you have entered to race you will be contacted in the next couple of days via SI Entries with the options available regarding refunds. Please do not attempt a refund before this time._

_Hopefully see you at a re-scheduled Cioch Mhor or another event before too long._

_With kind regards,_

_Sam Hesling._

_Race Organiser_

_Highland Hill Runners_
