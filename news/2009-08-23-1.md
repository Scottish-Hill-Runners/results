_From organiser Roy Morrison:_

How did we get a dry day? - luck seems to have followed this race and the Straiton Fun Day for several years! The turn out was lower than in previous years, runners probably thinking about wet conditions! The hill itself was slightly damp apart from a short soggy section at the bottom. Wood and field sections were a bit wet but it did not detract from a new ladies' record for Toni MacIntosh, shaving two secsonds from the mark she set last year.

The race was won in a good time of 17.46 by Nick Emsley of Arran Runners, almost a minute faster than his time of last year. Juniors Michael Jenks (Arran), William Limond and Ross Paton (both unattached) posted great times for youngsters with promising futures.

Not only the start of the Straiton Fun Day, this is the penultimate race in the Ayrshire Hill Runners championship so it was a good mix of fun and serious runners, all welcomed. Thanks to all marshals and helpers - come again next year and bring your friends to a great wee event - 21 August 2010 - I think!
