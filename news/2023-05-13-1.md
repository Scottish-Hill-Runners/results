---
title: "Gargunnock Hill Race"
date: 2023-05-13
excerpt: "Details for the race on Saturday 3rd June"
---

Entries on day only from 10:30am onwards in white tent beside main show ring at Gargunnock show (opposite primary school).

If runners make stewards on the gate aware they are doing hill race, they should not need to pay entrance to show - only for the race.

Race starts 12:30pm. Prizes for top 3 males and top 3 females.
