title: "Mark Harris first at Largo Law, 8 August"
date: 2009-08-10
excerpt: "Mark Harris (Fife AC) wins from Peter Buchanan (Portobello RC); Ali Hughes (Aberdeen Metro) is first lady...  '"
---

Vets were in the ascendancy at Largo Law, taking the first four places overall and providing two of the top three women. The race was won by Mark Harris (Fife AC), with Peter Buchanan (Portobello RC) second and Martin Laing (Fife AC) third.

Ali Hughes (Aberdeen Metro) was the first woman home, followed by Susan Johnston (Corstorphine AAC) and Jocelyn Scott (Fife AC).
