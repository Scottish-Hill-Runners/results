---
title: "WMRA Grand Prix bronze for Joe Symonds"
date: 2007-10-10
excerpt: "Whilst brother Andy was winning the Pentland Skyline at the weekend, Joe Symonds did well enough in the final WMRA Grand Prix Race to take the overall bronze medal."
---

Joe Symonds "had a rather disappointing run and could only finish 8th" in the final WMRA Grand Prix race on the Smarna Gora, in Ljubljana (Slovenia) on Saturday. However that was enough to give him third place overall in the WMRA Grand Prix Series for 2007. Well done Joe!
