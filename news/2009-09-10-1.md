Rather belated congratulations to the Scottish junior men - Alex Hendry (15th), Robbie Simpson (18th) and Scott Macdonald (19th) - whose excellent performances led them to take a bronze team medal in the World Championships, held in Italy on 6 September. This result matched their earlier showing in the European Championships in July. The only other Scot in the GB teams - gaining World Championship status for the first time has meant the loss of the Scottish team - was Joe Symonds, who finished a very creditable 40th amongst the senior men. The GB senior women finished a magnificent 2nd, led home by Sarah Tunstall who missed out on an individual medal by a mere 25 seconds in finishing 4th.

There's a press release [here]( http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=4486) and the full results can be viewed [here]( http://www.wmrc2009.org/results). There was also a very interesting article about the championships in US magazine [Running Times]( http://www.runningtimes.com/Article.aspx?ArticleID=17558).
