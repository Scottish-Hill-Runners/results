> If you have a few minutes spare then taking part in this study on psychological factors and overuse injuries in runners and triathletes might be of interest. The  study explores the relationship between individual psychological factors and the onset of overuse injuries. Specifically, the study explores exercise dependency, perfection, and previous injuries. It has ethical approval from the University of St Andrews.
