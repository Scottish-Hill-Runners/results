---
title: "Cosmics' Summer Hill Race Series begins on Tuesday (15 May)"
date: 2007-05-14
excerpt: "The Durris Mast Race on Tuesday 15 May is the first of the four races in the Cosmics' Summer Series."
---

Cosmics are organizing a Summer Hill Race Series – in and around Aberdeen. The four races vary from short and sandy to long and craggy. Prizes will be awarded for the best results over the four races.

Durris Mast Race Tues 15 May

Balmedie Beach Bash Tues 19 June

Mither Tap Tues 17 July

Cheyne Hill Tues 21 Aug

The first race of the series is the Durris Mast Race on Tuesday 15 May at 7pm. A new race for 2007, the route uses landrover tracks, small forest trails and a short section through heather. There are two major climbs and a downhill finish. For more details go to the [Cosmics web site](http://www.cosmics.org.uk).
