---

The first counter in the Dumfries and Galloway hill running series takes place this Sunday at Criffel. The series is best four results from six regional race: Criffel, Screel, Carsphairn Show, Durisdeer, Moffat Beeftub and Merrick. An additional race has been added this year which seems to have gone under the radar for a while, The Carsphairn Show hill race takes place on the 6th June at 15:00. The race is around 4km with a shorter race for juniors, it’s about as low key as a race can get, I was the only adult runner last year accompanied a short way by the only junior entrant who ran in shepherds waterproofs and wellies in a very respectable time. The organiser of the show has offered a £50 prize for the fastest time. Adam Anderson
