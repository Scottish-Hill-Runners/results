The record-breaking fell runner Nicky Spinks will appear as a headline afternoon presenter at this year’s Peebles Outdoor Film Festival (27-29 Jan). Nicky’s Live to Run session begins at 2pm, Sun 29 Jan with a wonderful selection of short films that celebrate the joy of running in beautiful places, before Nicky takes to the stage at 3pm to talk about her remarkable ultra-running life – including last year’s Double Bob Graham Round.
