title: "Whitehead better than ever at Grandtully, 30 March"
date: 2008-04-02

---

From Race Organiser Stuart Simpson:

It turned out to be a perfect evening for a hill race with Dan Whitehead knocking 6 seconds off his own course record in less than perfect conditions underfoot. Messrs Gorman and Smith were second and third respectively with first lady to Brenda Paul of Westies.

A big thanks to Irish Canoe Team coach Taidgh McIntyre for doing the race timing and arriving at the start by a most appropriate means of transport, down the Tay in a canoe. Taidgh's brother is the Mcintyre who likes to investigate things on Channel 4 so maybe he'll be along next year to investigate the Grandtully Hill Race.

Nobody wanted the magic brush set at the prize-giving so that's been kept over till next year as a booby prize for whoever I see fit to give it to.
