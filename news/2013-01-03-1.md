_From Ian Adams_

It was close only one second in it ,but the usual record breaking Finlay wild never quite made it today at the Aonach mor uphill,so Robbie Simpson hangs on to his record set in 2009 ,but only just, had the under foot conditions been more favourable on the top half of the course I am sure Finlay would have claimed another record,but overnight snow above 200m made it difficult for all our runners, as for the over head conditions close to perfect for running , but very cold for our marshalls and timers, a big thank you to these guy's also, Nevis range, and also all 86 competitors who helped us kick of the new year in Lochaber.
