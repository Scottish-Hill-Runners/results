The weather gods kept changing their minds as they looked down on the third staging of this race on East Lomond; a day of bright sunshine, strong cold winds and a brief smattering of rain. Winter snow drifts had finally melted, leaving decent underfoot conditions. A healthy total of 39 athletes from 12 clubs were well supported over demanding, rugged terrain and the wind helped carry the runners along to some fine finishes; five of the day’s winners improved on the leading times set over the same courses in 2012. Well done to all competitors, and we look forward to welcoming you back next year; bring your friends, your woolly hats and your sun cream.  
Very many thanks to Dave Francis for marking up the course, to Graham Kirby for taking photos, and to the large band of Fife AC members, (and an Anster Haddie too), for their marshalling, time-keeping and distribution of chocolate and juice.  
The Junior League bandwagon rolls on next to Ben Lomond on the 11th May, where the races double up as the Scottish National Championships.  
Roger Rees
