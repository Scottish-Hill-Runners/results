_From Stuart Simpson_

A very closely contested race saw Ochill's Gary Fraser overtaking 3 runners on the descent to win by 3 seconds from last year’s winner Alan Smith. Julia Connor of Tinto was first Lady (and first female vet), with first and only runner in the senior men’s category, Owen O'Neil fourth. Junior runner Andrew Barnett came in in sixth place and the first place in the 'true love' category went to Gus and Jean Bowman who crossed the finish line holding hands. Tilly Smith did not finish last as she predicted before the race and Brian Brennan did not beat Davie Duncan but did give it a good try. A slight to correction to the calendar for the race in Harris on 13 July. Although in the Calendar the race is advertised as 'The Clisham,' the race is to be known as 'Away you go and take a run up the Cliseam you,' which is a local term for telling someone to shut up when they are talking rubbish and all proceeds of the race shall be going to Hebrides Search and Rescue who are being very helpful with my attempts to turn a road/track/cross country club, Stornoway R.A.C. into a hill running club.
