Previous UK competitors in this event include Craig Mattocks and Angela Mudge. This is a message from the organiser:

Our web site [www.everestmarathon.org.uk](http://www.everestmarathon.org.uk) has now been updated with details of the next Everest Marathon in Nov/Dec 2017. Click on **Next Race** where you will also find an application form.  
This asks you for details of recent fell, mountain, trail and endurance events.  
We cannot offer places to people who only have experience of road running.  
If you require any more information, just telephone or e-mail!

Once you have been offered a place, we shall send you a password so that you can access the ‘Members’ area of the web site so that you can check out other details before you return your booking form and deposit.

I look forward to hearing from you.  
Diana Penny Sherpani  
Race Organiser
