**Long Classics Series Update after 4 races**

Latest scores after 4th race in series, Arrochar Alps, as per link below.

We have a new leader in the men’s table, **Tim Morgan** of **Carnethy**, after a fine win at Arrochar Alps at weekend.

**Chloe Lumsdon** of **Ochils**, continues to lead the women, with an equally fine win at Arrochar.

Also well done to **Chloe** and **Kerry Cunningham** of **Westerlands**, who are the only 2 people to have completed all 4 races in the series to date!

Next race up in series is Ben Rinnes, on 30th July, organised by 4 times Long Classics series winner, Jill Stephen!

Race is part of Dufftown Highland Games which always makes for a great atmosphere. Entries now open.
