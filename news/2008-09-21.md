From organiser Clare Martin:

It was a very warm day for the Morven Hill Race, with strong headwinds to the top. First to the top and well ahead at the finish was Graeme Bartlett of Forres Harriers, showing the younger lads how it is done. Ruth MacKenzie of Deeside Runners (also a vet) was first female. There were 44 finishers.

There was a new course record for the Mini Morven, which is for U12s/U14s and is run over 1.8km with 108m climb, Hamish Battle completing the course in 8 minutes 55 seconds. It is good to see a couple of father-and-son combinations in the results. No mother and daughter yet - maybe next year?
