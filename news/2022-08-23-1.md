For any clubs still planning to enter the British Athletics Fell and Hill Relays on 15th October, we (Carnethy HRC, the organising team) will be starting to approve entries from the start of September, since we know that a lot of you will be eager to start organising transport and accommodation.

We have a limit of 175 for the event and have 110 teams in pre-selection so far, so may need to prioritise A teams across categories before accepting B teams in a first pass and are liaising with British Athletics on the fairest approach. We'd encourage you to try to enter as early as possible to help with this. If all entries are filled, we will use a waiting list which can be joined until entries close on 1st October.

Any questions at all, see [](https://carnethy.com/british-fell-relays-2022/)or email [carnethyrelays2022@gmail.com](mailto:carnethyrelays2022@gmail.com)

We are excited to see you all up in Scotland!
