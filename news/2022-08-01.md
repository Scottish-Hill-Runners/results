The 2022 edition of the British Athletics Fell and Hill Relays, which will be hosted by Carnethy HRC at Broughton Heights in the Scottish Borders on October 15th, is now open for entries.

The race website is live at [Carnethy race website](https://carnethy.com/carnethy-organised-races/british-fell-relays-2022/) and entries are on Si Entries at [SiEntries for this event](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=10071).
