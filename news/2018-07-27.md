title: "The Stag To Hind Hill Race - Tuesday 31st July"
date: 2018-07-27
excerpt: "Annan & District race a 7pm on Tuesday 31st July evening. Donations to Moffat Mountain Rescue"
---

The Stag to Hind Hill Race is a short 3ish miles Hill race, it starts outside the Stag Hotel in the Moffat market place, and is to the top of Hind HIll then back to the Stag. Free sandwiches and chips are then served in the pub afterwards, there is no entry fee but donations to Moffat Mountain Rescue are accepted. Registration opens at 18:15pm and closes at 18:45pm. The first women and the first man get an impressive trophy to keep for one year of a Red Hind and Red Stag respectively.
