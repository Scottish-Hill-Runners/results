The Senior Men's championship is now down to a battle between Rory Abernethy (Ochil) and Robin Downie (Lochaber) while the Senior Women's is going to Ella of Westerlands (although whether that is Peters or Foreman remains to be seen!). The other yet to be decided categories are: Men's Over 50, which is between Kenny Richmond (Shettleston) and Andrew Macrae (Carnethy); Over 55 Women between Jane Oliver (Deeside) and Nicky Spinks (Dark Peak); Over 60 Women, one of Jane Oliver, Joan Wilson (Lomond) or Hilary Ritchie (Fife); Over 65 Men is between Gordon Pryde (Lomonds), and the Westerlands pair of Don Reid and David Dickson.

Men's Senior team is between Carnethy and Ochils, while the Men's Over 40 team is between Shettleson and Carnethy. Westerlands has already won the Women's Senior team.
