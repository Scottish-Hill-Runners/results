Robbie Simpson was in a class apart as he beat his own course record at the Kilpatricks race, knocking 8 seconds off his 2009 time to record 42.59. Kenny Richmond was second (and first V40) and Matt Sullivan third, whilst Christina Rankin was first woman, ahead of Julia Connor and Alayne Finlay. Fiona Dunlop was first FV40.
