# TAP O’NOTH HILL RACE

 This year the race will be held on Saturday 19th August and will also have a junior race.  As ever the race is also part of the Rhynie gala so a great day out. 

Pre-entry for the main race can be made online through the link below however entries will be accepted on the day (although pre-entry makes it all easier).  Please note that pre-entry does not cover payment so no financial loss if you enter but can't make the race.  

[Tap O Noth Hill Race 2023 | Event registration | Webscorer](https://www.webscorer.com/register?raceid=316592)
