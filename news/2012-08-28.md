---

The day started warm and sunny – this year we had planned to set up the finish early so we would not be caught out by the first finisher. And we almost made it – Murray Strain o HBT finished in a new course record of 2:39:17 just are we almost had everything setup. He was so fast he managed to beat the rain, the same cannot be said for the rest of the field all of whom got soaked. Second Place was Paul Raistrick in Inverness Harriers in 2:52:29 closely followed by the first super vet – Stuart Whitlie of Carnethy in 2:53:08. The women’s race was won by Jasmin Paris in 3:08:38 followed by a very close race for second place won by Sarah O’Neil in 3:21:47 with Andrea Priestly right behind her. Everyone made it to the finish inside 6 hours just as the rain stopped.

Alex King
