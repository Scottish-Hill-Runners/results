**Articles**

We are looking for articles on hill running, hill races, training for the hills, fun in the hills, fun off the hills, adventures far afield or close to home; prose or poetry, fact or fiction - all styles, plots and viewpoints welcome.

It's been an unusual year for everyone, but with more time on your hands why not put pen to paper and tell us your stories!

**Short-Cuts**

In addition to standard articles, this year we're trying something slightly different and are asking people to submit 'Short-Cuts'. These are mini-articles of 200 - 300 words on the same topic.

With everyone in lockdown, we're looking for people to write on the topic of 'What I Miss About Hill Running'.

If you would like to make or discuss a submission please get in touch with [james.callender@live.co.uk](mailto:james.callender@live.co.uk)

**\*\*\*Deadline - 31st December\*\*\***
