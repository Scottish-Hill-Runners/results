---
title: "White Tops Race Results and Report"
date: 2018-06-18
excerpt: "Wins for Joseph Wright (Deeside) and Anwen Darlington (Dundee Road Runners)"
---

There was a record field for this year’s race with 83 completing the course on a cool but dry night. First home from Deeside Runners was Joseph Wright closely followed by Andrew Merry of Glasgow University Hounds and Hares. The battle for 3rd place was contested by veterans Brian Bonneyman (Westerlands) and Ochils’ Bob Wiseman with Brian managing to hold off the challenge along the road to the finish.

First lady home was Anwen Darlington of Dundee Road Runners who retained her title while getting close to her course record set last year. Second went to Maria Sheeman of Forfar Road Runners followed by Louise Andrews of Dundee and Angus Canicross club.

Male team prizes went to Deeside Runners with Dundee Road runners claiming the Female title.

Billy Gibson (race organiser)
