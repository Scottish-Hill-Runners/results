Stewart Whitlie leads the Scottish Long Classics series by the narrowest of margins from Brian Marshall. Mark Harris has moved into 3rd place. Emma O'Shea leads Jacqui Higginbottom in the women's competition with 3 races remaining. The next race in the series is Ochil 2000s on 28th August.
