Keswick will host the trial race for the World Mountain Running Championships (UKA team) and the Commonwealth (Up and Down) Mountain Running Championships (Scotland Team) on Sunday 16th August.

Final details for the event, which include the selection policy for the UKA and Scottish teams, are now available [here]( http://shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=COMMONWEALTH WORLD Trial.doc).
