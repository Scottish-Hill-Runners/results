---
title: "Colquhoun finishes 12th and first Briton in World 100k Challenge"
date: 2008-11-06
excerpt: "Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) finished an excellent 12th, and first GB lady, in the IAU's 100k World Challenge in Italy on 8 November."
---

Lucy Colquhoun had an excellent debut in the World 100km Challenge in Italy on Saturday, finishing as first British Lady in 12th place, in a time of 8:09:27. There's a report on the Carnethy website [here](http://www.carnethy.com/#November)

Her selection was a well-deserved recognition of Lucy's fine recent form, notably her spectacular victory in the 98km Courmayeur Champex Chamonix (CCC) trail race round Mont Blanc in late August. Her wins over the hills in 2008 include the Highland Fling, the Lairig Ghru, the Lomonds of Fife, Morrone (at the Braemar Highland Gathering), the Two Breweries and the Pentland Skyline.

There's more information on the **scottish**athletics web site [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=3847). (Lucy is also on the front cover of and features inside the latest edition of [myRace](http://www.myrace.co.uk/).)
