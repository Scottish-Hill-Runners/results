The [Long Classics Series](http://shr.uk.com/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006) is shaping up for a fitting finale at Pentland Skyline on Sunday 13th October.

Al Anthony has taken a slender lead over Mark Harris after finishing 3rd at the Two Breweries, but Mark could still win the series with a strong run at the Skyline.

Lisa Gamble now leads the womens' competition, although Jasmin Paris and Charlotte Morgan are both well positioned on 3 races, so either Jasmin or Charlotte could win if they complete the final race.
