---
title: "Records tumble at Conic as Prasad and Mudge lead the charge"
date: 2012-02-26
excerpt: "On a fine February day Angela Mudge and Prasad Prasad take the honours home (which in both their cases isn't far).....  '"
---

On a fine day for a hill run a record 139 runners completed the challenge of the climb up Conic hill. Equally challenging were some sketchy sections on the descent that tested grip of many a fell shoe.

At the sharp end Prasad Prasad went one better than his Carnethy result and in doing so took the victory and a couple of minutes off the course record.

Westies U20 Alastair Graves took a fine 2nd place, just holding off Portobello's Gareth Green.

Ladies winner Angela Mudge was in fine form demolishing the previous course record and finishing in tenth overall

A great run by Manny Gorman won him the M40 race, likewise John Stevenson took the M50 prize after a storming descent making light work of the eroded sections of the West Highland (worth noting that proceeds from the race are going to path repairs)
