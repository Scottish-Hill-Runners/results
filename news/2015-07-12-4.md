title: "DMRT - An-Teallach Hill Race Results"
date: 2015-07-12
excerpt: "Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) and Sue Wood (Highland Hill Runners) won the inaugural An-Teallach Hill Race on Saturday 11th July."
---

The inaugural An-Teallach Hill Race in aid of fund-raising for the new Dundonnel Mountain Rescue team base was won by Iain Gilmore (Carnethy). David Wilby (Highland Hill Runners) and Alan Smith (Deeside) were 2nd and 3rd respectively.

Sue Wood (HHR) was first female ahead of Gill Irvine (HHR) and Helen Renard (Lochaber).

Sue and Gill, along with team-mate Shona Mackie, won the female team prize for Highland Hill Runners. The male team prize was won by Carnethy – Andrew & Iain Gilmore and Steven Fallon.
