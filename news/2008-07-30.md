The summer race up Aonach Mor is organised by [Off Beat Bikes](http://www.offbeatbikes.co.uk/). Martin of OBB reports:

The conditions were dry and fine with a good turn out of local and visiting runners who enjoyed a good ascent up the mountain bike track to the top station where a wee prize-giving was held. Thanks to everyone for turning out and also to Nevis Range for letting us run the race and use their facilities.
