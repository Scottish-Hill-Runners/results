The annual school meal and hill race that is the Carnethy 5 takes place tomorrow. Those who are taking part will have had emails from the race organiser reminding them of the race protocol (and to use the buses and not to park on the A702). This is a wee reminder that there will be an SHR stand in the registration hall. Those of you already paid up can collect your calendars et al, and those of you still to join or renew can pay your subs on the day.

If you have joined but are not going along to Carnethy but have a friend/team mate attending please arrange for them to collect your calendar if possible.

If you have not yet joined and are not going to Carnethy please see the link above for how to join on-line.
