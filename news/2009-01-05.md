---
title: "More records blown away: Aonach Mor, 1 January"
date: 2009-01-05
excerpt: "Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven) wins again; Nicola Meekin (Lochaber) joins him as the new course record-holders...  '"
---

The traditional New Year's Day race up Aonach Mor got 2009 off to a record-breaking start. Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven) followed his Law Breaker win four days before by beating local favourite Dave Rodgers (Lochaber) and taking his record as well. Nicola Meekin (Lochaber) was also in record-breaking form, holding off Dawn Scott (Lochaber) for the ladies' prize.
