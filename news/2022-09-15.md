On 5th August Robin Downie completed the inaugural Martin Moran Round in 20 hours 51 minutes. An initial report, with links to other sources, is available here.

[MM Round report](https://scottishhillrunners.uk/download.aspx?item=Martin Moran Round.docx)

In due course the report will be extended with further information by Peter Duggan, the SHR Long Distance Records archivist, and will be available in that section of this website.

This is a significant addition to the major long-distance challenges in the UK and it offers interest and difficulties not always found in the others.
