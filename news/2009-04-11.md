---
title: "Donnelly takes Norman's Law, 11 April"
date: 2009-04-11
excerpt: "Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC) won in a field of 84; Morgan Windram-Geddes (Fife AC) was the first women.  '"
---

Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC) won Fife AC's traditional Easter Saturday race up and down Norman's Law from 83 others after a close tussle with the host club's Chris Russell. Neil Martin, also of Fife AC, was third and Konrad Rawlik fourth.

Fife AC supplied the first and third women (Morgan Windram-Geddes and Susan Thomson); they were separated by Meike Cooper (Perth RR).

The Emily Hill Junior Race went to another Fife AC member, Adam Scott; Jennifer Cruickshanks (Anster Haddies) was second
