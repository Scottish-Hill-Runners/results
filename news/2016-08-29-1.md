A challenging but beautiful off road run taking in views over The Clyde, Arrochar Alps, Loch Lomond and the Trossachs.

[http://www.strava.com/activities/245820843](http://www.strava.com/activities/245820843)

Starting from Helensburgh (Hermitage Academy). The course uses the 3 Lochs Way and John Muir Way to provide a stunning off road route that packs an impressive 1253ft of elevation into 12.5km. It's not easy but it's worth it.

From Hel' n back is now open to runners aged 18 years or older.
