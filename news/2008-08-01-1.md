A reminder of the Comrie Hills Relay.

All hill running clubs may wish to consider getting some teams together for the Comrie Hills Relay. This is a superb 4-stage relay totalling 43 miles and 2150m and takes place on Sunday the 14th of September.

Although this is a tough event the 1st and last legs are more akin to trail running whilst the 2nd and 3rd will test seasoned hill runners.

Now in its 4th year this friendly event boasts wonderful scenery and a great barbeque at the end. For more information go to [www.strathearnharriers.org.uk](http://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/comriehillsrelayrace.htm)
