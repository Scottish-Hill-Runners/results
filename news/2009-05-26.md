It was the worst conditions at Jura for many a year with rain, low clouds and strong winds so times were the slowest since 1995 when, with similar conditions, the winning time was 4:26. This year Ian Holmes took the leading group off course, lost 15 mins or so, but pulled back through the field and won for the 7th (?) time.

Alec Keith took full advantage of other people's navigational mishaps to lead for much of the race before eventually Ian ran him down, leaving Alec 2nd and the only other sub 4 finisher, while a superb run from Al Anthony in his first ever Jura race (sensibly keeping with the right people including Angela Mudge) saw him race through the field from Corra Bheinn to the finish to come home 3rd in 4:04. Angela Mudge won again, but not so easily as before due to a slight error off the 3rd Pap that let Helene Whittaker past with Angela only regaining the lead on the run down to the Three Arch Bridge.

An epic event with the whole race being a survival experience, but one that people will talk about for quite a while.
