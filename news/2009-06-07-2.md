The LAMM, held in Kintail this year, enjoyed good weather all the while that competitors down in the Welsh 1000m peaks were being airlifted to safety in very poor conditions. Headline results were a win for Andy Symonds and Jethro Lennox in the Elite, The Carnethy pairing of Jon Ascroft (obviously recovered from his Screel ordeal) and Steve Fallon winning the A class, and young guns Oleg Chepelin and Robbie Simpson blasting the B.
