The FRA are looking for articles for the next edition and are keen to have representation from the other home countries. They need any articles by October.

Please contact them at [Fellrunner magazine](https://www.fellrunner.org.uk/fellrunner.php) for further details and the absolute deadline. The magazine editor's email link can be found on the Committee page. Graham Breeze is the fundamental archivist and historian of all things Fell Runner.

This thick magazine is normally published about three times a year.
