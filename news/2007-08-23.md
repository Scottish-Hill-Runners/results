Scottish Hill Runners have made a hotel booking for the [World Masters]( http://www.berglaufteam.com/index_e.html) in Bludenz, Austria.

The space is nearly full up and you need to book now if you'd like to join us at this great event.

We’re using the [Hotel Einhorn](http://www.schlosshotel.cc/einhorn.html). It’s the cheaper annexe to the Schloss Hotel, and is on the edge of the town centre and convenient for the race start.

The cost is 29 euros per person per night for bed and breakfast when sharing triple rooms, or 32 euros pppn if you want a double room. The hotel is booked for the 3 nights of Friday, Saturday, Sunday - 21st, 22nd, 23rd September. The actual race is on the Sunday.

To secure your place please send a 50% deposit (that’s £60 for a double room, or £27.50 for a bed in a triple room) to:

Louise Burt, 14 Wallace Avenue, St Andrews, KY16 8AP

E-mail her to check availability: louiseATmcs.st-and.ac.uk

Please make cheques payable to \`Scottish Hill Runners\`.
