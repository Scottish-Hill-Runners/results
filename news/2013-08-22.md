It was fine evening for a race, no rain and no midges and a good turnout of 47 finishes, 3 having lost the route on the way.  
The course was a little soft and a bit over grown with tall bracken in places so a course record was unlikely.  
A short but varied course on a mixture of single track paths through forest, heather and bracken. - approximately 4 miles, 740 ft of climb (6.5km / 226m).

Note that there were three juniors racing, but two lost their way.
