SHR navigation course - Pentlands – 6th/7th April 2013

Following the success of previous courses, and in an effort to help more runners run safely in the hills, SHR are organising another course for hill runners on the weekend of 6th/7th April based somewhere in the Pentlands (hopefully Hillend ski centre).

The course will take place both in the classroom and outside over a weekend, and is aimed at developing hill runners’ navigational skills with regard to races and general trips to the hills. The course is intended both for runners who have limited experience of hill running and for experienced hill runners who wish to brush up their skills. The principal instructor will be Steve Fallon, qualified mountain leader, keen hill runner, and multiple Munroist.

The course will run from around 0900 until 1700 on both the Saturday and Sunday. There will be practical outdoor sessions on local hills on both afternoons. The cost of the course will be £35 for SHR members (£60 non members) – accommodation extra.

Places are limited and acceptance will be on a first come first served basis. To register your interest, please contact Dave Scott at dcbscottATtiscali.co.uk as soon as possible.

Further details and application forms available soon!
