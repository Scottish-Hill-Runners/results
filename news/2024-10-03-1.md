Entry via Protay - [Kirk Craigs Hill Race 2024 (protay.co.uk)](https://protay.co.uk/kirkcraigs2024/)

**Date and Time**Saturday 21st December 2024 @ 11.00am  
**Registration/Bib collection**9.30am at Centenary Hall, Hamilton Street, Tillicoultry. Please bring cash on the day.  
**Race Start/Finish Location**At the top of steps on the east side of Tillicoulty Glen (the same as for the Maddy Moss Race start)  
**Refreshments & Prize Giving**Prize-giving, tea and cakes back at the hall after the race.  
**Refunds/Transfers**All money raised will got to local charities. Entry fee is a minimum of £10.00 but please feel free to pay more.

  
**Parking**  
Please park considerately. More space at Upper Mill St. near the race start.  
**Important Info**Full waterproof body cover, hat, gloves, whistle, map and compass are compulsory.
