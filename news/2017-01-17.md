SHR navigation course - Pentlands – 11th/12th March 2017

Following the success of previous courses, and in an effort to help more runners run safely in the hills, SHR in conjunction with [Steven Fallon](http://www.stevenfallon.co.uk/index.html) are organising another course for hill runners on the weekend of 11th/12th March in the Pentlands.

[More details]( http://www.stevenfallon.co.uk/navigation-shr.html)
