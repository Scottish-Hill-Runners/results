The inaugural Beinn Resipol hill race will be held on the beautiful Ardnamurchan peninsula on the 19th September 2015. With a peak elevation of 845m climbed to from sea level over approximately seven miles of running, competitors will be treated to glorious views of Loch Shiel and Sunart. All proceeds of the race will be donated to a designated local charity.

Entry

Race HQ will be based in the Beinn Resipol campsite with the start and finish lines very close to hand. You can enter the race now through Entry Central. Entries on the day will be £5 but pre-entries will be discounted.
