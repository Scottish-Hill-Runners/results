Alec Keith continues to lead the [Scottish Long Series](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006), following his 6th place Arrochar Alps, and having completed the first 4 races.

In 2nd place in the Long Series, and first MV50 and the only other person to have completed the first 4 races is Martin Hulme.

Strongly placed in 3rd position is Simon Peachey, with a near perfect 2996 points out of a potential 3000 points for 3 races. Simon still has 5 races remaining in which to achieve 2 more wins and close out on the maximum 4000 points.

First lady overall is Ellen Homewood, closely followed by Joanne Anderson, with Jo Schreiber in 3rd place.

There are 5 races left in the series, with the next race being [Ben Rinnes](http://scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0082) on July 26th.
