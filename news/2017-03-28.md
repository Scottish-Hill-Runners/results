Join the April version of the Aonach Mor Uphill running race which uses the Nevis Range Downhill Red Mountain Bike Route for an Uphill Race!

*   Enter on the day,
*   Registration opens at 2pm,
*   Race starts at 3pm,
*   Length is 5km and 500metres of ascent,
*   Cost £5 per person to include gondola return to bottom station,
*   Great race for beginners to hill running,
*   Everyone welcome!
