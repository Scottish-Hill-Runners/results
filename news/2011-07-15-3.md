_From organiser Andy Kitchin_

A fine evening, a fine race enjoyed by a select 40 of whom Ailsa Bennet won the junior race with brother Struan first boy home. Andy Fallas was the quickest over the full course whilst women’s winner was Claire Gordon. The support and enthusiasm of the farmer, Mr Fletcher, over whose land the race is held is a joy to behold and greatly appreciated.
