Here's details of this year's Achnamara 10k race. The run is a circular route on forestry track passing a couple of small lochs (one of which has a couple of resident beavers!). The rest of the information about the timing of registration etc you'll find on the forms below. The route has 889ft of ascent and 966ft of descent.
