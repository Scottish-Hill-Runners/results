Durisdeer race organiser Adam Anderson has been in touch to ask that we remind runners that entries will be taken on the day (Saturday). See the race entry in the wee box on the right hand side for further information.

The recheduled Knock of Crieff race will now take place on Wednesday the 20th of July at 7:30. All the rest of the race details remain unchanged.
