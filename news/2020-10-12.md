---
title: "Birnam & Deuchary Postponed Until November"
date: 2020-10-12
excerpt: "The Birnam & Deuchary races will not go ahead on 17th/18th October as planned. If circumstances permit, they may go ahead on 14th & 15th November"
---

Unfortunately due to the coronovirus we have postponed the Birnam and Deuchary Hill races until 14-15th November. Hopefully restrictions will be lifted by then. We have cancelled Craig O Barns completely this year as it has become impractical to run both Birnam, Deuchary and Craig o Barns so closely together.

Please contact me if you need further information.

Refunds or deferrals will be organised through Sl entries and an email will inform all participants in due course.
