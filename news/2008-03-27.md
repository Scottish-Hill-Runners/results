Dear Friends,

At short notice, Jackie Newton at Puma has arranged for "The 2 Kips", two of Africa's rising stars, who are in town for the World Cross, to visit Run and Become for an informal question and answer session.

This is a unique opportunity to be inspired by 2 of the world's most promising young athletes: 10,000m commonwealth champion from Melbourne 2006, Boniface Kiprop and 5,000 m bronze medallist from Osaka 2007, Moses Kipsiro.

Look forward to seeing some of you on Monday evening, 31 March - 6.45pm.

Adrian
