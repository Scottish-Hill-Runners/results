**Date**: Saturday 15th June 2024  
**Cost**: £2 (please bring correct change or send entry fee by bank transfer, see above)  
**Age**: U11's and over. See below for race details.  
**Terrain**:  Hill race with grassy and rocky underfoot conditions.

**Kit**: You may need to run with full body cover as required per Scottish Hill Running rules. We will make a decision on the day depending on weather conditions. Expect to run with kit (Waterproof jacket, trousers, hat/buff, gloves, map & compass).  
**Registration**:  Registration will be held at the Greenock Glenpark Harriers club house, Orangefield, Greenock until 1:00pm. From 1pm onwards registration will be at the start location at Woodstock Road, Greenock, PA16 9HS.  
**Start and Finish Location**: (What 3 Words location) ///memory.march.comet  
**Route**:  [www.strava.com/routes/2903625505479723262](https://urldefense.com/v3/__http:/www.strava.com/routes/2903625505479723262__;!!O7V3aRRsHkZJLA!Cifm61q7p1sojd-2VbkZLBiJ0qMtOA2O71ecXQymfbP-7_6SW9def47Va8U9Oz7HBbbVWglfItQzA13p08PAch0AtJv0$)  
**Getting here**: The easiest way is by train to 'Greenock West', a short walk to the GGH Clubhouse at Orangefield (other trains available). There are plenty of parking options for those driving. Please carshare where possible.  
**Scottish Athletics**: please note that to be eligible for Scottish Athletics championship medals, you will need to be a paid member of Scottish Athletics. By providing your SA number below will confirm your eligibility. Junior runner will also gain points which will go towards the [Scottish Junior Hill League](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/www.scottishathletics.org.uk/events/leagues-and-grands-prix/junior-hill-league/__;!!O7V3aRRsHkZJLA!Cifm61q7p1sojd-2VbkZLBiJ0qMtOA2O71ecXQymfbP-7_6SW9def47Va8U9Oz7HBbbVWglfItQzA13p08PAcukhhKkB$).  
**Post race**:  Medals will be awarded once all races have finished.
