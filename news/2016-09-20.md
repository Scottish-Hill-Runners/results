Junior Home International Mountain Running Championships

17th September, Skiddaw, Keswick

Scotland won 2 individual golds and 2 team golds in the JHI. Euan Gillham (Kilbarchan AAC) was a clear winner in the U20 boys and Grace Whelan (Moray) won the U17 girls in a closely fought battle, the first 3 finishers crossing the line within seconds. The courses were uphill courses on the slopes of Skiddaw, all four races finished on Little Man in gorgeous sunshine.

TEAMS

Gold for U17G and U20B

Silver for U17B

Bronze for U20G

INDIVS

Gold for Euan Gillham (Kilbarchan AAC) and Grace Whelan (Moray)

Silver for Lynn McKenna (Giffnock North)

Bronze for Tristan Rees (Fife AC)

4th place for Charlotte McKenna (Giffnock North) & Logan Rees (Fife AC)
