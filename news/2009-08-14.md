**Men's competition**

Murray Strain of HBT has won the 2009 Bog and Burn series with 3 race wins plus a 2nd place at Caerketton. Andrew Wright of Carnegie finished 2nd overall, with Kenny Richmond 3rd. Oleg Chepelin finished 4th despite leading the Bog and Burn for much of the early part of the season.

In the male vet 40s, Steven Fallon finished first, followed by Andy Birnie and Bruce Smith.

The male V50s competition was hotly contested between David Dickson and Russell Anderson, with David just getting the better of Russell in the overall results.

Ian McManus finished as first Over 60, despite taking a tumble at Dumyat and breaking ribs and then missing some of the mid-summer races.

And for at least the third year in a row, Davie Duncan of Ochils completed all 10 races in the series.

**Women's competition**

Val Houston of Westerlands won the ladies competition with Bellahouston's Iona Robertson and Emma Birnie in 2nd and 3rd positions.

Jackie McGuire of Bellahouston was first female Over 40.

Alison West of Carnegie won the Over 50 age category as well as completing the most races amongst the women, with 7 of 10.
