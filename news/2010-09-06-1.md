---
title: "Fraser is first again at Braemar, 5 September"
date: 2010-09-06
excerpt: "Scott Fraser (Edinburgh) and Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) win the Morrone Hill Race at the Braemar Highland Gathering...  '"
---

Scott Fraser (Edinburgh), who was sixth in the Long event at last month's World Orienteering Championships, repeated his win of 2008 in the Morrone Hill Race (part of the Braemar Highland Gathering). He was chased home by two current members of Edinburgh University Hare and Hounds, Hector Haines and Duncan Coombs.

The women's race was a 1-2 for Cosmics, Clare Whitehead retaining her title from Veronique Oldham. Becky Law (Eryri Harriers) was third.

Unfortunately eleven runners didn't make the 45-minute cut-off at the finish (imposed because of the busy Games schedule).
