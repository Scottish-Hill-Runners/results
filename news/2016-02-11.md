---
title: "2016 SHR Race Calendar and Journal will be available at the Carnethy 5"
date: 2016-02-11
excerpt: "Calendars and Journals can be collected from the hall at registration"
---

The 2016 SHR Race Calendar and 2015 Journal will be available on Saturday in the school hall at registration at the Carnethy 5.

If you are a SHR member, please come along and collect your copy as this will save on costs posting them out to members.

And you’ll be able to join the SHR or renew your membership at the hall if you haven’t done so already.

We will also have the 2015 SHR trophies and mugs for those who haven’t yet collected theirs.
