**SHR Championship Mugs - Get yours now!**

**This year's SHR Champs mug was designed by Bill Breckenridge from Shettleston, following stiff competition from lots of talented hill runner creative types.** 

**Mugs will be handed out (to all SHR members who have completed 4 of this year's 6 races) at this year's awards and ceilidh on 8th November. We will try and make sure someone from each club collects for those that can't make it.**

**We've ordered a few extra as we knew they would be popular :). These can be purchased on a first come first served basis for cost price of a fiver. If you'd like to get on the list then email [alpringle68@gmail.com](mailto:alpringle68@gmail.com) and we will let you know if we have enough and how to pay.**
