If you are entitled to a 2022 (or 2021) SHR mug then I will have them at Birnam on Saturday so please seek me out and let me pass it across to you.

Hopefully I will be parked close to the registration venue at the Guides Hall behind the school. If you don’t know me then just ask at registration please.

If you are unable to be at Birnam, can you ask a club mate to pick it up for you?

I’m hoping that there will be a at least someone from each of the clubs that will be prepared to take away the relevant mugs for those who have earned them.

Alternatively, let me know and I’ll arrange with you to get the mug to you at another race sometime during the season…….  Contact me via email address on the website - [committee8@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:committee8@scottishhillrunners.uk)

But ideally I would like to get all the mugs to their rightful owners on Saturday before the race.
