DMRT An Teallach Hill Race Report & Results

It was good to see a lot of runners from the central belt this year which was a direct result of the Scottish Championship race in Torridon the previous day.

A total of 43 runners took part with many having tired legs from the previous day.

We have been plagued with bad weather in the past, but this year was perfect with clear views of the ridge and a breeze at the start to keep away the midges.

The record was broken by almost 2 minutes by Alister Masson Carnethy in 1:12:50 followed by Joe Mann HHR, and Sam Alexander Westies.

For the ladies Fiona Bunn Edinburgh Uni was first home Followed by Lizzie Knox, Carnethy and Oonagh Thinn u/a

I haven't done the final sums yet but we will have raised about £1000 for DMRT

Many thanks to all the runners who entered and those who helped to make the race a success.

Ray Wilby
