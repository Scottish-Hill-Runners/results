_Dumfries and Galloway Hill Running Championships

It is intended to hold a hill running championship during 2012 over five Dumfries and Galloway races.

Criffel, Screel, Durisdeer, Merrick and Devils Beeftub.

Winners from each age category would be decided on best four results from the five races. If no-one in an age group has completed four races then it will be decided on three but four completed races would always win over three regardless of result.

The championship is open to all who complete the required races. At the moment trophies are available for some categories but I'm looking into securing more so they can be presented to all age group winners.

These are all excellent races so come and enjoy the South West.

Adam Anderson
