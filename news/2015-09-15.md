Carnethy won this year's edition of the Simon Wake Comrie Relay after leading from the gun. It was cool, overcast and dry, so good running conditions and also for the cake-fest & BBQ afterwards, for which Strathearn Harriers always do a brilliant job. Luckily the rain held off until after the prize-giving.

Carnethy led from the first leg, helped by fastest leg runs on the first two legs. However, they were pushed hard all the way by a strong Ochils team, who finished just under 3 mins behind, and had the fastest leg (and a new record) on leg 3. Westerlands had to content with 3rd despite a storming last leg which was nearly 4 mins faster than any other team. HBT were first female team, although only just in front of Ochil female vets team, who therefore were first female vets. Carnethy were first male vets, Westerlands first Mixed Vets and HBT first Mixed.
