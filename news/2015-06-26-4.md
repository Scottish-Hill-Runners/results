Note that the race is full.

The Race Organiser will compile a reserve list on the following basis.

Applications for entry allready posted and in the post system will be allocated first available places, should they arise.

Thereafter if people email their note of interest I will allocate them in order that the emails are received. Please email [lmacewan@hotmail.co.uk](mailto:lmacewan@hotmail.co.uk). Include name, address, age, club and evidence of three applicable races, including dates.

Any postal applications received after today would have been too late for an entry anyway. I will however endeavour to contact them and get them to email a request in and they will join list 2 above.

Please note that emails to any other address than that shown above will not be counted.

I will run the list until the 25th July so if people who have a place and can’t run can get in touch as soon as possible via [lmacewan@hotmail.co.uk](mailto:lmacewan@hotmail.co.uk) this will let me allocate places to people on the list in turn.

Linda MacEwan

Race Organiser Two Inns
