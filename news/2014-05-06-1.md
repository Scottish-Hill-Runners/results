_From Alec Keith_

This year’s Highlander Mountain Marathon was based in Glenfinnan, and took place on the weekend 26/27 April. On Day 1 the courses took competitors westwards over the rugged Rois-bheinn hills of Moidart to the bijou overnight camp in the grounds of Inverailort House. There was the usual marquee with catering, drinks licence and ceilidh. Day 2 saw competitors return to Glenfinnan via the remote hills around Loch Beoraid on the north side of the Mallaig road). On Day 1 the weather started damp and cloudy, which may have contributed to one competitor breaking a leg early on, necessitating a helicopter rescue. Conditions improved thereafter, with a pleasant afternoon, and Day 2 was a beautiful warm summer’s day, so no excuses for navigational misplacements! 211 teams entered, although there were a number of retirals as the toughness of the terrain took its toll. The event website (with route details, photos and results) can be found at http://www.handsonevents.co.uk/?page\_id=186
