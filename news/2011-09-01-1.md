The Morven race is at 1pm on Sat 17th September. In the evening Deeside Runners are organising a ceilidh at Dinnet village hall. Start 8pm. All runners and runners friends welcome. Tickets £10, £5 for children. Contact deeside.runners@rowanbank.eclipse.co.uk if you want to go.
