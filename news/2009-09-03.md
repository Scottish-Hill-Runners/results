The Scottish teams for the inaugural [Commonwealth Championships](http://www.cumbriacommonwealthchampionships.org/) to be held in Keswick later this month have been named. There are mens and womens teams for both uphill only and up and down events:

There's a press release giving more details [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=4479)
