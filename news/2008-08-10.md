In very testing windy conditions over a shortened course, it was Murray Strain who emerged as the winner of the Cairngorm uphill race. In a close finish he crossed the line ahead of Al Anthony, Jethro Lennox, Colin Donnelly, Prasad Prasad and Ray Ward. In a similarly close finish to the ladies race, it was Lyn Wilson who got the verdict, after a long battle with Elke Schmidt. Claire Gordon was 3rd, followed by Edel Mooney and Sue Ridley. Mhairi Inglis won the Junior Women's race, ahead of Jessica Martin and Christina Rankin. Robbie Simpson won the Junior Men's race, and was followed in by Scott Macdonald and Alexander Hendry. The Scottish teams for the World Trophy will be announced shortly.
