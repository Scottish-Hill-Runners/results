**A cold but clear and sunny night greeted the 55 runners on the Newtyle hill race this year. First home was David Elder bounding down the hill to win in 32.17. First home for the ladies was Diana MacDonald, making it a habit of winning in Dunkeld. Thanks to the landowners and one marshall Graham Nicol. Well done the juniors too, a good turn-out maybe more next year!**

**We were short of marshalls and therefore there may be some discrepancy with the times ater the first 10 so please let me know if we have the wrong finish times... apologies, more helpers needed next time.**

**Thanks**

**Adrian Davis**
