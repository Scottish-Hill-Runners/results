The fine run of form of Catriona Buchanan (Ochils) continued at Largo Law, where she finished ninth overall and broke the women's course record.

AndrewWright (Carnegie) was first home. ahead of Jonny Knox (Anster Haddies) and Bob Wiseman (Ochils).
