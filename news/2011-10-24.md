Both Finlay Wild and Catriona Buchanan broke their respective records to be victorious in the Run of the Mill race in the Ochils. On a fast day second place Al Anthony was also inside the old record and James Waldie continued his fine form with a 3rd place to finish ahead of 1st M40 Mark Harris. The top three ladies all finished inside the old record with Sarah Ridgeway just behind Catriona and 1st F40 Shona Robertson just a couple of minutes back
