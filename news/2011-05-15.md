Andrew Fallas, Paul Faulkner and Stewart Whitlie made it a Carnethy 1-3-4 at Goatfell Race. First lady (and first local) was Hazel Lynne Tod (Unattached).

A trademark Brian Marshall descent broke up the Carnethy trio's summit 1-2-3 and was rewarded with first V40. Alan Smith repeated his V50 success from Ben Lomond
