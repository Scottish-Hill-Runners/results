Lochaber AC's Triple Hirple consists of Melantee (12 July), the Half Ben (13 July) and finally Cow Hill (14 July).

The best overall time was Manny Gorman (Westerlands), followed by Tom Smith and John Hepburn (both Lochaber AC) . Amanda Blackhall was the first lady, closely followed by Sarah MacKenzie (both Lochaber AC) .
