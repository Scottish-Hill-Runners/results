The 2015 Dumyat Hill Race takes place on Wednesday 6 May, 2015.

For the first time in its running, entries are being managed online.

[Enter here](http://www.q-buster.co.uk/index.php?rn=738&action=show_detail) - [https://www.q-buster.co.uk/index.php?rn=738&action=show\_detail](https://www.q-buster.co.uk/index.php?rn=738&action=show_detail)

Entry is priced £6.50 and includes supply of numbers and timing chips as well as complimentary post-race Stovies.

On 6 May, race entrants are asked to report to the University's Gannochy Sports Centre between 5.30pm and 6.30pm to collect numbers and timing chips.
