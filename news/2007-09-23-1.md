---
title: "Tracey Brindley wins gold at World Masters, 23 September"
date: 2007-09-23
excerpt: "Congratulations to Tracey Brindley (Scottish Athletics) on winning the W35 race in the World Masters at Bludenz.  '"
---

In her first competitive run for a couple of years, Tracey Brindley (Scottish Athletics) comfortably won the W35 title at the World Masters. Her time of 50:20.9 was over two minutes faster than her nearest rival's.

Other top-ten finishers spotted: Phyllis Lemoncello (Fife AC), eighth W60; Ewan Paterson (Moray RR), fifth M55; Keith Varney (Cosmic HB), seventh M50; and Anita Hamilton (Cosmic HB), tenth W50.
