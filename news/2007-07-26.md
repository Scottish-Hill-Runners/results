---
title: "Turnhouse Hill Race, Wednesday 25th July"
date: 2007-07-26
excerpt: "The final race in the Pentland Ridge Series, which was also a Bog and Burn counter, was held in warm, sunny conditions and with a field double that of the previous races in the series. Al Anthony (Ochils) won the race but Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) in 3rd took the Ridge Race series title.  '"
---

60 runners took an interesting tour of Turnhouse Hill, in the Pentlands, showing once again the inability of fell runners to follow a flagged route ! But as everyone took the same wrong route up Turnhouse, it wasn't a problem and it was a lovely sunny evening so nobody minded the extra distance. Al Anthony (Ochil) and Grant Stewart (City of Edinburgh) battled at the front closely followed by Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy). Elke Schmidt (Bellahouston) had a great climb, but not so good descent, though had no problem being first woman.

In the three race Pentland Ridge series, Stewart Whitlie was first, Steven Fallon (Carnethy), 2nd, and Des Crowe (Shettleston), 3rd. Elsie Scott (Westies) won the ladies.
