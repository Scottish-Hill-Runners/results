---
title: "Bishop Hill Race Results, Report & Photos"
date: 2017-03-05
excerpt: "Wins for Graham Nash (Carnethy) and Jill Stephen (HBT) at the Bishop Hill Race on Sunday 5th March."
---

80 runners lined up for the race. Overhead conditions were pleasant, though underfoot it was muddy and slippery due to heavy overnight rain. This slowed the running somewhat but everyone arrived back unscathed despite some slides and falls. A good battle ensued with Carnethy vet40 Graham Nash taking the lead on the descent to get ahead of Westies Craig Freshwater. Martin Davidson of PH Racing came third.  
Jill Stephen of HBT dominated the women's race, keeping her shield won last year. Pitreavie junior Zoe Nicholson was second and Hilary Ritchie of Fife AC third- and first V50.  
Several juniors did the route to halfway. Struan Bennett (Kinross) was a clear winner. Isla Bennett was first girl. Seniors take note, the juniors started with the main race field and Struan arrived at the halfway gate in third position.  
Many thanks to the large number who helped on the day, on the hill or in the hall.

Richard Gatehouse (Race Organiser)
