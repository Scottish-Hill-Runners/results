Sod's Law decreed that the glorious weather of recent weeks came to an abrupt end just in time for the revamped Yetholm Shepherds Show races. However the rain relented and the sun shone on the 9 hardy souls who did turn up.

Seniors & Juniors set off together to run the original Brae House Race to the Venchen and back with Darrell Hastie of Gala Harriers leading from start to build a 3 minute lead over the chasing group of 4 who were never separated by more than 30 secs throughout the race.

They stormed back through the show field on their way to the second hill with 11yr old Ben Murray John bearing down on them towards the Junior finish line. Darrell continued to extend his lead on the out & back run to Staerough eventually finishing nearly 8 mins clear.

Ben Murray John won the junior race in 17:24 and Darrell Hastie won the senior race in 32:52

The deceptively tough new route proved popular with both runners & spectators with the latter being able to enjoy almost uninterrupted views of the whole race......also an enjoyable event for the organisers & marshals who were able to enjoy a run over Staerough and a dip in the Bowmont before retiring to the beer tent. Hopefully we will be asked to do it all again next year and hopefully with better weather and more runners.

Norham RC
