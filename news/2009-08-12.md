Tonight's Caerketton Hill Race concludes the 2009 Bog and Burn Series. Prize-giving for the series will take place at [The Steading](http://www.streetmap.co.uk/map.srf?X=324962&Y=667085&A=Y&Z=120) after the race.

**Men's competition:**

Andrew Wright has a slender lead over Kenny Richmond going into the final race, with both on equal points, and Oleg Chepelin only 1 point behind. However Murray Strain has 3 wins from the last 3 races, and only needs to turn up to Caerketton to win the series.

Steven Fallon would appear to have the edge on Andy Birnie in the V40 category, and David Dickson should hold off Russell Anderson in the V50 age group. In the V60 category, Ian McManus has completed the most races, and has finished ahead of Graham Benny each time they've met, the only other V60 to complete the series.

**Women's competition:**

In the women's competition, Val Houston is first F40 and overall leader, with Bellahouston's Iona Robertson and Emma Birnie 2nd and 3rd. Iona Robertson could still win the series if she finishes as first lady at Caerketton.

Jackie McGuire or Tilly Smith will finish as 2nd F40 after Val Houston, and Alison West has already won the F50 category.

Deborah Stokoe of Westerlands is the only junior runner to have completed the series.
