title: "Mykura nearly breaks the Yetholm record running 'backwards', 1 June"
date: 2008-06-02
excerpt: "Will Horsley (Northumberland FR) won the race, which was run counter-clockwise this year. Jill Mykura (Carnethy) just missed the women's record.  '"
---

The race was run counter-clockwise - 'backwards' - this year, in pretty dreadful conditions. Not just backwards in that sense for some, because Rob Stephens tells us: "With its being a reversed route and in thick mist, most of us managed to miss the turn at Latchly Hill and headed for the low-level Pennine Way alternative route, having then to make a longer trip up The Curr and back".

Will Horsley (Northumberland FR) was first home, over two minutes ahead of Adam Fletcher (Berwick). Less than two minutes then separated the next eight to finish, Jill Mykura (Carnethy) amongst them. Her time of 1:17:05 was a mere two seconds outside Clare Miller's (clockwise) course record. Val Houston (Westies), the second woman, had the satisfaction of leading Westies to the women's team prize.

There's a longer report by Phil Green on the [Northumberland Fell Runners' web site](http://www.northumberlandfellrunners.co.uk/), with photos by Pat Dunn and Rob Stephens [here](http://www.flickr.com/photos/8011535@N08/sets/72157605392431857/) and [here](http://www.flickr.com/photos/8011535@N08/sets/72157605386968916/).
