---
title: "Angela sets record at Everest Marathon, 5th December"
date: 2007-12-06
excerpt: "Angela Mudge has just set a new record at the Everest Marathon on 5th December recording 5:02:17 for 8th place overall.  '"
---

Angela Mudge (Carnethy) broke the record at the Everest Marathon recording 5:02:17 for 8th place overall. She was second non-Nelapese to finish. The race was won by local runner, Lok Bahadur Rokaya in 4:12:21, 20 mins ahead of the second.

Several other Scots took part including Jim Stevenson (Irvine) who was 21st in 5:56:20; 25th Peter Buchanan (Portobello) 6:11:31; 32nd Derek Jablonski (Lomond) 6:56:38; 53rd Helen Murray (Lothian) 8:32:30
