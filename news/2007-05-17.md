From Mike Pearson:

It was another damp evening for the Gypsy Glen race, but at least this year the results were legible and not completely sodden.

Will Hynd of Moorfoot was first in 34:05 (and also first vet), closely followed by David Moffat (34:07) and Bruce Smith (34:10). Rory Methven was fourth overall, and first junior.

The run of the night, though, was by Lucy Colquhoun. She was first female (9th overall) in 37:37, taking the best part of a minute off Susan Ridley's record.
