The 2015 Cairn Table Race took place in Muirkirk on Wednesday 22nd July. An impressive 67 runners took part with only one retiree. Part of the Bog and Burn Hill Running Series, the race up Ayrshire’s finest hills covers 7.2km and climbs 330metres. It turned out to be a changeable night with both dry and wet weather and the ground was very wet and boggy underfoot. Jack Wood (Edinburgh Uni H& H) won ahead of Westies Owen O'Neill and Niall McAlinden. The first female to cross the line was Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) in 18th position overall and the oldest competitor was Ian Mcmanus, 73 Years old, from Kilwinning who ran in at 75.20 a great account, especially in the heavy conditions.
