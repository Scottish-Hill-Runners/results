Saturday 11th October 2014 at McLaren Hall, Killin

This year our annual do and prize giving will take place after the Meall nan Tarmachan race, the last event in the SHR championship. Make a weekend of it and also run the Sron a Clachain race on the Sunday!

The venue will be the McLaren Hall in Killin, and will take place in the evening, with a three course buffet meal and dancing to a local ceilidh band.

More details and booking form to follow soon!
