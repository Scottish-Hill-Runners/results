_From Michael Milmoe:_

Despite the Tomintoul Highland Games being cancelled on the Saturday morning due to the heavy rain the previous day and the poor forecast for the Saturday, it was decided to go ahead with the Hill Race if any competitors turned up. The forecast for Saturday was correct and it rained for much of the day…

A select field of 13 registered for the race, which was run in reasonable conditions – light rain and wind, but very wet underfoot. Francis Williams (Highland HR) won in 32.07, followed by Jason Williamson (Cosmic HB) in 32.35. Dave Weir, from Aberlour but running for Fife AC, was third in 32.48.

Czech Republic runner Zuzana Dubovska, from Tomintoul, was first in the ladies race in 42.12, followed by Kirstie Rogan (Forres H) in 43.26. Ursula Bass (Deeside R) was third in 45.26.

Many thanks for the generosity of the Tomintoul Highland Games Committee, who gave the prizes for the race. We hope to see more runners competing in 2010, as Tomnabat is a great race with lots of character. Also, the Tomintoul Highland Games would appreciate your support after the disappointment of having to cancel the 2009 games.
