Entries for the World Long Distance Mountain Running Challenge 2008, also known as the Three Peaks Race, were full within a few days of opening. Substitutions are allowed, providing that the substituting runner meets the qualifying standard. The race is on April 26th. You can view the race details and entry list [here]( http://www.threepeaksrace.org.uk).
