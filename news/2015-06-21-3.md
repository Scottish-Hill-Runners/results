---
title: "Eildons Hill Race Results"
date: 2015-06-21
excerpt: "Wins for Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) and Louise Mercer (Edinburgh Uni H&H) on Saturday 20th June"
---

Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) won the Eildon 3 Hills Race in a time of 38:36 just ahead of George Foster (Dundee Hawkhill) and Ian Stewart (Westies) in 2 nd & 3rd respectively.

The female winner was Louise Mercer (Edinburgh Uni H&H) in 40:22.
