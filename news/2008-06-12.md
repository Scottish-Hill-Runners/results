---
title: "Kenny Richmond equals Cort-ma Law Record"
date: 2008-06-12
excerpt: "Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston RR) won the Cort-ma Law Hill Race in a time of 48:16, equalling the record set by John Stevenson in 1999. First lady was Sarah Pickett (Central AC) in 59:43.  '"
---

Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston RR) had a comfortable win at the Cort-ma Law Hill Race, leading from start to finish, and making up in part for last year's disqualification due to a navigational misunderstanding. In near perfect conditions due to the recent drought, Kenny managed to equal John Stevenson's long-standing record from 1999 in exactly the same time of 48:16!

With defending Bog and Burn champion Angela Mudge resting up from her recent Buff Sky Race win in Italy, and saving herself for the Durisdeer British Championship race on Saturday, first lady was Sarah Pickett (Central AC) in 59:43.
