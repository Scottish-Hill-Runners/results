---
title: "Planning to run Ben Lomond on 10 May?"
date: 2008-02-14
excerpt: "You can enter on-line from Monday 18 February for the Ben Lomond Race on Saturday 10 May."
---

Seniors can enter on-line [here](http://www.racentries.com/) from Monday 18 February for the Ben Lomond Race on Saturday 10 May (1pm start). There is a limit of 175, so please only enter if you're as sure as you can be that you'll be running on the day.

This is nominally the only way of entering the race - but if you have problems with it contact the race organiser Don Reid (donATmadasafish.com; 01355-237 060) as soon as possible and he will try to arrange your entry for you.

The senior race is preceded by four races for juniors, who should enter on the day in the usual way.
