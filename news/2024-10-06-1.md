**Yetholm Border Shepherds Show Report & Results**

Summer returned for the Best Race in the World with wins for Bruce Crombie & Anna Galbraith and 41 runners welcoming the splash through the Bowmont mid way through the race.

Thanks to the generosity of the competitors and the show committee NRC were delighted to be able to donate the sum of £275 to the Border Search & Rescue Unit.

Thanks to all helpers and runners,

Les Turnburll (Race Organiser)
