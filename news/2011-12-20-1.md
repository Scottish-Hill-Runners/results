For those of you planning ahead for 2012 SHR are happy to announce the following races will comprise the championship series;

**Short**

*   Glamaig
*   Creag Dhubh
    
    **Medium**
    
*   Clachnaben
*   Durisdeer
    
    **Long**
    
*   Arrochar Alps
*   Pentland Skyline
    
    The under 23 championships will consist of Creag Dhubh, Dumyat and Durisdeer
    
    Once again we are pleased to have the support of run4it
    
    The full dates and details for the races will be in the SHR Calendar which will be available free of charge to all members at Carnethy 5 (members not attending Carnethy will have their calendars sent out soon after).
    
    In a slight change to previous years, although the competition remains open to everyone who completes 4 of the 6 races\* (at least 1 from each category) the completion mugs will only be available to SHR members.
    
    _\*to complete the U23 championships runners need to complete 2 out of the 3 races._
