Scottish Athletics Junior Hill league 2022

The final races of the Scottish Athletics Junior Hill league 2022 was held at the end of September. There were nine races in all with the first one starting in April at Dumyat. Three races are needed for a runner to gain a final position in the league. And this year we were very pleased to have thirty-one junior hill runners completing the league across all age and gender groups. Well done to all of you! And many thanks to all the parents, friends and guardians for supporting the league. Each finisher will get a prize so please be in touch if you do not hear from us in the next few months or so. Any errors or comments to Mark Harris <[harrismark1@mac.com](mailto:harrismark1@mac.com)\>
