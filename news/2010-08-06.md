_From Phil Mestecky:_

Information on this year's Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay is now available [here](http://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/comriehillsrelayrace.htm). With the race only a month or so away, now's the time to start pulling your teams together and deciding who's on which leg etc.

For those that haven't done this one before, it's a 4-stage relay with leg 2 run in pairs (so 5 runners per team). The stages range from marked undulating trail (Legs 1 & 4) suitable for those that may not consider themselves hard-core hillrunners to a classic runnable hill stage (Leg 3) and the shortish but rough n tough Leg 2 with an exciting heathery, bouldery descent.

There are prizes for teams and stage winners. The day is rounded off with a barbecue for all competitors included in the price of the team entry.

We have a limit on team numbers so do try to get your entries in as soon as possible! Entry fee remains the same as last year at £25 per team plus £2 non SAF members. Any profits from the day go to supporting pancreatic cancer research.
