_From Edmund Rooney:_

Twenty-two runners took part in the new Oxton hill race, staged as part of the revived Oxton Border Games. The course took runners out along (and through!) the river and the old Oxton-Fountainhall railway, before a short but punishing hill, followed by a fast descent back to the Games field.

Hector Haines (Edinburgh Uni, 17:50) came in a clear first, ahead of Colin Menzies (Lasswade, 18:57) and Wull Hynd (Moorfoot, 19:11), with Scout Adkin (Gala) first lady and local runner Mark Bryson taking first vet. The Junior race was won by Mathew Wilson (6:53).
