**Kingscavil Hill Climb**: Linlithgow, 17 October

This event is a climb which was inaugurated last year as a road cycling hill climb time trial.

This year, the promoting club (West Lothian Clarion Cycling Club) has decided to make more use of the unique circumstances of having a closed public road to open it up to runners and also young cyclists.

It’s a short blast compared with what many hill runners are used to (about 900m with 75m elevation) and a good runner should be able to do it in under 4min. The running event will be held as a separate time trial from the cycling event (you can enter both as a Duathlon).

There's more information [here](http://westlothianclarion.blogspot.com/2010/09/kingscavil-closed-road-hill-climb.html).

**S2S Ultra**: Kinlochleven to Tyndrum, 14 November

This sounds more like a one-off charity run than a race. There's more information [here](http://www.yamaatrust.com/news.html#news).
