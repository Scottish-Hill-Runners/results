In the first big race of the season, Andrew Douglas of Inverclyde led from the gun to win the 2015 Carnethy 5 Hill Race from a field of 515. A fairly close second was Andrew Fallas of Carnethy. They recorded 49:23 and 49:43 resp. First woman was Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) in a fast 56:43 with clubmate, Jasmin Paris taking second in 58:07.

Helm Hill Runners from Kendal packed well to take the men's team prize (and claymore) in a close contest with Carnethy. Carnethy Women beat Edinburgh University H&H to take the women's team trophy.
