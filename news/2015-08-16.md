title: "James Espie & Jill Stephen win at Tap 'O Noth"

---

James Espie (Deeside) won the Tap ‘O Noth race on Saturday 15th Aug in a time of 34.10. 2nd and 3rd places went to Duncan McKellar (SRAC) and Rob Brookes (Cosmics).

The ladies races was won by Jill Stephen with Veronique Oldham (Cosmics) and Jaqui Higginbottom (Carnethy) in 2nd and 3rd

[Results](RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0094&Year=2015)

Note that Race Organiser, Simon Beeson is considering a 'possible one off' return to one of the old routes in 2016 which would see the runners go to the top as they do now but descend over the back of the Tap 'O Noth on the land rover track, lose lots of height then a slow climb back-up to approximately half height on a track that skirts around the base and joins up with the original uphill section before returning.

Simon is keen to canvass opinion from SHR runners, so if you’ve got views then please email him at simon@rhynie.vispa.com
