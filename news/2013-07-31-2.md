Iain Stewart (Westerlands) leads the Bog and Burn series after 11 races from Iain Whiteside (Carnethy), with Alan Smith now in 3rd place. Val Houston leads from Sarah Adam (both Westerlands) in the women's competition.

A series prize-giving will be held at The Steading after Caerketton on 14th August, the final race in the series.
