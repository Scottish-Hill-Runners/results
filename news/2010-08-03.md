David Rodgers (Lochaber AC) won the Cow Hill Race at his local Highland Games from Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) and Tom Smith (Lochaber AC). The first woman was Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT), followed by Lyndsey Shaw (Lochaber AC) and Ruth Crewe (Perth RR).
