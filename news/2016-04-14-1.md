All participants – please be aware of the following –

**IMPORTANT SAFETY RULES**

_Because this year’s junior races at Ben Lomond are uphill only and the athletes will be finishing on exposed hillside, the event will closely adhere to the SHR mandatory safety rules for hill race competitors, one of which reads as follows:-_

**_‘Arrive at the venue with as much kit as the organisers are likely to require you to carry on the day. This means windproof and waterproof full body cover, hat, gloves, map, compass, whistle, food, and any other items you deem necessary for your protection.’_**

This means that, all runners will be required to carry full body cover, so that they have an item of clothing to change into at the finish. Therefore, could all runners bring a bumbag with at least a jacket and bottoms, hat and gloves. If the weather on the day is good, then it will be up to the individual whether they want to carry any kit or have someone take it to the finish for them. If a runner gets to the event early, then it may be possible for the marshals to carry a small amount of kit to the finish on their behalf.
