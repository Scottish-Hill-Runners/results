The Junior Home International Trials and Open Race were held at Bonaly, over the courses that will be used for the Home Internationals on 12 October.

There was a Fun Run, followed by races for U14 Boys, Under 16 Girls, Under 16 Boys and Under 19 Girls (together), and finally Under 19 Boys and the Open Race (also together). Sadly no Under 14 Girls entered.
