The SHR AGM will take place straight after the prize giving at Symington village hall after the Tinto Hill Race on Saturday 10th November.

If you are a current member and have any points for discussion or motions you want to have raised at the meeting or if you wish to join the committee then please contact the secretary (secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk).
