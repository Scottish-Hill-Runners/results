---
title: "Falkland Hill Race, 21 June"
date: 2008-06-22
excerpt: "Lars Ottemoller and Jacqui Higginbottom (both Carnethy) were the main winners.  '"
---

The Falkland Festival Hill Race, from the Fountain in the High Street to the top of East Lomond and back, was won by Lars Ottemoller (Carnethy). He was 5 seconds ahead of Steven Fallon (also Carnethy), who had the consolation of being the first M40. The leading woman (and first F40) was yet another Carnethy, Jacqui Higginbottom.
