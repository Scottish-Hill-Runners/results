**Nevis Range Loops Race 2015 Race Report**

Winter weather arrived for the new Lochaber Athletics Club race route at Nevis Range on Sunday 29th November.

A two lap course, involving the ascent up the north face climbers path, gave a challenging race over 13 miles.

Most runners reported they enjoyed the new course.

1st was Finlay Wild, Male (Lochaber AC) 01:31:48, 2nd Andrew Barrington, Male (Lochaber AC) 01:34:08, 3rd Stephen Burns, Male Vet 40 (Lochaber AC) 01:39:39, and for the Ladies was 1st Amanda Blackhall, Female Vet 40 (Lochaber AC) 01:57:01,

2nd Suzy Devey Female, (Lochaber AC) 02:14:27, 3rd Vikki MacKinnon, Female (Lochaber AC) 02:14:33.

Thanks to all the course markers, timers and marshalls who contributed to the success of this Winter League race.
