The massive field of 123 teams was led home once again by Shettleston. After taking the lead on the 3rd leg, they finished over four and a half minutes clear of a youthful Carnethy team. In 3rd place, a minute behind, were Central AC, while Corstorphine, who had led through the first two legs were 4th.

HBT led the women's race from start to finish, with second place, Carnethy, closely following the entire race.

Organising club, Fife AC had success in the Over 40 category, with the men winning a close contest from Carnethy and the women easily winning their class from Cosmic. Carnethy led from start to finish to take the Male Over 50 with Dundee HH taking second place.

Fastest runners on leg 1 were David Simpson and Kris Berry of Corstorphine. On leg 2 Robbie Simpson (Deeside) blew away the field with an astounding run of 23:45 (into a strong headwind all the way) to take Deeside up to 4th. Jethro Lennox and Tom Owens had an equally good run on leg 3 with the fastest time of 53:32 to take Shettleston into the lead. Their leg 4 runner Paul Sorrie also recorded the fastest leg 4 with a time of 25:12.
