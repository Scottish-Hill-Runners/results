**The SHR are seeking a new insurance officer and would welcome anyone interested in the role to get in touch with Jill Stephen: [jillianstephen@live.co.uk](mailto:jillianstephen@live.co.uk)**

**Join the SHR Committee - SHR seek new insurance officer**

* * *

**SHR Insurance Role Description**

This is a key role within the **Scottish Hill Runners (SHR) Committee**, offering a great opportunity to engage with race organisers (ROs) across Scotland and play a vital part in the ongoing success and safety of our sport. The role is supported by the SHR Committee and provides a unique chance to contribute to the smooth running of races throughout the year.

* * *

**Key Responsibilities:**

*   **Insurance Coordination**  
    Manage the insurance process for SHR-affiliated races (approximately 125 races per year). This includes:
    

*   Receiving and responding to insurance requests submitted via the SHR website.
    
*   Confirming insurance cover and, where necessary, requesting additional details (e.g., risk assessment or route map) — particularly for new races or where significant changes/issues have occurred.
    

*   **Guidance and Support**
    

*   Maintain up-to-date knowledge of SHR Health & Safety guidelines.
    
*   Proactively update ROs on relevant guidelines, including seasonal variations (summer/winter).
    
*   Provide occasional support to ROs in the event of race incidents or insurance-related queries.
    

*   **Insurance Renewal**
    

*   Organise the annual renewal of SHR’s insurance cover through the broker, with support from the SHR Committee.
    

*   **Stakeholder Engagement**
    

*   Keep abreast of developments in insurance and safety guidance from related bodies such as the **Fell Runners Association (FRA)**.
    

*   **Process Improvement**
    

*   As part of SHR’s web development project, review and update the existing insurance request form to enhance user experience and improve engagement with ROs.
