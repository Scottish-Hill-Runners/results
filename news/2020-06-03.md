title: "SHR Championships 2020"
date: 2020-06-03
excerpt: "The Scottish Hill Running Championships for 2020 have been cancelled. The Bog and Burn series and the Long Classics series have also been cancelled."
---

The Scottish Hill Running Championships for 2020 have been cancelled.

The Bog and Burn series and the Long Classics series have also been cancelled.

  

We trust that members will avoid unnecessary travel and continue to exercise caution and common sense while on the hills. Further updates will follow as and when the current lockdown restrictions are eased.
