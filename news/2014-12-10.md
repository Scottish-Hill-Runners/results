Gaelforce Sky Run - March 7th 2015

## 22km Mountain run across three peaks of the Northern Maamturks in Connemara

This is a unique event and the only one of its kind on the west coast of Ireland.**22km challenging race route** involving steep ascents and descents, for**experienced mountain runners** only.

Special access has been granted to this stunning route by local landowners making it an amazing opportunity for mountain runners.

## Limited spaces - enter early as this will sell out

**200 places available**

## Prizes

**€1000 cash prizes** for 22km race

## Introductory 12km mountain run:

We will also be holding a run on the same day for those who are of reasonable fitness but new to mountain running.

While there are few trails on the route the course will be marked and so this will be an ideal opportunity to try mountain running.

100 places available in the 12km event
