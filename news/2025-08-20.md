**Strathearn Harriers Junior Hill Race - Saturday 30th August**

Strathearn Harriers are hosting our annual Junior Hill Race. The race is a perfect introduction for any young runners seeking a new challenge. There will be two different age categories. U9 which will run 1 mile with all under six to be accompanied by and adult. 9 years old and over will run 1.5 miles with a few more hills.

Chocolate and juice for all taking part with prizes for the first three back in their category. The race will start at 2.30om. Registraion will close 15 minutes before the start. So please be there in plenty time.

Entry fee : Free but donations are welcome!
