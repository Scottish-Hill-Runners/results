---
title: "SHR 2021 Race Calendar"
date: 2020-12-20
excerpt: "And notification of SHR 2021 Championship Races and British Championship Races"
---

As stated earlier, the SHR committee have agreed that due to the continuing uncertainty there is around the prospect of a return to hill racing at this point, there is little merit in producing a printed calendar of races for 2021.

But race organisers are urged to get in touch with us once they are in a position to confirm race dates for their 2021 races. And they will be included in the online calendar.

It is hoped that a SHR Championship can take place in 2021 and the races along with their provisional dates have been set up in the online calendar. The two long races will be Stuc a Chroin and Glenshee 9. The two medium length races will be at Dollar and Cioch Mhor and the two short races will be Eildon 3 Hills and Yetholm Border Shepherds Show Race.

The British Championship races scheduled are the same as those planned in 2020. Aran Race (Wales), Seven Sevens (N Ireland), Glenshee 9 (Scotland) and Arnison Crag Horseshoe (England).
