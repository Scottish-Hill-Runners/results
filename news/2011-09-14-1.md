---
title: "Charlotte smashes record at windy Caerketton Downhill"
date: 2011-09-14
excerpt: "Al Anthony was first man and Charlotte Morgan first woman in the Caerketton Downhill race from a large field, with Charlotte setting a new women's record of 5:59.  '"
---

People travelled far and wide to take part in this year's Caerketton Downhill race, with a record 56 runners attracted by a sunny evening - though they shivered in the strong, cold westerly wind while waiting to start.

Al Anthony won for the 3rd time in 4 years, but the run of the night was by the first woman, Charlotte Morgan who recorded the first ever sub 6 min time by a woman and beat the bookies favourite Jasmin Paris, who was also under the old record. Many age records were broken including the Men's Over 50s by Gordon Pryde.
