title: "2015 'Get A Grip' Downhill Series"
date: 2015-09-04
excerpt: "Placings after Round 2 of the 2015 'Get A Grip' Downhill Series. 3rd & final race at Caerketton next week."
---

Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) managed to secure the 2015 'Get a Grip' downhill series after a win at both The Nebit and Bishop downhills. Iain managed to grab victory at Bishop by seven seconds over clubamte Alex McVey, who sneaked ahead of Iain's brother Andrew, the 2014 series champion, by only one second. Hot on their heels was Gary Fraser (Ochils), who has secured first V40 in the series. Steven Fallon leads the V50s by a good margin after a couple of victories.

Lizzie Knox (Anster) won again and is looking strong for the series title, though defending champion Fiona Kelsall (Ochils) could still defend the title with a strong run at Caerketton on Wednesday, so it's all to play for...
