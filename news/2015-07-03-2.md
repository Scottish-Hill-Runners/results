Creag Choinnich hill race on Saturday 11th July 2015 has been cancelled and will be re-scheduled to a new date in the Autumn, which has yet to be finalised.

The Heart of the Park Challenge on Sunday 12th July 2015 will be taking place as advertised: [braemar-trail-run](http://www.entrycentral.com/braemar-trail-run).
