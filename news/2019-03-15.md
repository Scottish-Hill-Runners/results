Every year since 2010 SHR has been organising a navigation course for hill runners, both for members and non-members. Early courses were held in Peebles and Fort William and were delivered by Bruce Poll, whilst more recently they have taken place in the Pentlands under the expert guidance of Steven Fallon. Another successful course took place last weekend – read all about it in Steven’s [blog post](https://www.stevenfallon.co.uk/blog/2019-03-09-navigation-for-runners.html) !
