Wet and cloudy as per usual but not too cold on the tops. Good turnout considering the busy weekend. Angela Mudge lead stunningly well all the way up and along the ridge but was just passed on the descent. New womens record set by Angela Mudge in 55 minutes 51 seconds, Brian Marshalls winning time 53 minutes 48 seconds. _Stephen Rawlinson_
