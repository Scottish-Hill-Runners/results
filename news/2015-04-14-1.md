title: "Clachnaben Hill Race - Results"
date: 2015-04-14
excerpt: "Wins for Tim Gomersall (Grampian Orienteers) and Steph Provan (Deeside) at Clachnaben on Saturday 11th April"
---

After a week of great weather race day arrived with rain and a little sleet.

However, by 9am this had cleared only to reveal fresh snow on the hill.  
  

86 racers started at noon with all the snow gone and blue skies overhead. The warm weather had dried the course a lot from the previous weekends preview so faster running conditions prevailed.  
  

In the mens race it was close between Tim Gomersall and Peter Henry with Tim proving too strong at the end.

Similarly in the womens race Moira Davie couldn't catch Steph Provan towards the end.  
  

At the finish beer from our sponsor Deeside Brewery was enjoyed by all relaxing in the sun.

  
Bob Sheridan

(Race Organiser)
