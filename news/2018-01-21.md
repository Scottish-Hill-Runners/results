Due to the extreme weather we have had over the last week, the decision was taken on Friday to shorten the route. Weather forecast was for more heavy snow and the course already had a huge amount of snow on it! All the feedback was that it was the correct decision and the shorter route still proved to be a cracker! he results reflect the amended, shorter route.

We had quite a few call offs due to the forecast and people not keen to travel but pleased to still get 197 turn up!

Sheila Cochrane  

Race Organiser
