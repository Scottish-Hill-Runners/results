First home in the 18 mile challenge was Matt Sullivan, with Alec Keith the first V40 in second place and Claire Gordon winning the ladies race.

In the 8 mile race under 21 runner Calum McLeod came home first, with vet Steve Worsley 2nd just one place in front of leading lady Kathleen Henderson
