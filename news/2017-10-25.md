The six races have been selected and five of the six race dates have been confirmed. The exception is Chapelgill although it is likely to be on the 17th March

The six races are :-

1.  Chapelgill – (Short) – Saturday 17th March (tbc)
    
2.  Yetholm – (Medium) – Sunday 3rd June
    
3.  Glamaig – (Short) – Saturday 7th July
    
4.  Ben Rhinnes (Long) – Saturday 28th July
    
5.  Ochil 2000s (Long) – Saturday 18th August
    
6.  Beinn Resipol (Medium) – Saturday 15th September
    

The Scottish counter in the 2018 British Championship will be Ben Nevis (Saturday 1st September).
