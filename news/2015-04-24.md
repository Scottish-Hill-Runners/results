The first Bog and Burn race of 2015 took place in perfect racing conditions at Beinn Dubh - Luss. Joe Symonds cruised home over 90 seconds inside his previous record set last year in a time of 35 minutes.

The ladies record was also bettered by over 3 minutes by Ruth Fraser-Moodie in a time of 43 minutes.

The fantastic weather also attracted a record turnout of 97 runners. Thanks go to all helpers, sponsors and Arrochar MRT for their support.
