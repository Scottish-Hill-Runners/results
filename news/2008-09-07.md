title: "Silver for Waldie at the World Masters, 6 September"
date: 2008-09-07
excerpt: "Brian Waldie (Carnethy) was second in the M70s...  '"
---

Brian Waldie (Carnethy) was second in the M70 race in the World Masters in the Czech Republic. Carnethy member Alex Menarry (but entered under Durham and England) did even better, winning the M75s.

Other Scots spotted in the results include Keith Burns (Carnethy), 14th M65; Jim Knox (Teviotdale), 19th M60; Elaine Stewart (Cosmics), 11th W50; Stuart Symington (Dunbar) and Colin Laidlaw (Lytham), 22nd and 44th M50; Lindsey Knox (Teviotdale), 21st W45; and Steve McLoone (Greenock Glenpark), 17th M45.
