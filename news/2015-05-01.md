There are now just 30 places available for the Goatfell Race on Saturday 16th May. Please enter using the SIEntries web site.

The good news is that ferry fares are much cheaper than previous years, due to Road Equivalent Tariff pricing, meaning a return ticket is just £7.50 per passenger. Car tickets are also significantly cheaper, but the ferries are getting fully booked much earlier than usual, so if you are planning on bringing a car, get it booked as soon as you can.

As a tiny running club, Arran Runners is struggling to get sufficient people capable of recording finishing times and numbers at the end of the race. Ideally, we require 4 volunteers for this job. If you can help, or are doing the race and can provide a travelling companion to 'volunteer', please contact the organisers at [paulemsley@waitrose.com](mailto:paulemsley@waitrose.com). Travel Expenses will be paid. Many thanks, Paul Emsley, Arran Runners
