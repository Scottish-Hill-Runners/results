title: "Carnethy Hill Race, 11 July"
date: 2007-07-12
excerpt: "The winners were Carnethy's Stewart Whitlie and Jill Mykura, both of whom have just been selected for the Scottish team for the Snowdon International.  '"
---

The Carnethy Hill Race is the second of the three making up the new Pentland Ridge Series. A fortnight ago the setting sun was right in the runners' eyes as they tried to pick out the markers leading them up South Black Hill; this time it started off, benignly, at their backs.

Adam Ward (Carnethy) and Dan Gay (HBT) set out determined to give Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) a run for his money and led him until the real climb up Carnethy Hill. But then Stewart's strength told and his speed on the fast descent from the summit kept him clear of his pursuers.

Jill Mykura (Carnethy) was the first woman, ahead of Megan Mowbray (HBT).

The final race in the Series is [Turnhouse Hill](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0520&System=Metric&Month=7) on 25 July.
