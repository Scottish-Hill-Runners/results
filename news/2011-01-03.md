The Birnie Trust supports individual young athletes in non-mainstream sports by making grants to assist with travel, kit, coaching, etc.

The Trust is keen to receive more applications from athletes aged between 13 and 18: one of the Trustees is himself a hill-runner. A number of junior hill runners in the past have received grants from the Trust.

The Trust aims to help those individuals who have shown potential at either a district or national level of representation and who have been unable to secure adequate additional funding from other sources to compete internationally.

The Trust's [website](http://www.thebirnietrust.co.uk/index.php) gives full information about the Trust, what they try to achieve when making grants, and a downloadable application form.
