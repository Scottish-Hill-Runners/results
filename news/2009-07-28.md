Former England international Kate Bailey, now running for Welsh club Merionydd, was the winning woman at the Lochaber Games Cow Hill Race, ahead of locals Lyndsey Shaw and Sara Byrne. It was a blue and whitewash for the Lochaber men - Davy Rodgers took the race win, followed by Colin Donnelly and Tom Smith.
