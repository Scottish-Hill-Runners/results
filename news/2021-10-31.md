The Deeside Runners’ Night Race Series is back with races in December at Cnoc Dubh on Thursday 16th and at Knockargety on Tuesday 28 th.

The race dates in January & February at Dess and Potarch are still to be confirmed.

Full info on the Deeside Runners’ website - [Night Race Series | Deeside Runners](https://www.deesiderunners.com/night-race-series/)
