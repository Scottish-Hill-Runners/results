_From Tom Smith_

Due to the storm damage from 2011, harvesting and no other suitable alternative race route, with regret I will have to pull Beinn Lora race from the callender this year. However, To satisfy the Lochaber AC winter league an alternative race will be held at Sutherlands Grove (Barcaldine NM965422) 4 miles north east of Beinn lora on the A828.

The intended route will include forest trail, mountain bike single track, mud and 300m climb over 4.8 mles. All welcome. Parking will be tight so please car share or cycle if relativley local. Entry fee will be 50p. Changing ficilities and prizes will be nill!
