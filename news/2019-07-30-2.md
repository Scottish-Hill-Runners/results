Lomonds of Fife Hill Race – Saturday 18th August

**New Lomonds of Fife route** .

[https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=5812](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=5812)

Starts at Strathmiglo Hall running up East Lomond top then West Lomond top and back. Full description on 'Scottish Hill Racing' - [http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0196](http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0196)

*   **Start Time:** 1:00pm
*   **Venue:** Strathmiglo Village Hall
*   **Distance:** 16 km
*   **Climb:** 750 m

**Current Record Holders:** (M) 1:22:10 - Jonathan Crickmore (2018) and (F) 1:47:55 - Jenny McCall (2018)

Please bring reusable cup for use at water station  
No dogs on course  
Map provided  
Park at the hall or safely nearby.
