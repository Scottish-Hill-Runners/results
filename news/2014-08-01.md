Just a quick reminder that the Glenshee9 on Sunday is the first long race in the SHR champs for 2014. There is still time to place an entry on line, which is preferable for the organiser, but there will also be entries available on the day on a first come first served basis.

From race organiser Al Hubbard

_Online entry for Glenshee9 is available at this link:- http://www.entrycentral.com/Glenshee-9-Race_

_Please pre-enter if you definitely intend competing in order to ease admin on the day. Online entries will be accepted until Saturday 2nd August._

_Note on race maps._

_Please ensure your map extends at least 2km beyond your race route:- http://www.stevenfallon.co.uk/glensheeroute.html Ideally your map boundaries should be Eastings GR NO 08 and GR NO 24 and Northings GR NO 73 and GR NO 83._

_The BMC 1:40 000 Cairngorms & Lochnagar map would be acceptable http://www.nicolsonmaps.com/acatalog/harveys-maps.html_

_Your map must also be waterproof/water protected._

_Thanks_

_Al Hubbard_
