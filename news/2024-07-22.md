Wins for Irivine Welsh (Gala Harriers) and Grace Norman (Unattached) at the Lee Pen Hill Race on Saturday 20th July.

Fulll results are on the SHR results tab.

4 local local lads ran it in support of their great friend Craig Angus who sadly passed away last year aged 20 from cancer.

Craig was from Innerleithen, and was a popular member of the Moorfoot runners club throughout his teenage years.

The lads were raising funds for the fantastic Macmillan Cancer Charity and can be supported by donating below.
