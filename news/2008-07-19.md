---
title: "Records tumble at magnificent Mull Monsterette"
date: 2008-07-19
excerpt: "Jill Mykura (Carnethy) and Brian Marshall (HELP) smashed the ladies and mens records respectively in the Mull race.....  '"
---

All sorts of records were beaten at a glorious Isle of Mull race. Jill Mykura (Carnethy) smashed the ladies record by over 20 minutes to come home in 2.03.57, over three minutes clear of 2nd lady Dawn Scott (Lochaber). Brian Marshall (HELP) used his legendary descending skills to pull away from Stewart Whitlie on the last drop from Ben More to lower his own record by almost 10 minutes to 1.46.02. Stewart had the consolation of first vet and leading the Carnethy men to a comfortable victory in the team competition. A record turnout too, of 105 starters of which all finished!
