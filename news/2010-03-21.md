---
title: "Rogan and Reid win at Craig Dunain, 20 March"
date: 2010-03-21
excerpt: "Forty runners started and completed this year's Craig Dunain race, run in near perfect early Highland spring conditions. Winners were Paul Rogan (Forres Harriers) and Eleanor Reid (Inverness Harriers).  '"
---

A number of other local sporting events - including the local football (grudge) derby of Ross County and Inverness Caley Thistle - may have contributed to a slightly lower than normal turn out for this year's Craig Dunain race on Saturday. In the event, weather and underfoot conditions were excellent and a good day's racing was had by all 40 competitors.

In the men's race, in-form Paul Rogan (Forres Harriers) took off at a brisk pace but was passed on the climb about half way up by Lochaber's Ewan Thorburn who opened up a significant gap at the top. Paul however enjoyed a very swift descent and came home a comfortable winner with Ewan a long way clear in second. Local Invernessian Andrew MacRae ran a good race to take third, whilst Dave Cummins (HHR) just nicked the V40 top spot in 4th.

The women's race always threatened to be a close run thing between 2 well-known local athletes, and so it proved, with Inverness Harriers' Eleanor Reid just getting the better of HHR's Roxy Bannerman.
