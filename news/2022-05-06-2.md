Shout out for anybody who can help marshal at the Trotternish Ridge Race on Saturday 4th June.

Marshals are required to help our on the hill at check points and also to cover a couple of road crossings.

Anyone helping will be given a free slap up meal at the prize giving.

If you can help, please contact the race organiser - Neil Turner - [neilsturner@btinternet.com](mailto:neilsturner@btinternet.com)
