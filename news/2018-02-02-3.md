Scottish Hill Runners will again have a table at the Carnethy Race where members will be able to pick up their 2018 Calendars and their copy of the latest Journal. As in previous years it will also be possible to join or renew individual memberships.

As most of the SHR committee members will also be running the race the table will close around 1.00PM. Whilst forms for joining SHR will be available on the day in order to speed up the process it would be helpful, if you are planning to join on the day, if you brought a completed form with you. Blank forms are available for download on the website at Join SHR.

If you have already joined and will be at Carnethy please arrange to pick up your Calendar and Journal. If you will not be there on the day it would be good if you could arrange for someone else to pick up these for you. For those where this is difficult arrangements will be made to post these out but we would like to reduce the total numbers that require posting.

Gordon Pryde

SHR Membership Secretary
