date: 2009-09-19
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) beats Graeme Bartlett (Forres) and Veronique Oldham (Cosmics) beats Ruth Mackenzie (Deeside), both by one second...  '"
---

Fifty runners turned out for this year's race. Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) and Graeme Bartlett (Forres) had a great battle for first place, the former eventually taking it by just one second. Getting on for three minutes later Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston) got the better of Jason Williamson (Cosmics) for third.

The women's race was equally tight, Veronique Oldham (Cosmics) winning it from Ruth Mackenzie (Deeside), with Lois Noble (Cosmics) third.

The Half Morven (junior) race was won by Sam Burgess (U16, Deeside Runners) in 29.11, from Gordon McIntyre (U18, Ronhill Cambuslang).

The results for the Mini Morven were:
