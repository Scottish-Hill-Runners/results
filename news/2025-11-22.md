[Night Race Series – Deeside Runners](https://www.deesiderunners.com/races/night-race-series/)

Four cafes, four forests, four races.

Turn up, pay a quid, win a chocolate rabbit…

Deeside’s notorious Night Race Series. Bogs, ice, holes to fall down in the dark, very disappointing prizes, cafes serving well-earned refreshments (bring some money!), what’s not to like?

And … for the second year in a row, we are proud to be sponsored by Out & Back Store in Aberdeen alongside Norwegian head torch brand Moonlight Mountain Gear and this year Finnish footwear brand VJ Shoes. They are giving race entrants the opportunity to try out head torches and/or footwear for free at all of the event races. They will also be providing a prize for the male and female outright winners of the series. If you would like to take advantage of this offer, please e-mail info@outandbackstore.com once you have signed up for any of the races and they will confirm your test product for your chosen race(s)
