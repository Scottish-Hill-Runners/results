The [Cosmics' Summer Series](http://www.cosmics.org.uk/) of four races started on Thursday evening with the run up to Durris Mast and back. (The Mast is the [tallest structure in Scotland](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Durris_transmitting_station).)

Dan Whitehead (Cosmic HB) and Helen Johnson (Deeside R) opened their accounts for the Series with healthy balances, both setting new fastest times for the course.
