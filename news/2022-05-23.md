---
title: "Kilpatricks Kaper  Race - Important Information"
date: 2022-05-23
excerpt: "Important info re parking if you are attending the Kilpatricks Kaper race on Wednesday 25th May."
---

Parking is extremely limited at the race start. Race parking is provided at Clydebank Dalnottar Crematorium (G81 4SL) 6-10.30pm, under 1 mile from the race start by path. Competitors are strongly encouraged to car-share or travel by public transport – Kilpatrick railway station is 200 m from the race start.
