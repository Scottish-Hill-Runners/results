_From Roger Rees_

The weather turned out better than forecast, but the mist never left East Lomond for the races which formed part of the National Junior Hill Running League for 2014.

Records tumbled at this, the fourth staging of the event: a record field of entrants, fifteen clubs represented (from near and far – well done to families, friends and coaches for driving), and course records for Gavin McWhinnie (U13 boys), Beth Hobbs (U13 girls), George Rees (U15 boys) and Ailsa Cruickshank (U17 females, with Emily Nicholson within the old record time too)! It was terrific too to see another new record, set by Solly Jones - this race’s youngest ever contestant! Very well done to everyone who ran, and we hope to see you back next year, when we promise the sun will shine.

Very many thanks to Dave and his team of marshalls out on the course; to Graham, Tony and Ron on the start/finish line, and to Kate and Margaret on the juice and choccies.
