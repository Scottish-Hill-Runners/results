Please note that the Meall a Bhuachaille race start time has been changed to 14.30 and registration is between 12.30 and 14.00

  
Due to increased costs at Badaguish, the entry fee is £10 which includes food in the Bothy after the race.  
  
Note- There is a mountain bike race passing through Badaguish so all runners must take care (especially children) crossing the road.

There will also be a marshal directing traffic so slow right down as you drive in.
