---
title: "Cioch Mhor Race Report"
date: 2018-03-29
excerpt: "Race report of Saturday's race from RO, Sam Hesling"
---

Race day dawned cold and sunny. Fortunately the weather held out for much of the duration with only a hail squall and cold wind sent to try the runners and hill marshals.

The senior race kicked off just after midday with 55 tackling the eight and a half mile course. In the men's race Robbie Patterson (Moray Road Runners) led to the Cioch Mhor summit, a lead he retained for first place in 1:10:04. (Andy Macrae (Inverness Harriers) and Francisco Valdes (Highland Hill Runners) filled the second and third places.

Kirstie Rogan (Moray Road Runners) led home the women's race in a time of 1:25:11 with Rachel Armitage (Inverness Harriers) and Jenny Gillies (Unattached) in second and third respectively.

In the junior race, boasting a record 17 starters, Hamish Hickey (Ross County AC) in 22:16 and Katie Slimon (Highland Hill Runners) in 28:02 won their respective races.

Thanks to the volunteers who braved the elements this early season hill race continues to go from strength to strength, and we look forward to hosting it again in 2019 !

Sam Hesling

Race Director
