---
title: "Cairn Table goes to pot-hunter Kennedy, 14 June"
date: 2008-06-16
excerpt: "John Kennedy (Clydesdale) finished first in 33:48 at the Muirkirk Cairn Table Run.  '"
---

There was a field of twenty for the Cairn Table Run at Muirkirk.

Whilst the big guns were battling it out at nearby Durisdeer British Championship race, John Kennedy (Clydesdale) took advantage of some pot-hunting to lead home the field in 33:48, followed by Kilmarnock's David White in 34:33 and Alastair Murray in 36:58. Nicola Milligan (Bellahouston) was the first woman in 46:07.
