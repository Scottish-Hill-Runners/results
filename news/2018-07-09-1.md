---
title: "Finlay & Jill break race records at Glamaig"
date: 2018-07-09
excerpt: "Several records were broken at the SHR Championship race at Glamaig on Saturday 7th July. Congratulations to Finlay Wild (Lochaber) and Jill Stephen (HBT) who broke the male and female records (Jill becoming the second woman ever to break the hour), but also to Alasdair Anthony, who broke the Over 40s records and there was a record number of finishers."
---

All sorts of records were broken in the David Shepherd Memorial Glamaig Hill Race, as Finlay Wild broke his own race record by five seconds, Jill Stephen took a second off the 28 year old existing female record (held previously by the great Trish Calder) and Alasdair Anthony (48:40) took a big chunk off Brian Marshalls Over 40 record (50:26). On top of that it was the largest field ever with 199 finishers !

Jill became only the second woman after Trish Calder to run under the hour in the 31 year history of the race, but minutes late, Clare Whitehead became the 3rd as she sneaked under 60 mins ! A list of all female sub 70 min runs is [here](http://carnethy.com/ri_results/glamaig/glam_sub70.htm) and a list of all sub 52 min runs is [here](http://carnethy.com/ri_results/glamaig/glam_sub52.htm).

This was the third counter in this year's SHR championship.
