---
title: "Williamson takes a sweaty Dunnydeer, 8 June"
date: 2008-06-08
excerpt: "Jason Williamson and Sonia Armitage (both Cosmics) took the top spots.  '"
---

Jason Williamson (Cosmics) took less than 18 minutes to complete the Dunnydeer Hill Race but it was a hot and sweaty effort nevertheless. Metro Aberdeen's Mark Johnson (the first vet) and Neil Jaffrey were second and third.

Sonia Armitage (Cosmics) beat daughter Rachael by over a minute to be first woman (so presumably there was no need for her to pull rank).
