Think WINTER: Winter Running

Mountaineering Scotland (in conjunction with Trail Running Scotland and Scottish Athletics) have published an article on Winter Running as part of their ThinkWINTER Campaign. It covers Winter safety, navigation, weather, snow conditions, running crampons and hypothermia. It's worth reminding yourself how to be safe on the hills this Winter.
