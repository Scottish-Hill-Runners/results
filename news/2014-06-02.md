The organiser has asked us to remind people intending to enter that Cort-ma Law is a category AM race. Under SHR safety requirements all runners are required to carry windproof whole body cover, other body cover appropriate for the weather conditions, a map of the route, a compass and a whistle. This rule will be strictly enforced.
