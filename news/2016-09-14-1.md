For whatever reason, numbers were slightly down for the Comrie Relay this year, which meant more cakes to go round from what was already one of the best spreads in Scottish hill running !

The Carnethy 'A' team easily won after being fastest on each of the four legs. At the end they had 24 minutes on the hosts, Strathearn Harriers, who placed second. Ochils were first women's team but they were also the first female vets team, so the first women's prize went to Westerlands who were just 30 seconds behind Ochil. Ochils also won the male vets prize, while Kinross took the mixed prize in front of Carnethy.
