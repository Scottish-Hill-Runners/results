The short counter in the British Championship was held at the Devil's Beeftub race in pelting rain and low cloud. But that didn't stop several records. Morgan Donnelly (Borrowdale) won in a new record time of 19:45, 5 secs off Brian Marshall's 2007 time. The total field of 126 over the two races (separate races for men and women) was easily a record and Lloyd Taggart (Dark Peak), who was a close second to Morgan, set a new Over 40s record by knocking 4 secs from Stewart Whitlie's previous record. Pippa Maddams (Keswick) won the women's race.
