A record 112 runners completed the 2011 Run4 It SHR Hill Running Championship. If you are one of them, you are entitled to another unique mug to add to your collection!

Ronnie has come up with another great design this year (see picture). Mugs will be available for collection at the Tinto Hill Race and SHR AGM on 12th November. If you are not going to be there, please arrange for a fellow club member to collect on your behalf.
