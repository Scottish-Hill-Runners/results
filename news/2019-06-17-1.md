---
title: "White Tops Hill Race Results"
date: 2019-06-17
excerpt: "Wins for Joseph Wright (Deeside) and Heather Anderson (Fife AC) at the White Tops race on Friday 14th June"
---

It was another record field with Joseph Wright leading the race home for the second year running in a time of 46:09. Heather Anderson of Fife won the female race setting a new course record of 51:11.

Male team prize went to the Hawks with Dundee Road runners winning the female team prize.
