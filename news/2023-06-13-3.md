date: 2023-06-13
excerpt: "You can sign up for Ben Rinnes on SiEntries from Friday 16th June. This will be the first of the long counters in this year's SHR Championship and the fourth race in the series."
---

Entries for Ben Rinnes will open on Friday the 16th  June at 10:00am on SiEntries. 

There will be a limited number of on the day entries also available.

The race is the first long counter in the SHR Championship and this year's North district hill running championship.
