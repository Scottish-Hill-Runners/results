---
title: "Angela Mudge wins 2007 Buff Skyrunner World Series"
date: 2007-09-25
excerpt: "Angela did enough to win the 2007 championship at the last race at the 42km Zergama Aizkorri race in the Basque region of Spain on Sunday...."
---

Angela Mudge (Carnethy) has defended her 2006 Buff Skyrunner World Series Champion title after a great run in the final race of the series in Zegama (Spain), where she finished 2nd, completing the tough 42km course - with 2736m of climb and the same descent - in 4:53:46, just over 3 minutes behind the race winner Corinne Favre (4:50:01). Angela led at the first summit but after she had a fall, Corinne got past and held the gap to the finish.

Going into the final race (race seven) there were four athletes - including Angela - chasing the title, but with three wins (and three race records) pre-Zegama (Berga, Andorra and Dolomiti), and with the best four races to count, 2nd place was enough for her to retain the title.
