title: "Lyndsey Shaw's second win in Lochaber's Triple Hirple: Cow Hill, 5 July"
date: 2010-07-06
excerpt: "There was a different winner for each of the three races in Lochaber's Triple Hirple, Tom Smith taking Cow Hill. Lyndsey Shaw was first woman, repeating her success at Melantee.  '"
---

Cow Hill was the third and final race in Lochaber's Triple Hirple. It went to Tom Smith (Lochaber), who pushed the winners of the Half Nevis (Craig Mitchell, Lochaber) and Melantee (Alan Smith, Deeside) into second and third respectively. Lyndsey Shaw (Lochaber) repeated her success at Melantee, giving bherself two firsts and a third.
