title: "Whitehead and Mudge add Kinnoull to their CVs for 2008, 21 May"
date: 2008-05-24
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead (Cosmic HB) and Angela Mudge (Carnethy HRC) were the main winners at Kinnoull, the third Bog and Burn Race of the summer.  '"
---

From Race Organiser David Knight:

The race went well. We had 122 finishers, obviously not distracted by the Champions League final; and the weather was good, with the course in perfect condition, nice and dry. Dan Whitehead of Cosmics was a clear winner in 25.09 from last year's runner-up Don Nayler (HBT, 26.14) with Ross McLennan in third place a further 20 seconds back. World Champion Angela Mudge was the first female home in twelfth place overall (28.15) with Sula Young, back from her travels, not far behind in fifteenth (28.35). Judith Taylor was third in 31.05. First male Vet was Des Crowe in sixth place overall in 27.22, with the first female vet being Val Houston, 45th in 31.27.
