We trust that many of you, having trained hard through the cruel winter, are looking forward to Saturday's Carnethy race!

There's further excitement guaranteed because the new 2011 SHR Calendar of Races, together with the 2010 Journal will be available at the SHR stand in the school hall. But of course you need to be a fully paid-up member to get your hands on these!

To help us cope with the rush (!) on the day, it would be great if you could send in your renewal beforehand - you can do it online in a few minutes. But if you cant, and you need to renew on the day, it would be good if you could complete a membership form beforehand (see How to Join page) and also have your exact payment ready, in cash or cheque.

We'll also have hats, car stickers and some other items of SHR-branded gear for purchase.

Many thanks and look forward to seeing you on Saturday.
