Saturday 3rd May will see the the 20th anniversary of the [Stuc a\`Chroin hill race](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0033).

To mark this special occasion there will be extra prizes, including Vet 60, special mementos for all finishers, and long-service awards for all runners who have completed 11 or more Stuc a\`Chroin races.

Stuc a\`Chroin is also the first race in the 2008 SHR [Long Classics Series](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006).
