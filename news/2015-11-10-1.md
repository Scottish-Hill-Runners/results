The SHR AGM will take place straight after the prize giving at Symington village hall after the Tinto Hill Race on Saturday 14th November.

The hall is booked up until 6pm so the meeting won’t go on beyond that.

If you are a current member and have any points for discussion or motions you want to have raised at the meeting or if you wish to join the committee then please contact the secretary (neilgilmore@blueyonder.co.uk).
