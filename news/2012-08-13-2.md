Niall McAlinden of Westerlands has overtaken John Stevenson as the new leader of the Bog and Burn Series following Caerketton. Julia Connor continues to lead the ladies competition, with 2 races remaining.

Please note there will be soup, sandwiches and prize-giving after Cairn Table, the final race in the series, on 22nd August at [Muirkirk Caravan Park](http://goo.gl/maps/wjkEM).
