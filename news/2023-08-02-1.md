Unfortunately the Pentland Skyline Race on the 8th October 2023 has been cancelled this year due to landowner refusal of the race route and other land access issues. At the time of writing, we are sadly uncertain of the race's future at this point. 

Declan Valters

Carnethy HRC Secretary
