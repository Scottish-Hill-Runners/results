date: 2016-08-09

The fifth race in the 2016 SHR Championship and Scottish counter in the British Championship is the Merrick Hill Race. It will be held on Saturday 17th September starting at 13:00. The summit is at 750m climbed to from sea level with a total distance of7.7miles, 12.4km. The race will start at Bruce’s Stone car park, there’s limited parking at the start but there’s another car park about 0.7 miles away, see map.

Enter on-line at [http://www.entrycentral.com/event/105006](http://www.entrycentral.com/event/105006)

Entries will be taken on the day if there is still space.
