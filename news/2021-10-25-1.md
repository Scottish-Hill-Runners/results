The Scottish Hill Runners 2021 Journal is looking for submissions! Hopefully with more travel, more training and more races this year, people will have more material to base some excellent writing on. No need to be a member of Scottish Hill Runners to contribute an article, the more contributions the better!

**Articles**

We are looking for articles on hill running, hill races, training for the hills, fun in the hills, fun off the hills, adventures far afield or close to home; prose or poetry, fact or fiction - all styles, plots and viewpoints welcome. 

**Short-Cuts**

Don't think you're able to write a whole article? Well never fear, following the success of the format in the last journal we are once again looking for **Short Cuts**, 200-300 word mini-articles on a similar theme. 

Taking the opportunity to reflect on the past, we're looking for people to write on the topic of '**My first hill run'.**

**Deadline**

The deadline for submissions is **10th January 2022.** 

If you would like to make or discuss a submission please get in touch with James Callender - [committee4@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:committee4@scottishhillrunners.uk)

The Journal will be available to order once published - free for members of Scottish Hill Runners.

We look forward to hearing from you!

Yours,

James
