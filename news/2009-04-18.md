---
title: "Two-one for the Bannermans at Cioch Mhor, 18 April"
date: 2009-04-18
excerpt: "Alec Keith wins in record time from Ross Bannerman; Roxy Bannerman breaks the women's record.  '"
---

Alec Keith (Inverness H) took thirty seconds off the course record in winning Cioch Mhor by a couple of minutes from Ross Bannerman (Highland HR). Fran Williams (also Highland HR) was a further couple of minutes behind and Craig Mitchell (Lochaber AC) was fourth.

Roxy Bannerman (Highland HR) improved the women's record by one-and-a-half minutes, finishing comfortably ahead of Marsella McLeod (Highland HR). Anne MacKay (Tain R) was third.

The junior race was won by David Gardner (Inverness H) from Alistair Cooper (East Sutherland), Ruaridh Gollan (East Sutherland) and Tom Pumphrey (Highland HR).
