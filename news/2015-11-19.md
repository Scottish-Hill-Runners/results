**Canal Bank Race 2015 Report**

The rain stopped to allow the first winter league race to take place. 75 runners registered for the first race all ready and eager to go.

It was a fast paced race with Findlay Wild being a clear winner in 27 minutes, he was followed by Stephen Burns MV40 in 28:57 then 3rd place was Steven Burns in 29:06 the first three were all Lochaber Athletic Club.

2nd male was Ryan Stewart 29:44, 3rd male Michael Bossard 29:58, both LAC. 2nd MV40 was Jon Gay 31:14, 3rd Andy Craig 31:57 both LAC. 1st MV50 was Tim Brand 29:49 LAC, 2nd Alan Smith, 31:52, Owen Bass 35:24 both Deeside Runners. 1st MV60 was Mike Connolly 34:01 LAC, 2nd Davy Duncan 35:10 Ochill Hill Runners, 3rd Jim Treasurer 38:33 LAC. Erne Orr MV70 46:25 LAC.

The female race was won by Diane Baum in a fantastic time of 31:41 LAC, 2nd Johana Mackova U/A in 34:15 and 3rd Amanda Blackhall FV40, 35:26. Lisa Conlon, LAC was 4th but 3rd female in 35:37. 2nd FV40 Nix Foster 36:28, 3rd FV40 Lucy Prins 37:10 both LAC. 1st FV 50 Emma Pearce 39:32, 2nd Kathy Tighe 43:11 both LAC, 3rd Jane Oliver, Deeside Runners.

Very big thank you to the Marshall's Ian Adams, Graham Macnab, Karen MacGinty and Iain MacKellaig. Also to the timers Andrew Cameron and Ellen Morrison. Also a big thank you to John Stafford for his help and guidance.

Race Organiser, Aileen Duncan.
