---

The Ben Rhinnes race on Saturday 30th July was won by Es Tresidder (Lochaber) just ahead of Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) with Andrew Barrington (Lochaber) in 3rd.

The female race was won by Helen Gertig. 2nd and 3rd places went to Moira Davie (Forres) and Jill Stephen (HBT) respectively.
