From Henry Blake:

A brave bunch of runners from all over Britain took part in the Highlander Mountain Marathon over the weekend 5-6 April and all thoughts of the snow and wind were washed away by the food, drink and ceilidh dancing at the midway camp!

Headline results:

*   Team Inov8's Tim Higginbottom and Chris Near Dominate A Class
*   B Class won by Robert Beaumont and Falk Schlaghecke
*   C Class won by Stephen Martin and Tim Martin
*   D Class won by Kean Rowlands and Sandra Rowlands
*   Score won by Finlay Wild and Ade Pottinger

Full results [here](http://www.highlandermountainmarathon.org.uk/).
