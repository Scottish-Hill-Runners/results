---
title: "Cairngorm Hills & Trails Race"
date: 2014-10-06
excerpt: "The inaugural Cairngorm Hills & Trails Race was held on Saturday 4th October."
---

Cairngorm Runners were delighted to welcome a small but talented field of runners for the inaugural Cairngorm Hills & Trails Race. For the Challenging 23km race Johannes Felter, BASOC, was the first man home, with a blistering time of 01:59:55 followed closely by Cairngorm Runner Tom Jefferson then David Pearks from HHR. This route suited the ladies of HHR's who put in superb performances; Shona Mackie and Gill Irvine were 1st and 2nd with local lady Nikki Diggins of Cairngorm Runners in 3rd place.

The shorter 10.5km route was won by Ben Lee, unattached in 55:15 and first lady home was Cairngorm Runner Jude Thomson 01:08:28.

Although there was a dusting of snow on the high tops and some chilly showers through the morning, spirits remained high. This race is planned to take place on Saturday the 3rd of October in 2015.
