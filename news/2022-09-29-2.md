**Long Classics Series Update after 7 races**

 Latest scores after Two Breweries, 7th race in series, as per link below.

Very close in the Men’s competition with **Tim Morgan** of **Carnethy** still holding on to top spot just ahead of teammate **Ali Mason** who won the Two Breweries at the weekend.

No change at the top of the women’s standings with **Chloe Lumsdon** of **Ochils** still out in front although **Catriona Graves** of **Highland Hill Runners** looking in a strong position after her 3rd win of series at the Two Breweries.

All to play for with 1 race remaining in the series, Pentland Skyline on 9th October.
