---
title: "Cort Ma Law survives the midges - just!"
date: 2007-06-22
excerpt: "Cort Ma Law was won by Matt Williamson (Bellahouston) in 52.17, whilst Angela Mudge smashed the ladies record.  '"
---

Race organizer David Rogers reports:

“The rain stopped, the wind dropped and the midges swarmed. Then former SHR supremo Pete Baxter was put through ritual humiliation to celebrate his graduation as a supervet. But none of that put off Angela who cruised across the bogs of Cort ma Law to take 1 minute 20 seconds off Tracey Brindley's 1999 record, finishing in 5th place overall in 53.17. First place went to Matt Williamson (Bellahouston) in 52.17 and second to David Riach (first V40) in 52.38. Westerlands (Riach, Upson and Bonnyman) beat Bellahouston to the team prize. As far as the organiser can remember the remainder of the 62 runners seemed to have enjoyed themselves, but at the time he was too busy scratching to care.”
