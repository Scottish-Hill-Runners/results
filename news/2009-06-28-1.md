Graham Arthur has taken back the lead in the SHR Long Classics Series following a good run at Arrochar. Ellen Homewood continues to lead the women's competition after the first 6 races.

The next Long Classic race is Ben Rinnes on Saturday 25th July.

There are still 5 races left in the series, so still time to gain a qualifying position.
