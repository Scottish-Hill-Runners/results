---
title: "Invercharron Games 15th September 2018 cancelled"
date: 2018-09-12
excerpt: "The 2018 games and hill race have sadly been cancelled, more info here..."
---

The games and hill race have sadly been cancelled.

The games organisers write: "Yes the Invercharron Highland Games is definitely cancelled this year and also the Hill Race. I really appreciate your getting in touch as I thought I had contacted everyone to let them know.

I would be grateful if you could please contact as many people as you know that may be coming to the games to let them know. We sadly have no field this year and therefore cannot hold the games. Please keep checking the website for next year as we are negotiating for a new field and hope to be back on track then."

The games website is [Invercharron Games](https://www.invercharronhighlandgames.co.uk/).
