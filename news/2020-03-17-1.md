SHR are being advised of race postponements and cancellations. As these are confirmed we will remove them from the online calendar.

And in the case of them being rescheduled later in the year, we will advise on those rescheduled dates through the website.

This Saturday's race at Craig Dunain has been postponed as per the current guidelines. Like Cioch Mhor, no new date is available but we will let you know if and when they are decided upon.

The same applies to the Glas Tulaichean Race at the end of May. If circumstances change and it can be rescheduled then it will, but at present this seems unlikely.

The Trotternish Ridge and Normans Law race have both cancelled the 2020 editions of the races. In the case of the Trotternish Ridge where there is pre-entry, all  
entrants will receive an email from Sientries and will receive a refund.

Stuc a Chroin also confirmed as cancelled. And Kaim also, although they are hoping to rearrange with a new date.
