The race was a week earlier this year to avoid a clash with the Solstice Triathlon.

We lucked out with a very hot, sunny evening .. almost too hot to run!!

  

Good wins from Eoin Lennon and Jill Stephen

  

Eoin was just outside Hector Haines course record from last year by 1 sec!! and beat Gregor Stewart and Ben Stevenson into 2nd and 3rd place

  

Jill was closely followed by Fiona Goulding of CAAC, and then youngster Lisa Christy of Glasgow Uni

  

Grant Baxter of Ochil and Gillian Cairns of Pencuik were the V40 winners

  

Jon Stevenson , got beat by son Ben, but still won the MV50 category

Val Brunton of Westerlands won the the FV50 category

  

Thanks to the local landowners for permissions to run the race again this year. Parking - we'll need to keep the corner clear on the backroad next year , and if we can get more folk cycling to the start that would really help!!

  

Thanks to Gash and the nice folk at Active Root for providing the refreshments at the finish line, and Paul at the Grey Horse Inn for post-race soup and beer - maybe I should order ice cream instead next year!!

  

And thanks to Don, Joanne, Oonagh , Sinead , Vicky , Sandra and the kids, Jill and other racers / marshalls who helped out on the night! Specially the Ochils runner I grabbed to help sort the car parking!

  

Cheers

  

Jamie Thin

(Race Organiser)
