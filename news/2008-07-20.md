---
title: "Cammack takes Tomnabat, 19 July"
date: 2008-07-20
excerpt: "Robbie Cammack (Moray RR) won the race and Helen Johnson (Deeside R) set a definitive women's record.  '"
---

The Tomnabat Hill Race is part of the Tomintoul Highland Games, over a course re-designed last year by Alan Smith (Deeside R). Local knowledge wasn't enough to give him victory though - he had to be content with second place behind Under 16 International Robbie Cammack (Moray RR).

Helen Johnson (Deeside R) won the women's race, setting a definitive course record of 37:42 in so doing. (The previous record was "greater than 39:45".)
