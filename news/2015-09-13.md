---

For various reasons the race was moved into September this year, a month earlier than the usual October date, and rubbed shoulders with a cycle time trial and an adventure triathlon.

The bikes of the triathlon went through the race start area and forced a shift in the start time to 1430. The combination of the date change and clash with other events saw a drop in numbers but that didn't deter the enthusiasm of the runners and marshals.

  

The race was won on his first time round the route by Ted Mason (Wharfedale Harriers) after a meaty battle with Dave Wilby (HHR). Third was James Espie (Deeside). First Lady was Lisa Allan (Cairngorm Runners), wining by a whisker from Rachel Limbrick (HHR). A little further back, finishing strongly was Shona Mackie (HHR).

Both team prizes were won by HHR (Rachel Limbrick, Shona Mackie & Diana MacDonald) and (Dave Wilby, Jack Trevelyan & Stuart Forrest).

The Jnr race, which is an out and back on the final part of the main race, was won convincingly by Rachel Keith (Inverness Harriers).

  

A special mention to Ray Wilby wining the M70 class on the tenth anniversary of winning the M60 world champs.

Many thanks to all who helped on the day and I apologise for the September midges.

  

Russell McKechnie

(Race Organiser)
