This year our annual do and prize giving will take place after the Meall nan Tarmachan race, the last event in the SHR championship. The venue will be the McLaren Hall in Killin, as also used for race registration earlier in the day.

Prize giving for the SHR Championship will take place at around 6.30pm. We will again be awarding a range of trophies to category winners in this year’s Senior Championship, handmade by the Buchlyvie Pottery.

Prize giving will be followed by the dinner at around 7.30pm. There will be a three course meal with a variety of choices including veggie. Drinks will be available to purchase from the bar (including real ale), and there will be dancing afterwards to a local ceilidh band (Jack McRobbie).

There is plenty of accommodation available in Killin, both hotels and B&Bs. Possible B&Bs include:

Ardlochay Lodge Guest House 01567 820 962

Kilchurn 01567 8230298

Dunlochay 820254

The cost for the do is just £20. Please complete a booking form as soon as possible to be sure of your place!

Further details from Dave Scott at dcbscott@tiscali.co.uk, 07754 756141.
