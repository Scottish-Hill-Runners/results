The Great Wilderness Challenge race results are on the website - The 25 mile route are on the results tab. Congratulations to Jack Blaiklock and Jenny Gillies for the wins. Due to a fatality on on one of the walks, police forced a road closure which resulted in only 16 getting through on the 25 mile route. The 13 milers all finished thankfully. And the full results for the 13 mile route are on the spreadsheet available below. James May and Lucy Aitchison were the winners.
