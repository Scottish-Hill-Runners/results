---
title: "Tom Smith triumphs at Eagle Crag, 26 June"
date: 2010-06-27
excerpt: "Tom Smith and Dawn Scott (both Lochaber) are first man and first woman.  '"
---

After weeks of fine and dry weather, conditions underfoot for this year's Eagle Crag race were dry and firm. Weather conditions too were excellent with a pleasant breeze much appreciated by the record turn out of 37 runners.

Tom Smith (Lochaber) and Dave Cummins (HHR) opened up a significant gap up the brutal first climb and continued together along the ridge to Eagle Crag summit itself. Tom's superior descending skills then saw him pull away on the steep descent down to the West Highland Way section. Although Dave managed to claw back a little time on the second climb back to join the outward route, Tom's lead was never really in doubt and he finished over 1 min clear. Paul Maclean and John Hepburn (both Lochaber) were never far apart for the whole race but with Paul edging 3rd place. In the women's race, Dawn Scott back on the hills after a lengthy absence had a fine run and was a very popular winner.
