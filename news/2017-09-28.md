The SHR Journal is printed and distributed along with the SHR Calendar ready for the Carnethy 5 race in February.

The editor, Ronnie Gallagher, is looking for items to include in this year’s publication.

Please submit your articles to SHR or persuade a friend with an interesting story from the year to do likewise.

Send the articles to Ronnie - [committee5@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:committee5@scottishhillrunners.uk)

Note that Ronnie is looking to relinquish the Editor’s role after the next publication so if you are interested in helping SHR and taking on this role please get in touch with Ronnie.
