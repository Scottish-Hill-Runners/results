Joe Symonds (Kendal), who has been in fine form recently, won the Llangynhafal Loop by 5 seconds from Tim Davies (Mercia). Jethro Lennox (Shettleston) was 10th, and Ian Wellock the first Hunters Bog Trotter, 1 second ahead of Ray Ward. Other Scots in the men’s race included Matt Bell (City of Edinburgh), Malcolm Patterson (Shettleston), Tom McGaff (Cheshire), who was second M50, and Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston). Claire Ward (HBT) was the only Scottish representative in the women’s race.

This was the second [British Championship](http://www.fellrunner.org.uk/championships.htm#brit) race of 2007.
