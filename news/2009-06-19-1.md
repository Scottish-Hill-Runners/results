title: "Whitehead is best on the Beach, 18 June"
date: 2009-06-19
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead won the Balmedie Beach Bash, the second race in the Cosmics' summer series.  '"
---

The Balmedie Beach Bash (once called "A hill race without any hills") is the second race in the Cosmics' summer series. Dan Whitehead won it from Simon Peachey and Rob Brookes. Veronique Oldham was the first woman, followed by Carolyn Macleod and Moira Davie.

_Anita Hamilton says:_

There was a good turnout and the rain kept off so we had a good barbecue too. Thanks to John Colegrave for organising the race and to helpers Sarah, Elaine, Dan, Fiona, Gillian, Dennis, Rob and Mike who variously marked the course, marshaled, did the timing, entries, lit the barbecue and collected in the flags afterwards.

For the series after two races Simon Peachey is in the lead followed by Rob Brookes and Alan Smith. Carolyn McLeod is the leading woman. The next race is Mither Tap on 16th July. Remember to bring your number, you keep the same one for the whole series.
