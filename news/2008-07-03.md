---
title: "Naylor and Colquhoun regain their Lairig Ghru titles, 29 June"
date: 2008-07-03
excerpt: "Don Naylor (HBT) and Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) win the Lairig Ghru Race, as they did in 2006.  '"
---

Over 80 runners completed the race from Braemar to Aviemore through the Lairig Ghru. Donald Naylor (HBT) won it, as he did in 2006, but without quite managing to regain the course record. Dan Gay (aka Donald Gray!) also of HBT was second, and Fife's Mark Harris finished third.

Carnethy's Lucy Colquhoun (winner in 2006 and course record-holder) and Jacqui Higginbottom were the first and third women, separated by Megan Wright of HBT.

Quite incredibly [Allen Smalls](http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/RunnerDetails.aspx?RunnerID=R5999) of Colchester Harriers finished 10th overall in 3:42:33, having completed the Arrochar Alps Race the day before. This is all part of Allen's training for the 100 mile Ultra Tour du Mont Blanc later in the summer.
