The Great Wilderness Challenge 25 and 13 mile races were on Saturday 12th August.

Douglas Bartholomew (Kinlochewe) and Maisie Baptist (Gairloch) were the winners in the 25 mile race race. 

Full results are on the SHR results tab.

In the 13 mile race, the winners were Derek Martin (Glasgow) and Megan Bee (Highland HR).
