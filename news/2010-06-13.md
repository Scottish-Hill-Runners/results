---
title: "Hunters Bog Trotters clean up at Traprain Law, 12 June"
date: 2010-06-13
excerpt: "Don Naylor and Megan Wright are first man and woman; HBT wins the team prize….  '"
---

The Tyne was low despite the recent rain but the aggressive riverside vegetation and its thriving population of insects made the course ‘interesting’ for the seventy-plus runners. Don Naylor (HBT) held off Brian Marshall (HELP) to win in 39:12. Al Hart (HBT) was third and with Jamie Thin not far behind HBT took the team prize. Megan Wright (HBT) staggered away with the women’s trophy, a highly-polished lump of Traprain Law rock.
