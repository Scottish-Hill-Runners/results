Latest scores after 6 races are as per link. No change in top 3 Men with Luke Taylor of Highland Hill Runners still out in front from clubmate Andrew Gilmore and Felix Wilson of Carnethy in 3rd spot. Although Felix has narrowed gap with his fine win at Arrochar at the week. A close race with Felix just edging Luke on the last descent.

In the Women’s score, we have a new leader in Lizzie Wastnedge of HBT, so well done to Lizzie. Shona Robertson of Shettleston and Bethan Nadin of Highland Hill Runners currently make up the podium positions in 2nd and 3rd.

With 12 long races counting this year, just a reminder it will be best 5 scores to count in the series. Bit of a break until next long classic which is Ben Rinnes on 26th July.
