---
title: "Whitehead double at Balmedie, 17 June"
date: 2010-06-24
excerpt: "Dan and Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) are first man and woman...  '"
---

The Balmedie Beach Bash was the second race in the Cosmics' Summer Series. Dan Whitehead won it from Simon Peachey, thus reversing their positions at Durris Mast, the first of the Series.

Clare Whitehead was first woman and fifth overall, follwed by Veronique Oldham (twelfth) and Moira Davie (fifteenth).
