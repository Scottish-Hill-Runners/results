Its time to get your entry in for this not-to-be- missed event before it fills up! This message from one of the organisres, Henry Blake:

"Whether you're new to hill running or mountain marathons, a keen intermediate or already up there with the elite entrants, the 2009 Highlander is the ideal launch event for this season. The two-day marathon on April 18 and 19 is organised by an experienced and local outdoors events company and is set amid some of Scotland's most rugged and scenic countryside in the Highlands.

Friendly, fun and with a superb record the Highlander is also highly acclaimed for having a great overnight camp. Participants are welcome to join the communal camp feast and join a traditional ceilidh dance and meal. Oh, and the event is guaranteed to be midge-free!

The cost is £45 per person and £43 if you have you own SPORTident card (one SPORTident card per team). Enter one of five different classes, including four fixed-route classes and a score class [here](http://www.highlandermountainmarathon.org.uk)
