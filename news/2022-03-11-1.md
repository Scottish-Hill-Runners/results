Chapegill registration via the Protay site - [https://protay.co.uk/chapelgill/](https://protay.co.uk/chapelgill/)

Saturday 19th March – 3pm   £5 entry

Payment is on the day, but please pre-enter to assist the race organisers.

Please note that no dogs are allowed due to lambing in the race area.

Full body cover to be carried or worn.

Chapellgill is one of the longest running hill races in Scotland and was started back in 1984 by the late great Robin Morris, with a field of just 10 runners. It claims to be the steepest hill race in Britain.
