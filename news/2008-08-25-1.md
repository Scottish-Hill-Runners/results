Simon Peachey is the new leader of the 2008 Scottish Long Series following Glenshee 9 and Ochil 2000s, with Stewart Whitlie moving into 2nd position ahead of Alec Keith who has finally slipped to 3rd place.

Joanne Anderson of Carnethy continues as highest ranked woman in the series.
