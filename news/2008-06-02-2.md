date: 2008-06-02

---

Please note that it would be a major help to the race organiser if you would pre-enter Durisdeer. For anyone who has not run in the race previously there are usually copious quantities of home-baked goodies courtesy of the good folk of Durisdeer. This is a small village and the people put a lot of work into the event.

For 2008, the race is a British and SHR Championship event, so allow plenty of time for getting there and please be courteous to everyone who is helping with the event, but then you all are!

Entry forms can be downloaded from the [Carnethy website](http://www.carnethy.com/ri_durisdeer.htm)
