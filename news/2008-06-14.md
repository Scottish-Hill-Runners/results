---
title: "Hart takes Traprain Law, 14 June"
date: 2008-06-14
excerpt: "Hunters Bog Trotters, led by Al Hart, filled three of the first four places.  '"
---

Given all the competing interests, the turnout of 64 runners for East Linton's annual Gala Day race to Traprain Law and back was more than satisfactory.

Al Hart (HBT), fortunate not to have his progress blocked by two canoes being carried over the bridge as he neared it on his return journey, won comfortably enough in the end from Phil Mowbray (HBT). Carnethy's captain Steven Fallon was third (and first vet), with Daryl Phillip third for HBT and fourth overall.

Fionn Nairn (HELP) and Rhona Anderson (Dunbar), both vets, took the first two places in the women's race; Jane Hart (HBT) was third.
