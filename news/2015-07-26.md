title: "Oxton Border Games Hill Race Results"
date: 2015-07-26
excerpt: "Colin Donnelly wins at Oxton - Saturday 25th July"
---

A small but select field turned out for the sixth Oxton Border Games hill race. It was a clean sweep for the veterans, with Colin Donnelly taking first place in 17:59, seconds ahead of Haddington's Brian Marshall (18:19, first veteran), with Nick Williamson of Dunbar in third (19:34). Teviotdale's Lindsey Knox took the prize for first lady with a time of 27:50. Thanks go to the landowners, timekeepers and marshalls for making it possible, and to the runners for making it worthwhile. Eb Rooney (Race Organiser) Full results on the tab above.
