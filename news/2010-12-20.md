---
title: "Contributions sought for the SHR's Journal for 2010"
date: 2010-12-20
excerpt: "We're looking for contributions for the forthcoming Journal..."
---

If you're spending the long evenings reflecting on the hill running year that's slipping away now, what about putting your thoughts down on paper and sending them to Ronnie Gallagher for inclusion in the SHR's Journal for 2010? Similarly, please let Ronnie know if you've seen an article on your Club's web site that you think deserves a wider audience.

The Journal comes out in mid-February each year and is sent to members along with the Calendar. It's intended for material that's more substantial than, for example, the routine race reports on the SHR's web site.

Please e-mail contributions to ronnie\_gallagherATyahoo.co.uk by the beginning of January. (Remember to replace the AT.) Thank you!
