The Northern England [BOFRA](http://www.bofra.co.uk/Alva.php) machine rolled into town for the Alva Highland Games hill race. Hardly a Scottish runner in sight.

BOFRA specialise in brutally short, sharp, steep and rough hill races.

The race winner for the 3rd year in a row was Alastair Dunn from Kendal-based Helm Hill, finishing in 20:22, and 30 seconds clear of Stirling University's Ewan McCarthy who was first to turn at the summit.

First lady was Lisa Lacon of Holmfirth in 25:49
