Beinn Resipol Hill race and fun run is taking place this coming Saturday (10th Sept) over in Ardnamurchan. This is the races second year and last year was a brilliant day with all the funds raised going to Ewen's room, an Ardnamurchan health and well-being group. Here's a promo video from last year's event: [https://vimeo.com/160313536](https://vimeo.com/160313536)
