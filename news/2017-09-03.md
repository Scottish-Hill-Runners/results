“In 2017 the junior hill league consists of five races from across Scotland. These races include the Scottishanthletics junior hill championships (held at East Lomond) and the trial race for the Junior Home International (at Peebles). To gain a final position athletes have to complete three races.

The winners of each category this year were:

Anna Hedley (Fife AC) - Under 15 female.

Cliona MacCheyne (Inverclyde) Under 17 female.

Anisha Badiel (Aberdeen AC) Under 20 female.

Ben Cameron (Forres Harriers) Under 15 male.

Levi Sweeney (Strathern Harriers) Under 17 male.

No Under 20 male overall winner.

In all 11 junior hill runners completed the league. Aside from the winners they were:

Libby Smith (Fife AC, 2nd under 15 female),

Ailsa Bennet (Fife AC 3rd under 15 female),

Struan Bennet (Fife AC, 2nd under 15 male),

Sam Griffin (Aberdeen AC, 3rd under 15 male),

Andrew Winter (Inverclyde, 4th under 15 male).

Ross Wyse (Fife AC, 2nd U17 male)

Congratulations to everyone!

Everyone will get a prize. Please contact me if I don’t get in touch to request an address.

Mark Harris (Scottishathletic junior hill running coordinator) [harrismark1ATmac.com](http://harrismark1atmac.com/)"
