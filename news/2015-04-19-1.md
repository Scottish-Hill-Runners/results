---
title: "British Championship - Finlay & Jasmin win in Wales"
date: 2015-04-19
excerpt: "Finlay Wild (Lochaber) and Jasmin Paris (Carnethy) won the respective male and female races at the British Championship Ras y Moelwyn."
---

Congratulations to Finlay Wild (Lochaber) and Jasmin Paris (Carnethy) who won the British Championship Race in Wales on Saturday 18th April – Ras y Moelwyn at Blaenau Ffestiniog.

Finlay won the race in 1:13:09, three minutes ahead of the opposition.

Jasmin finished with a time of 01:26:52
