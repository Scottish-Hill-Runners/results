_From Andy Birnie_

The weather gods were kind to Ben Venue again this year, with unseasonably warm temperatures & no wind. This provided the platform for some excellent racing, with superbly quick times, with both male & female records threatened but left intact… but only just.

The race was won by Hector Haines of HBT in a time of 1:12:27, chased home by Alasdair McLeod & Ross Campbell, with the Vets of Kenny Richmond & Craig Harvey not far behind.

The ladies race was won by Tessa Hill, making it a HBT double, in a time of 1:26:58, holding off Diane Baum from Lochaber. 1st ladies vet was Val Houston of Westies.

The team prizes went to host club Bellahouston Road Runners (Ali Ewen, Matt Williamson & Grant MacDonald) and Carnethy (Jacqui Higginbottom, Lorna Ashcroft & Helen Wise).

Everyone enjoyed a picnic feast after the race in glorious balmy October sunshine, as they all told tales of face plants in bogs, slips, slides & wrong turns – just a typical Ben Venue race! But the real winners of the day were the Lomond Mountain Rescue Team whose funds were boosted by £550 - on behalf of the team, many thanks, and look forward to seeing you all next year.
