---
title: "Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay"
date: 2023-08-08
excerpt: "Limited Places For Teams Still Available"
---

In response to the big demand for places at this year's Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay, the organisers have increased the race limit to 40 teams so there are now an additional 5 places available. This is the absolute maximum to avoid too much traffic disruption  and parking issues on the single track Glen Lednock road. All teams are urged to car share as much as possible and, if possible, consider bringing a bike to get to and from leg starts and finishes. More info and sign-ups here: [Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay](https://www.entrycentral.com/SWCHR)
