Stewart Whitlie has strengthened his lead at the top of the Scottish Long Classics series with 3rd place at Ochil 2000s, and with just 2 races remaining, the only runners who could mathematically catch Stewart are Brian Marshall and Alasdair Anthony.

In the women's competition, Emma O'Shea continues to lead, however Andrea Priestley is strongly placed on 4 races, and will almost certainly clinch the series if she runs either of the last 2 races.
