The Entry Central registration link for the Lomonds of Fife Race on Sunday 18th August is now available

[Lomonds of Fife Hill Race - EntryCentral](https://www.entrycentral.com/event/125111)

Distance: 16km, 750m ascent

Lomond Hill Runners bring you the yearly classic over both East and West Lomond and down the famous 'bum slide' to the Bunnet Stane before a short ascent and descent back to the finish. The terrain and route will test all your hill skills with checkpoints at key locations enroute.

A route map and gpx file can be found on the races section of the Lomies website.

[https://www.lomondhillrunners.co.uk/races.html](https://www.lomondhillrunners.co.uk/races.html)

If the race cannot take place on the date scheduled for reasons outwith our control, we will reschedule the event and carry your entry forward to the revised date. However the race has taken place in all conditions in the past, with the only change likely to avoid the bum slide in very wet conditions.

All entries are non-refundable.

PARKING, REGISTRATION & CHANGING FACILITIES

There is parking at the hall and within various locations around the village of Strathmiglo, whilst registration is from 11.30am for an 1pm start.  Toilets and changing at the registration hall.

REFRESHMENTS

It wouldn't be a Lomie event without our welcome post race vegetarian/vegan hot soup, water, hot drinks and snacks which will be at registration.

PRIZES: If male or female records are broken, we add £50 to our generous money and booze prizes for Senior 1st, as well as prizes for 2nd and 3rd, first V40, V50, V60+, and random spot prizes for placings depending on numbers on the day eg. 25th, 40th, 100th places etc.

Early Start Option: Please let the organiser know via email if you want to start 30 minutes before the main race. This option as it is more awkward to organise on the day so please let us know when you sign up.

Transfers up to 5 days before race day, requiring Name, DOB, address, next of kin name and number.
