title: "Keith posts another impressive win at Cioch Mhor, 17 April"
date: 2010-04-18
excerpt: "A record 45 runners turned out for this year's race and as in 2009, the race was dominated by HBT's Alec Keith. He very quickly established a significant lead and had opened up a very convincing 6 minute gap by the finish. In the women's race, it was all quite close at the half way stage, but Roxy Bannerman (HHR) went on to win comfortably in a new record time.  '"
---

After the fine Spring weather of recent days, conditions for Sat's Cioch Mhor race were much cooler with a strong wind, making for tough going on the outward leg of the race, but providing runners with a good blow-home on the return.

Alec Keith won last year's race in record time and admits to finding the course very much to his liking. On cue, he put in another very strong and impressive run and the race result was never in any doubt. At the half way point, a trailing group of around 5 runners were all quite closely bunched, but Dave Cummins (HHR) pulled away to finish a clear second, whilst Paul Maclean (Lochaber) and David Gallie (Inverness Harriers) both ran well and enjoyed a fine tussle for 3rd and 4th spots respectively. Inverness Harriers were deserved winners of the men's team prize, whilst a first ever women's team prize was awarded to HHR.

Excellent post race refreshments and plenty of generous prizes were dished out back at Dingwall Sports Centre making for a very relaxed and enjoyable end to the proceedings. Many thanks to all the organisers and helpers of this fine, not to be underestimated, Ross-shire race.
