---
title: "Ben Sheann Results"
date: 2016-06-21
excerpt: "James Espie (Deeside) and Jill Stephen (HBT) were the winners at the Bog & Burn Race at Ben Sheann on Wendesday 15th June"
---

In a closely fought contest, James Espie from Deeside Runners, came in front man at the 28th Ben Sheann Hill Race with a time of 26:46. Sam Alexander from Westerlands came in 2nd (27:02), just squeezing in ahead of local man Stephen Rawlinson from Lochtayside (27:09).

For the females, Jill Stephen, Hunters Bog Trotters, came in 1st on 33:11, Sarah MacKenzie, Lochaber, 39:58, and Shelie Mathieson, (unattached) came in 3rd on 45:07. Val Houston and Michelle Hetherington of Westies were actually 2nd and 3rd ladies but were given the F50 prizes instead.

A light drizzle just before the race and wet conditions over the previous days made the going a bit slippery underfoot, but there were no injuries and the finishing time was just outside the course record of 26:32
