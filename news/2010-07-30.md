_From organiser Andy Kitchin:_

A record turnout by far for the junior race, superb to see a good sized group of a dozen youngsters enjoying the evening’s one-mile run up to the wall corner and back, with juice and biscuits for afters.

In the senior race Christina Rankin lead the women home and broke the record by 10 seconds, clocking 30:29.

Prior to this Al Anthony had also set a new men’s record taking the race under 25 minutes for the first time with 24:58.
