The first SHR championship race is the Carnethy 5 on the 16th of February. Entries are open for this all week on sientries.com after which time a selective ballot will be made, see [www.carnethy.com](http://www.carnethy.com) for full details.

The provisional date the final race in the championships (Run of the Mill) has been put back to the 16th of November to avoid a clash with FRA relays.

Also, as suggested at the AGM, there will be a new category of Over 70 for the championship this year. The rules for this are the same as for the Over 60s (more details on Championship web page)
