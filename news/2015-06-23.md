We have now completed 4 of the 6 races in the Scottish Hill Runners Championship for 2015. To qualify for one of prized SHR Mugs you need to be a paid up member in the current year. Also if you are reading this and have not joined or renewed your membership it is worth noting the benefits of being a member, one of them being the development and maintenance of this website.
