---
title: "Knockfarrel Hill Race, 14 April"
date: 2007-04-14
excerpt: "Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) and Mari Todd (Highland Hillrunners) won the main prizes at Knockfarrel."
---

Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) beat fellow-vet Dave Cummins (Highland Hillrunners) by nearly a minute to win the Knockfarrel Hill Race. Lochaber also took third and fourth places thanks to Tom Smith and Paul MacLean, whilst Highland Hillrunners' Ross Bannerman was fifth.

Mari Todd (Highlland Hillrunners) was sixth overall and first lady, well ahead of Lochaber's Dawn Scott and Miranda Grant of HBT.

The full results are [here](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0025&RaceYear=2007), and there are photos on the Forres Harriers web site [here](http://mysite.orange.co.uk/forres-harriers3/Photos/Album6.htm).
