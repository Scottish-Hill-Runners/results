Rob Jebb had a storming run at the Isle of Jura race, to win in 3:07:49, just missing Mark Rigby's 1994 record by 50 seconds. Ian Holmes was second in 3:15:23, beating Jethro Lennox by 4 seconds in a sprint finish.

Angela Mudge once again set a new women's record, knocking an incredible 5 minutes off her previous best, to finish 7th overall in 3:40:33
