The latest Scottish Championship positions after Stuc a'Chroin are now available. As the two races are completely difference, it is not surprising that there are only a few categories with the same winner in both races, leaving most categories very open. So well done Joe Symonds (O/40), Angela Mudge (O/50), Veronique Oldham (O/55) and Colin Donnelly (O/60) for obtaining maximum points.

The latest championship tables are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2025 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2025 - Female.xls) for women.
