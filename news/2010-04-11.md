---
title: "Symonds shines at Silent Valley, 10 April"
date: 2010-04-11
excerpt: "Andy Symonds (running for Kendal) wins the first of this year's British Championship races...  '"
---

Silent Valley, in the Mournes in Northern Ireland, was the first of this year's British Championship races. It was won by Andy Symonds (running for Kendal) with Robbie Simpson (Deeside) a couple of minutes behind in fourth place overall and first MU23.

Ronnie Gallagher (Carnethy) caught the eye as first M50 and so did Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) as fourth woman.
