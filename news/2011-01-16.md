_From the Baxters:_

Another excellent race under benign conditions for a change. A bit windy but relatively warm and sunny. Considering the race was facing cancellation on Wednesday, the big thaw did us a favour.

For the first time in any duathlon we had a dead heat. Congratulations to Colin May and Ewan Taylor. The ladies' race was almost as close, with Joanne Thom just pipping Keri Macphee by six seconds. The team race was won by Peter Nimmo and Hazel Sutherland. There was a bit of needle for second and third Team with Mr Baxter/Lucy Grant beating Mrs Baxter/Jake Law. Lucy did have to claw back 39 places. Great to take part in our own race, and thanks to the other Marshals who took over the Fat Controller mantle.

A big thanks to everyone who helped out on the day and many thanks for support from Hub in the Forest and Adrian at Run and Become. We are grateful to Forest Enterprise for helping us have a race considering the icy conditions during the week.

See you at the Long on 27 February.
