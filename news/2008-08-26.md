---
title: "Familiar faces to the fore at the Great Wilderness Challenge, 23 August"
date: 2008-08-26
excerpt: "Alec Keith and Claire Gordon (both HBT) took the honours in the 25-mile race, and Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston H) won the 13-miler.  '"
---

The 25-mile race was won by Alec Keith (HBT), with Highland Hillrunners' Henry Blake and Paul Rodden second and fourth. Fife AC supplied fifth and seventh: Graham Bee and John Kay. Claire Gordon (HBT) was the first woman, follwed by Gillian Irvine and Roxy Bannerman (both Highland HR) in second and third.

The 13-mile race went to Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston H).
