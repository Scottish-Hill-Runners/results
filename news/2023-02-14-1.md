The Highland Boundary Races – Birnam & Deuchary Hill Races, April 2023

 Senior & junior races at Birnam on 1st April and note the 12.00noon start time for Deuchary on 2nd April. Also, new registration point for both races.

 The Birnam Hill Race 1 April 12noon start (SHR Championship race) and Junior races from 11am. Registration at guides hall, behind the school from 10am.

*   The Deuchary Hill race 2 April 12noon start. Registration at guides hall, behind the school from 10.30am. Early starters register at 10.30 for 11.30 start
*   Prizegiving at Birnam Guest House from 2pm.

Food and refreshments at finish of each race. 

Please visit  [https://www.wildoutdoorscotland.com/](https://www.wildoutdoorscotland.com/) for full details of the event. Or contact Adrian on 07761673231 [wildoutdoorscotland@gmail.com](mailto:wildoutdoorscotland@gmail.com)
