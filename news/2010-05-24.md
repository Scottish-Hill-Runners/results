Scottish runners were out in force at the Inter Counties race, held this year in Lletty, North Wales. Representing the four regions - North, Central, East and West - there were mixed fortunes as the heat took its toll. Highest placed runner in their category was Sula Young (East) who had a great race to finish 4th woman behind Victoria Wilkinson (Yorkshire). Clare Whitehead (North) wasnt far behind in 6th, and Andrea Priestley (Central) was 10th and 1st LV40. Robbie Simpson (North) led the Scottish men home, finishing 10th in a quality race won by Martin Cox (Leicestershire). Tom Owens (West), still on the comeback trail after injury, was 14th, whilst Ben Cole (East) was 23rd.

Scotland East women brought the only medals back across the border by finishing a fine 3rd, but (very) hot on their heels were North in 4th and East in 5th, with the West in 8th. Highest team place amongst the men went to East in 6th, one place ahead of the West boys, whilst North were 13th.
