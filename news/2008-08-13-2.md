---
title: "Anthony takes the Strain at Caerketton, 13 August"
date: 2008-08-13
excerpt: "Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) turns the tables on Murray Strain (HBT) to win the race and the Bog and Burn Series. Claire Gordon (HBT) is first woman.  '"
---

On an evening that was more bog-and-midge than Bog and Burn 74 runners turned out for the Caerketton Hill Race, the last of the ten counters in this summer's Series.

Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) lost out by 10 seconds to Murray Strain (HBT) in last Saturday's Cairngorm Mountain Run. The two were side-by-side again as they began the final descent but this time it was Anthony who had the upper hand, tearing down the hill to win by 17 seconds. Carnethy veterans filled five of the next six places, led by Adam Ward and split by Al Hart (HBT).

In the women's race Claire Gordon (HBT) showed again that she is in fine form, winning from Scout Adkin (Moorfoots) with Lisa Gamble (HBT) third.

The junior race was dominated by the Dunbar Laws; Helen Rogers (Westies) was the first girl.

Bog and Burn round-up to come.
