Morgan Donnelly wins Yetholm, with Victoria Wilkinson first female.

Many thanks from Norham RC to everyone who turned out at Yetholm for what was a very big day for a very small club, great to see so many new faces at our race we hope you all enjoyed it as much as we did.........the weather certainly helped and the cake didn't go wrong either!!! We were delighted to be able to donate £1000 to the Border Search & Rescue team and £500 to the Yetholm Macmillan cancer group and Yetholm Village Hall will also benefit........a great day all round. There were a few items of lost property handed in please contact us and we will try to get you reunited asap. Next year it's back to a low key event, maybe even run the opposite direction.....we hope to see some of you then

A' the best, Les
