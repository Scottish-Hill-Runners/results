Cairn William Hill Race Report:

37 runners were rewarded by sunshine and dry weather for the 2017 edition of Cairn William, although conditions were still wet underfoot given the recent rain. The race was won by Veteran Jim Tole of Metro, who held off a strong challenge on the descent from Felix Wilson. Hayden Lorimer made up the podium with 3rd place. In the ladies race, Veronique Oldham of Cosmics took a comfortable win with Hilary Cameron of Forres taking 2nd and Petra Hampton 3rd place. Veronique was also first super vet and Hilary first vet home.

The men's super-vet category was won by Dennis McDonald of Cosmics.

10 juniors enjoyed distances of 2km, 4.5km and 8km, with Joseph Wight (U18) of Deeside completing an 8km circuit of Pitfichie and Cairn William, Millie Brooks winning the 2km U12 race and Ben Cameron (U16) winning the 4.5km Pitfichie race. Hector Lorimer won the U14 category over the same distance.
