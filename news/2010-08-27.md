The Argyllshire Gathering was held at Oban on 26 August. The hill race was won by Hector Haines (Edinburgh) by just four seconds from Tom Smith (Oban). Niciola Meekin (Oban) finished fifth, a minute ahead of Sarah Inglis (Lothian) and 42 seconds inside the women's course record set by Alison's Simpson in 2003.
