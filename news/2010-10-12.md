_Bruce Poll reports:_

Twenty brave souls turned out in Fort William for the latest SHR sponsored navigation course. Saturday was a little showery out on the hill, and provided some good practice at placing the compass on a wet map and getting the bearing accurate.

The Sunday long 'O' took in part of the Eagle Crag race route and when the cloud came down added a touch of reality with small ring contours appearing to quadruple in size in the shadows. Everyone was surprised at how much they learnt over the weekend, and the confidence that goes with it to trust it. The next few months provide great practice conditions for navigation.
