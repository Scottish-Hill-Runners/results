Congratulations to Lewis Taylor and Pippa Dakin (both Carnethy HRC) for their wins at the inaugural Allermuir Hill Race. The 53 competitors and around a dozen marshals and supporters all arrived at the race via public transport, on foot or by bicycle, in what was a vehicle-free, waste-free race. Physical numbers were replaced with sustainability-themed words written on arms, with Lewis adorned with 'urban' and Pippa branded with 'smog'.
