_From Ranjit Thomas_

Despite predictions of apocalyptic conditions, a short break in the weather allowed the Criffel race to go ahead with 54 runners. Unfortunately , several runners were caught up in bad weather en route to Dumfries and couldn’t make it in time. As one would expect of Criffel, conditions were very treacherous. However the snow fall overnight and minimum amounts of ice, helped keep the surface relatively runnable. The descents were particularly slippery but there were no serious injuries. Carnethy swept up the first prizes, Andrew Gilmore was the first male completing in 60.33 minutes with Charlotte Morgan, as first female at just over 68 minutes.
