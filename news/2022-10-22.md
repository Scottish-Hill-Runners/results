---

The six races that will comprise the 2023 SHR Championship are confirmed –

*   1st April                         Birnam                            (Short)
*   20th May                        Goatfell                           (Medium)
*   1st July                          Meall an t-Suidhe             (Short)
*   29th July                        Ben Rinnes                      (Long)
*   26th Aug (TBC)               Ochil 2000s                     (Long)
*   17th September              Merrick Hill Race              (Medium)

And the four races in the 2023 British Championship will be –

*   25th March           Mourne Marauder (Medium) – Newcastle, Co. Down, N Ireland
*   1st July                Meall an t-Suidhe (Short) – Fort William, Scotland
*   5th August           Maesgwm Detour (Medium) – Llanberis, Gwynedd, Wales
*   20th August         Sedbergh Hills (Long) – Sedbergh, Cumbria, England
