title: "WMRT Trial Races, Aberfoyle, 11 August"
date: 2007-07-10
excerpt: "There's information about the trial races for anyone thinking of running, helping or spectating."
---

The trial races for the World Mountain Running Trophy will take place near Aberfoyle on Saturday 11 August. There are races for anyone born in 1991 or earlier, and runners of all standards are welcome. The Junior Women start at 12.30pm, followed at 1.00pm by the Senior Women and Junior Men, and then at 2.00pm by the Senior Men.

If you don't want to run, what about helping Race Organiser Mark Johnston by (for example) marshalling? Or join the midges, enjoy the spectacular views, and spectate?
