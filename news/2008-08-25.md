Alasdair Anthony waited until Caerketton, the final race of the series, to leapfrog Ochils clubmate Bob Wiseman into first place and win the 2008 series. David Duncan and Ian McManus both completed all 10 races.

Summary of category winners:

Senior men:  
1\. Alasdair Anthony, Ochils  
2\. Bob Wiseman, Ochils  
3\. Kenny Richmond, Bellahouston RR

V40 men:  
1\. Steven Fallon, Carnethy  
2\. David Riach, Westerlands  
3\. Chris Upson, Westerlands

V50 men:  
1\. Martin Dean, FVO  
2\. Don Reid, Westerlands

V60 men:  
1\. Ian McManus, Irvine AC

Senior women:  
1\. Angela Mudge, Carnethy  
2\. Ellen Homewood, Westerlands
