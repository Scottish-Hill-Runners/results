The 36th Isle of Jura Fell Race was won by Finlay Wild in dry conditions on a warm and sunny day. He was just 3 mins 23 secs outside Hector Haines' 2014 record.

The first lady was Jasmin Paris in a time just 2 mins 17 secs outside her 2015 record. This could at least partly be put down to a navigational error on the descent from CP6.

Three records were broken this year:

**MV50:** went for the third time in 5 races. Ian Holmes knocked 1 min 15 secs off Stewart Whitlie's 2013 record. This was a long-standing ambition for Ian.

**FV50:** Andrea Priestley knocked almost a minute off Fiona Maxwell's 2011 record.

**MV70:** Gareth Bryan-Jones casually reduced the MV70 record set (for the first time) by Roger Ashby in 2013 by about an hour.
