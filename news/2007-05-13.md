---
title: "Peachey and Palmer prevail at Ben Lomond, 12 May"
date: 2007-05-13
excerpt: "Simon Peachey (Carnethy) and Helen Palmer (Bellahouston) were the victors at Ben Lomond hill race.  '"
---

Simon Peachey (Carnethy) coped admirably with the added pressure of being labelled the pre-race favourite, and was at the front throughout to record a comfortable victory. Even if, arguably, he might not have had it all his own way if Brian Marshall\`s characteristically aggresive descent had not been cut short by an unfortunate injury.

There was a new name on the ladies trophy too. Helen Palmer (Bellahouston) may be relatively unknown to hillrunners, but given that she's an established international orienteer, her victory shouldn't come as any great surprise.

[Results](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0035&RaceYear=2007)    [More photos](http://www.flickr.com/photos/scottishhillrunners/sets/72157600293486714/)

The race was preceded by the **scottish**athletics' Junior Championships, of which more information when we get it, and by a fun run open to all. Martin Hyman, who organised the latter, says "Well done everyone! The smiles were brighter than ever. Come back next year and bring your grandma. Many thanks to all the marshals, timekeepers and other helpers. Particular thanks to Robert Inglis who spent hours marking the fun run and the junior race courses on Friday."
