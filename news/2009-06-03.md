Robbie Simpson made the headlines yet again by smashing the long standing course record for the Kilpatricks race, coming home well clear of Kenny Richmond and Oleg Chepelin. Val Houston was first woman (and first vet), whilst John Kennedy was first male vet. Bellahouston were first team and Westerlands had a 1-2-3 to take the women's award.
