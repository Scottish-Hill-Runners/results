Cort-ma Law results

A day’s break in the rain provided perfect weather for this year’s race, but soggier than usual going. James Espie (Deeside) was first home in 47:23 followed closely by Robert Simpson (Shettleston) in 47:26. Edel Mooney (Ochil) was first woman (for the second successive year) and 21st overall in 60:46, with Charlotte Heath (Westerlands) in second place in 69:13. Shettleston won the male team competition, but unfortunately no club had enough women running to win the female competition. Congratulations to Finn Friel who was the first and only junior.

A big thank you from the organiser to Westerlands and friends for marshalling and administration.

Dave Rogers (Race Organiser)
