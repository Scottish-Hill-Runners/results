Helen Rowe, secretary of the Borders Search and Rescue Unit has organised a sponsored walk and challenge run in the Scottish Cheviots

_Hi There,_

_I am running an event in the Scottish Cheviots on Sunday 2nd October which I think members of your group might be interested in._

_We have two running routes available; 14km and 24km, with both taking in some of the best of the Scottish Cheviots and in support of a local mountain rescue team._

_Full details are available at www.bordersar.org.uk_

_Thank you,_

_Helen_

Helen Rowe Secretary, Border SAR Unit
