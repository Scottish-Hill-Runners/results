An Teallach Hill Race.

Note that the printed calendar states that race is entry on the day. But as it is anticipated that this year's race will be busy with people doubling up with the Meall a Ghuithais SHR championhsip race which is on the day before. So, it is important for the race organisers to know the numbers beforehand mainly to make sure that parking is taken account of.

It is preferred that entry to the race is done by the online method - 

Entry for the race will be by SiEntries – [https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=9674](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=9674)

Entries will open on Friday 1st April
