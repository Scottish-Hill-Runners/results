---
title: "Run-4-It Scottish Hill Running champs after Slioch"
date: 2011-05-31
excerpt: "Now the statistician has returned from Jura, the latest Scottish Hill Running Championship listings after the second race at Slioch are available."
---

With some of the leading runners going off course at Slioch, there were some upsets and the championship looks very open. Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston) is leading the Senior Men's and the Over 40s. Kenny is one of three Over 40s in the top four of the Senior list! Catriona Buchanan(Ochil) leads the Senior Women from two Over 40s, of whom Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy) just has the edge in the Over 40s list.

Highland Hill Runners must age well as they are leading both the men's and women's Over 50 championships with Gordon Stewart and Gill Irvine resp. Former Ben Nevis record holder Ros Evans (Lochaber) leads the women's Over 60 and Martin Hulme (Corstorphine) has made a great start to the men's Over 60 by winning both races.

The men's Senior team has been the same top three in the same order in both races with Shettleston leading the way, while Carnethy are dominating the vets (from Highland Hill Runners). A strong Ochil squad are leading the women's Senior team with Carnethy doing the same in the Over 40s.
