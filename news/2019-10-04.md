The championship listings after the Two Breweries Race are now available. Only one race to go, though quite a few people need to do that race to get their mug or even, for some, to get in the medals in the championship. Remember the awards are given out at the 'do' after the last race, although not the mugs, they will be given out once we know how many to order.

Interestingly, all the female champions have already been decided with one race spare, though second and third places are up for grabs. The person or club at the top of each of the lists cannot now be beaten. The male categories are much more open, partly due to many of the favourites only having done three races and obviously saving themselves for the last race! The only male categories decided are the Under 23 and both Team categories.

Current positons are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2019 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2019 - Female.xls) for women or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.
