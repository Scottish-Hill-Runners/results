_From Alex Macrae_

On a day of almost incessant rain, blustery headwinds and courses holding a lot of water – and debris - as the result of the previous weekend’s monsoon, the races went ahead as planned, with understandably slower times this year. The races provided three new winners, with Ross Bannerman (Highland Hill Runners) taking the 25 mile victory on his 10th appearance at the event. Melanie Gaff (Wester Ross AC) won the ladies race on her debut. The 13 mile race was won by previous winner Chris Scott-Park from Gartocharn and Ist Lady was newcomer Aisling McGhee from Glasgow. Special thanks go to all the marshals and helpers, Dundonnell Mountain Rescue, HM Coastguard and PDG Helicoptersfor ensuring a safe and successful event in unseasonal conditions.
