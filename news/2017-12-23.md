Ever thought of competing in a mountain marathon? This years LAMM is going to the first mountain marathon on the Isle of Harris.

Check out [www.lamm.co.uk](http://www.lamm.co.uk)

The event is on Friday 1st June to Sat 2nd, it can’t take place on a Sunday on Harris. It promises to be one of the best.
