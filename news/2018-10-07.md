title: "Meall nan Tarmachan Race Results & Report"
date: 2018-10-07
excerpt: "Wins for Tom Harris and Catherine Joanne Macphail (Anster Haddies) at the Meall nan Tarmachan race on Saturday 6th September."
---

A rare window of good weather graced us for the Tarmachan race, reflected in the number of runners being up on recent years. Tom Harris was first in 52.11 with Catherine Joanne Macphail first female in 1.06.06. We made £565 to split between National Trust for Scotland for our conservation work on Tarmachan and Ben Lawers and the Killin Mountain Rescue Team who provided marshals and safety cover as always. This year they also went home with a couple of cases of Innis and Gunn beer. We received an amazingly vast quantity from the brewery which meant anyone who wanted went away with a few bottles. Thanks are also due to Killin Co-op, the Real Food Cafe, the Green Welly, Tesco in Perth and Tunnocks for additional donations. It will be the 10th Tarmachan Race next year (how time flies) so we'll have to see if we can outdo the piles of goodies for that!  

Dan Watson (Race Organiser)
