Hector and Rachel Haines (HBT) won at the Dumyat race on Wednesday 6th May.

Ben Stevenson (Edinburgh Uni) and Gregor Stewart (Westies) were 2nd and 3rd in the male race and Georgia Tindley (Edinburgh Uni) and Angela Richardson (Scottish Athletics) were 2nd and 3rd in the female race.
