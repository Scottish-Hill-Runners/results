There was a great turnout of 60 for the annual SHR “do” and prize giving at the Forth Inn on Saturday. Many thanks to Ian White of Run 4 It who has sponsored the SHR championship again this year and presented the trophies. Thanks to all those who supported the event – a great evening was had by all. Excellent grub from the Forth Inn, followed by dancing to the Yoohoo ceilidh band!
