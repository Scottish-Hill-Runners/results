---
title: "Ben Rinnes 5 Tops Race Results and Report"
date: 2018-07-31
excerpt: "Wins and new course records to Eoin Lennon & Jill Stephen at the SHR 2018 Championship race on Saturday 28th July"
---

Unfortunately,following a three month drought the Dufftown Highland Games fell on a day with severe weather warnings for heavy rain and lightning storms but thankfully the latter never materialised and the scenario of delayed starts,a shortened course and last minute cancellation was averted.However the spectators who did brave the elements witnessed a record field of 171 starters with new mens and womens records being set.

By all accounts the race conditions were “spicy” with sporadic downpours and blustery winds with a bit of hail and sleet thrown in and yet underfoot conditions were still fast. A big thank you must go to all the volunteer marshals in the games park and on the hill who were backed up by off-road support group COTAG providing reassurance on what could have turned out a much more serious day.

Graeme Bartlett (race organiser)
