The inaugural race witnessed a tremendous battle at the front this year. Local British orienteering champion, Alasdair McLeod knows the hills here so well and had Gordon Lennox pushing him all the way and they both scorched round the course in 1.28 with Alisdair prevailing by seconds.  
Karen Lyons who is in great form this year, had a comfortable win in a time of 1.52 ahead of Amanda Blackhall in 1.58.  
40 runners took part and great to see the Lochaber mini bus back again after the recent Cioch Mhor race with most of the wine seeming to head back west!  
Many thanks to the many marshalls that were scattered around the course at checkpoints and tricky junctions etc that meant nobody got lost and also to Sheilagh who organises the village Gala on the day as well.

Ross Bannerman
