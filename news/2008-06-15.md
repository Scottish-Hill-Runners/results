---
title: "Peachey and Mykura retain their Seven Hills titles, 15 June"
date: 2008-06-15
excerpt: "Carnethy's Simon Peachey and Jill Mykura won Sunday's race over the Seven Hills of Edinburgh.  '"
---

Simon Peachey and Jill Mykura warmed up for the successful defence of their [Seven Hills](http://www.seven-hills.org.uk/) titles on Sunday by running in the British Championship race at Durisdeer on Saturday. Third-placed Stewart Whitlie's preparations included the birth of his second child on Friday. Matt Bell (Edinburgh AC) split Peachey and Whitlie by finishing second, whilst Keith Hood (Corstorphine) was fourth.

Sarah Balancier (Edinburgh AC) and Amanda Henderson (Portobello) took the minor places in the women's race.
