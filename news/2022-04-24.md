Full race results for the recent races at Kaim and Newtyle (both on Wednesday 20th April) and The Cobbler and Hunters Bog Trot (both on Saturday 23rd April) are now on the SHR results tab.

Wins for James Waldie and Lyn Gatherer (both Perth Road Runners) at Kaim. James’ time of 30:02 was a new course record.

Harrison Stubbs (Westies) and Edel Mooney (Ochils) were the winners at Kaim.

Sasha Chepelin (Carnethy) and Amy Frankland (Lasswade) were the winner at the HBT race.

And The Cobbler was raced for the first time – Ralph McInally (Unattached) and Alison Banford (Hartfell HR) were the inaugural winners.
