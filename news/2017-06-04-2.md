---
title: "Cort-ma Law – Kit Requirements"
date: 2017-06-04
excerpt: "The Cort-ma Law race is on Wednesday 7th June at 19.30 and as usual, whatever the conditions, runners will be required to wear or carry standard safety equipment."
---

You will be required to carry the following standard safety equipment:

• water and wind-proof whole body cover, plus other body cover appropriate for the weather conditions  
• a map of the route, a compass and a whistle.

Your kit will be checked at the start of the race. No kit, no run.
