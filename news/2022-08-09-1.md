Craig Dunain hill race - 20th August  
New route - new venue.

Distance: 10km Climb: 390m (BM)

Registration from 12:00  
Briefing: 13.50  
Start Time: 14.00

Venue: Registration and parking at Great Glen House IV3 8NW  
Race starts near the roundabout on Foresters Way. Approx 500m from Great Glen House.

[https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=10105](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=10105)

Cioch Mhor hill race - 24th September  
Distance: 14km Climb: 660m (AM)

Registration from 10:00  
Briefing: 11.50  
Start Time: 12.00

Registration and parking at Dingwall Leisure Centre IV15 9LH  
Race starts just behind Tulloch Castle hotel - 10 minute walk from the leisure centre.
