We are now half-way through the 2014 SHR Championship and it is still very open in many classes, e.g. in the Senior Men there has been a different winner in each of the races with the current leader, Kenny Richmond (Shettleston), not one of them! Kenny, however, looks to have the Over 40s category sewn up. Long distance specialist, Jasmin Paris (Carnethy), is leading the women’s and will be hard to beat. Veronique Oldham (Cosmic) is leading the Over 40s, but Shona Robertson (Shettleston) could come through and take it. Which is a similar situation to that in the men’s Over 50s, as current leader Alan Smith (Deeside) will have to run well to hold on and not let current champion Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) nip past him. Reigning champions, Ros Evans (Westerlands) and Ewan Paterson (Moray), are both looking the strongest in the over 60 categories. The Men’s Over 70 is fairly open, with Gareth Bryan-Jones (Ochil) perhaps the strongest.
