Deeside Night Race Series

Four cafes, four forests, four races.

Friday December 6th 2019: Riverside Cottage Café, Cambus O’May: Cnoc Dubh

Friday December 27th 2019: Angies Cafe, Tarland. Knockargety

Friday January 17th 2020: Deeside Activity Park, Dess, Aboyne: Dess Woods

Thursday February 20th 2020: Potarch Inn, Kincardine O’Neil: Potarch Hill

All the races have a 7:30 start and the entry fee is £1

Full details here: [http://www.deesiderunners.com/night-race-series/](http://www.deesiderunners.com/night-race-series/)
