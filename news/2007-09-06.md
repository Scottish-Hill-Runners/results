The final results of the Junior League are now available in the [Junior section](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/Juniors.aspx) . Saturday's race at Bonaly completed the three race series, following Ben Lomond and Scolty.

Well done to everyone who scored points in the League and congratulations to category winners:

Halina Rees (U14G)

Adam Gatens (U14B)

Catriona Buchanan (U16G)

Robbie Simpson (U16B)

Jessica Martin (U19G)

Michael McCloone (U19B)

Team winners were Kilbarchan (Girls) and Fife (Boys).
