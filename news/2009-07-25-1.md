After the first 7 races in the SHR Long Classics Series, Chris Upson is holding a temporary lead at the top of the table. Ellen Homewood is highest place female runner in 6th place overall.

Upson's breakaway lead with 5 races will be short-lived, as the peloton of Simon Peachey, Brian Marshall, Stewart Whitlie and Dave Cummins (all on 4 races) are poised to sweep past at Glenshee 9 on August 9th.
