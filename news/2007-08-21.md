title: "Team for World Trophy announced"
date: 2007-08-21
excerpt: "The team consists of six senior men, four junior men and three junior women, but no senior women."
---

The Scottish team for the World Trophy in Switzerland is on the **scottish**athletics [web site](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=3008").

It is made up of six senior men, four junior men and three junior women. No senior women have been included.

Joe Symonds (Dundee HH), who was pre-selected for the team, completed his preparations with second place in the [Weasdale Horseshoe Fell Race](http://www.ukresults.net/2007/weasdale.html) last Saturday.

Good luck to all concerned!

There’s a discussion on this selection news started up on the [Forum](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Forum/viewtopic.php?t=55) .
