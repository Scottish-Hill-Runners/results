---
title: "New rule on GPS devices in Championship races"
date: 2021-09-09
excerpt: "GPS devices must not be used for navigation in Championship races ..."
---

A new rule regarding the use of GPS devices in SHR Championship races has been added. A couple of other rules have been slightly clarified.

Please check this out on the [SHR Championship page](https://scottishhillrunners.uk/championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-001).

Reliance on any kind of electronic device for navigation is unsafe. Competitors must carry and be able to use a physical map and compass.

There is also the issue of fairness to all competitors. Mobile phones are often carried, and sometimes required to be carried, at the race organiser's discretion, and smart phones are GPS devices, so there is an element of trust
