SHR 2014 Championship mugs will be available at Tinto for all those who completed the required number of races and who are paid up SHR members. Those who are not members can claim a mug by paying £4.50 to cover costs incurred by SHR. If you qualified for a mug, but won't be at Tinto, please ask someone from your club to pick it up for you if possible. Alternatively, they will also be available at other events e.g. Carnethy. Please note that SHR do not mail out the mugs, it is your own responsibility to arrange to collect them.
