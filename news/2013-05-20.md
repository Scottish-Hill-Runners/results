_From Paul Emsley_

Finlay Wild (Lochaber AC) won the Goatfell Hill Race in fine style, with a time of 1:15:56, a time that has only been bettered on the current course by Andy Peace in 1994. Veteran Categories were won by Mark Harris (Fife AC – M40 – 1:21:42), Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy – M50 – 1:22:43), Ewan Paterson (Moray Road Runners – M60 – 1:44:44) and Charlie Love (Dundee Hawkhill Harriers – M70 – 2:26:46). Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) took the honours in the Ladies’ Race, winning in 1:34:50. The other category winners were Shona Robertson (Shettleston Harriers – W40 – 1:35:58), Joan Wilson (Carnethy – W50 – 1:53:59) and Ros Evans (Westerlands – W60 – 2:21:04). The Carnethy team of Andrew Fallas, Stewart Whitlie and Iain Whiteside easily won the team trophy.

Many of the competitors asked me to thank all the marshals, particularly the Arran Mountain Rescue Team, who had to endure rain, hail and gale force winds for several hours.
