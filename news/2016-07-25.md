date: 2016-07-25
excerpt: "Team Gilmore won the Ochils 8 Relay Race on Sunday 24th July ahead of Forres Harriers in 2nd and Torry Elites in 3rd."

The inaugural Ochils 8 Hill Relay race was won yesterday by Carnethy’s Team Gilmore.

A tough route in testing conditions for the 25 teams who took part. Great organisation from Edel Mooney and Gary Fraser.
