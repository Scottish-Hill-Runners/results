Note that there will be a junior race that start’s 15 minutes before the senior race

Link for signups via Protay now available –  [Chapelgill Hill Race 2024 (protay.co.uk)](https://protay.co.uk/chapelgill24/)

Payment (£7) is on the day, but please pre-enter to assist the race organisers.

Chapellgill is one of the longest running hill races in Scotland and was started back in the late 70’s by the late great Robin Morris. It claims to be the steepest hill race in Britain.

Venue: Glenkirk farm, Glenholm, Broughton.
