title: "Scottish Championship update after Merrick"
date: 2007-09-17
excerpt: "Five races out of the six are now completed and there are still championship medals and mugs there for the taking."
---

Only one one race to go now in the 2007 championship, at Morven next weekend, and the Men's and Ladies' Senior championships have still be be decided. Brian Marshall (HELP) really had to win at Merrick to keep in the hunt and did just that, and like Brian, Elke Schmidt's (Bellahouston) win gives her the chance of jumping to the top after the final race. Lots of minor places still up for grabs as well.

So far, if my calculations are correct, after Merrick 33 people have earned the prestigious SHR championship mugs and another 34 could still earn one provided they run at Morven.
