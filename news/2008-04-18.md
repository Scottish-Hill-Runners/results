From Mark Johnston, Chairman of Selectors:

The 2008 Inter Counties Fell Race takes place at the Belmont Winter Hill Race (4.5 miles, 1000ft climb) near Bolton on Saturday 10th May. Four Scottish district teams have been selected for the event, with the teams plus reserves listed below. Main team selections were based on the results from the Carnethy 5, Bishop Hill and Knockfarrel races, with reserve places selected on recent and previous form.

Competitors will be supplied with a district vest and there will be a subsidy towards travel costs, though you'll be expected to coordinate your own travel (and accommodation if necessary).

We’re hoping to put together full Scottish district teams (4 men/4 women) to take on the English county teams such as Cumbria, Yorkshire and Lancashire. If your name is listed below as either a selected athlete OR a reserve, please contact Mark Johnston (email preferable) at [Mark\_Johnston@tiscali.co.uk](mailto:Mark_Johnston@tiscali.co.uk) or phone 01506 670184, as soon as possible to indicate availability (yes/maybe/no) so that the teams can be finalised as soon as possible. A prompt response would be very much appreciated.

If you know anyone in the list below (including reserves) who does not regularly check the SHR website, then please alert them to their selection to speed up the process.
