---
title: "SHR Calendar 2010 - Please confirm race details"
date: 2009-11-29
excerpt: "Please could all Scottish hill race organisers confirm the details of their races for the 2010 Calendar as soon as possible. '"
---

Please could all Scottish hill race organisers confirm the details of their races for 2010 by emailing the following information to Chris Upson at [chris.upson@yahoo.co.uk](mailto:chris.upson@yahoo.co.uk) :

*   **Race Name, Date, Time, Venue, Entry Fee, Distance/Climb** , plus any other comments - eg. new race records, junior races or modified course.
    
*   The **minimum age limit**(s) for the race. These _must_ comply with the junior safety guidelines - see below.
    
*   **Race Organiser**: Name & address, Phone no., email, website.
    
    If race details are unchanged from 2009, then simply confirm the **date** and **time** of the race.
    
    Many thanks
    
    _Chris_
    
    NOTE ON AGE LIMITS: Race organisers must stipulate minimum age limits for their events and the following limits for younger runners must be observed:
    
    Race distance (km) Minimum age (on 1 January 2010)
    
    Up to 3 km : Organiser's discretion
    
    3.1 to 5 km : 12 yrs
    
    5.1 to 6 km : 14 yrs
    
    6.1 to 10 km : 16 yrs
    
    Over 10 km : 18 yrs
