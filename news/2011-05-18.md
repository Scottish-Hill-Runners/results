_From organiser Mark Harris_

The junior hill league is run by Scottish Athletics and consist of four races (Lomonds of Fife, Ben Lomond, Scolty and Menstrie).

Overall the league after two races is as follows:

U19 boys Zac Delaney (Anster Haddies) - second in both races. U19 girls Scout Adkin (Moorfoots) - first and second in the race age category.

U16 boys Lucas Cheskin (Moorfoot) - good runs in both races. U16 girls jointly led by Louise Mercer (Teviotdale) and Corryn Christie (Fife AC) - both winners, no runner did both races.

U14 boys Logan Rees (Fife AC) - the only runner to win both races outright. U14 girls Ana Brocklebank (Fife AC) - first and third in her races.

Two more league races to go: Scolty Sunday 5th June, and Menstrie, Sunday 28th August (JHI selection race).

For more information, or corrections, please contact Mark Harris, harrismark1ATmac.com
