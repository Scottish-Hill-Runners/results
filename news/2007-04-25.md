---
title: "Scottish Team for Knockdhu International"
date: 2007-04-25
excerpt: "A strong Scottish team has been selected to run in the first international hill race of the 2007 season, at Knockdhu in Northern Ireland."
---

The 2007 international hillrunning season gets underway on Saturday, with the Knockdhu Home International, which will be held on the hills above Larne, Northern Ireland.  
  
Congratulations to the following runners who have gained selection:
