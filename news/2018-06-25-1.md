---
title: "Eagle Crag Hill Race Results and Report"
date: 2018-06-25
excerpt: "Wins for Lochaber's Es Tresidder and Listette Walter at the Eagle Crag race on Saturday 23rd June"
---

David Marshalls record from 2007 still stands at 00:58:28 ,But Lochaber's Es Treisdder got within a minute of this at Saturdays Eagle Crag race. Es is the first Lochaber runner to get under the hour for this race. 35 runners took part in some fine racing weather - dry with a light breeze. First Lady home was Lochaber's Lisette Walter to complete the double for Lochaber.
