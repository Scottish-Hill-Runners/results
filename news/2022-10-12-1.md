**2022 Long Classics Series – Final placings**

Final scores after Pentland Skyline race at the weekend, which was last race in the series, as per link below.

In the Men’s competition, **Ali Mason** of **Carnethy** overtook his teammate,  long standing leader, **Tim Morgan** after the final race to pick up top spot. Impressive finish to the season for Ali winning 3 out of the final 4 long races. **Andy Fallas** finished in 3rd place just ahead of **Eliot Sedman**. Also both **Carnethy**.

In the Women’s competition, long-standing leader, **Chloe Lumsdon** of **Ochils** retained her position to finish in top spot ahead of **Diana Challands** of **Moorfoot Runners**. **Veronique Oldham** of **Cosmics** completed the podium positions for the Women.

There were 25 runners who completed 4 or more races in the series so a special well done to them.
