There were big winning margins for both man and woman at a windswept Pentland Skyline. Al Anthony won the race by 9 minutes from Es Tresidder, who in contrast was second by two seconds from Stewart Whitlie. Lucy Colquhoun was even more dominant in the ladies race, finishing 11 minutes clear of Jill Mykura, with Rachel Vincent another 14 minutes behind in third.
