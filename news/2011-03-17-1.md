Following the success of last year’s navigation courses, SHR are organising another course for hill runners on the weekend of 7-8 May, based at the [Dounans Outdoor Centre](http://www.soec.org.uk/Pages/Dounans.asp), Aberfoyle, in the Trossachs.

The course provider will again be qualified mountain instructor, keen hill runner and mountain marathoner, Bruce Poll. The course is intended both for runners who have limited experience of hill running and for experienced hill runners who wish to brush up their skills.

The closing date for applications is 8 April 2011. Places are limited and acceptance will be based on a first come first served basis.
