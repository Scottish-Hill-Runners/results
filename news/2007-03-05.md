---
title: "Jebb and Mudge triumph at Carnethy"
date: 2007-03-05
excerpt: "Rob Jebb (Bingley) successfully defended his Carnethy 5 title, whilst Angela Mudge (Carnethy) won the women's race for the umpteenth time."
---

Rob Jebb (Bingley) successfully defended his Carnethy 5 title, though it wasn't until the final climb up Carnethy that he managed to shake off Jethro Lennox (Shettleston). Scott Fraser (Edinburgh University) was third.  
  
Angela Mudge (Carnethy) won the women's race for the umpteenth time, from the 2006 and 2005 British Champions Natalie White (Bingley) and Jill Mykura (Carnethy).  
  
The results are on the [Carnethy web site](http://www.carnethy.com/ri_results/c5/c5_2007.htm).
