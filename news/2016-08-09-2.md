---
title: "Wins for Kristian Jones (Dark Peak) and Anwen Darlington (Dundee RR) at Glenshee"
date: 2016-08-09
excerpt: "Bad weather necessitated a reduced route at Glenshee on Sunday 7th August"
---

The Glenshee9 became the Glenshee1 as un-seasonal storm force winds forced a change of route for this year’s race. The alternative route still managed to include 1375metres of climb and a distance of nearly 15 miles. Any lingering doubts about the need for a changed course were easily dispelled following the runners experience on topping out onto the only Munro visited into winds which reached nearly 100mph on the neighbouring mountain.

First placed Kristan Jones came home in a time of 2:18:33 with Doug Tullie and Al Anthony following on a few minutes later. The ladies winner was Anwen Darlington in a time of 2:58:34 with Georgia Tinley in second place a few minutes later. Third lady was Moira Davie.

Ist MV40 was Billy Gibson. Deeside Runners completed a double in the V50's with Alan Smith first MV50 and Lina Mardall first FV50. Richard Toller was first MV60.

As ever, thanks to Braemar MRT who provided marshal and safety cover for the race, without whose assistance the race would be unviable and also to Glenshee Ski Centre for hosting the event.

All profits are donated to BMRT.

Ali Hubbard
