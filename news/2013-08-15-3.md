Once again, Caerketton Hill Race proved a fitting finale for the midweek Bog and Burn Series. The overall series winner was Iain Stewart (Westerlands) followed by Iain Whiteside (Carnethy). Val Houston won the women's competition, followed by Sarah Adam (both Westerlands).

**_Main category winners:_**

**Men**  
1\. Iain Stewart, Westerlands  
2\. Iain Whiteside, Carnethy  
3\. Alan Smith, Deeside (and 1st M50)  
4\. Bob Wiseman, Ochils (3rd senior male)  
  
1st M40 - Gary Fraser, Ochils  
1st M60 - Pat Fitzpatrick, Ochils  
1st M70 - Ian McManus, Irvine  

**Women**  
1\. Val Houston, Westerlands  
2\. Sarah Adam, Westerlands  
3\. Julia Connor, Tinto Hill Runners  
1st F60 - Pat McLaughlin, Westerlands  

57 runners completed the 5 race series.

Alan Smith and Gary Fraser completed all 12 races.
