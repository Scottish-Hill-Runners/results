_From race organiser Chris Colmer:_

The winner, Hector Haines, went one better than last year when he was second. Ruth Joss repeated last year's success finishing as first female. Despite the hot sunny weather, several of the athletes who also ran the Hill Race last year improved on their times this year, obviously getting to know the course.
