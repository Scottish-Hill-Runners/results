---
title: "Comrie Hills Relay, 9 September"
date: 2007-07-04
excerpt: "The organisers (Strathearn Harriers) are now inviting entries for the Relay. The number they'll accept is capped at 30."
---

Strathearn Harriers would like to invite entries for the second running of the Comrie Hills Relay.

This is a tough 4-leg relay that takes place in a stunning setting and was very well-received last year. The race will take place on Sunday 9 September and details are available [here](http://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/comriehillsrelayrace.htm).

We will limit the number of teams to 30 this year, so please contact us as soon as possible to avoid disappointment.
