Iain Gilmore was the winner, just two seconds clear of his brother Andrew and Gary Fraser who tied for 2nd place in the first of the downhill series of races at the Nebit on Wednesday 19th August.

Gary was first V40 and Steven Fallon was first V50. David Elder, in 4th place was first Junior.

In the female race, Lizzie Knox won ahead of Fiona Kelsall and Eilidh Anthony in 2nd & 3rd respectively. First female junior was Lizzie’s sister Rosie Knox.

Next round of the competition is at Bishop Hill in two weeks.
