---
title: "New course for hill race at Tomintoul HG, 21 July"
date: 2007-07-17
excerpt: "Alan Smith has devised a new course for the hill race at the Tomintoul Highland Games on Saturday 21 July."
---

Alan Smith was given the task of finding a new route for the Games' hill race. He has come up with one which is about 5km long, rough, steep in places and certainly not designed for road runners.

[Same time, same place](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0150&System=Metric&Month=7) but a different route.
