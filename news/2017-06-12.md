With short races still to come everything is still up in the air in most categories, but due to the rules of the Over 60s and Over 70s, which allow any 4 races, the 5 people in these categories who have run all 4 races so far have now earned a mug ! These are Christine Menhennet, Jocelyn Scott, Philip Kammer, David Scott and Roderick Pugh. Of these, Philip and Christine are now unbeatable in their categories, though mathematically could still have to share the first place.

The championship listings are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2017 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2017 - Female.xls) for women,  
or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.
