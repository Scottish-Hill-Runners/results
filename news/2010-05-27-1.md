Durris Mast is the first race is Cosmics' Summer Hill Series. Simon Peachey (Cosmics) won it in record time from Dan Whitehead (Deeside) with Steve Terwey (Stonehaven) third. Sally Wallis (U/A) was first lady, ahead of the Cosmics duo of Anita Hamilton and Pauline Larmour.
