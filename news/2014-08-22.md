Next Wednesday (27th August) sees the first race in the three race ‘Get a Grip’ downhill running series. The same three races as last years’ series feature in this years’ series, though first up (or down!) this year will be the Nebit Downfall. The remaining two races are the Bishop Downhill and the well-established Caerketton Doonhiller.

All the races are enter on the night for some cheap, cheerful and good fun competition. The scoring for the series will be the same as last year with the best two scores from three to count. At each race the winner gains 1000 points and everyone else’s score is dependent on the time gap behind the winner, so every second counts!

The series champions from 2013 were Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) and Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT) both of whom won a pair of Inov8 Baregrip shoes. Iain set a blistering course record at the Nebit last year so the pressure will be on from the start.

Details for each race can be found on the SHR & Scottishhillracing websites.
