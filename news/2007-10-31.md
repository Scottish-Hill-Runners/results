We are very sad to hear of the sudden death of Dick Amour of Highland Hill Runners, a top class orienteer and hill runner. Like George Murdoch before him, he had been out running, in Dick's case in Drumashie on Tuesday.

In addition to his orienteering successes, Dick's long list of achievements in hill running include being SHR Scottish Over 50 Champion in 1990 and again in 1992 and runner-up in 1991. In 2007, at the age of 68, he had finished 2nd over 60 in the Lake District Mountain Trial, after being 12th O60 at Carnethy.

He was also a long standing member of that exclusive club of "compleatists" of both the Munros and the Corbetts.

There is a BBC news item [here](http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/scotland/highlands_and_islands/7070490.stm) and an appreciation [here](http://www.scottish-orienteering.org/index/soa/page/dick-amour-invoc).
