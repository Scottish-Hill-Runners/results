_Scottish Borders Hill Race Weekend, 9-11 August 2019_

_With the established Cademuir Rollercoaster in Peebles and Philiphaugh Hill Race in Selkirk falling on the same weekend later this year on Saturday 10th and Sunday 11th August respectively, the race organisers, Moorfoot Runners and Selkirk Fund Runners, are joining forces to promote a Scottish Borders Hill Race Weekend. Adding to the attraction will be a brand new short uphill only race, The Meigle Hill Race, on the evening of Friday 9th August in Galashiels organised by Gala Harriers. So three races - short, medium and long - on three consecutive days over one weekend in the beautiful Borders hills, each with junior race options too. The organisers will be looking to provide cumulative results for those brave enough to take on the challenge of all three races._
