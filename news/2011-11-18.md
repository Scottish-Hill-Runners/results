SHR will again be publishing its much anticipated annual journal early in 2012, and invites articles for inclusion. Possible subjects include long distance challenges, book reviews, special days out on the hill, race experiences, training tips, international events, general interest.

So if you have any articles, already written or waiting to pour forth from an eager pen, please do send them in to Ronnie Gallagher (ronnie\_gallagherATyahoo.co.uk – replace AT with @) by the mid December deadline!
