title: "Gore Glentress Long Duathlon, 4th March"
date: 2007-03-05
excerpt: "In the Gore Glentress Long Duathlon, Andy Barlow and Steve Wales took an impressive victory for the team section, whilst Hugh McInnes and Jill Mykura were the first solo competitors."
---

The foul weather forecast was a bit behind schedule and missed the bike section, but hit the later runners still well up Dunslair Heights when the icy rain came on a very strong wind.  
  
Andy Barlow and Steve Wales took an impressive victory for the team section, winning with a margin of almost six minutes from outstanding juniors Connor Irving and Stewart Fraser in second place overall.  
  
First solo competitor was Hugh McInnes, only ten minutes longer than the winning pair, and letting only four pairs beat him. Veteran Andy Spenceley was second solo, only eight seconds behind Hugh, and also beaten by only four pairs. Lesley Ingram and Martin Coyle were fastest mixed pair in 8th place overall. Jill Mykura was winning senior woman in 13th overall.  
  
Bob Soutter and Ewan Gowrie were fastest men’s all-vets in 11th place overall. Lesley Kirkwood was fastest solo in the women’s vets in 57th overall. Hazel Sutherland and Helen Murray were fastest women’s all-vets in 20th overall. John Goldsworthy was fastest solo men’s V50 in 37th overall. Adam Anderson senior was fastest solo men’s V60 in 61st overall.  
  
64 teams or individuals competed in the placings.
