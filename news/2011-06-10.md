Wednesday’s thunderstorms abated to allow Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston RR) to chalk up his fourth successive win at Cort-ma Law, with three new fence crossings and particularly damp going only slowing him down marginally compared to his own course record. Second and third places went to James Tullie (Shettleston) and Niall McAlinden (Westerlands). First (and only) junior was Alastair Graves (unattached) in an impressive sixth place overall. The first lady was Julia Connor (Moorfoot), closely followed by Jenny Gillies (Lochaber), with Caroline Millins (Westerlands) in third position. Westerlands took both the men's team prize (ahead of Ochil Hill Runners) and the women's. Thanks very much to Westerlands colleagues for marshalling and time-keeping and to Lomond Mountain Rescue for safety cover.
