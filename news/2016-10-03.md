Another enjoyable day at the "Shepherds Show"....numbers on the rise (slowly) for what is a challenging wee race. First home was the evergreen Colin Donnelly a mere 16secs outside the record and showing his experience by keeping his feet dry on the run in to the finish......I can safely predict he **won't** be able to do that next year! Kate Kitchin led the ladies home setting a new ladies record of 44:05secs.

Les Turnbull (Race Organiser)
