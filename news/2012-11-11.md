Shettleston's Matthew Sullivan took first place at this years Run And Become Tinto Hill Race in 32-05 while Carnethy's Angela Mudge took the Ladies' prize with 35-47.

Fortunately the threatened rain stayed away long enough to allow the 259 official entrants to get up and down without any problems.

Unfortunately at least 2 people ran in the race unofficially. Not only does this cause errors in the results (as does leaving the finish funnel by stepping sideways over the tape), it is potentialy dangerous as it causes problems with the race safety system. This is a very serious issue and was discussed at the SHR AGM after the race.

I'd like to thank Adrian at Run And Become, all the marshals and helpers (from Carnethy and elsewhere) and the staff at Symington for helping to make this another enjoyable event.

Please note that next year the pre-entry and waiting list system will be strictly adhered to.

_Cameron Scott, Race Organiser_
