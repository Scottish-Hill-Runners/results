---
title: "Craig Dunain Race Results"
date: 2015-03-25
excerpt: "Gordon Lennox won the Craig Dunain race on Saturday 21st March"
---

Gordon Lennox (Forres Harriers) won the race in 35:25 ahead of James Tullie (Cairngorm Runners) and Donnie MacDonald (Inverness Harriers) in 2nd and 3rd respectively.
