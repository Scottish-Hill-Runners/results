---
title: "Lennox and Mudge dominate the <em>Run and Become</em> Tinto Race yet again, 13 November"
date: 2010-11-14
excerpt: "Jethro Lennox (Shettleston) and Angela Mudge (Carnethy) both win for the fifth time in the last eight years...  '"
---

Jethro Lennox (Shettleston) returned to winning form on the hills with his fifth victory at Tinto in the last eight years. He was followed by Alasdair Anthony (Ochils), who was in the top three for the fifth time in the last eight years, and Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston).

Angela Mudge (Carnethy) added to the five-in-eight theme with a comfortable win in the women's race, from Fiona Maxwell (Shettleston) and Claire Gordon (HBT).

There were twelve entrants for the Tinto Tiptoe Fun Run, the results of which are [here](Download.aspx?Item=TintoTiptoe2010.pdf). (The next Fun Run is the Cockleroy Chaser on Boxing Day.)

_From Tinto organiser Cameron Scott:_

Once again the _Run And Become_ Tinto Hill Race attracted lots of novice runners from far and wide. We even had a group of lads over from a club in Dublin who were on a stag night and celebrated with a run up Tinto! Everyone seems to have had a good day and we were lucky with the weather. I'd like to thank all the runners, the many marshals and helpers and Adrian at _Run And Become_ for helping make my first edition as organiser so easy and enjoyable. See you all next year. Cheers!

P.S.: I've got a pair of black tracksters that were left at the start. If the owner contacts me I'll arrange their return.
