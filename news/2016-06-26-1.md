In conjunction with the Falkland Festival the Hill Race was run in glorious sunshine with thunder threatening. Bob Wiseman ran like lightening to win the overall race. Nuala McCheyne stormed to the Ladies prize.

Huge age variation in this race from a few 13 year olds and representatives in the V60 V70 V80 catagories.

Thanks to our army of helpers.

Laurie Anderson RO
