At the 34km Glenshee 9 race on Sunday 7th August, Andy Fallas (Carnethy) was first; Mark Harris (Fife AC) second (and first Over 40); Konrad Rawlik (Carnethy) third and Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) fourth, so Carnethy won the team prize. First lady was Eilidh Raistick (Highland HR). Report from race organiser Ali Noble:

"The 2011 Glenshee 9 race took place 7 August. A less than promising weather forecast perhaps kept some runners away but we were pleased to have 38 starters with 33 finishers. The weather conditions were poor at the start and gradually improved although the conditions were such that times were a bit slower than in previous years.

Andy Fallas was a clear winner from Mark Harris with Konrad Rawlik close behind. Eilidh Raistrick ran well to be first lady from Alayne Finlay and Helen Bonsor. The MV40, MV50, MV60 prizes went to Stewart Whitlie, Alan Smith and Geoff Hodges respectively.

A big thanks to members of Braemar Mountain Rescue Team who provided safety cover on the hill. All proceeds from the race will go to them. Further thanks to the girls at registration, our non-rescue team marshalls and to Hazel and staff at the Glenshee ski centre. "
