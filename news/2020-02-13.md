There will be the usual SHR desk at the registration hall at Penicuik for Saturday’s race – please visit and take the opportunity to join up.

We have just taken delivery of this year’s SHR Calendar so it will be available to you. As will the new edition of the SHR Journal.

If you are not running the Carnethy 5 this year, and won’t be along, consider asking a club mate to pick up your calendar and journal for you. This will save us some money on postage.

And for those who have still to collect your 2019 SHR Championship mugs – we’ll have them for you at the desk. Again, if you’re not able to get there yourself, please get someone who will be there to pick it up for you.
