Due to the imminent arrival of Storm Amy, the show (and therefore the race) have unfortunately had to be cancelled.

**Yetholm Border Shepherds Show Race**

Saturday 4th October @ 12.45pm

Auld school traditional hill race, just turn up on the day, pay show entry and the race is free, there will also be the opportunity to make a voluntary donation at registration to the Border Search & Rescue Unit who will be helping us on the day.

Probably the Best Race in the World?
