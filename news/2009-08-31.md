There were some well attended and close fought races on excellent courses at Menstrie on Sunday. The results will be used as the main means of selecting Scottish junior teams to compete in the Junior Home International (where Scotland will be defending the City of Edinburgh Trophy - photo) and British Junior Championships, to be held in Sedburgh in October.
