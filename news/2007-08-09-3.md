---
title: "WMRT Trial Races, 11 August"
date: 2007-08-09
excerpt: "Report and results from the WMRT Trial Races at Aberfoyle."
---

The trial races took place in the woods above Aberfoyle and although the rain was unrelenting the going underfoot was firm throughout and these were good conditions for the 41 competitors.

The first race, consisting of one lap of a course expertly designed by Mark Johnston to replicate as far as possible the actual World Trophy course in Switzerland, was for the junior women. The race was won by Mhairi Inglis (Lothian) in 18.34. Catriona Buchanan (Central) was 2nd in 19.06, Jessica Martin of Kilbarchan 3rd in 19.52. Clubmate Christina Rankin was 4th, Aberdeen's Hilary Armitage 5th and Laura McCord (Whitemoss) was 6th. [Results](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0528&RaceYear=2007) .

The junior men and senior women did the same two lap circuit. First junior man was Scott McDonald of Central AC in 33.49, followed by Robbie Cammack (Moray) in 35.09, Matthew Gillespie (Central - 36.24), Ross Hastings (Nithsdale) and Michael McLoone (Greenock). [Results](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0527&RaceYear=2007) .

Irish international Edel Mooney (Lothian) had a strong run to finish 1st woman in 37.04, some distance ahead of Jill Mykura of Carnethy in 2nd with 39.57. Sue Ridley (Edinburgh AC) was next in 40.30, just ahead of Bellahouston's Helen Stuart (40.34). 5th was Alison Docherty (Central AC) and 6th Dawn Scott (Lochaber). [Results](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0530&RaceYear=2007) .

In the mens race Grant Stewart (Edinburgh) ran a very well paced race to overhaul early leader Jethro Lennox of Shettleston on the third and final lap to finish in 49.19 compared to Jethro's 50.24. Al Anthony (Ochil) finished a close 3rd in 50.30 with Fife's Graham Bee another 18 seconds behind. His clubmate Andrew Liston took 5th, with David Phee (Giffnock) in 6th. [Results](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0529&RaceYear=2007) .
