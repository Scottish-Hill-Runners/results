title: "New ladies record set for the Tranter Round"
date: 2014-08-22
excerpt: "Congratulations to Jasmin Paris of Carnethy, who set a new ladies record for the Tranter round on Saturday 26th July. She ran anti-clockwise and finished in 12 hours 41minutes, thus breaking Nicky Spink’s ladies record of 15 hours 10 minutes by a large margin......."
---

Jasmin Paris of Carnethy has set a new record for the Tranter round. She set off on 3:00am on Sat 26th July going anti-clockwise and finished in 12 hours 41minutes. The unusually early start was due to some bad weather forecast to come in later in the day, with conditions rather rapidly deteriorating for the last section from just before Aonach Beag. She ran self-supported, i.e. carried her own stuff, although she had company in the form of Jon Ascroft and Konrad Rawlik.

Konrad Rawlik adds: “As far as I know this is the second fastest time for the Tranter in which case like for the Ramsay the 1st & 2nd fastest times are the Mens and Ladies records respectively, which is a rather pleasing configuration I think.”
