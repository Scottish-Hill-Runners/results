The Arrochar Alps results has caused some interesting glitches (or will they turn into permanent results!?) in the latest listings. Noticeably Dave Scott, as well as leading the Over 50s, has jumped into the lead in the Men's Over 40s. Similarly Jacqui Higginbottom leads the Over 40s, but now is the current leader in the Senior Women's as well. Charlie Love has moved into the lead in the Over 60s, while Tom Owens, despite only having done 3 races, still leads the Senior Men (something to do with people going off course?). Virtually all the categories are wide open and most have a whole handful of people who could win.

Only one category has been decided after the 4 races and that is the Men's Team which has been won by Carnethy - congratulations. However, Fife could still catch Carnethy in the Male Vets team. Both the female team categories are still very open.
