[![Scottish Hill Runners Calendar 2009](http://farm1.static.flickr.com/254/3263312081_7057ca1e92_t.jpg)](http://www.flickr.com/photos/scottishhillrunners/3263312081/ "Scottish Hill Runners Calendar 2009") The Scottish Hill Runners Calendar for 2009 and Journal for 2008 will be available for collection by all paid-up SHR members at the Carnethy 5 Hill Race on 14th February. If you aren’t yet a member, then you can join or renew on the day. Visit our stand in the registration hall.
