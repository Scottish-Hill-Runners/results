---
title: "Symonds beats Wyatt in Austria"
date: 2007-08-01
excerpt: "Joe Symonds beats five times World Champion Jonathan Wyatt."
---

Word has just reached us of a superlative performance by Joe Symonds, building on the great form he's already shown at home and on the continent this season. Contesting Sunday's Kaunertaler Berglauf in the Austrian Tirol, Joe emerged victorious in 1.17.41, over a minute clear of five times World Champion Jonathan Wyatt, with experienced England international Martin Cox in third. The 15km race has just under 1000m of ascent.
