The third race in the 2025 SHR Championship is on Sunday 1st June at Yetholm.

Note that entries on Entry Central close at 8pm on Wednesday 28th May.

So don't miss out, get signed up for the race now - [Yetholm Hill Race - EntryCentral](https://www.entrycentral.com/event/128023)

The race is pre-entry only. There are no entries on the day. 

Kit will be checked at registration so make sure you have everything with you.
