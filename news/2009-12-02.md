_From organiser Peter Baxter:_

Some Race!

The Glentress Medium race was run in probably the worst conditions of any duathlon since we started in 2003ish. More than 130 souls braved the freezing rain and snow to compete in the first race of the series. We knew it was going to be bad when we got to the race to find the Marquee wrapped round the trees...

The race was dominated by Andy and Joe Symonds, although Dave Arnott from PCC came in with Andy on the bike and ended up fourth overall. The ladies' race saw Elke Schmidt finish in eighth place, probably the highest ladies' finish to date. Well done to everyone who took part, including those who liked the course so much they did a bit extra... No matter how much tape and marshals we cannot corral everyone.

Thanks to our marshalls and timekeepers who braved terrible conditions to ensure a safe and successful race. Well done Peebles Cycle Club, Westies and Carnethy.

A big thanks to our sponsors Helly Hansen, Run and Become and the girls in the Hub (especially Teenie for the packed lunches!).

The next race is the Short, on Sunday 24 January 2010.
