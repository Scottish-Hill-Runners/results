title: "Get A Grip Downhill Series"
date: 2016-08-25
excerpt: "Time to ‘Get a Grip’ as the summer season heads downhill…"
---

Next Wednesday (31st August) sees the first race in the fourth edition of the three race ‘Get a Grip’ downhill running series. The Lomond, Pentland and Ochil Hills once again host the races at Bishop Hill, Caerketton and the Nebit, with the series climax at the Nebit for the first time.

All the races are enter on the night for some cheap, cheerful and good fun competition. The scoring for the series will be the best two scores from three to count. At each race the fastest finisher gains 1000 points and everyone else’s score is dependent on the time gap behind them, so every second counts!

The series champions from 2015 were Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) and Fionna Kelsall (Ochils), both of whom won the series for the second time, so there’s scope for a hat trick or two in 2016.

The series prize giving will take place at the annual SHR gathering.

Details for each race can be found on the SHR website.
