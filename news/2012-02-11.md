The English dominated this year's race with a win for Shaftesbury's Orlando Edwards (running for HBT) and there were 3 other English in the top 5. Only Prasad Prasad's 2nd place stopped a clean English sweep.

Angela Mudge easily won the women's with her 13th victory at this race! Sarah McCormack took a great 2nd place and was well under the hour, in what was a very competitive race for the other placings.
