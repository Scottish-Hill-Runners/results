_From Mark Harris_

Some of you may have seen the printed SHR calendar (junior and senior) where details of the league for 2013 were given. Due to confusion over different age categories we have withdrawn the following races - Bishop Hill, Chapelgill, Norman's Law and Scolty - from the original list. The league now consists of nine excellent races - full information can be found in the attached file.

The Junior Home International will take place in Scotland this year at Aberfoyle on 21st September. So the last league race and the JHI trial will proceed on the courses for the JHI races on 1st September.

Full updated details of the league [here](http://www.shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=junior hill league 2013.pdf)

For more information please contact Mark Harris harrismark1ATmac.com
