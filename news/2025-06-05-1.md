SHR Championships end of year awards ceilidh and that all important mug! Great to see so many of you out competing at the most recent Champs race at Yetholm. Halfway through this year's championships, so still plenty to play for. We will be holding our end of year social on Saturday 4th October after Morven, the last of the Champs races. We will be giving out the Championship awards, followed by a ceilidh in the Victoria Hall in Aboyne. Camping should be available at the Aboyne Loch Caravan Park, more info to follow on that. We will also be handing out the 2025 mugs to all SHR members who manage to complete 4 of the 6 races. Following our competition earlier this year, Bill Breckenridge from Shettleston Harriers had the winning design for our 2025 mug. The SHR committee were so impressed with the quality of the designs that we chose one for 2026 and one for 2027 as well! Not an official entry, but thank you Iain Pender for this....made us laugh a lot... ![](https://scottishhillrunners.uk//Resources/mug.jpg)
