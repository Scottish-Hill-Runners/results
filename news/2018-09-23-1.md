---
title: "Cairn William Race Report and Results"
date: 2018-09-23
excerpt: "Wins for Same Alexander (Westies) and Veronique Oldham (Cosmics) at the Cairn William Race on Saturday 22nd September."
---

We had 38 runners for the Cairn William Hillrace this year on a blustery day with a few showers, but mainly fine weather.

Given the wind and the annually deepening heather the winning time of 48:04 by Sam Alexander of Westerlands is a very good time.

Second and third places overall were made up by the two Veterans David Kerr of Highland Hillrunners and Jason Williamson of Cosmics. First men’s super vet was Miles Newman of Deeside with John Colegrave of Cosmics being the first over 60 runner home.

Veronique Oldham of Cosmics won the women’s race outright in a time of 57:58 as well as being the First Ladies supervet. Sally Wallis of Deeside was 2nd followed by Fiona McDonald in 3rd. Marie Entwistle of Deeside was 4th in the ladies race and made up the 3rd place in the Veteran’s Race behind Veronique and Fiona.

Just two Juniors raced this year with the McDonald sisters competing over the 2km course, Samatha taking the spoils over her younger sister Joanna in a time of 14.36.

Although this is a small race it has a loyal following who enjoyed tea and cake in Monymusk after the race with local honey donated by Dave Wilkinson being the highlight in the prize offering.

Chris Hill
