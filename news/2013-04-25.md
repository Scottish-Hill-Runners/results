Damien Theaker reports: Ideal running conditions for the first Beinn Dubh Hill Race saw 91 runners head to Luss. David Parrish from Dumfries who arrived about 5 minutes before the start after being held up in traffic en route, had to sprint to the start and carried on sprinting to come home first 37.38. David was closely by Adam Harris of Shettleston and Gregor Stewart of Westerlands. First female finisher was Sarah Adam of Westerlands.
