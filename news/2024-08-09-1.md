Dear Hillunners,

For years I've enjoyed running but now I'm getting slow and tired. My big 70th birthday is near, on Friday 16th August and next day is Rannoch Games and Craig Varr Hill Race.

At Strathtummel, Christine and I have a fairly steep field with a few wee flatish bits. The plan is to have a birthday gathering at the filed on Friday, camp overnight and go to Rannoch next day. And back to the field after the games.

We'll have food on Friday, Saturday and Sunday (if you're lucky!).

Over the years I've crossed paths with lots of interesting people from many great clubs and made lasting friendships. All welcome but it would help if we knew you planned to come.
