The 10K starts and finishes at Birse and Feughside Church where there is access to parking, registration space and toilets. The route takes runners through trails in Finzean Woods then crosses the road and climbs up to the stunning viewpoint of Tom's Cairn summit. After a fast descent and recrossing the road, the trail race crosses fields and descends down Finzean Community Path to the finish line back at the Church. Most of the route is within Finzean Estate (with a small amount of road running included), and the organisers are grateful for their permission to hold the event.

There will be a short fun race for Primary children, and for the first time in 2017 a route for under 16s, which will take place at the same time as the 10K.

All runners will receive a small token of appreciation upon their finish, as well as a tea/coffee, home-bake, and many congratulations!
