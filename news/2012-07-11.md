date: 2012-07-11
excerpt: "The new listings after the toughest race of the championship, Arrochar Alps, are now available. Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) is hanging onto the lead in the Senior Men's but we have a new leader of the Senior Women's - Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy)."
---

In the Senior Men's, Alasdair Anthony (Ochil) is just keeping in front of the Carnethy pairing of Stewart Whitlie and Paul Faulkner, but Joe Symonds is looking ominous in 4th place. In the Senior Women's Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) takes the lead for the first time in front of Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT) and Veronique Oldham (Cosmic), but the danger people are Catriona Buchanan (Ochil) and Arrochar winner, Jasmin Paris (Carnethy) just behind.

Former Ben Nevis record holder, Ros Evans (Westerlands), is dominating the Over 60s, but is also in the lead in the Over 50s and is in 2nd place in the Over 40s, behind Veronique Oldham !

The older runners are also doing well in the male categories as in the Over 40s the first four are all over 50 with Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) leading from Alan Smith (Deeside) - expect Kenny Richmond and Mark Harris to do something about this, though. The position is reversed in the Over 50s (due to birthday timings) as Alan is hanging on to the lead and trying to hold off Stewart, who is the firm favourite. The male Over 60s is looking to be between leader Euan Patterson (Moray) and Gordon Watson (Dundee HH).

Carnethy have taken the lead in both male team championships as well as the Senior Womens team, but Cosmics are now the leaders in the Female Over 40s team.
