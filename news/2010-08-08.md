Following the success of the first navigation course held in Peebles in May and continuing interest shown by members, SHR are organising another course for hill runners (whether or not they are members of SHR) on the weekend of 2nd/3rd October based in Fort William.

The course provider will again be qualified mountain instructor, keen hill runner and mountain marathoner, Bruce Poll. The course is intended both for runners who have limited experience of hill running and for experienced hill runners who wish to brush up their skills.

The closing date for applications is 4th September 2010. Places are limited and acceptance will be based on a first come first served basis. All those who applied for the first course but for whom there were no places left will be given first priority.
