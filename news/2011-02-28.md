66 runners turned out for the inaugural Conic Hill Race, in bright spring sunshine.

The field was graced by 2 former Ben Nevis winners, in the form of Bobby Shields and Ros Evans (née Coates)

Gareth Green of Portobello lead the charge to the summit, before Peter Devenport (Bellahouston RR) took over and blasted away on the descent to take the win by 32 seconds.

Shona Robertson (Westerlands) won the ladies race with a comfortable 3 minute margin.
