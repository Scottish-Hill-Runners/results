---
title: "Simpson and Palmer win in the Kilpatricks, 6 June"
date: 2007-06-08
excerpt: "Kilpatricks Race winners were Stuart Simpson (Ochil) and Helen Palmer (Bellahouston).  '"
---

It was a balmy sunny evening for the Kilpatricks race on Wednesday, the fourth race in the 2007 Bog and Burn championship. Winners were Stuart Simpson (Ochil) and Helen Palmer (Bellahouston).
