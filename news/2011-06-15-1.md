---
title: "White Tops Race: change of race route"
date: 2011-06-15
excerpt: "Organiser Dave Hanlon has been forced to make changes to the route for the White Tops race"
---

From organiser Dave Hanlon

_After recent renovation work to the race route, access road and car park where this race normally starts and finishes, the Forestry Commission have demanded a £50 charge for the privilege of running over land which they manage._

_As organiser of this race I object to this and have reluctantly decided to re-route part of the race this year. 75% of the original race route will remain the same but after surveying alternative routes I am convinced that the new route is an improvement. With more climbing and fast descending involved, I am convinced runners will enjoy this race even more._

_The race will now start and finish in the playing field across the road from the registration hall in Kirkton of Auchterhouse village._

_I will need to classify this as a new route and therefore the old race records will no longer be valid (sorry Angela!). This presents an opportunity for new course records this year. The race will still be category BM._

_The race is 6.5 miles with 364 metres of climb_

_As usual, there will be a good feed at the end of the race and Des has promised to empty his garage of left over beer from his 50th Party for prizes._

_There will be a course reccy run on Thursday 23rd June at 6:15 pm if anyone wants to try out the new course. Meet at the playing field._

_There is ample parking in the village but please park with consideration to the local community. Please contact me on 07894 098632 if you need more info_
