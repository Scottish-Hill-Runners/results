Race report from Adam Anderson:

A record (non-championship) entry of 45 toed the line in near perfect conditions, sunny with a breeze on the tops. A cross border raid injected some fizz at the front with Jim Davies of Borrowdale running in a comfortable winner but was gracious enough to say he raced all the way because you just don't know who can come through. Julia Connor of Moorfoot had a similar margin winning the ladies race. The rain more or less held off until the prize giving where the conveyor belt of tea and home baking refreshed and refuelled. Thanks as always to Moffat Mountain Rescue Team, SHR, marshals and timekeepers and of course the Durisdeer Hill Race catering department.
