---
title: "Glenshee 9 - Sunday 7th August"
date: 2016-08-03
excerpt: "Details for entry on the day and minimum kit requirements"
---

Entries for the Glenshee9 will be taken on the day but **must be made by 10.15am.** For those who have pre-entered, registration will close at 10.40am, to allow the race to start on time at 11am.

Online entry is available until Saturday 6th Aug at [www.entrycentral.com/Glenshee-9-Race](http://www.entrycentral.com/Glenshee-9-Race)

Registration opens at 9am at Glenshee Ski Centre base cafe.

For those intending to enter on the day, please note the minimum kit requirements (including a fully waterproofed map which extends beyond the race route by at least 2km in all directions) and prior race experience requirement.
