A late addition to the calendar! £10 entry with all proceeds going to the Save The Crook appeal. The landowners have said they will see how this years race goes, with a view to hopefully being able to reinstate it again as an annual race if all goes well. So please come and support and lets show them how good the fell running community is.

See race calendar entry for full details.
