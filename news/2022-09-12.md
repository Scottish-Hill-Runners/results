The Suidhe Hill was on Saturday 10th Sept at Kincraig and the Creag Bheag race was on Sunday 11th Sept at Kingussie.

Andrew Gilmore (Highland Hill Runners) and Freya Mowbray (Lasswade AC) were the winners at Suidhe Hill. And Harris Pagett (Strathearn Harriers) and Claire Hopkins were the winners at Creag Bheag.

Full results for both races are on the SHR results tab.
