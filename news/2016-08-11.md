Here are the latest standings with four races now completed and including one race at each distance, so people who have done all four have already earned an SHR mug !

A few categories have already been decided, at least for the winners. These include both men's team, the senior women's team and in the individuals, the Senior Women's (Stephanie Provan) and Men's Over 70 categories (Gareth Bryan-Jones).

The listings are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2016 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2016 - Female.xls) for women,  
or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.
