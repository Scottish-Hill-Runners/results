---
title: "El-Brim-Ick Dash (28 March) postponed"
date: 2010-03-25
excerpt: "The El-Brim-Ick-Dash, which was due to take place on 28 March, has had to be postponed..."
---

We understand that the El-Brim-Ick Dash, which was put back from the beginning of January to 28 March, has unfortunately had to be postponed again. The new date is likely to be a Thursday night towards end of April as part of Cosmics' summer series.

Apologies from the organisers!
