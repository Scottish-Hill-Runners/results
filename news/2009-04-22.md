**scottish**athletics has selected the following athletes for the Scottish Central, East and North District Team for the Inter Counties Fell Running Championship (7 miles, 1300ft), which takes place at the Hutton Roof Crags Fell Race near Kendal on Saturday 23rd May.

**scottish**athletics would be grateful if the athletes listed below (including reserves) were to contact their district team manager to indicate their availability. A prompt response would be very much appreciated.
