**Caerketton Hill Race - Race Report & Results**

 

Aug 13, 2025

A lovely warm evening on Wednesday for the annual Caerketton Hill race, last race in Bog & Burn Series. We had an impressive turnout with 108 runners taking part in the senior race and 5 taking part in the Junior race.

Despite the warm weather, we had some super-fast times with first 6 runners dipping under 18 mins and congratulations to Moray Pryde of Lothian RC who won the race and set a new course record, 17:10, lowering course record set last year by 1 second! Emily Atkinson of Haddington also had a fantastic run winning the ladies race for 3rd year in row. Particularly impressive given she is still only 16 years of age!
