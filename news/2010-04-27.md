---
title: "Craig Stewart wins the Highland Fling, 24 April"
date: 2010-04-27
excerpt: "Craig Stewart (Forfar) wins easily whilst Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy), fifth overall, is first woman...  '"
---

Two hundred and seventy one runners completed the 53-mile Milngavie-to-Tyndrum Highland Fling. Craig Stewart (Forfar) won by nearly 25 minutes from Thomas Loehndorf (Greenock Glenpark). Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) was the first woman to finish, fifth overall and more than an hour ahead of second-placed Sharon Law (Garscube).

You can read Peter Buchanan's account of every step of the way [here](http://www.portobellorunners.co.uk/fling10.htm) and the results are [here](http://shr.uk.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0518&RaceYear=2010).
