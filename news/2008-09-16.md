Carnethy made up for the navigational mistakes of last year by setting a new record in winning the Comrie Relay. Prasad Prasad standing in for Ochil HR finished first on Leg 1, but stage records for Steve Fallon/Ronnie Gallagher on Leg 2, Stewart Whitlie on Leg 3 and Adrian Davis on Leg 4 took Carnethy well clear.

Fife were first female team and first male vets team, while Beacon won the mixed category.
