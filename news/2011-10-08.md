_Message from Trevor Shaw:_

The registration is at the Maclaren Hall in Killin for both races (15/16 Oct), not at the Ben Lawers carpark as stated on the printed calendar. Registration is from 9am to 10.15 am. A great lunch and prizegiving is in the hall afterwards. There is a Ceilidh with buffet on the Sat night tickets £15 See you all there, bring nice weather.
