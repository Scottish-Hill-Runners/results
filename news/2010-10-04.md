---
title: "UKA Fell and Hill Running Relay Championships (17 October): B teams"
date: 2010-10-04
excerpt: "Clubs can enter B teams for the Championships and have until 10 October to do so..."
---

Please note that if Clubs would like to enter B teams (second teams in any category) then they can do so. The deadline for entries is 10 October.

The event is based in Falkland and full details are on the [Fife AC website](http://www.fifeac.co.uk).
