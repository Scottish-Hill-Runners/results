NATIONAL HILL RUNNING CHAMPS CORNALEES, 15th June 2024

There were records a-plenty at Cornalees on Saturday. A record total of 60 junior hill runners from 21 different clubs took part and 5 course records were broken!

Big thanks to the host club, Greenock Glenpark Harriers, for putting on the event and congratulations to all of the champions and medal winners listed below.

Cornalees also served as Round 4 (of 8) in the 2024 Junior Hill League. At the halfway stage, there's still everything to play for but our current leaders are Angus Wright and Rachel Giffen in the U20s, Eliza Koenig of Fife and Alastair Maclean in the U17s and Anna Meek and Seb Darlow in the U15s. The league now moves on to the fifth round next Saturday in Melrose with the Eildon Three Hill Race and Junior Races ([https://www.entrycentral.com/EildonJuniorrace](https://www.entrycentral.com/EildonJuniorrace)).
