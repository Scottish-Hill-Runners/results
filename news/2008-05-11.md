date: 2008-05-11

---

This 66 mile race, either consists of a relay race or an individual race. The relay race was won in 10 hours 17 minutes by the Giffnock Hillbashers squad of five, but second across the line with an amazing run was Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy), first outright in the individual race in one minute over the 11 hours, 50 mins in front of second place individual John Kennedy (Clydesdale). The Kintyre Lads threesome were second in the relay about an hour behind Giffnock.
