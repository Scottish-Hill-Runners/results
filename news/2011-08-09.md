Alasdair Anthony has won the competition as has an unassailable lead over John Stevenson and Niall McAlinden but Kenny Richmond or Garry Rankin could jump into 2nd place if they turn up to Caerketton. Christina Rankin has dominated the women's competition. 45 runners have so far competed 5 counting races with including the super keen Julia Connor (Moorfoots) and David Duncan (Ochil), who have both competed all 10 races!
