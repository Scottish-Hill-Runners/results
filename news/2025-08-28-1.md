---

It was the first time the race had been run in a clockwise direction, this seemed to keep the runners together better and give them a 3.5-mile descent on a grassy path to the finish. 

Calum Edenborough (Unattached) was first place followed closely by Adam Park (HHRC) for second, in third place was Arran Horne (Westerlands).

The HHRC ladies took the first three places -  Katy Brown, Claire Ingram and Reese Green.

A big thank you as ever to the landowners and all the marshals that gave up their time in order that the event could go a ahead.

Kev Plummer

(Race Organiser)
