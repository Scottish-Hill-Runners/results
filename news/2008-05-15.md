---
title: "Susan Ridley regains Peebles Gypsy Glen record, 14 May"
date: 2008-05-15
excerpt: "David Moffat (Unatt) won the race whilst Sue Ridley (Edinburgh AC) regained the women's record from Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy HRC).  '"
---

Perhaps thanks to the football match in Manchester there was a good turn-out of 56 runners for Peebles' Gypsy Glen Hill Race. It was won by David Moffat (Unatt) from host Club Moorfoot Runners' Wull Hynd and Michael McGovern.

The women's race was won by Sue Ridley (Edinburgh AC), who beat course record-holder Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy HRC) and in so doing regained the course record for herself. (Lucy had warmed up for the race by by running all 66 miles of the Kintyre Way last Saturday.)
