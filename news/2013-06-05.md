After three race and one from each distance, the senior leaders are Finlay Wild (Lochaber) and Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) from Andrew Fallas (Carnethy) and Veronique Oldam (Cosmic) resp.

Veronique and Jacqueline Higginbottom are tying for the Over 40 lead while Kenny Richmond (Shettleston) is in front in the men's Over 40.

The Under 23 titles have already been decided (the final race being Yetholm) with Andrew Gilmore (Carnethy) taking the men's from his brother, Iain, and Neah Evans (Shettleston) the women's.

In the other categories, holder Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) looks unbeatable in the men's Over 50s, while clubmate Joan Wilson is a new leader in the women's Over 50. Other holders, Ewan Patterson (Moray) and Ros Evans (Westerlands), are dominating the Over 60 categories again. Finally Charlie Love (Dundee HH) is currently leading the Over 70s.

At the moment, Carnethy are leading in all four team categoies including being unbeaten in both the male ones (Shettleston second in both of these).
