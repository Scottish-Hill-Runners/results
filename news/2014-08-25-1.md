_From Gary Gutteridge_

A great evening for the race as the rain had finally stopped and we had a hint of the late sun. A good turn out of 40 people for the senior race and 5 for the junior race. The 4th and final race in the Cosmic and Deeside Summer Series Hill Races 2014. No mishaps and nobody got lost. Although no race records were broken this year there were age category improvements all round especially in the juniors where the Chapman brothers both set new records.

On the day it was a very wet short and varied course on a mixture of single track paths through forest, heather and bracken. The race route was only very slightly different from previous years to avoid fallen tress and very thick bracken. The race route was marked throughout its length and at all changes in direction. Ideal as a beginner’s introduction to cross country and hill racing. We thank Run4It Aberdeen for ongoing support. We also thank the Landowners for access permission.
