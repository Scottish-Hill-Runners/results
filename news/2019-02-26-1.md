In line with Hartfell Hill Running Club's aim of promoting hill running in Dumfries and Galloway we will be assisting in administrating and promoting the Dumfries and Galloway Area Hill Running Championship.

Historically, this has been run by Adam Anderson on his own (now a HHRC member).

This Championship will be open to everyone irrespective of which club they belong to or if the are unattached. This is separate to HHRC club championships which are for members only.

Trophies will be awarded for 1st place in the following classes, Senior, V40 and V50 in both sexes. The criteria for the 2019 will be that the best four results from the following 6 D&G Hill races will count for the championship.

Criffel Hill Race.

Screel Hill Race.

Durisdeer Hill Race.

Hartfell Horseshoe Hill Race.

The Devil's Beeftub Hill Race.

Merrick Hill Race.

HHRC wishes the best of luck to all Hill Runners taking part in the championship irrespective of which club they run for.

Best Regards

Hartfell Hill Running Club.
