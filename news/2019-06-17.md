On a windy night, Westerlands had first placed man, Sam Alexander in 49:58 ahead of James Bowden of HBT (53:43). First woman was Catriona Graves of Edinburgh University Hares and Hounds in 58:18 ahead of Lynne Allen (Kirkintilloch Olympians, 63:16).
