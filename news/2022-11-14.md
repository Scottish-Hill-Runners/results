---
title: "Kirk Craigs Race - Additional Information"
date: 2022-11-14
excerpt: "The Kirk Craigs Christmas Cracker Race is on Saturday 17th December and there is some additional information here."
---

Registration starts 9.30am at Centenary Hall, Hamilton Street, Tillicoultry.

Please park considerately. More space at Upper Mill St. near the race start.

Race start is at the top of steps on the east side of Tillicoulty Glen (the same as for the Maddy Moss Race start).

Prize-giving, tea and cakes back at the hall after the race.

All money raised will got to local charities. Entry fee is a minimum of £7 but please feel free to pay more.

Full waterproof body cover, hat, gloves, whistle, map and compass are compulsory.
