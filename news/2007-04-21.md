---
title: "Campbell spikes Donnelly's winning sequence and Keith's record at Cioch Mhor, 21 April"
date: 2007-04-21
excerpt: "Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) and Alec Keith (Inverness Harriers) were both inside the latter's course record set in 2006 but still failed to stop Graeme Campbell (Highland Hillrunners) from winning the Cioch Mhor race."
---

Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) won at Craig Dunain (7 April) and Knockfarrel (14 April) but, despite being inside the old record for Cioch Mhor, just failed to make it three wins in successive weekends. His nemesis was Graeme Campbell (Highland Hillrunners), whose time of 1:08:38 was over two minutes less than that record. Alec Keith (running for Inverness Harriers) in third was also comfortably inside his own record. Alan Smith (Deeside) was fifth and, together with Donnelly, will be in [the Scottish men's vets team](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=2739) for the Knockdhu International next weekend.

Eilidh Patterson (Highland Hillrunners) was the first woman and John McKechnie (Highland Hillrunners) won the junior race.

The full results are [here](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0052&RaceYear=2007).
