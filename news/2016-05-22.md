date: 2016-05-22

Wet and overcast conditions for the Gypsy Glen Hill Race on Wednesday 18th May. 88 runners were led home by James Waldie (Carnethy) with Gavin Bryson (Edinburgh AC) in 2nd and Alexander Chepelin (Edinburgh Uni) in 3rd.

James’ time of 31:04 is a new course record.

First lady was Jill Stephen (HBT) with 2nd and 3rd places going to Jennifer Forbes and Fiona Dalgleish, both running for Gala.

Full results now on the SHR website.
