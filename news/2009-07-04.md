Manny Gorman has completed his epic 10-week voyage through the Corbetts, sumitting Ben Loyal his final hill at 4.30pm on Friday 3rd July. The entire trip was completed on foot, on bike and under sail, starting with Clisham highpoint of Harris on 25th April.

Manny's partner Brenda Paul, who supported and made the trip possible, was desperately unlucky to miss the final hill having badly broken her shoulder the day before the grand finale.
