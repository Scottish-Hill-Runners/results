Snow Running Glenshee '22

  

A hill running event in the Scottish mountains, starting at dusk and into the winter night. On the pisted snow of Glenshee Ski station if the snow gods are smiling, but the event can be run regardless of snow cover.

Short course: 6.5km, 500m climb & Long course: 12km, 900m climb.

Trophies & 12year old Dewar's Aberfeldy whisky for the winners Spot prizes including a Decathlon paddleboard.

More info and registration via www.snowrunning.uk
