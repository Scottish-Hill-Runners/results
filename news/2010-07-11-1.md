_From race organiser Alastair Hubbard:_

On a mostly bright if breezy day, 53 runners completed the 12km multi-terrain route around the trails, tracks, bogs and pinewoods of Braemar. Unfortunately, heavy overnight rain meant that the route had to be altered slightly as water levels were just too high for the river crossings, so participants didn't get their usual "swim" in the River Dee or Clunie Water. However, hopefully normal service will resume next year!

Nearly 25 marshals from the local community were posted along the route, including the local Scouts, and their endeavours were much appreciated!

This is a great fun and friendly event - once discovered, participants return year after year. Put it in your diary.
