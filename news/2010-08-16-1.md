Six athletes have been selected to represent Scotland at this year's World Long Distance Mountain Running Challenge, which takes place this Saturday, August 21st. The Challenge takes place over what is arguably the most famous race route in the United States, Pike’s Peak in Colorado. The race is uphill only and at altitude, starting at a mere 1920metres and climbing over 2300 metres in 21km to finish on the summit at 4302metres.

The Scottish team, who are all experienced athletes who have competed in this event in previous years, is:

Men

Simon Peachey (Cosmic)

Paul Raistrick (Highland)

Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston)

Women

Claire Gordon (HBT)

Fiona Maxwell (Shettleston)

Iona Robertson (Shettleston)

Event details can be viewed [here](http://www.pikespeakmarathon.org/index.htm)
