There is a low key race on Thursday 12 May starting at 7 pm. It's the Allermuir Gallop - 2 miles with 1000 ft of climb. It’s a classic running route in the north Pentlands at Swanston.

Just turn up on the day, pay and run.

Registration in the Swanston Golf Club Car Park between 6:30 & 6:58.

Organised by HBT’s Ian Campbell.

Donations to a school in Cambodia as entry fee . An ideal way to recover from Dumyat !!
