**Lomonds Junior Hill Race 2024**

**Race**: 28th April 2024

**Organiser**: Frank McLaren ([frankmclaren26.2@btinternet.com](mailto:frankmclaren26.2@btinternet.com)) Louise Provan (louiseprovan@hotmail.com)

**Registration**: on the day, no fee

On Sunday 28th April, Fife AC is hosting the 2024 Lomonds Junior Hill Race as part of Scottish Athletics Junior Hill Running League (the full list of races in the league can be found here: [https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Juniors2.aspx](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Juniors2.aspx)

The races are as follows:

*   U17 (born 2008-2009) (c.5.8k, 200m ascent) 12.30pm start: See [course map](https://www.fifeac.org/files/races/maps/lomonds_junior_hill_races/purin_hill_map_2022.jpg) ABCDEFGBCDEFGBA
*   U15 (born 2010-2011) (c.3k, 100m ascent) 12.30pm start: See [course map](https://www.fifeac.org/files/races/maps/lomonds_junior_hill_races/purin_hill_map_2022.jpg) ABCDEFGBA
*   U13s (born 2012-2013) (c.3k, 100m ascent) 12.30pm start: See [course map](https://www.fifeac.org/files/races/maps/lomonds_junior_hill_races/purin_hill_map_2022.jpg) ABCDEFGBA

Fun run. There will also be a race (c.2.25k, 100m ascent) for runners born in 2014 or after, with those born in 2015 or later to be accompanied by an adult). 12.00pm start. See [course map](https://www.fifeac.org/files/races/maps/lomonds_junior_hill_races/purin_hill_map_2022.jpg) ABCDEFDCBA

Registration opens at 11.00am, at the Purin Hill car park (as in previous years), near East Lomond, (the car-park is one mile up the hill midway off the Falkland road A912). No entry fee, and a snack and drink will be provided for all runners. We have been lucky with the weather on race day before, but just in case, be sure to bring plenty of warm and waterproof clothing, as there is nowhere to hide. All races start and finish near the car park, and ascend East Lomond, with routes varying according to age. The courses are suitable for hill shoes or trainers, but not for spikes. All routes will be marked and marshalled.

All dogs must be kept on leads at all times.
