**Simon Wake Comrie Hill Relay now open for entries**

It's time to start sorting out your teams for this year's Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay. This year, it's on Saturday 30th August and will once again offer the same highly scenic route over four legs of varying terrain and difficulty to suit most people plus our usual post-race food and drinks for all competitors. 

Entries are now open at: [https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=15775](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=15775)"
