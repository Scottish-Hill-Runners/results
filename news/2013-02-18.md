The Scottish watershed is the line of high and mountainous ground that defines whether rainfall runs into the Atlantic or the North Sea. It weaves its way through the full length of Scotland and includes some of the country’s most remote and trackless areas. In 30 days in May and June 2012, ultra runner and climber Colin Meek, covered the whole 650 mile route in 30 days. On Tuesday 26th February Colin is coming to Run and Become's Edinburgh shop in Queensferry Street, to talk about his run, what inspired him to do it, how he prepared for it and also how much fun the little adventure was. He said he was inspired to tackle the Watershed because it is a linear route easily defined and includes some of Scotland’s most challenging scenery.

Full details and to sign up for the evening are [here.](http://run.runandbecome.com/event/edinburgh-watershed-run-inspiration-evening/)
