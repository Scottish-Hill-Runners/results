---
title: "Falkland Hill Race - Results & Report"
date: 2017-06-25
excerpt: "Wins for Ben Hukins (Cambuslang) and Anna Hedley (Fife AC) at the Falkland Hill Race on Saturday 24th June"
---

Despite a strong wind blowing against the runners on the ascent both the winning times for the men and women were the fastest for a numbers of years. Those who have done the race many times think that the new path makes for a harder descent now. Given these conditions for Ben Hukins to be only 40 seconds behind the record set by Dermot McGonigle in 1986 was particularly impressive.

What was also great was that the first 3 in the Ladies were all Juniors, with Anna Hedley an under 15 Junior winning the race in a very fast time. The race had 82 competitors, the entrants being boosted by both Falkland Trail Runners and Kinross Road Runners having it as a counter in their respective club championship.

Gordon Pryde
