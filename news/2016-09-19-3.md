The 5th SHR 2016 Championship race, and Scottish counter in the British Championship was won by Rhys Findlay- Robinson (Dark Peak) ahead of Sam Tosh (Rossendale) and Carl Bell (Kesswick) in 2nd and 3rd. In the ladies race, Louise Roberts (Ambleside) was the winner with Nichola Jackson (Preston Harriers) and Sharon Taylor (Helm Hill) in 2nd and 3rd.
