Aonach Mor UpHill Race 2017

Well done to all that took part in today’s Aonach Mor Uphill Race 2017.

123 took part in what turned out to be ideal conditions for a hill race or just to clear the head from new year celebrations.

In the men’s race it was Lochaber’s Ewan Thorburn, that took 1st position in a time of 22:37, 2nd place was Ochil Hill Runners’ Alisdair Anthony in 23:20 and 3rd place went to Alistair Hodges (Cambridge & Coleridge AC) in 23:49.

In the womens’ race, 1st place went to Sula Gay (Hunter Bog Trotters) in a time of 27:24, in 2nd Place was Morag MacClelland (Hunter Bog Trotters) in 29:30 and 3rd place was Elizabeth Adams (Westies) in 29:46.

Well done to all that took part and massive thanks to organisers, marshalls and timekeepers, Corrina, Cammy, Ian, Elizabeth, Rhona, Aileen, Elaine any others I may have missed. Also Nevis Range and sponsors, Run4it, Cotswold’s, and Vicki Anderson.

Many Thanks To You All And A Happy New Year

Jim Hamilton, Race Organiser
