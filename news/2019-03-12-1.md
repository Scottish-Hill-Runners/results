Craig Dunain Hill Race - Saturday 16th March.

There has been a lot of building work going on in the area over the last few years so there is a slight change to the race route again. Runners please note that the route will now involve a road crossing that is busier than where we were able to cross before. The crossing will be marshalled but runners will not have right of way on the road and should take care when crossing. Route map available below.

Other details if needed: Race route approx 9km with 270m ascent. Entry on the day only (£5), registration at the Inverness Harriers hut, Queen's Park Stadium, Inverness, 12-30-1.30pm. Start 2pm on the canal banks. Minimum age of runners 16 years.

Many thanks,

Caroline Marwick (race organiser)
