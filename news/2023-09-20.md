Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) and Cat Morrison (DDJC) were the winners at the Suidhe Hill Race on Saturday 16th September.

And on Sunday at Creag Bheag, Angus Wright (Team East Lothian) and Isla Paterson (Gala Harriers) were the winners.

Iain broke the course record at Suidhe and Angus and Isla broke the male and female records at Creag Bheag.
