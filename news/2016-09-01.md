Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) and Lizzie Knox (Anster Haddies) won at the Bishop Hill Downhill Race on Wednesday evening.

They take an early lead in the three race “Scottish ‘Get A Grip’ Downhill Series 2016”

Next race is at Caerketton next Wednesday (7th Sept) and the final one in the series the following week at the Nebit on Wednesday 14 th.

Full results for the Bishop Hill Race are on the SHR website on the results tab.
