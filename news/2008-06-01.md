---
title: "Knock Hill (Crieff) title goes south, 28 May"
date: 2008-06-01
excerpt: "Paul Dugdale (Kendal AC) won from orienteer Ross McLennan; Judith Taylor (Carnegie H) was the first woman.  '"
---

Veteran Paul Dugdale (Kendal AC) won the Knock Hill Race at Crieff by 15 seconds from Ross McLennan (Forth Valley Orienteers) with local Club Strathearn Harriers' Will Manners in third. The first two in the women's race were also veterans, Judith Taylor (Carnegie H) having a couple of minutes in hand over Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy HRC).
