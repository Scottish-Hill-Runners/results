A record turn out of 79 starters for the Cheyne Wood Hill Race, the last in the Deeside and Cosmic Hillbashers 2016 summer series. A dry overcast evening with a slight breeze were perfect conditions for the 4 mile and 833ft climb (6.4km and 254m) race over single track paths in forest and over grassy moorland.

The course is very slightly faster and shorter this year but with more height gain the results are not directly comparable with previous years.

Unfortunately a couple of the leading runners went astray, possibly due to ‘head down’ and giving it their all and missed a route turning point. My apologies we will mark the course better next year.

Senior James Espie of Deeside runners won in 24:46 with a comfortable lead over 14 year old Findlay Rhind in 25:32 who also had a comfortable lead over Rowan Boswood in 26:43. Thereafter the runners came in quick succession.

Super Vet Veronique Oldham of Comics was first woman in 31:45 closely followed by Jennifer Rickets of Deeside runners in 31:55 followed by 14 year old Kirsty Oldham in 32:26.

Many thanks to all the race helpers

Gary Gutteridge

Race Organiser
