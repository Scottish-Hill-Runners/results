Ochils 2000s 24th August – pre-entries close today!

Pre-entries for the Ochil 2000s race on 24th August at the lower fee of £13 are being accepted by the organiser Alex King up until today. Entries on the day may be available at £16, but a place on the coach cannot be guaranteed! To guarantee your entry and make the organiser’s job easier please enter now!!

Could anyone who has their own orienteering dibber please bring it to the event.
