Bob Wiseman continues to lead the series, despite missing the last 3 races. Kenny Richmond moves into 2nd place, and Steven Fallon improves his 3rd-placed score.

Meanwhile Alasdair Anthony completed his 3rd race at Maddy Moss, and is now favourite to win the series provided he runs at Turnhouse or Caerketton.
