Details of the six races in the 2009 Scottish Hill Runners senior championship are now on the [Championships](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-001) page.

The races are: Clachnaben (M), Screel (S), Stuc a'Chroin (L) (also a British Championship race), Arrochar (L), Meall an t-Suidhe (S) and Meall a\` Bhuachaille (M).

SHR thanks the individual race organisers for providing their races as Championship counters, and Deeside Brewery for their sponsorship of the Clachnaben race.

Note on the Championship page that there has been a rule change in that Over 60s can count ANY 4 from the 6 races.

Details of the 11 races in the 2009 Long Classics Series are available [here](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006).
