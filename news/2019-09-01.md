Thirteen juniors completed the league in 2019, seven women and six men covering all age groups. Please contact Mark Harris if you have any questions. Prizes will be distributed in due course.

Many thanks to all juniors for participating in the league and to all parents and guardians for their support of junior hill running in Scotland. We look forward to seeing you next year.
