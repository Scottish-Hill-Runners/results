The Ben Lomond race on Sat 12th May is open for entrants on SiEntries - [https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=4445](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=4445)

As well as the open race, Ben Lomond will be the Scottish Athletics West Districts Hill Running Championships for 2018. Any runner who has entered and is a member of a West of Scotland club and is also a member of Scottish Athletics will be eligible for a Championship medal. There will be medals for the following categories (male and female): under 23; senior; vet 40; vet 50 and vet 60, as well as male and female team medals. Any questions about the Championships please contact [kenny.richmond@ntlworld.com](mailto:kenny.richmond@ntlworld.com)
