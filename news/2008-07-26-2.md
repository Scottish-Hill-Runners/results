---
title: "Bog and Burn update after Turnhouse"
date: 2008-07-26
excerpt: "Bob Wiseman continues to lead the Bog and Burn Series after 9 races, and a 2nd place at Turnhouse.  '"
---

Bob Wiseman continues to lead the Bog and Burn Series after 9 races. The only person who could now catch Bob is Alasdair Anthony should he chose to run his 4th race of the series.

Bellahouston pair Kenny Richmond and Dougal Ross are 2nd and 3rd on 17 points, with Kenny having the better head to head results.

Steven Fallon is first M40 and 4th overall.

Don Reid is currently first M50 but could be caught by Martin Dean if Martin choses to run at Caerketton.

The final race of the series is Caerketton 13th August.
