Alec Keith of HBT has taken an early lead in the [Scottish Long Classics Series](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006) following the first 2 races in the series, Stuc a'Chroin and Jura. In 2nd are 3rd places are David Riach and Richie Cunningham, with first V50 David Scott of Ochils in 4th place overall. Joan Wilson of Carnethy is currently leading the women's competition.

The next race in the series is the inaugural [Slioch Horseshoe Race](http://scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0214) on May 31st.
