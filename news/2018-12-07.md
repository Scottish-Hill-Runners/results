SHR navigation course - Pentlands – 9th/10th March 2019

Following the success of previous courses, and in an effort to help more runners run safely in the hills, SHR in conjunction with [Steven Fallon](http://www.stevenfallon.co.uk/index.html) are organising another course for hill runners on the weekend of 9th/10th March 2019 in the Pentlands. Cost £50 to SHR members.

[More details](http://www.stevenfallon.co.uk/navigation-shr.html)
