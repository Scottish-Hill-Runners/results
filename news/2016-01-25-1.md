In this talk, Adrian Davis provides a fascinating insight into his part in the charity fundraising 2013 Everest Marathon, listed in the Guinness Book of Records as the highest marathon in the world – it’s the world’s most spectacular race, often with snow and ice to contend with, held every 2 years, starting at Gorak Shep, 5184m, close to Everest Base Camp in Nepal and finishing at the Sherpa town of Namche Bazaar at 3446m, 26 miles over rough mountain trails.
