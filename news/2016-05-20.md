## Goatfell Race - Final information for runners

A reminder: by entering this race you confirm you have read the [Mandatory safety rules](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Safety.aspx) and agree to abide by them.

Given the [Mountain weather forecast](http://www.mwis.org.uk/scottish-forecast.asp?fa=WH&d=2016-05-21) for Saturday, all runners will be required to carry fully body cover. Any runner not carrying the required kit will be disqualified. Minimum requirement of hat, gloves, whistle and full waterproof whole body cover, which must have taped seams and an attached hood.

Race numbers will be distributed on the 9.45am ferry sailing from Ardrossan to Brodick. Please make the effort to go to the observatory room on the ferry to collect your number. There will be representatives from Shettleston Harriers on the ferry so please seek them out if you need directions or have any race enquiries. Those already on the island can collect their numbers from the Ormidale Pavilion.

See you all on Arran on Saturday.

**Shettleston Harriers**
