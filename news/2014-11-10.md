title: "Nebit Moonrace Announced"
date: 2014-11-10
excerpt: "This year's Nebit Moonrace will be on Wednesday 10th December at 19.00"
---

The Nebit Moontain Race 2014 will be held on Wednesday 10th December at 7pm.

Registration on the day at No.5 pub in Alva from 6pm. As in previous years, soup and mince pies will be available to runners in the pub afterwards. Entry fee £6.

Your kit will be checked when you register

The nebit moonrace is a category AS hill race run at night in mid-winter, organised by [Ochil Hill Runners](http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/). The following rules apply:

*   Over 18s only
*   All runners must wear or carry: a headtorch; waterproof coat and trousers; hat and gloves; whistle (or mobile phone).
*   No kit = no entry. REMEMBER YOUR HEADTORCH!

The route will be marked reasonably well by hill running standards; but remember it will be dark so you will need your wits about you. You must follow the marked route at all times.

Entry is on the night only at ‘[No. 5' Inn at Alva](http://www.no5alva.co.uk/). Entries open 6pm; last entry 6.45pm.

Entry fee is £6 and includes soup and mince pie after the race.
