Donnie Campbell has completed his continuous, self-propelled Munro round in 31 days, 23 hours, 2 minutes, taking more than seven days off Stephen Pyke's previous best.

Supported by his wife Rachael and others and starting on Mull at 6:00am on 1st August, he mopped up everything from Slioch northwards in an astonishing 47-hour final push to finish atop Ben Hope at 5:02am on Wednesday 2nd September.
