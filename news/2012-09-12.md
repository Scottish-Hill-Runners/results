_From Andy Birnie_

Entries have now opened for the Ben Venue Hill Race on Sat 6th October.

We’ll be taking a diversion through the forest this year to avoid some tree felling works, so the route will be a wee bit tougher than last year.

And in line with other forestry commission car parks, there is now a charge of £3 per car, so please car share wherever possible.

[More information here](http://www.bellahoustonroadrunners.co.uk/events/benvenuechallenge/) | [On-line pre-entries here](http://www.entrycentral.com/bella_ben_venue_challenge)
