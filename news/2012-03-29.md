The first Run4it SHR championship race takes place at Clachnaben on Easter Saturday, the 7th of April. A new, more runnable, route has been put together by Cosmics, so not only do runners get the chance of some championship points but the winners also have the chance to set a new course record!

Full race details of the revised route and entry details are available from the Cosmics site, which can be reached through the link to the race entry on the sidebar to the right or can be downloaded [here](http://www.shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=Clachnaben_new_2012.pdf)

Please note the start times and that parking is a short jog through the woods from the registration (both of these locations remain unchanged)

As this is the first championship race since we introduced a members-only-mug policy this would also be good time to remind runners that SHR membership fees are unchanged from previous years at the bargain price of £8. Please follow the links to sign up if you haven't already done so.
