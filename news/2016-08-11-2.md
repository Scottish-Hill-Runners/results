James Espie (Deeside) and Georgia Tindley (HBT) were the winners at the Caerketton Hill Race on Wednesday.

2nd place for both Joe Symonds (Shettleston) and Jill Stephen (HBT) was enough for them to seal the respective male and female 2016 Bog & Burn Series wins.
