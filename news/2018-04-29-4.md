title: "Normans Law Race Results"
date: 2018-04-29
excerpt: "Wins for Fife AC's Logan Rees and Heather Anderson"
---

A record field of 185 runners completed Fife AC’s Norman’s Law hill race on Saturday, with local and not so local clubs enjoying the sunshine at Luthrie. Underfoot conditions varied from muddy to bone dry, as the farmland recovers from the hard winter. Logan Rees (Fife AC) won the mens race and retained the Alan Milligan Memorial Quaich; there was a tight race behind him, with Jacob Dobson followed by Tom Harris (both unattached) and Gregor Malcolm (EHH/Fife AC). It was 1, 2, 3 for Fife AC in the womens race with Heather Anderson winning the Normans Law Shield, followed home by Halina Rees and Rhona Van Rensburg in second and third.
