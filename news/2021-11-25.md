SHR 2022 Membership Subscriptions

Subs for 2022 are now being accepted. At the recent online AGM it was agreed that the level of subs would be maintained at the previous rate of £10 for seniors, £5 for each subsequent senior at the same address and £5 for Juniors/Students.

As an independent organisation it is our subscription income that we receive which allows us to continue to provide services for hill runners in Scotland. Membership benefits include annual championships, race insurance, an annual journal and printed calendar.  

Online and postal subscription forms are available on the SHR Website.
