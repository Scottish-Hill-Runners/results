_From organiser Andy Kitchin:_

The breeze dropped at the off and runners enjoyed fine conditions for this alternate route up the famed Hill. The heather on the main climb had been largely burned away, though no records were broken.

Al Anthony won, a minute outside his record for this course. Sarah O’Neil was first Lady. (In fact the first runner of all to finish was M9 Peter Fawthrop, followed by M8 Jack Stephenson and then Al Anthony.)

A smaller field than last year. Surely hill runners aren’t that interested in football ?!
