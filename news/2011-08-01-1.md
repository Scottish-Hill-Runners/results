On a very hot and sticky day at Dufftown, conditions that slowed everyone else down, 19 year old Robbie Simpson (Deeside) had an astounding run to win the Ben Rinnes race by nearly 10 mins from a strong field and take 11 secs from the course record with a time of 1:54:02. Jethro Lennox finished 2nd not far in front of the first Over 40, Stewart Whitlie, who was 3rd. Highland Hill Runners had the first two women home with Eilidh Raistick first in 2:31:14 and Gill Irvine 2nd in 2:41:34.

With 4 in the top 10 Carnethy were easily first team. Since the race was an SHR championship counter there was a record field of 152 starters (144 finishers), the first time there has been more than 100 runners in the race.
