Entries are now open for the Dumyat Junior Hill Races. The event features five races, with each age category having its own race. This is open to participants of all ages, from under 17 and younger.  
  
[Click here for full race details and online entry.](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Races.aspx?RaceID=RA-0777)  
  
Under 20s races at start of season are Birnam and Screel.
