Joint record holder Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston) won the Cort-ma Law race for the 3rd year a row. Kilbarchan junior Garry Rankin was 2nd, and Dave Eiser of Ochil finished 3rd, just ahead of Westies junior Adam Gatens in 4th place.

Emma O'Shea of Deeside won the ladies race by reeling in HBT's Claire Gordon on the final descent. Christina Rankin of Kilbarchan finished in 3rd position.

David Rogers, the race organiser, adds:

"Ochil (Dave Eiser, John Stevenson and Stuart Simpson) took the men’s team prize ahead of Westerlands. There were no women’s teams so that prize was drunk by Lomond Mountain Rescue who did a great job providing safety cover for the race. The junior women’s prize was eaten by the marshalling and time-keeping team from Westerlands.

Whoever left a blue striped polo shirt at the finish can contact the organiser if they want it back."
