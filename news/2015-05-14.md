Could everyone hoping to run in the Kilpatricks Hill Race 2015 please note the following messages:

_Kit Checks_  
Kit checks will be carried out prior to the race. No kit = No race. Please come prepared with any kit that may be required on the evening; e.g. waterproof body cover, hat & gloves, map, compass and whistle.

_Parking_  
Parking is no longer permitted on the road at the start of the race route. This severely restricts the available parking. Could all those entering the race give thought to organising car shares or using public transport (the Old Kilpatrick train station is two minutes away from registration) to avoid congestion.

Looking forward to another cracking race.

James Callender
