Merrick Hill Race on Sunday June 8th will have a new (longer) route starting at [Glen Trool Visitor Centre](http://www.forestry.gov.uk/website/Recreation.nsf/LUWebDocsByKey/ScotlandDumfriesandGallowayGallowayForestParkGlenTroolGlentroolVisitorCentre), rather than at Bruce's Stone car park.

The new route is longer at just over 13 miles (22km), and is out and back the same way, with about half the course on forest tracks.
