Meall an-t Suidhe race and the Half Ben race were on Saturday and Sunday respectively.

Finlay Wild and Suzy Devey (both Lochaber) won at Meall an-t Suidhe and Finlay won again at the Half Ben with Lizzie Ingham (Halden Ski Kllubb) winning the ladies race.

The third race of the series at Cow Hill is on Monday @ 7pm
