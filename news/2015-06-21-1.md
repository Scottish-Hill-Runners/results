55 runners took part in the Ben Sheann Hill race which resulted in a very tight finish with only 30 seconds separating the first 3 runners.

Dan Whitehead from Deeside won in a time of 28:22 followed closely by Owen O’Neill (28:48) and Alan Smith (28:53). Dan was also 1st Senior and 1st MV40 and Alan was also first MV50.

The first female home was Jill Stephen from Hunters Bog Trotters in 36:11, 2nd was Vicky Coy (u/a) (40:12). Mairi McIntosh (Ochil) was third with (36:53) Chris Ryan (u/a) picked up the local prize (32:51) and Dan Hesp was the first male junior with a time of 33:32.

A well attended prize giving took place in The Inn at Strathyre where 23 prizes were awarded between the runners.

There was good feedback from runners despite a very wet course; there were no injuries, cuts or bruises, and marshals were placed at key points to guard against especially boggy areas and this was duly noted by the runners.

Many thanks to all our fantastic team who made the whole event run without a hitch. On the hill we had Frank, Roel, Greg, Aidrian, Iain, Sue, Roseanne, Wullie, Jim and Davey, and down at s/f Lorna, Keanna, Carol, Jim, Catriona and Graeme.

Thanks to Eoin and Shiela for supplying the power and thanks to the Inn, Steve & staff for hosting us.
