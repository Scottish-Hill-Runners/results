There was a 3rd place for Alasdair Anthony in the Northern Irish Knockdhu International on Saturday, closely followed by Grant Stewart and Colin Donnelly, who was also first vet. Ian Wellock (6th senior) completed the gold medal winning Scottish team. The race was won by guest runner Lloyd Taggart, a Manxman by birth but now an established England international. Jamie Thin was 2nd vet and Alan Smith 3rd.

In the Ladies competition a mere 6 seconds deprived Jill Mykura of victory, the race being won by “Gallic Scot” Mari Todd, clearly returning to good form. Jessica Martin was 2nd in the Junior Women's race and Alex Jones was 3rd in the Junior Men. Jamie Stevenson won the U17 race by a clear margin, with younger brother Ben in 3rd.
