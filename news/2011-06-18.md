Scotland, in the shape of Tom Owens (2nd), Robbie Simpson (3rd) and Jethro Lennox (4th) have been crowned World Long Distance champions in Slovenia. The womens team were also on the podium, with an excellent 3rd place, thanks to Fiona Maxwell (10th), Andrea Priestley (11th) and Claire Gordon (17th). Andy Fallas finished in 14th and Simon Peachey was 35th.
