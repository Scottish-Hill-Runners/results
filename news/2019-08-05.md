**SHR Annual “do”and prize giving, 26th October – a date for your diary!**

This year we are returning to the Badaguish Outdoor Centre near Aviemore for the annual SHR “do” and prize giving, to be held in the evening after the Meall a’ Bhuachaille race on 26th October, the last race in the SHR Championship. There will be a buffet meal and hopefully a ceilidh band! Further details to follow in due course.

Accommodation is available on site, including lodges, wigwams and camping - we suggest early booking (www.badaguish.org).
