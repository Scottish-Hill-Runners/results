Leanachan Race Report 25th January 2015

A damp blustery day greeted the hardy runners for today's race around the forest trails of Nevis Range. A fast pace ensued from the start with Finlay Wild and Mike Bossard out in front closely followed by Steven Burns.

On the long drag along the Puggy line, Finlay gradually pulled out a few seconds which he built upon eventually coming in first, just over a minute ahead.

Finlay's time was fast but not quite fast enough to beat the record from 2010 (23 seconds outside Davy Rogers).

In the women's race Diane Baum continued her fine form this season and managed to stay ahead of Amanda Blackhall who gave her a good contest in second. Diane's time was a minute slower than the record held by Nicola Meekin in 2011.

Thanks go to time keepers Jim and Andrew and all the marshals out on the course without whom we couldn’t run the race

James Hamilton, Lochaber
