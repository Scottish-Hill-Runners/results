---
title: "Another win for Carroll: Knock Hill, 26 May"
date: 2010-05-27
excerpt: "Mike Carroll (Perth RR) follows up his success at Kinnoull by winning Crieff's Knock Hill Race...  '"
---

Mike Carroll (Perth RR) won the Knock Hill Race at Crieff from a field of 72 in a time of 30:58. Max Aitchison (City of Glasgow) was second - and first junior - in 31:22 with James Tullie (U/A) third in 31:29. Peter Simpson (Carnegie) was the first vet in 33:41, just ahead of Iain Anderson (U/A) in 33:46 and Stuart Simpson (Ochils) in 33:52.

The first three ladies were all vets: Judith Turner (Carnegie) in 36:25, Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy) in 36:46 and Claire Douglas (Perth RR) in 36:51.
