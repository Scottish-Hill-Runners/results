---
title: "Scots pack well on Snowdon, 26 July"
date: 2008-07-28
excerpt: "Murray Strain (seventh overall) was the first Scot to finish in the Snowdon International.  '"
---

Murray Strain (HBT) led the Scottish contingent home in the Snowdon International Race, finishing seventh. The other three members of the men's team were closely-bunched: David Rodgers (Lochaber AC) in 16th, Prasad Prasad (Clydesdale H) in 17th and Douglas Tullie (Edinburgh University H & H) in 20th. This put the Men's team an excellent 3rd, behind England and Spain.

Andrea Priestley (Ochil HR) was the first Scottish woman, in a fine fourth place; Jill Mykura (Carnethy HRC) and Dawn Scott (Lochaber AC) were not far behind her, in fifth and seventh, giving the team another excellent 2nd place behind England.
