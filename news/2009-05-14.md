title: "Peebles Gypsy Glen Race, 13 May"
date: 2009-05-14
excerpt: "Alasdair Anthony and Sue Ridley win over the revised course.  '"
---

Alasdair Anthony (Ochil) won the Peebles Gypsy Glen Race on a course with a revised finish in a time that was nearly a minute inside Euan Jardine's old record. Sue Ridley (Edinburgh) retained the women's title, taking over a minute more than when she set the record last year.
