title: "Scots high up in the Alps"
date: 2007-07-02
excerpt: "Success stories from Italy and France."
---

The [Carnegie Harriers' web site](http://www.carnegie-harriers.co.uk/) reports on Andy Symonds' win in Sunday's Sentiero 4 Luglio Sky Marathon (held in northern Italy), with Jethro Lennox (Shettleston) tenth.

Meanwhile Andrea Priestley (Ochils) was second woman in the [Mont-Blanc Marathon](http://www.chamonet.com/whats_new_article.php?id_whats_new=2361&id_back=1), whilst Angela Mudge (Carnethy) set a new record in winning the shorter Cross event.
