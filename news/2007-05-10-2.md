The European Mountain Running Championships are in France on 8 July. The selection races for the GB teams for these Championships are being held in Snowdonia on 17 June. Information about the races and an entry form for them are in a pdf file [here](Download.aspx?Item=GBTrials2007.pdf).

Please note that the course for the men's race was amended on 15 May to that now shown in the pdf file.
