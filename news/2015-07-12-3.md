---
title: "Glamaig Results"
date: 2015-07-12
excerpt: "4th win for Finlay Wild at Glamaig on Saturday 4th July"
---

Finlay Wild (Lochaber) won Glamaig for the 4th year in a row ahead of Brian Marshall (HELP) and David Wilby (Highland Hill Runners).

The female race was won by Helen Gertig (Inverness Harriers) ahead of Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber) and Lina Mardall (Deeside)
