---
title: "Mudge and Symonds shine in the Alps, 8 June"
date: 2008-06-09
excerpt: "Angela Mudge was first woman and Andy Symonds seventh man in the second Buff Skyrunner World Series race."
---

The second of this year's [Buff Skyrunner World Series](http://www.buffskyrunner.com/introduction/) races was from Valmalenco in Italy to Valposchiavo in Switzerland.

Angela Mudge was a clear winner of the women's race, just 22 seconds outside her own course record. Andy Symonds was seventh (out of more than 400 finishers) in the men's event, one place and under 20 seconds behind Ricky Lightfoot.

There's more information [here](http://www.fsa-sky.org/new/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=78&Itemid=59).
