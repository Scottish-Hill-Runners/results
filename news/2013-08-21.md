The race was won by Dan Gay HBT with a new record time, but his speedy finish served him well as he pipped Brian Marshall by one second. Third place went to Fergus Johnston (Gala)  
First Lady home was Charlotte Morgan Carnethy, second Sula Gay HBT and third Sharon Bird U/A.

First V40's Brian Marshall and Jane Jackson, V50's Alan Smith and Suzanne Bird.  
101 finishers. On a windy dry day the classic 'bum slide' descent was unable to claim any victims much to the organiser's relief.  
  
Laurie
