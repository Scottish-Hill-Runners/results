SHR’s membership year started on 1 January, so now's a good time to renew your membership for 2011 or join for the first time. The fees are the same as last year’s, namely £8 for Seniors, £2 for Juniors / Students / Unwaged; plus £4 each for second and subsequent senior members living at the same address as the first senior member and opting to receive a single copy of SHR paper publications.

The information on how to pay your subscription is [here](HowToJoin.aspx). Note that you have the choice of renewing online or by post.

If you’re unsure about whether to opt for paper or electronic publications, please consider opting for the latter because they cost SHR considerably less time and money to distribute. In either event you will of course receive paper versions of the Calendar for 2011 and Journal for 2010, which we expect to have ready as usual by the time of the Carnethy 5 race in February.
