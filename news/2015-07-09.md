Maddy Moss, Wed 15th July: There is plenty of parking available on and around Upper Mill St. These are busy public roads. I ask competitors to respect local residents.  
Do not block public pathways, road entrances or private driveways.

Runners will carry with them adequate full body cover, map, compass and whistle

Runners should be familiar with [SHR safety guidelines](http://www.shr.uk.com/Safety.aspx).

Participants must be 18 or over.

Entry Fee: £4

Event Timetable:  
18:15 - registration opens  
19:25 - pre-race briefing and headcount  
19:30 - race start.

Further race details can be found at [www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk](http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/)  
It is recommended you check our website 24hr prior to race for any final updates.

Race Organiser
