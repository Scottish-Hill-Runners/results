_Yujiro has e-mailed us as follows:_

"This is Yujiro Iida, Japanese who ran for Edinburgh Uni Hare and Hounds last year. I graduated from the Uni last December and I now work inTokyo.

It was a a week ago that I received an OFFICIAL LETTER from Japan athletics which said that I was selected for running at IAAF World Mountain Running Championship (uphill only race!) in Slovenia on 5th Sepetember! I'm now become the first Japanese to run at the World Mountain Running Championships!This is what I has dreamed for long.....and I'm VERY VERY Happy!!!

I took up hill running in Scotland and my success in mountain running is due to scottish hill running and all of you guys. I'm really proud of running for Japan in September, and I want to thank all of you in Scottish Hill Runners from the bottom of my heart."
