The scottishathletics Hill Running Commission is looking for one or more Scottish clubs to organise the UK Athletics British Fell and Hill Running Relay Championships in 2010. This major fixture in the UK calendar was last held in Scotland in 2005, when it was at Alva. There’s more information [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=3865)
