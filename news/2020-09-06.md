title: "Meall nan Tarmachan race"
date: 2020-09-06
excerpt: "Unfortunately there will be no race in 2020"
---

Unfortunately the decision has been made not to go ahead with the Meall nan Tarmachan hill race this year, mainly because of the extra restrictions and controls which would almost certainly have to be put in place. Let's hope that we'll be back next year!

Dan Watson (race organiser)
