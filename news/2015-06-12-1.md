Jez Bragg has just broken Adrian Belton's long-standing Ramsay's Round record, taking 11 minutes off Belton's 1989 time to record a new figure of 18 hours 12 minutes. A well-deserved reward for both meticulous preparation and smart call after some postponements for 2015's so-far quite unseasonal summer to take the last of several proper June days in starting 24 hours earlier than finally scheduled.
