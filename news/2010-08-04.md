---
title: "Anthony gets the better of Rodgers at Ben Kenneth, 1 August"
date: 2010-08-04
excerpt: "Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) reverses the previous day's result at Cow Hill, winning from David Rodgers (Lochaber) in record time...  '"
---

Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) won the Ben Kenneth race ahead of David Rodgers (Lochaber), thus reversing the positions in which they'd finished the Cow Hill race the previous day. Anthony's time was over three minutes faster than the published course record, which was set 23 years ago. Rodgers and third-placed Alec Keith (HBT) were also inside the old record.

Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT) repeated her success at Cow Hill, winning in a time that was nearly ten minutes inside the published record.
