We are now into the 2016 season and subscriptions to SHR are due. If you do not want to remain as a member please let me know so that I can remove your details from the database.

The fees are unchanged at: £8 for senior, £4 for each subsequent senior at same address & £2 for juniors/students.

Online and Postal Subscription forms are available on the SHR website [http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/HowToJoin.aspx](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/HowToJoin.aspx)
