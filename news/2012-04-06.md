Adam Anderson, race organiser for the SHR Championship race at Durisdeer has asked for race entries to be sent in by the start of June. It may be a while away but as Adam says _"it would help the organiser if competitors could pre enter by the 9th June. Would be a travesty if there wasn't enough cakes to go round."
