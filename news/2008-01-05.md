We're told that conditions were "just above freezing, almost sunny but very wet and muddy underfoot". Craig Mitchell (Lochaber AC) must have approved, for he won the Dash by 20 seconds from Jason Williamson (Cosmic HB) in 22:41 with Manny Gorman (Westies) third. Fiona Dahl (Cosmic HB) finished 16 seconds ahead of her club-mate Anita Hamilton to be first lady.

In the Elrick Junior Race Duncan Gorman (Westies) went one better than his father by coming in second, behind Stuart Brooker (Alford Academy).
