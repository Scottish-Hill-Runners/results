**Caerketton Hill Race and Junior Race**

Caerketton is the shortest race in the Calendar and the ninth counter in the ten-race Bog and Burn series. Pre-race favourite Brian Marshall (HELP) was led to the first summit by Steven Fallon (Carnethy) via a slightly longer but arguably faster route than the direct one. Marshall had opened what looked like a decisive gap by the start of the final steep descent but a determined Fallon had reduced it to a mere one second by the finish. Manny Gorman (Westies) made up several places on the return journey from the summit of Caerketton to finsih third.

Elke Schmidt (Bellahouston) was a clear winner of the women's race, nearly two minutes ahead of Megan Mowbray (HBT), with Helen Murray (Lothian) third and first F40.

**Tain Hill Race**

Like Caerketton, the Tain Hill Race (a curtain-raiser for the Glenmorangie Highland Games) was blessed with a fine dry evening. Gordon Lennox (Alness) was followed home by Steven Worsley (Inverness Harriers) and John Shaw (Serpentine), the first of the visitors. Catriona Fraser (Inverness Harriers) was fifth overall and first woman; second was ninth-placed Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT).
