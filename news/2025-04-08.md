Lomonds of Fife race date change!! For all interested. We still have a number of discussions to have about the race route with local parties, and are postponing the race till mid-August, when will run a test event. It is still our intention to run the race on a date in May 2026 and take it from there. I appreciate many may have had their sights set on this for May, but I’m sure you can appreciate there are multiple groups we have to talk with, and I hope you will sign up for the new route mid-August. Your patience is much appreciated, and I attach a photo of the proposed new route as it stands for your information. Iain Simpson, Race Organiser.
