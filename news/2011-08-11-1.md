We've had the following communication from race organiser Dave Weatherhead

_I know you are probably aware of The UKA British Relay Championship to be held on Saturday 15 October 2011 and based on Kettlewell in the Yorkshire Dales. In fact I notice Shettleston have entered a very strong team and we also have entries from other Scottish clubs._

_

We would like to invite other team entries from Scotland to help make the event a truly National Championship.

The event website is www.relay.bingleyharriers.org.uk. Entries are open now (via the SPORTident website) and close on the 18 September.

If anyone needs any further information my mobile is 07972 787161.

Best regards

_

_David Weatherhead_
