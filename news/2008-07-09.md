Rosie Swale-Pope (61) is in the final stages of a 20,000 mile solo unsupported round-the-world run which began in October 2003.

She is currently running north-to-south through Britain and should reach Glasgow by Saturday evening (12 July).

Rob Reid is organising a welcome get-together on Sunday 13th, involving fellow long-distance runners, other supporters, and (hopefully) the media.

The plan is to meet up at 10:30am at the Botanic Gardens at corner of Great Western Road and Queen Margaret Drive, give Rosie a rousing welcome, and then escort her, with her unique tent-on-wheels, along to George Square, arriving there about 11:30.

Have a look at her website [www.rosiearoundtheworld.co.uk](http://www.rosiearoundtheworld.co.uk)

And if possible, come along on Sunday and help with fundraising and publicity for Rosie's two chosen good causes:  
  

*   Kitezh Children's Community in Russia  
      
    
*   The Prostate Cancer Charity
