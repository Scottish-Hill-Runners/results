Around 70 members completed the Run 4 It Scottish Hill Runners senior championship this year and are entitled to one of Ronnie’s mugs! The mugs feature a great panoramic view of the Arrochar Alps (see link). Mugs will be available for collection at the “do” this weekend or at Tinto on 10th November. If you are not going to be there, please arrange for a fellow club member to collect on your behalf.
