The scottishathletics Junior Hill Running League kicks off on Sunday 15th April with the Lomonds of Fife junior race.

The course will be different from last year! Registration opens at midday, at the Purin Hill car park (as last year), near East Lomond, (car-park is one mile up the hill midway off the Falkland road A912). No entry fee, and a snack and drink will be provided for all runners.

The under 12’s race starts at 1 pm, all other races at 1.30pm. All races start and finish near the car park, and ascend East Lomond, with routes varying according to age.

All routes will be marked and marshalled. Under 12 Males and Females 2.5km; Under 14 Males and Females 2.8km; Under 16 Males and Females 5.6km, Under 19 Females 5.6km, Under 19 Males 8.4km. The courses are suitable for hill shoes or trainers, but not for spikes.

Further junior league races will be held at Ben Lomond (May 12th) and Menstrie (September 2nd).

The Lomonds of Fife and Ben Lomond races are selection races for juniors born in 1995 and 1996 to represent Scotland at the International Youth Cup, to be held in Ireland on 24 June.

Please contact Mark Harris (harrismark1@mac.com) for more information. Or individual race organisers Roger Rees (Lomonds of Fife), Don Reid (Ben Lomond) and Hugh Buchanan (Menstrie).
