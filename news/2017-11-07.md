The SHR AGM will take place straight after the prize giving at Symington village hall after the Tinto Hill Race on Saturday 11th November.

Note that three topics have been added to the agenda and will be discussed under AOB –

*   Online entry for races
    
*   Introduction of a 5 year age category race
    
*   Long classics mug
    

If you are a current member and have any points for discussion or motions you want to have raised at the meeting or if you wish to join the committee then please contact the secretary, Hilary Ritchie, (secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk).
