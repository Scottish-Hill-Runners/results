This year's SHR AGM will take place after the Morven hill race, at 5:45pm on Saturday the 4th of October in the Victory hall on Ballater Road in Aboyne. We've timed it to take place just before the end of Championship awards and ceilidh which starts at 7:00pm and are in the same venue. 

If you plan to attend, then please email **[secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk)**  Similarly, if you have any motions or agenda items that you would like to be included at the meeting then please let us know.

Look forward to seeing you there.

The SHR Committee
