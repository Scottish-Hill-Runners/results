Angela Mudge completed the Everest Marathon in 5hrs 2 mins 17 secs. Euan Jardine correctly guessed 5.02 and wins a Salomon ruc sac for his efforts. Over £600 was raised for Starthcarron Hospice and The Everest Marathon Fund. Thanks to everyone who donated. Well done to Angela, and all those who helped by entering the competition.
