The Scottish Hill Runners Long Classics Championship 2016 will consist of 10 races, with the best five to count. There is a slight error in the printed calendar, but the website has all the relevant information here -

[http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-006)

The ten races will be Stuc a Chroin, Slioch, Jura, Trotternish Ridge, Arrochar Alps, Ben Rinnes, Glenshee 9, Ochil 2000’s, Two Breweries and the Pentland Skyline.
