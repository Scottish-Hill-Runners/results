_From organiser Gary Gutteridge:_

A record turn out of 63 for a race that remained dry - many thanks to a strong turnout from the local running club Stonehaven RC. This was the last race in a series of four where the best three results count. Cheyne Wood Hill Race is 6.4km and 225m climb over single track paths through heather, ferns, forest and moorland with the occasional boggy bits.

First place was won comfortably by Robbie Simpson from Deesside Road Runners in a new course record of 25m 49s knocking nearly 1min off the previous record. Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) was second and Chris Hill (Cosmics) third at Cheyne but first and second respectively in the Series. First M40 at both Cheyne and the Series was Dennis McDonald (Cosmics). First M50 was Davie Duncan (Ochils)

A new female course record was set by Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) in 29m 10s knocking over 3 minutes off last year's record winning time. First F40 was Jo Thom (Stonehaven RC); first F50 was Elaine Stewart (Cosmics) and 1st F60 was Anne Tough.

First junior M14 was Frazer Drummond who set a course record of 18m 39s for the junior race of 3.6km and 120m climb.
