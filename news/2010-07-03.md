_From organiser Dave Hanlon:_

Oleg Chepelin and Veronique Oldham were clear winners in the White Tops hill race. Conditions were fine, after the recent dry spell so fast times were always on the cards.

The race was rescued by Dundee Hawkhill Harriers after the previous organisers decided to let it go. A healthy turnout of 53 has given me the confidence that people want it to continue so I will ensure that it does.

Thanks to all those who helped out.
