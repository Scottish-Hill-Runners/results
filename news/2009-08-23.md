_From race organiser Alex Macrae:_

The normal 25 mile race had to be abandoned due to dangerous river levels, and a 22 mile course substituted. The 13 mile race went ahead as usual. A total of 671 entrants, including walkers, started and finished the event.

Paul Raistrick (Highland Hill Runners) led home a field of 68 runners in the revised 22 mile race, finishing over 5 min clear of Murray Strain (HBT) and Kenny Riddle (HHR). Claire Gordon (HBT) won the ladies' race for the fifth time in six appearances, finishing ijust 17 seconds ahead of club-mate Megan Mowbray. Gillian Irvine (HHR) was third. John Kay (Fife AC) won the M50 award and Graham Arthur (Ilkley Harriers) the M60 class.

Adrian Davis (Carnethy HRC) won the accompanying 13 mile race, Carol Bulmer (Unattached) retaining the ladies' trophy.
