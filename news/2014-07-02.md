With the Dollar being a combined British, SHR and SAL Championship race there was a big and very high quality field, with lots of people taking the chance of ticking off another race towards their championship completion.

Interestingly in both the men’s and the women’s veteran classes, each age categories has someone who has won both races so far (apart from men’s over 70), while the Senior category is much more open for both men and women and a lot depends on who among the front runners does enough of the races.

The latest listings can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right but are also linked from here:

[Women](http://www.shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2014 - Female.xls) and [Men](http://www.shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2014 - Male.xls)
