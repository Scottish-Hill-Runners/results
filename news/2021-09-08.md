---
title: "Birnam Hill Race Results & Report"
date: 2021-09-08
excerpt: "Wins for Blair Barnett-Johnston (Carnethy) and Kirstie Logan (Highland Hill Runners) at the Birnam hill race on Saturday 4th September."
---

The senior race was a keen race with Blair Johnston from Carnethy coming home first in 31mins 32 seconds. Robin Howie claimed 2nd and Julian Barrable 3rd. Mike Stewart had a fine run to claim 5th place, and first vet 40 and 50 in 34.28. Justin Carter was next vet 40 home. The ever present John Stevenson was first vet 60 in 43.28.

The ladies turned out in good numbers with Kirstie Logan from Highland hill runners claiming first in 39.15. Monica Padilla was 2nd and Katherine Farmer 3rd and first vet40 in 44.18. Kathleen Aubrey was first vet 50 in 44.46.

Thanks to all the Marshalls and landowners and support team including Pro Tay for the timing.

Best wishes 

Adrian Davis (Race Organisesr)
