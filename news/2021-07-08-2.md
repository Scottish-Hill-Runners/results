A Journal for 2020 has been published and a number have already been delivered to members via club contacts. As well as delivering through club contacts it will also be possible to pick up a Journal at a number of races.

Next Saturday (17th July) I will be at the Dollar Hill Race and I will bring a stock of Journals to the race. It would be good then if any member who is there could arrange to pick up a Journal from myself. Similarly, it would also be good if you know of other members who will not be there (club or otherwise) and you could arrange to pick up journals for them. If you wish to contact me beforehand to discuss then please contact me by email and [gordonpryde@talktalk.net](mailto:gordonpryde@talktalk.net) .

Gordon
