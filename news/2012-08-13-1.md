Entries for the Ochil 2000s race on 25th August at the lower fee of £12 are being accepted by the organiser Alex King up until August 20th. Entries on the day are available at £15, but a place on the coach cannot be guaranteed!

Please make the organiser’s job easier and enter early if you can!!
