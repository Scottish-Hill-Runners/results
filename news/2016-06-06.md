A busy weekend in the SHR calendar. Results for four of the seven races are now on the website under the results tab here - [http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceResults.aspx](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceResults.aspx)

And on SHRacing here - [http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/Home.aspx](http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/Home.aspx)

At Gargunnock, Graham Gristwood (Ochils) and Fanni Gyurko (Central AC) were the winners.

Jacob Atkin (Edinburgh Uni) and Christina Rankin (Kilbarchan) were the winners at Glas-Tulaichean Uphill race which was also on Saturday.

On Sunday, James Espie and Stephanie Provan (both Deeside) won at Scolty.

And at Yetholm, the winners were Douglas Tullie (HBT) and Emma Bain (Northumberland FR).

Results for Trotternish Ridge, Helensburgh Games and Saughill Race to follow when we get them.
