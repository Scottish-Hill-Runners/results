---
title: "SHR 2015 Championship Mugs"
date: 2015-11-12
excerpt: "If you haven't already got your mug, you can collect it from registration at the Tinto Hill Race on Satuday."
---

If you haven’t already collected your SHR mug from the Dumyat Dash race, SHR 2015 Championship mugs will be available at Tinto for all those who completed the required number of races and who are paid up SHR members. If you qualified for a mug, but won't be at Tinto, please ask someone from your club to pick it up for you if possible. Alternatively, contact SHR secretary, Neil Gilmore and I’ll arrange to get it to you.
