---
title: "Outcome of SHR championship age category survey"
date: 2015-03-10
excerpt: "Thank you to everyone who took the time to complete the survey. There was no clear resolution one way or another regarding the preferred system for defining age category winners and therefore the Committee have agreed to keep the existing system in place……"
---

Thank you to everyone who took the time to complete the survey. There were around 270 responses in total. As expected there was a diverse range of comments. However, responses to the two alternative approaches to defining age category winners were split roughly 50/50 in both cases. In view of this, and with no clear resolution one way or another, and no overwhelming mandate for change, the Committee have agreed to keep the existing system of defining age category winners (i.e. as used in previous years) in place.

With regard to question 1, this has been disregarded as it was only relevant if a change to the existing system had been favoured.

The response to question 4 was overwhelmingly in favour of a more equitable distribution of _prizes_ as is the case in many races, but more consideration needs to be given as to how the same approach might be taken for the distribution of the _trophies_ in the main SHR championship.
