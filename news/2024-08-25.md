**SHR Bog & Burn Championship Update**

Bog and Burn final positions.

A good turnout at Caerketton saw 12 runners get their 6th required race and 44 runners completed the series, well done to all.

In the Men's seniors Alistair Thornton took the win ahead of Kieran Cooper and Gregor Malcolm. In the Female seniors Helen Gordon won ahead of Rosalind Heron and Tilly Hamilton.

Other category winners -

M40 Robin Wilkins

M50 Grant Baxter

M60 Harry Gilmore

F40 Lisa Blackley

F60 Hazel Dean

M70 Murdo MacLeod

Ochil Hill Runners took the team prize
