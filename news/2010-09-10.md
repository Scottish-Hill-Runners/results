Winter training Tips

Summers nearly over, so enjoy the last few hillraces of the summer season... but don't forget the winter xcountry season is just about to start and still the best winter training to build speed , strength and endurance.

Local leagues in all areas .. plus the famous Borders Xc series

The other top tip for winter training from the Thinman is take care if you ever see a sign saying 'Dangerous Wild annimals'!

A few of us were out for a Sunday run in Perthshire the other morning and came to an 8-foot fence with just such a sign! Usual hillrunner reaction .. "Och how many wild animals do you get in Perthshire"... so we climbed over the fence and carried on running up the hill! a few minutes later we were escorted off the estate by the local warden in his 4x4 jeep ( a bit like that scene in Jurassic Park!) ... turned out the farm was a breeding station for the Zoo and the Bison Bulls were in full rutt!

So the moral of the story is ....there are wild animals in Perthshire, and yes we have a right to roam.. but take care!!

cheers Jamie
