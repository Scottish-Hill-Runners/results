**2016 Oxton Border Games Hill Race**

There was good weather and an excellent turnout of 24 runners for this short but testing race on the edge of the Lammermuirs.

Colin Donnelly (Cambuslang) and Brian Marshall (Haddington) repeated last year’s tussle for the lead, with Colin again prevailing to win in a time of 18:10 and Brian taking second (and the prize for first veteran) in 18:38. Greg Walker led a strong Teviotdale contingent home in third in a time of 18:56.

A closely fought battle for the Ladies’ prize saw Teviotdale’s Lauren Corbett take first in 24:23, just ahead of Gillian Millar (Lauderdale Limpers, 24:35), with Karen Meikle (U/A, 25:29) in third.

Gala junior Faisal Khursheed lost an early lead to the veterans on the hill, but still went on to take fourth position overall and First Junior in 19:04.

As ever, thanks go to the landowners, timekeepers, sweeper and marshalls for making it possible, to Border Events for generous prize sponsorship, and to the runners for making all the effort worthwhile.

Edmund Rooney

Race Organiser
