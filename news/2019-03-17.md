---
title: "Craig Dunain Race Report & Results"
date: 2019-03-17
excerpt: "Wins for Craig Campbell (Inverness Harriers) and Catriona Morrison (Highland Hill Runners) at the race on Saturday 16th March."
---

There was a good turnout of runners at the Craig Dunain hill race despite the horrible weather that was forecast. Conditions on the day were very wet, with runners reporting snow hampering their vision at the top.  
The course had been altered again this year due to the ongoing building works on the hill.  
The race was won by Craig Campbell (Inverness Harriers), followed by Ally Beaven (Highland Hill Runners) and Gordon Lennox (Inverness Harriers.  
First lady was Catriona Morrison, followed by Morvern MacKenzie and Karen Lyons (all Highland Hill Runners).  
Many thanks to all the marshals who stood out on a very wet hill and to all the other helpers today.

Caroline Marwick (Race Organiser)
