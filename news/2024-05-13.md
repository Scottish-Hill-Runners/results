The results for the Under 17 & Under 20 Junior Race at Broughton on Saturday 11th May are on the website.

In the Under 17 categories, Lachlan Thomas (Ross County AC) was first male and Ava Richardson (Gala Harriers) was first female.

And in the Under 20 categories, Ross Tayor (Lasswade AC) and Ruth Walsh (Giffnock North AAC) were first male and female respectively.
