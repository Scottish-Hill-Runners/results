_From organiser Rob Shaw:_

A scorching day, lots of fun runners, 6 juniors and 20 seniors. Dave Gallie was first man, taking three minutes off his time of last year, and Catriona Fraser first lady.

Craig Campbell (Inverness H) won the junior race in 27:50; Emma and Oonagh Dunnett (Edinburgh) were the leading girls in 37:07.
