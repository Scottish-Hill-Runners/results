Dave Eiser leads the Scottish Long Classics series from Jon Gay, after the first 4 races. Alayne Finlay leads the women's competition. The next counting race is Glenshee 9, on Sunday 4th August.

Note that the number of counting races has been reduced to 4, after the cancellation of Glen Rosa.
