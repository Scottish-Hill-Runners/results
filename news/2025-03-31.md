We’re sorry to let you know that this year’s Cobbler Hill Race has been cancelled. Despite our best efforts, the event is not economically viable to run this year.

This race is held in aid of Arrochar Mountain Rescue, and in order to continue supporting them effectively, the event needs to be financially sustainable.

All participants will receive a full refund, which will be processed in the coming days.

If you would still like to support Arrochar Mountain Rescue, you can do so by donating directly via this [link](https://www.arrocharmrt.org.uk/funding.html)

We’re fully committed to bringing the race back in April 2026, and we hope to see you there.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Warm regards,  
Arrochar Mountain Rescue
