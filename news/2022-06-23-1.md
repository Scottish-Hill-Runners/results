**Bog and Burn update after 8 races:**

Things are heating up in all categories as people and clubs have started to complete their 6 races. There is also the battle to complete all 12 bog and burn races. Currently, 3 people have completed 8 from 8, they are Andrew Fullwood, Chris Furse and Micky Anderson.

In the woman’s category, Lisa Blackley currently leads from Collette Ashworth. However, Edel Mooney who has completed 5 will overtake both with another strong result. The only person who could reasonably challenge Edel for the crown this year is Ellen Downs who has completed 3 races.

In the men’s category, Andrew McCall has a very healthy lead. It’s difficult to see anyone passing him at this point. Especially if he goes out to drop the two 9th place finishes from his score in the last 4 races. The only clear candidate who could challenge Andrew is Harry Pulham who has currently run 3 races.

In the club competition (which is a combined score of your top 3 men and top 3 women at each race) Westies are currently out in front. But in a shock result, Ochil’s took the win at Cort ma Law, meaning they are still in contention for the overall prize. They would need to win every remaining race to beat westies. However, the next two races are home races for them, so things could get very interesting.
