**Caerketton Downhill (1.8km, 310m descent)**

Andrew Gilmore (Carnethy) led home the field once again at the Caerketton Downhill Race (aka the Doonhiller). Gary Fraser (Ochils) posted another dominant veteran victory in 2nd overall with Alex McVey (Carnethy) making up the podium positions. David Elder (Lomonds) was first junior in 4 th, showing great form over the downhills this season, and one to watch for the future. Steven Fallon beat a competitive V50 field once more and Bob Waterhouse (Carnethy) was dominant in the V60 category.

Fiona Kelsall (Ochils) won by a fair margin over Lizzie and Rosie Knox of Anster Haddies, who tied for second. That gave Fiona victory overall in the Get a Grip downhill running series. Fiona was fist V40 with Heather Bell (Carnethy) and Cali Ingham (Carnethy) first V50 and V60.

The race was filmed by drone and go-pro so watch out for the coverage! Also, a set of keys were left behind so please contact the email address on the event website to claim.
