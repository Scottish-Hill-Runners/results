Graham Bennison wonders whether the 92 juniors who took part in the Mini-Tour of Fife's race up East Lomond have set a record entry for a junior hill race in Scotland...?

As for the details, he reports:

The [Mini Tour of Fife](http://www.minitour.org.uk/) – five races in five days – continued with a testing hill race up the East Lomond.

Ryan McIntosh (Fife AC) led up the initial 500ft climb to the Lomond summit but following the steep descent and further climb across the summit it was specialist hill runner Adam Harris that carved out a commanding lead to finish the 2 .25 mile course in 14 minutes 25 seconds with McIntosh following in 16.11.

Lucy Duff (Fife AC) finished as the leading girl in the under-18 age group timed at 18 minutes 48 seconds but it was under-15 club colleague Robyn Graham that posted the fastest female time over the 2.25 miles of 18 minutes 23 seconds.
