_From David Rogers_

Robbie Simpson (Deeside) set a new record time of 47 minutes and 37 seconds at Cort-ma Law Wednesday – 39 seconds faster than the previous record despite five new fence crossings which have turned the race into a boggy steeplechase. Second and third places went to Alastair Graves (Westerlands) and James Tullie (Shettleston). The first lady was Julia Connor (Moorfoot), for the second year running, with Polly Beard and Jane Simpson (both of Bellahouston Road Runners) in second and third places. The men's and women's team prizes were both won by Westerlands. Thanks very much to Westerlands colleagues for marshalling, time-keeping and surprising good humour in the clouds of midges.
