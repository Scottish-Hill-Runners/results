The four races that will constitute the British Championship in 2020 are :-

· 14 March Aran Race (Medium), Wales

· 25 April Seven Sevens (Long), N Ireland

· 01 August Glenshee 9 (Long), Scotland

· 29 August Arnison Crag Horseshoe (Short), England
