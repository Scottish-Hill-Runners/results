Dougal Ross of Bellahouston Road Runners is the new leader of the 2008 Bog and Burn Series following Kinnoull, the 3rd race in the series.

Angela Mudge is in a strong position to retain her Bog and Burn ladies' title with 3 wins out of 3 in the first three races.

The next race in the series is [Kilpatricks Hill Race](http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0045) on June 4th.
