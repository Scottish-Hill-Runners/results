SHR Annual General Meeting 2012

The AGM of Scottish Hill Runners will take place at Symington Sports Club on Saturday 10th November, immediately after the Tinto Hill Race prizegiving.

**Nominations for the main office bearers, the four elected posts on the Committee, or any motions for the agenda must be sent to the Secretary – the deadline is Monday 1st October.** That’s to: Bruce Smith, 19 Keith Gardens, Broxburn EH52 6XS, brucesmith64@blueyonder.co.uk

Volunteers are always sought to assist in the running of Scottish Hill Runners and whether you’re an active participant or interested supporter we would be delighted to have you on board, either in an official committee role or co-opted to help with a specific aspect. **_This year, we are specifically seeking nominations for the posts of Convenor and Treasurer, the current incumbents having served for three years. If you wish to discuss either of these positions, feel free to speak to David Scott (contact details on website)._**

We will issue the agenda papers at the start of October. If you receive your newsletter by email you’ll get the papers by email also. If you currently receive your newsletter by post, but would be prepared to receive the AGM papers by email (saves us time and postage), then please let the Secretary know asap.

We look forward to seeing you there to help shape the direction of Scottish hill running.
