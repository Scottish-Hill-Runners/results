date: 2025-08-23
excerpt: "Here at last are the latest championship tables after the Glenshee 9. So far, with four races down and The Ben and Morven to go, 27 people have qualified for the superb championship mug, but lots more are on target to get one provided they do one of both of the remaining races."
---

Very few categories have been decided yet. The only individual category to be decided is the Women's Over 55 in which Veronique Oldham (Cosmic) is unbeatable. In the teams' categories, Carnethy have won at least a share of the Men's Over 40 award. However, not quite decided but almost, Sasha Chepelin (Carnethy) leads the Senior Men and is in a very strong position. In fact only one runner can beat him (mathematically, as they say!) - Euan Brown of Corstorphine. But Euan would have to win both the last two races and Sasha to finish lower than second in both for this to happen. The Senior Women's is too open to call and really depends on who runs the final races.
