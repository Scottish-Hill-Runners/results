The SiEntries registration link for the Turnhouse Hill Race on Wednesday 12th June (19:30 start time) is now available

[SiEntries - Turnhouse Hill Race](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=13626)

The senior Turnhouse Hill race (5.2kms, 310 metres) is a traditional hill race suited for all runners with hill running experience. Although relatively short, the main ascent and descent to/from the summit are steep so hill shoes are advised. The route is mostly tracked although the steep climb up to the summit is through heather.

The race start is a few minutes jog up the glen road from the Flotterstone car park (NT229631).

There is also a shorter (2.2 kms, 160 metres) junior race (ages 10-15). This is turn up on the day and FREE.
