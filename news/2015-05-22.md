Red Moss Kips Hill race - Wednesday 10th June 2015

Entries for this event open on Monday 1st June 2015 at 12:00.

All runners must be over 18 years old.

The 2015 route is the same as for 2014.

The new route is 10.5km (with 350m of climb) and goes over West and East Kip.

The route is not marked , and there will be minimal marshalls - all runners are expected to be self-reliant and carry a windproof to keep them warm if they get injured.

All runners should study the map in advance, and if you haven't done the route before carry map and compass so you don't get lost. A runner got lost last year and descended down the wrong side of the Pentlands to Carlops - this was the wrong way!! The route is a loop starting and finishing at the same spot at the Red Moss Car park.

Bottle of beer for any finisher who beats or equals their age in minutes.  
Race record is 41:40 - so a good target for all Vets!!

Pre-enter via Si Entries (from Mon 1st June) , or cycle to the race for free entry on the night.  
Fast running on tracks and footpaths, with two climbs up the Kips.  
Free soup in the Grey Horse Inn , Balerno after the race  
Race entry limited to 100 - due to parking restrictions and landowner request - please car share.

The race will start from Red Moss car park, go along the gently climbing road (locally known as the Agony of Trees!), turn right and then left at top of road , then take the usual path towards the drove road (passing the turn-off to Hare Hill and instead continuing to the Kips), up West Kip , over East Kip , then turn sharp left at the col to follow the path back towards Lovers Loup and the Howe, then from the bottom of the glen turn left (north-east) to follow the foot-path through Green Cleuch, and return by the path, to the road at Bavelaw Castle and back to the Red moss car park.
