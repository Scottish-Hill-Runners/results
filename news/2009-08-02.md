Robbie Simson was a comfortable winner at the Newtonmore Highland Games Creag Dhubh race, finishing well ahead of Dave Rodgers, Andrew Wright and Colin Donnelly. Gill Irvine was 1st lady, followed by Ellen Homewood and Rachel Armitage.
