---
title: "Douglas triumphs at Tain, 13 August"
date: 2008-08-18
excerpt: "Andrew Douglas (Caithness AAC) and Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy HRC) take the top spots.  '"
---

From Fiona Sutherland, Secretary of the Tain Highland Gathering:

"A fantastic total of 42 runners took part on what was a lovely evening. I hope everyone who took part enjoyed themselves! Many thanks to any of your members who took part."

Andrew Douglas (Caithness AAC) won from Kenny Riddle (Highland HR) with last year's winner Gordon Lennox (Alness) third. Highland Hill Runners filled the next four places.

Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy HRC) was the first woman, followed Roxy Banerman (Highland HR) and Susan McDonald (Tain R).
