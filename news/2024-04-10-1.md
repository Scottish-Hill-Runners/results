# The Cobbler Hill Race 2024

## SHR 2024 Championship race

#### Saturday 13th April 2024

 Full information & maps on the Protay site - [Cobbler Hill Race 2024 (protay.co.uk)](https://protay.co.uk/cobbler24/)

**Race day information:**  
Registration opens at 9.30am at Ardgartan hotel. Registration closes 11.30am. The registration will at the rear of the hotel. Please remove hill shoes when entering the hotel.

**Please allow plenty of time to get from car park, to registration then to the race start.** The start is approx 3km from the hotel.

**Kit - Spot checks at the start line - Full body cover must carried as per rules of race organiser and Scottish Hill Runners. [Click here for SHR Safety guide](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Safety.aspx)**.

**Car Parking**:  
**There will be parking marshals please follow their instructions.**  
  
**Parking zone 1** - Ardgartan hotels - cars & mini buses. Limited numbers of spaces for cars - first come first served. **All vehicles assigned to the hotel car park must be moved by 4 p.m.**  
  
**Parking zone 2 & 3** - north of the hotel - Vans & large vehicles.  
**  
Parking zone 4 & 5** - Forest Holidays cabin - cars & vans. There a pedestrian footbridge which takes you to registration.
