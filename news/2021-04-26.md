title: "Return To Racing......."
date: 2021-04-26
excerpt: "Update on guidance."
---

Hill racing can restart now!

From Monday 26th April we can race in your local area and events must be socially distanced.

From 17th May, if the government follows their current route map, we can race across Scotland. At the moment numbers will be limited to 200 and starts will be in waves of 30.
