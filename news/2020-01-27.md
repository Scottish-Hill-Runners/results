152 teams finished the "new look" Devil's Burdens very successfully organised by Fife AC. All legs now start and finish in the same place, (the same place as was previously used for the FRA Relays) making it a much more sociable occasion.

In a keenly fought race, Carnethy came out on top, 2 mins clear of Shettleston, with Westerlands taking the last podium position. The women's race was just as keenly fought with the lead changing on every leg until finally Fife AC, came home just one minute in front of Carnethy with Hunters Bog Trotters taking the third spot.

Results and routes are [here](http://fifeac.org/events/fife-ac-events/devils-burdens/1347-devil-s-burdens-2020.html).
