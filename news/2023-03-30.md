**Bake Sale at Birnam.**  
  
There‘ll be a bake sale (and raffle thickets) at Birnam Hill Race to raise money for the British Mountain Running Team.

We’ll be based at the back of Birnam guesthouse.

Please support the sale as last year 11 Scots represented Britain at the World Championship.

Thanks in advance,

Angela and the (yet to be) selected team
