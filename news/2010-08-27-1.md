_From Strathearn Harriers:_

At the request of one of the local landowners we have had to make some changes to this year's race. We will be working with them to try to restore the original routes in time for the 2011 race but for now we have had no option but to make some changes:

Legs 1 and 3 remain unchanged but legs 2 and 4 have had to be significantly altered. Full details can be found on the [race website](http://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/comriehillsrelayrace.htm). In both cases we have tried to find routes that are of similar length and of a similar nature to the usual routes. Extra marshals are being drafted in to minimize the chance of people going astray. We know some people have already been to Comrie for a recce and would like to apologise for any inconvenience these late changes may cause.

If you are planning to send a team or two, please send in your completed [entry form](http://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/SWCHRentry2010v3.pdf) by 6 September.
