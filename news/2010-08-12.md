Caerketton rounded off the Bog and Burn Series for the year on a breezy Pentlands with showers passing through.

The first winners of the evening were Gregor and Hamish Brown (Beacon) aged 11 and 5, who ran to the stile and back. They were followed by 11-year old Cameron Law (Dunbar), first home in the junior race - astonishingly, for the fourth year in a row!

The senior race was won by Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) with a flying descent from Carnethy's Sam Hesling and Andy Fallas. HBT dominated the women's race with the first four finishers, led by Claire Gordon.
