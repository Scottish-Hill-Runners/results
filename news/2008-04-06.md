---
title: "Scots amongst the medals in the Mourne Peaks Race, 5 April"
date: 2008-04-06
excerpt: "There were fine performances from Jethro Lennox (Shettleston H), Angela Mudge (Carnethy HRC), David Rodgers (Lochaber AC) and the Carnethy Women's Team."
---

The Mourne Peaks Race was the opening round in this year's British Mountain Running Championships.

Jethro Lennox (Shettleston H) so nearly won it - he finished just five seconds behind Rob Hope (Pudsey and Bramley) in 2:10:05. Jethro had a slight lead with just 50m to go, but took a full dive into a proverbial Irish bog allowing Rob to step over him and claim victory ! David Rodgers (Lochaber AC) was twelfth overall and third M40.

Angela Mudge made no mistake in the women's race; she was twelve minutes ahead of her nearest rival, veteran Fiona Maxwell (Mourne R). Elke Schmidt (listed as Unattached) was fourth, less than a minute further back.

Carnethy was the only Scottish Club with full teams: an excellent second in the Women's, plus tenth in the Veteran Men's and thirteenth in the Men's.
