---
title: "Cole is King at the Bog Trot, 17 April"
date: 2010-04-18
excerpt: "Edinburgh University's Ben Cole keeps the Bog Trotters at bay...  '"
---

Ben Cole (EUHH) won the Hunter's Bog Trot in fine style from the host Club's Ray Ward and Al Hart. The early leader Matthew Sutherland (EUHH) was fourth and Ochil's Ross McLennan fifth.

HBT dominated the women's race, though, supplying the first three to finish: Kate Jenkins, Eilis McKechanie and race organiser Morag McCracken. First veteran woman was Hilary Spenceley (Carnethy) and first veteran man, Peter Buchanan (Portobello).
