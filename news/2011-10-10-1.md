Mark Harris and Jasmin Paris are the main winners in the SHR Scottish Long Classics Series.

Mark came out on top after a closely fought battle throughout the series with Stewart Whitlie, Al Anthony, Andy Fallas and Konrad Rawlik. The other closely fought title was 1st M60, with Tom Bowie jumping ahead of long time leader Ray Murdoch in the final race.

Jasmin Paris emerged as a clear unchallenged leader in the women's competition, after Eilidh Raistrick lead for the first half of the season.

The series category winners are:

**Men**  
1\. Mark Harris, Fife AC  
2\. Stewart Whitlie, Carnethy  
3\. Al Anthony, Ochils  
1st M40 (not including above) John Binnie, Lochaber AC  
1st M50 - Alan Smith, Deeside Runners  
1st M60 - Tom Bowie, Carnegie Harriers

**Women**  
1\. Jasmin Paris, Carnethy  
2\. Eilidh Raistrick, Highland HR  
3\. Alayne Finlay, Ochils  
1st F40 - Jacqui Higginbottom, Carnethy  
1st F50 - Gill Irvine, Highland HR

Well done to the 39 runners who completed the series, and to all 676 runners who completed at least one race!
