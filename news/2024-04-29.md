**Short British Championship Race**

**Blisco Dash - Saturday 27th July**

Entries for the Short British Championship Race open on Wednesday 1st May on the following link. [https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=13554](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=13554)

There is a new format for 2024 where there is a Short and Long Championship. The team championship will be calculated from the two individual championship events. Also new for 2024 are 5 year age groups in the masters categories and you can only count in your age category. eg If you are 56, you only count in the V55 not the V50, V45 etc
