---
title: "Sidlaw Sunset Chase Race Cancelled"
date: 2018-03-16
excerpt: "Note that tomorrow's Sidlaw Sunset Chase Race has been cancelled due to bad weather."
---

There are currently white-out conditions on the hill and the temperatures are due to drop significantly tomorrow which will mean the paths (if any are visible) will be iced up.

The race will be re-scheduled to a suitable date within the next couple of months hopefully
