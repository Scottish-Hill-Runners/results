2017 sees a new race to the calendar in the form of the Houff of Ury hill race. The race is organized by Stonehaven Running Club in association with the already well established and popular Stonehaven Highland Games.  
  
Race details:  
Sunday 16th July

Stonehaven Mackie Academy playing field (games arena)  
Registration from 10:00 at games enterance, race starts at 11:15  
5 miles with 430ft climbing  
Over 18s only  
£5 entry fee includes entry to games for runners
