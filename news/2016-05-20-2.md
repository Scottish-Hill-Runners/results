Could everyone hoping to run in the Kilpatricks Hill Race 2016 please note the following:

**Kit Checks**

Kit checks will be carried out prior to the race. No kit = No race. Please come prepared with any kit that may be required on the evening; e.g. waterproof body cover, hat & gloves, map and whistle.

**Parking**

Parking is no longer permitted on the road at the start of the race route. This severely limits the available parking. Could all those entering the race give thought to organising car shares or using public transport (the Old Kilpatrick train station is two minutes away from registration) to avoid problems.

Looking forward to another cracking race.
