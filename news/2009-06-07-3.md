title: "Whitehead wins a wet Scolty, 7 June"
date: 2009-06-07
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead won comfortably from Jamie Stevenson, whilst Sonia Armitage finally got the better of Veronique Oldham to be first woman.  '"
---

_From organiser David Hirst:_

For the first time anyone can remember it rained for the Scolty race. The heavy downpours in the morning might have put off some runners because the turnout was much lower than usual, with only 38 for the senior race and 17 juniors. Alternatively, everyone might have been at the Lowe Alpine Mountain Marathon. Thankfully the rain stopped for the actual race, and although it was very boggy in parts the conditions were pretty good. Certainly good enough for Dan Whitehead who won easily in a good time of 34.29, nearly 3 minutes ahead of Jamie Stevenson.

The women's race was much closer, with Sonia Armitage only getting away from Veronique Oldham on the last descent, to win by 28s in 41.08. A good day for hosts Deeside Runners saw then winning the men's team prize, and also taking the first 3 over 40 places.

The junior race was one of the selection races for the World Mountain Running Association Youth Challenge, which explains James Waldie's long journey from Cornwall to easily win the U19 race. The U12 race was a 1,2,3 for the Rees brothers from Fife.
