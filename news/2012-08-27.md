We've been made aware of the sad death of Graham Brooks of Lochaber athletic club. His funeral his on Monday 27th in Fort William.

Manny Gorman reflects on his contribution to the local hill running scene

_The passing of Graham Brooks of Lochaber AC is the end of an era. Graham and his late brother Billy were keystones of their club for many years._

_He was well known and respected by runners from all clubs during the decades that he competed with distinction across Scotland._

_Outside running, Graham and his late wife Doris were known throughout Lochaber as a great team, Graham for the many years of service in Lochaber Leisure Centre and his legendary contributions to the local "fishing industry", the River Nevis proving very productive for him over the years!_

_He touched many people with his inspiration, his humour and his genuine interest in others._

_He will be sadly missed by all who knew him._
