---
title: "Simpson wins his own race: Grandtully, 12 April"
date: 2009-04-14
excerpt: "Organiser Stuart Simpson wins Grandtully from a small field...  '"
---

From Stuart Simpson:

"Excellent evening for a hill race at Grandtully again this year. A host of folk could have won this race with a course record, but they never came along to it so they didn't. Instead I got to add 80 seconds to the record of slowest winning time, followed closely by Adrian Davis and fellow-Ochil Gary Fraser. Lianne Haining of Gala Harries came home as first lady with Rob Holdway of Stafford and Stone Canoe Club being the first of only two senior men. The race is mainly intended as a fun event after a canoeing competition but people don't seem too interested in it any more. However I'll give them another chance at it next year..."
