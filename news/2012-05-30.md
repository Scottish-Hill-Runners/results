Please help the organisers of the SHR championship Durisdeer race by sending in your entry form as soon as you can.

Not that they need any assistance to make the cakes (they are usually devoured with relish by the hungry runners) but they would appreciate advance warning of numbers.

Entry forms are [here](http://www.shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=durisdeer hill race entry form 2012.doc)
