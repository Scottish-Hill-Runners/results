---
title: "Cnoc Dubh night race results"
date: 2017-12-17
excerpt: "Wins for Hamish Battle (Metro Aberdeen) and Fiona McDonald (Cosmics) at the first of this season's Deeside Runners' night races."
---

It was a cold, clear night with quite a lot of ice in the first km. We had to shorten the course by about 300m to avoid the very icy car park and track crossing, so even though Hamish Battle beat last year's winning time by nearly two minutes we can't really count it as a course record. In fact since the conditions are so different every year we probably shouldn't bother with records at all. Once the runners got into the woods the conditions were pretty good, though it paid to pay attention and shoes with metal studs were a big help. There were a few slips but no injuries. Great soup and cakes in the Riverside Cafe afterwards.
