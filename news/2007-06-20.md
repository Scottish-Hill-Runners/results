Andy Kitchin's new Ridge Race Series on the Pentlands kicks off next Wednesday (27th) with the South Black Hill race.

The Ridge Race Series has been organised to raise monies for the British Orienteering Squad support funds, which give small grants to individual athletes to help meet the costs of training and preparation.

After South Black Hill, there are two more races to make up the series, which are Carnethy Hill (not to be confused with the Carnethy 5!) on July 11th and Turnhouse Hill on July 25th.

Entry fees are £4 per race or £10 for the series, and there are junior races on each night as well – entry fee is a bargain 20p, with a small prize for all finishers. Series prizes will be presented after the final race at Turnhouse.

The races will be run by a small band of Edinburgh orienteers but any offers of on the day assistance will be most gratefully received.
