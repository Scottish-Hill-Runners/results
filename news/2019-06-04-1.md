Entries are now open for the Broughton Race.

The senior Race is pre-entry only at [https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=5725](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=5725)

Time – Seniors 13:00 (previously 13:30), Juniors from 12:00

Notes –

Race start times & distances :

12.00pm : Under 11 Hill Fun Run (minimum age 6 on day of race) : 1.3km, 110m ascent/descent

12.15pm : Under 13/Under 15 Race : 2.1km, 180m ascent/descent

12.25pm : Under 17/Under 20 Race : 5.1km, 320m ascent/descent

1.00pm : Senior Race : 9.6km, 600m ascent/descent

The senior race will host the 2019 scottishathletics National and East District Hill Running Championships (championships open to scottishathletics members).

The junior races for U20 (born 2000, 2001, 2002) and U17 (born 2003, 2004) will host the Junior Home International Trial.

Junior races are entry on the day only.

Note the races are open to all. Eligibility only required for the championship/trial.
