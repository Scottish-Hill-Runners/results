Rob Jebb (Bingley) held off the overseas charge to win Ben Nevis by almost 3 minutes from Danny Hope (Pudsey) and Tofol Castanyer (Spain). But in the womens race it was Spain's Mireia Miro who was victorious, ahead of Natalie White (Saab Salomon) and Italy's Emanuela Brizio.
