Forty four hardy runners took part in Lochaber’s first leg of the triple hirple. This short but brutal race is normally tough enough in fair conditions but turn the heat up to 27 degrees and it becomes a different prospect all together. Having said that it didn’t seem to bother our winner here today - finishing in a time of 00:31:11 was James Espie , but he was the exception today , all the other runners crossing the line looking exhausted.
