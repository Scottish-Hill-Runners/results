Mourne Mountains, Northern Ireland. AS 9.6km/762m NIMRA brings you the Granite Peaks - British Short Championship race 2025. The course will take you over some of the finest mountains we have to offer. With three 2,000 foot peaks in just over 9km and 750 m climb, it packs in lots of mountain for a short race and will provide a fitting test to determine the 2025 Champions. Entries for the British Champs short race in Northern Ireland are now open. [Entries on SI](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=15335)
