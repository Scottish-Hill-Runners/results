108 Took part in what turned out to be perfect conditions for a hill race, or just to clear the cobwebs from new year celebrations, in the men’s race it was Deeside’s Robbie Simpson that took 1st position by taking 1min.2secs of his own record, in a time of 21:13, well done Robbie, 2nd place was Lochaber’s, Finlay Wild in 22:28, 3rd place went to Inverness Harriers, Graham Bee, in 23:27. In the women’s, 1st place went to Lochaber’s Diane Baum in 29:29, in 2nd Place was Carnethy Hill RC, Lisa Gamble, in 31:19 3rd place was Megan Bee of Highland Hill Runners in 32:05. Well done to all that took part and massive thanks to organisers, Marshall’s and timekeepers.
