Here are the final standings for 2016 after the Pentland Skyline and by my reckoning 75 people have earned a fantastic SHR mug this year (the mug designers are hard at work as I write !). Hopefully they will be given out at the SHR prize-giving lunch after the Dumyat Dash on Sat 29th Oct (which also features a talk by Jasmin Paris on her Bob Graham, Ramsay and Paddy Buckley round records)

Anyway, the champions this year are:  
Seniors : Joe Symonds (Shettleston) & Stephanie Provan (Deeside)  
Over 40s : Kenny Richmond (Shettleston) & Veronique Oldham (Cosmics)  
Over 50s : Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) & Veronique Oldham (Cosmics)  
Over 60s : Ewan Paterson (Moray) & Jocelyn Scott (Fife)  
Over 70s : Gareth Bryan-Jones (Ochils)  
Under 23s : Hamish Battle (Aberdeen Metro)

Male Senior Team : Shettleston  
Female Senior Team : Deeside  
Male Veterans' Team : Shettleston  
Female Veterans' Team : Fife

It is the first time Shettleston have won the male vets team (in fact the first time since it started that Carnethy have not won it!), and the last time neither Shettleston or Carnethy won the Male Seniors team was 1993 (when Lochaber won it) !  
Deeside previously won the Senior women's team in 2014, while Fife retain the Veteran Women's team title.
