The first championship listings for this year, after Dollar, are now available. [Here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2021 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2021 - Female.xls) for women.

Due to the late cancellation of Yetholm Shepherds Show Race - another casualty of COVID19 - the championship will be best 3 from 5, although all but Over 60s and 70s must do at least one Long and one Medium.

Any queries to [Andy Spenceley](mailto:committee10@scottishhillrunners.uk) SHR Statistician
