---

Andrew Gilmore (Carnethy) won the race ahead of Mark Harris (Lomonds) and David Fulton (HBT) in 2nd & 3rd.

The ladies race was won by Megan Mowbray (HBT) with Sarah O’Neill (HBT) 2nd and Alice Scriven (Unattached) 3rd.
