All prize winners listed on next page"

The new female champion, Catriona Buchanan, has completed a unique double double as she retained both the titles she won in 2011 - the U23 and the Senior. For Al Anthony it is his first title after years of dominating the front end of races and he won it by a set of consistent results in the championship races - never winning but always in the top few.

74 people have earned an valuable SHR mug this year (although 9 of them, virtually all well known faces, haven't yet paid the 2012 sub so might be disappointed !).

Quite a few runners defended their 2011 championship title, these being Kenny Richmond, James Waldie, Carnethy Over 40s team, Catriona Buchanan (both Senior and U23), Veronique Oldham and Ros Evans.
