Sorry but its full for this time!!

The annual hill running weekend in Scotland's highest village, Wanlockhead, takes place shortly...

The weekend is designed to help any endurance runner who wishes to develop their hill running and navigational abilities. Juniors who are at least 12 years old are especially welcome and all sessions are designed to be suitable for their capabilities and guidance from experienced adults is offered throughout the weekend's activities.

Wanlockhead is an ideal and accessible venue for this midwinter get-together, being surrounded by grassy hills which you can run over in all weathers. After the outdoor sessions you can return to warm accommodation with good facilities, and the runs are complemented by indoor discussions and other informal group activities.
