title: "Applecross Duathlon cancelled"
date: 2019-09-13
excerpt: "Due to the forecast for SW gales and heavy rain, tomorrow's Duathlon has reluctantly had to be cancelled ..."
---

Due to forecast SW gales and heavy rain, the Duathlon has reluctantly been cancelled for tomorrow. There was judged to be too much danger for cyclists on the (mainly south westerly heading) coast road, and of hypothermia for slower competitors and marshals.

The wake at the Walled Garden will still be held in the evening, and it is hoped whoever can will be there.
