Yetholm Hill Race 2014; Sun June 1st 1.00pm

Category AM 12.8k/760m

Registration at start/finish area at Halterburn.

Entry: £5.00 on the day only.

Runners must over 18 on the day of the race.

Clockwise course records: 62.02 John Brooks Lochaber AC (2000) 77.03 Claire Millor Cosmics (2000)

Prizes; Limited prizes as event is a fundraiser for the Border Search & Rescue Unit. Nearest toilets; Town Yetholm

Further info from: Les Turnbull, Todrig, Leitholm, Berwickshire, TD12 4JA Mob; 07854866370 email; les1984@btinternet.com

PLEASE BE AWARE THAT UNDER SHR SAFETY REQUIREMENTS, MINIMUM KIT IS MANDATORY FOR ALL CATEGORY 'A' LONG AND MEDIUM RACES WHATEVER THE WEATHER OR TIME OF YEAR.

Kit Requirements

*   Windproof complete body cover
*   Other body cover appropriate for the weather conditions
*   Map and compass suitable for navigating the course
*   Whistle
