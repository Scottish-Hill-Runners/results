---

The weather for days before the isle of Jura Fell Race had been so bad that it looked right until the last minute that the bad weather route woudl have to be used, however on the Saturday morning there was a sudden improvement, the clouds lifed off the tops, the rain stopped (for most of the time, anyway) and the sun even came out (for some of the time). Although there was still a strong wind, it was manageable, so the full route was on.

From a large field, Es Tresidder (Dark Peak) was a convincing winner in 3 hr 21m 51s, nearly 7 mins in front of 6 times winner, Ian Holmes (Bingley), 3:28:33. Close behind Ian was Carnethy's Andy Fallas who ran superbly to be first Scot and break 3hr 30, finishing in 3:29:47. Dan Gay (HBT) was next Scot home in 5th place in 3:42:06.

First woman was Sarah Ridgeway (Eryri), 4:09:29, with Fiona Maxwell having a great run to take the runner up spot as well as being first Over 50 female in 4:19:47. Ian Holmes was the first Over 40 man, while Alan Smith (Deeside) had a great run to break 4 hours and take the Over 50 award.

192 finished the race with Dark Peak winning both men's and ladies team awards.
