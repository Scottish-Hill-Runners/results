Prasad Prasad (Squadra Porcini) crossed the line first with a very fast 30:51, almost two minutes ahead of Al Anthony with Carnethy's James Waldie a close 3rd. First woman was Charlotte Morgan from Edinburgh, over half a minute ahead of Shona Robertson (Shettleston).
