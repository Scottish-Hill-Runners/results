_From Jason Williamson_

3rd race in the Cosmics Summer Series. Fine conditions with a brisk north wind brought out a field of 71 senior and 4 junior entries. 1st Male was Chris Hill (Cosmic) in 33:38 and 1st Female was Sarah MacKenzie Cosmic in 41.44.

We had 4 juniors running their race with Liam Clark taking 1st male in 13:45 and Molly Williams taking 1st female in 18:37

Special thanks to Monymusk Honey (Dave Wilkinson) for the prizes, Forestry Commission and Pittodrie Estate for permission to cross their land and to all the volunteers: Marshalls: Ewen Rennie , Audrey Leel, Colin Russell, Rob Brookes, Cathy (Isla French’s mum), Phil Dawson and daughter Clyde Williamson. Time Keepers: Sarah O’Sullivan, Claire, Matt Brettle, Rupert Williams. Registration: Gillian Clunas, Anita Hamilton, Sarah O’Sullivan, Claire, Elaine Stewart

Thank you. Next and final race in the summer series is Cheyne Hill on Thursday 16 August 2012.
