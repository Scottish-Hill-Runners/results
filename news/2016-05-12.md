---
title: "Dumyat Hill Race Results"
date: 2016-05-12
excerpt: "Wins for Joe Symonds and Georgia Cottle at Dumyat"
---

The 43rd Dumyat Hill Race on Wednesday 11th May was won by Joe Symonds (Shettleston) ahead of James Waldie (Carnethy) and Hamish Battle (Metro Aberdeen).

First female was Georgia Cottle (Edinburgh Uni) with Charlotte Watson (Edinburgh Uni) and Georgia Tindley (HBT) 2nd and 3rd.
