**Braemar Junior League Race on Saturday 22 July**

A good turnout of 37 young hillrunners took part in Saturday's Braemar Junior Highland Games Hill Races. Congratulations to Rowan Taylor of Lasswade and Ariane Baillie of Pitreavie for their wins in the combined U15/U17/U20 race over 5km. In the U13 race over 2km, there were wins for Harris Turnbull of Strathearn and Eilidh Murdoch of Banchory Stonehaven. 

The Junior Hill Running League Standings after Round 5 are attached. The next two races, Cademuir Rollercoaster on August 12th and the Scottish Junior Champs at Creag Bheag on September 17th, both carry double points for the league, so it's still all to play for in all categories.
