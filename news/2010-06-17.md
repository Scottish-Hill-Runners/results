date: 2010-06-17
excerpt: "Adam Gatens (Westies) wins his first senior hill race; Catriona Buchanan (Ochils) inches out Emma O'Shea (Deeside) to be first woman...  '"
---

As a result of forestry work in the area the Ben Sheann race was run over a revised route. It clearly suited Adam Gatens (Westies), still a junior, who finished ahead of three chasing vets: Steven Fallon (Carnethy), Alan Smith (Deeside) and John Stevenson (Ochils).

The race to be first woman was won by Catriona Buchanan (Ochils), who came in just four seconds ahead of Emma O'Shea (Deeside).
