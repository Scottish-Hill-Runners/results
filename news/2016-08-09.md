Because of the new forestry in Menstrie Glen, we are changing the route of the Ochil 2000s between Blairdenon and Dumyat.

As the routes through the forestry are changing each year as the trees grow, we have decided to choose a southerly route sticking to one of the tracks.

The new route goes via the Summit of Colsnaur hill then down to main track down to Menstrie, with new checkpoints being on Colsnaur and at the Deer fence on the track. It is then suggested to drop down to Menstrie and then take the track up Dumyat. This means more climb, but over better terrain for running. The linked map shows the details.
