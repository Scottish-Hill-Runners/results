There’s an exciting new addition to the race calendar – the Two Inns Race which will take place on August 21st. It’s a testing point-to-point from Creagan to that most glorious of finishing venues, the Clachaig Inn in Glencoe. Just a little matter of 24km and 1770m of climb to complete before you take your refreshment……

FINAL DETAILS are [here]( http://shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=TWOINNS2010Race details.doc).

You can download an entry form [here]( http://shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=Two Inns Race 2010 Entry Form.doc) . (you can enter on the day but pre-entry appreciated.)

Chris Upson has kindly provided a course map [here]( http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0256 ).
