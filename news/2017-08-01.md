---
title: "Ben Rinnes Race Results and Report"
date: 2017-08-01
excerpt: "Wins for Andy Fallas (Carnethy) and Jill Stephen (HBT) at the Ben Rinnes Hill Race on Saturday 28th July"
---

Apart from the last championship race, the field of ninety eight runners was the largest we have ever had.Weather was very good at the games field and on race route with only one refreshing light passing shower.Excellent visibility.

An interesting battle at the front between the serious dedication of Andrew Fallas,youthful talent of Jamie Crowe and “been there,done that”experience of twice past winner and former long time record holder Jon Duncan all the way from Norway. Andrew held on to enjoy a rousing reception for his victory lap round the field but didn’t quite make his target of joining the sub two hour club.

Ladies winner and former local lass Jill Stephen also enjoyed a roaring reception at the finish as she stormed round the course recording the third quickest time only a minute and a half shy off the record.I’m sure it’s within her capabilities.

Des Crowe finished ten seconds ahead of Jill and no doubt thought the applause was for him. Sorry Des, no. I’m sure he lapped it up all the same.

Thanks to Robert MacLean for the biscuits and of course all the race marshalls.
