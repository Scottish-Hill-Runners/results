The following have been selected to represent Scotland at the [Snowdon International](http://www.snowdonrace.com/saesneg/welcome.html) on 18 July:

**Men**  
David Rodgers (Lochaber)  
Tom Smith (Lochaber)  
Murray Strain (HBT)

**Women**  
Fiona Maxwell (Shettleston)  
Nicola Meekin (Lochaber)  
Sue Ridley (Edinburgh)

Good luck to all - especially to Tom, Fiona and Nicola, who will be making their Scotland debuts.
