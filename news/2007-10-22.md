George Murdoch died suddenly whilst out for a run on Thursday evening (18th October 2007).

George was a close friend to many and a very keen club member having joined the Carnegie Harriers with his wife Gail over eleven years ago. His love of running saw him become a keen hill runner and after joining the club’s committee he later became Carnegie's Hill Running Club Captain.

George was a true gentleman and inspiration to all. He will be sadly missed by all who knew him. Our thoughts are with Gail and her family at this time.

George’s funeral will take place at 11.30am on Thursday morning (25th October) at Dunfermline Crematorium. Gail has asked for those attending not to wear black ties (either colourful ties or none) as the service will be a celebration of George’s life.
