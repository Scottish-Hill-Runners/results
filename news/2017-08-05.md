The annual SHR Bog and Burn mid-week hill race series concludes for 2017 with the Caerketton hill race on Wed 9th August, and the usual prize giving will be in the pub afterwards - for all the craic, soup, beer and banter - see you all at the Steading pub at Hillend, Edinburgh.

Prize winners from last year - please return trophies to Jamie Thin (HBT) before next Wed , or bring them on the day, or pass it to a pal who is going to Caerketton.

It is best 6 races to count and after 11 races , with one more race to go.. here are the leading contenders..  

James Espie (Deeside) is leading the men, and Jill Stephen (HBT) is leading the women

with Don Naylor (HBT) leading MV40, Chris Upson ( Westies) and Val Houston (Westies) leading V50s, and Roderick Pugh (Kilmarnock) leading MV60

Other contenders in with a shout are : Gregor Stewart, Thomas Callan, Craig Freshwater, Justin Carter, Neil Gilmore, Don Reid, Murdo Macleod, Michelle Hetherington and two runners have done all 11 races so far.. Stuart Brodie(Ochil) and Jamie Brown (HBT) - who will get the full house of all 12 races?!

The summer monsoon has seen some proper old-school bogs and burns this year - with thunder and lightning and torrential rain at least one mid-week race to keep it exciting!! More excitement in store for us at Caerketton!!  

Thanks to Chris Upson for collating the results again this year!!  

Jamie Thin
