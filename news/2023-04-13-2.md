Entries for the Glenshee9 will open on Monday 17th April at midday via [Protay](https://protay.co.uk/upcoming-events/):

[https://protay.co.uk/glenshee9-23/index.php](https://protay.co.uk/glenshee9-23/index.php)

The race takes place on Saturday 5th August from Glenshee ski centre. It is run every two years, so don't miss the opportunity to enter and visit 9 Munro's in one race!

Entrants will be required to have previous experience at longer fell races and should be capable of navigating in poor visibility (hopefully not the case though).

We look forward to welcoming old faces and new. All profits will go to Braemar MRT who will be providing race safety cover on the day.
