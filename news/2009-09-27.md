_From organiser David Armitage:_

The 23rd running of the Cairn William Hill Race was held in perfect weather under clear blue skies. Last year’s winner, Simon Peachey, retained his title and improved his time by over a minute to 46:34, just 16 seconds short of Dan Whitehead’s 2007 record for the current version of the course. Greame Bartlett was first veteran in second place overall, 20 years after the first of his three victories in the race. Cosmic Hillbashers took the team title from Deeside Runners by just two points. Ruth MacKenzie made up for this by leading Deeside Runners to a women’s team victory. Her winning time of 60:13 was over five minutes clear of runner up Jane Kerridge.

Ewan Kerridge won the longer Pitfichie Hill Junior Race and John Angus the shorter one.
