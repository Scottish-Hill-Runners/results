We are pleased to welcome Peter Duggan of Lochaber AC as the new “caretaker” of the SHR Long Distance Records on our website. Peter has a Ramsay Round to his name and has a keen interest in the big rounds. He has already started to update certain pages, notably on the Tranter Round and the Munros, and will be working on others in due course. So if anyone has any long distance exploits they would like recorded for posterity, please get in touch with Peter at shr AT petestack.com
