[![](http://chris-upson.com/images/AndrewWright_tn.jpg "Andrew Wright at the Whangie Whizz")](http://chris-upson.com/images/AndrewWright.jpg) After 9 races, Andrew Wright has taken the lead in the Bog and Burn Series by the narrowest of margins, edging ahead of Kenny Richmond on equal points, due to finishing ahead of Kenny in 3 out of 4 races where they've met.

With one race remaining, Caerketton on August 12th, the series is still up for grabs between Andrew Wright, Kenny Richmond and Oleg Chepelin, with Murray Strain lurking in the wings with 3 straight wins in the last 3 races.

In the ladies competition, Val Houston is leading but could still be overhauled in the final race if Iona Robertson wins at Caerketton.
