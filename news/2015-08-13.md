The final Bog & Burn race at Caerketton was won by Gregor Stevens (Westies) in a new record time (18:08) ahead of Mark Sutherland (Lauderdale) and Iain Gilmore (Carnethy).

The female race was won by Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) in a new record time (20:51) too. Sophie Horrocks (Edin Uni H&H) and Jill Stephen (HBT) in 2 nd & 3rd.

[Results](RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0090&Year=2015)

Overall Bog & Burn standings ended up as Men: 1. Gregor Stewart, 2. Owen O'Neill, 3. Iain Gilmore, M40 John Connaghan, M50 Steven Fallon, M60 Murdo MacLeod

  
Women: 1. Madeline Robinson, 2. Jill Stephen, 3. Val Houston, F50 Michelle Hetherington
