A catch up on some of the races from last weekend.

The full results are on the SHR website for Gargunnock, Yetholm and the Helensburgh Games race.

Wins for Rory Abernethy and Edel Mooney (both Ochils) at Gargunnock. Iain Stewart (Gala) and Karen Robertson (Northumberland FR) were the winners at Yetholm and Kris Blake and Meg Stuart (both Westies) got the wins at Helensburgh.
