The race organiser (Stuart Simpson, Ochils) surprised himself – and everyone else – by winning his own race. He writes:

"More runners than ever turned up for this years race, held after a canoeing competition on the Tay. Unfortunately the canoeists weren’t into running it this year for some reason. The organiser surprised himself and everybody else by catching Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) towards the bottom of the descent to win his own creation. Third place went to Manny Gorman (Westies) and Adrian Davis (Carnethy) was first vet. Sarah Legge (Fife) won the ladies’ race with Gala Harriers’ Leanne Haining (whose daughter shows a fair bit of talent as a kayaker) first female vet."
