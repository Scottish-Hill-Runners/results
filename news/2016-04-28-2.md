Westies 123 and Edel Mooney sets new ladies record at Beinn Dubh:

At the start of the race, the summit was obscured by heavy snow and strong winds. Fortunately by the time the leaders reached the summit, this had been replaced by clear skies and sunshine. Sam Alexander led home the leading pack of four Westerlands runners finishing in 36.02. Not far behind was Edel Mooney setting a new ladies record time of 42.09.
