Following Ben Sheann, the 6th race in the Bog and Burn Series, Bob Wiseman continues to lead the rankings, however Steven Fallon has moved up into 2nd place, with Dougal Ross slipping to 3rd.

Angela Mudge has essentially retained her title with 4 wins out of 4 races.
