_From Damien Theaker_

86 Runners turned out for the first race in the Bog and Burn Series 2014. Joe Symonds (HBT) and Madeline Robinson (Helensburgh) set new course records in ideal running conditions. Joe raced away from the field on the first steep climb and opened up a significant lead to finish in 36:21. Madeline Robinson (Female Junior) put in a stunning descent to finish in 46:57 and promises much more as she improved by over 3 minutes from the previous year.
