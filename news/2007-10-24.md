The Scotsman has a supplement on a Wednesday called "Recommends" and this week it lists the [5 recommended Hill Races in Scotland](http://thescotsman.scotsman.com/s2.cfm?id=1692332007) ! Everybody will have their own list, but the 5 listed by the Scotsman are Carnethy 5, Jura, North Berwick Law, Creag Dubh and Ben Nevis and the paper even includes a photo of runners in the Carnethy 5 (reproduced below).

The list was sent in by SHR member Ian Nimmo, who won a case of wine for the best recommended list...the competition included the 5 best Walkers' Bars; the 5 best Solo Artists; the 5 best Toyshops and Ian even beat best the 5 best Scottish Goals !

All good publicity for hill running........
