title: "Murray Strain 3rd at Snowdon International, 18 July"
date: 2009-07-18
excerpt: "HBT's Murray Strain had a storming run to finish 3rd at Snowdon International.  '"
---

HBT's Murray Strain had a storming run to finish 3rd at the Snowdon International race at Llanberis, North Wales. Murray was 2nd to the summit but was passed by descender supreme Ian Holmes. The race was won for an historic 4th year in a row by Andi Jones of Salford Harriers who was also first Brit at London Marathon this year. Andi clocked 1:08:15, with Ian Holmes in 1:09:00 and Murray Strain a gun time of 1:09:43.

The other 2 Scottish counters were David Rodgers and Tom Smith both from Lochaber AC who finished 15th and 29th in 1:13:21 and 1:18:45.

The women's race was won by Katie Ingram in 1:15:34. The Scottish team of Fiona Maxwell, Nicola Meekin and Sue Ridley finished 5th, 6th and 10th in 1:26:42, 1:27:17 and 1:30:07 respectively.
