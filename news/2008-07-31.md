---
title: "Deadline for entering Cairngorm Mountain Run approaches"
date: 2008-07-31
excerpt: "Entries close on 6 August; the race is on 9 August..."
---

There is now less than a week to go until entries close for the Cairngorm Mountain Run, an uphill-only event which incorporates the senior and junior World Mountain Trophy Trial races and World Masters vest competiton. The event is pre-entry only with entries closing on Wednesday 6th August, three days before the event.

Note - the closing date really is 6 August and not 2 August as stated on the event web site.

Click [here](http://www.cairngormmountainrun.co.uk/) for an entry form and race information.
