From Lothian Running Club's [news page](http://www.lothianrunningclub.co.uk/news.php):

"Congratulations to all who took part with a smile. To Matthew Galloway for breaking the record of his elder brother Christopher by one second, and to his younger brother Sam, for a remarkable time for a 9-year old.

Laura Van Kreiken improved her record by seven seconds to retain her title, but Lauren Arbuckle improved by ten seconds to finish only one second behind.

At the end you may think that Molly and Sam Dudchenko, aged four and two respectively, were probably carried round. But not a bit of it. It was great to see them finishing like a train, under their own steam. It is never too early to start to enjoy our great sport."
