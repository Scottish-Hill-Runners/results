The 2009 Bog and Burn Series kicked off with almost 160 runners at the Whangie Whizz just north of Glasgow.

The race winner by some margin was Hector Haines of Edinburgh University. In second place was Oleg Chepelin, and 3rd was Tom Owens (both of Shettleston)

The women's race was won by Sarah Wiseman of Central AC, with Catriona Buchanan 2nd (also Central) and Iona Robertson of Bellahouston finished 3rd.
