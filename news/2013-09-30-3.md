_From Keith Murray_

A fine day and best turnout yet for Penchrise Pen Hill Race. Both the male and female records went with Moorfoot's Andrew Cox taking overall honours in a time of 35.10 while local Dianne Lauder running for Gala Harriers took over a minute of the previous ladies record in 38.10. Thanks to all who made the effort to come along, check Teviotdale Harriers website for photos.
