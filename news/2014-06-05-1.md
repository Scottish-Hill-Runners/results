Wednesday evening at Cort-ma Law saw the traditional midges replaced by persistent rain and low cloud and the wettest conditions underfoot for quite a few years. Nevertheless 79 of the 80 starters finished. First man was Joe Symonds (HBT) in 48.06, closely followed by Iain MacCorquodale (Cambuslang) in 48:26. Third man and first V40 was Don Naylor (HBT). First MV50, 60 and 70 were respectively Des Crowe (Dundee), Murdo MacLeod (Westies) and Ian McManus (Irvine). First woman was Madeline Robinson (unattached) in 62.37. Second and first FV40 in 68:58 was Karen Kealey (Ochil) in 68.58. First FV50 and FV60 were Michelle Hetherington (Helensburgh) and Ros Evans (Saltcoats). There were no women’s teams, but the men’s team prize was won (or would have been won, had they waited to collect it) by Joe, Don and Chris Waters of HBT. Thanks very much to everyone for turning out to brave the elements with such excellent humour and to Westies colleagues for marshalling and time-keeping in such trying conditions.
