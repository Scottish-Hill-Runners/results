Westerlands CCC dominated at the Kaim Hill Race with 1-2-3 in the mens' race and also first lady.

Congratulations to Niall McAlinden who just beat clubmates Gregor Stewart and Harrison Stubbs and also to Ruth Crewe who won the ladies race.

Full results on the SHR results tab and lots of photos on the North Ayrshire AC facebook page.
