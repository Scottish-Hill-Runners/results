date: 2010-06-29

Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston) won by five minutes from Edward Catmur (Thames H&H) with first vet Graham Kirby (Fife) ten minutes further back in third.

Morgan Windram-Geddes (Fife) was the first woman to finish, comfortably clear of the HBT duo of Claire Gordon and Kate Jenkins.
