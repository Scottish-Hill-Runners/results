In drizzly conditions, Michael Reid (Moorfoots) led for most of the race to record a comfortable victory in the Seven Hills Race in 1:44:22. Michael's time was just 13 secs slower than he recorded at Durisdeer the day before...nearly 3 hours of racing over the weekend (just one of of several who did the double). First woman was Eilis McKechanie (HBT). 153 finished the race, while another 286 ran in the non-competitive Seven Hills Challenge.

[Details and 2012 Results](http://www.seven-hills.org.uk/)
