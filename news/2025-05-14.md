Goatfell Ferry Update 2 Having assessed the impact of those runners affected, the RO has made the decision to go ahead as planned with the race starting at 12pm. There is still availability on the 6:30 sailing from Troon on Saturday morning. Calmac has advised that there is the option to pre-book the 10:50 sailing and go on standby for the 9:15, but is in no way guaranteed. Alternatively, you take your chances on the day with a walk on ticket.
