title: "Creag Dhubh Results"
date: 2016-08-07
excerpt: "Alasdair McLeod (Shettleston) and Stephanie Provan (Deeside) were the winners at the SHR Championship race at Creag Dhubh on Saturday 6th August."
---

Alasdair McLeod (Shettleston) won Creag Dhubh ahead of Murray Strain (HBT) and James Espie (Deeside) in 2nd & 3rd.

The ladies race was won by Deeside’s Stephanie Provan with Jessica Tullie (Cairngorm Runners) and Clare Whitehead (Deeside) in 2nd & 3 rd.

Full results on the SHR Results tab.
