---
title: "Creag Dhubh Hill Race Results"
date: 2019-08-04
excerpt: "Wins (and a new male record) for Finlay Wild (Lochaber) and Kelli Roberts (Helm Hill) at the Scottish counter for the British Champs race at Creag Dhubh on Saturday 3rd August."
---

Finlay Wild of Lochaber broke John Brooks's 22 year old record in a time of 26:49. Kelli Roberts of Helm Hill Runners was first women in 33:01 beating Jill Stephen of HBT by 12 seconds. Scottish clubs filled 5 of the top 6 places.
