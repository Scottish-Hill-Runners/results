_From Ian Adams:_

The sunshine brought out a good number of runners (68 in total) for the Billy Brooks Memorial. David Rodgers won the men's race with Craig Mitchell close behind him in a sprint to the line. The ladies' race was won by Nicola Meekin with Deeside's Emma O'Shea in second.
