**SHR Committee Positions Available – Convenor & Treasurer**

With Gordon Pryde (Convener) and Brian Brennan (Treasurer) having fulfilled these roles for the last three years, there is an opening for these positions to be filled by SHR members who are keen to join the committee.

The positions will be available when Gordon and Brian relinquish their duties at the SHR AGM at Symington Village Hall after the Tinto Hill race on Saturday 14th November.

Please contact the SHR Secretary, Neil Gilmore (neilgilmore@blueyonder.co.uk) if you are interested.
