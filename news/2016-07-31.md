The fourth (and second short) race in the 2016 SHR Championship is on Saturday 6th August at Creag Dhubh, part of the Newtonmore Highland Games.

Entry forms can be downloaded here -

[Newtonmore Games entry forms](http://c2967182.myzen.co.uk/forms/)

or register on the day. Registration is at the Shinty Pavillion from 12.30 and the race is at 3.15pm.
