title: "Wylie wins Saughhill, 1 June"
date: 2008-06-01
excerpt: "Stephen Wylie (Girvan AAC) won the senior race from Colin Glencorse (Strathearn H) and Alan Cherry (Ayr Seaforth). Girvan AAC's Connor Gracie and Chloe Melville headed the list in the junior race.  '"
---

Stephen Wylie (Girvan AAC) took the senior Saughhill title with Colin Glencorse (Strathearn H) runner-up and Alan Cherry (Ayr Seaforth) third. Don Reid (Westies), now an M50, was the first vet in fourth. There were no women in the race.

Girvan AAC's youngsters turned out in force for the junior event. Under-12 Connor Gracie won it from two under-10s, Nathan Clark and Gareth Powell. Chloe Melville (under-12) was the first girl; under-10 Jenna Browne was second and under-12 Sarah Laird third.
