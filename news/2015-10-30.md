We have over 60 booked for the lunch and prize giving tomorrow after Dumyat Dash, many thanks for your support! Prizes will be awarded for the SHR Championship and for the Long Classics Series. There are changing rooms and showers available at the Centre. Look forward to seeing you all tomorrow! Any last minute queries, please contact Dave Scott 07754 756145.
