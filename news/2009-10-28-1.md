Congratulations to all of you who have completed the requisite 4 from 6 races in order to be able to claim that coveted SHR Championship mug from Ronnie Gallagher. Many of you collected your mug at the weekend, some only minutes after finishing the [Meall a'Bhuachaille](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0594) Race! If you didnt get chance to get your mitts on your mug then contact [Ronnie](mailto: ronnie_gallagher@yahoo.co.uk ) to arrange collection.
