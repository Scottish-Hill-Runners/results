---

Only a small field of runners were up to the challenge of a very rough and demanding Tom na Bat Hill Race. Part of the Tomintoul Highland Games, it has not been held for 3yrs.  The vegetation, particularly the trackless heather, made it hard going.  Wins for Neil Walker (Altrinchan) and Jenny Gillies (Unattached).
