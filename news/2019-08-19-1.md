A big thank you to all that turned up to race.

Many thanks for virtually everyone pre entering, it does make the results so much quicker, easier and more accurate.

The weather held fine with a gentle breeze that kept all the midges at bay.

I think a couple strayed slightly off course slightly but nobody cut short or got lost, always a bonus.

The race was slightly shorter this year due to mega growth of ferns, I hope that didn’t spoil it for anyone.

Many thanks to all the helpers, it couldn’t be done without you

Gary Gutteridge

(Race Organiser)
