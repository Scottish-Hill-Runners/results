**Sunday 9 December 2018 Craig O Barns hill Race and Junior races**

Race starts at 11.30am for kids and juniors, 12 noon for adults, registration at the Polney Saw mill, 0.5mile north of Dunkeld on the old A9, registration from 10.30am

Entries Adult £10 on the day. Juniors and kids 4-18 free. Children under 9 must run with an adult.
