---
title: "Lee Pen Hill Race Report & Results"
date: 2018-07-25
excerpt: "Wins for Scott Fraser (HBT) and Kathleen Henderson at the Lee Pen Hill Race on Saturday 21st July"
---

The Lee Pen Hill Race was won by Scott Fraser of Hunters Bog Trotters in the very quick time of 24:45, slightly ahead of his team mate James Dunn (25:15) who already has had success this season in the Tweed Valley by winning the Peebles Gypsy Glen race in May.

The evergreen Colin Donnelly was third (27:00), having recently returned with an M55 individual silver medal from the World Masters Mountain Running Championships in Slovenia. First Moorfoot home was Andy Cox in 4th in a new PB for race in 27:17 and picked up the first local prize.

First female home was Innerleithen local Kathleen Henderson with a very impressive 32:56, managing to catch the 1st Woman Vet, Gillian Cairns of Penicuik Harriers on the descent (34:16).

Many thanks to Bob Johnson of Gala Harriers, and Walkerburn resident, for sponsoring the Hill Race, as well as finishing 17th in it.
