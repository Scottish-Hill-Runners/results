---
title: "Reminder about kit required for AM and AL races"
date: 2010-06-05
excerpt: "Please remember that full kit is required for **all** AM and AL races, including Bog and Burn events such as Cort-ma Law and Maddy Moss. Further details..."
---

Scottish Hill Runners would like to remind everyone that on **all** category AM and AL races they must carry windproof whole body cover; other body cover appropriate to the weather conditions; map, whistle and compass; and (on long races) emergency food.

There are two races in the Bog and Burn Series which some may not realise are category AM, namely Cort-ma Law (9 June) and Maddy Moss (14 July); so if you're entering these please remember to bring the necessary kit with you.
