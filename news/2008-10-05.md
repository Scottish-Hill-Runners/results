title: "Peachey is the Lord of Aberdeenshire: Bennachie, 5 October"
date: 2008-10-05
excerpt: "Simon Peachey (Carnethy) wins the Bennachie Hill Race to take the Aberdeenshire Hill Series; Sonia Armitage (Cosmics) wins the Race and Ruth MacKenzie (Deeside R) the Series.  Results: '"
---

Simon Peachey (Carnethy HRC) won the Bennachie Hill Race from 130 others; Craig Mitchell (Lochaber AC) was second, a minute behind Peachey, with Kyle Greig (Forres H) less than 10 seconds further back in third.

The first three places in the women's race went to veterans, Sonia Armitage (Cosmics HB) getting the better of Veronique Oldham (Norham) and Ruth MacKenzie (Deeside R).

The Aberdeenshire Hill Series takes runners on successive weekends to three of the region's fine viewpoints - Morven, Cairn William, Bennachie - and is based on the aggregate times for the three races on these hills. Simon Peachey, with a second and two firsts, was the clear winner from Alan Sealy (Deeside R). The women's title went to Ruth MacKenzie with Fiona Dahl (Cosmic HB) the runner-up.

Only one second separated the first two in the Bennachie Junior Race, Cameron Leel (Chapel Cheetas) and Craig Wallace (Aberdeen AAC); Katie Wood (Chapel Cheetas) was the first girl.
