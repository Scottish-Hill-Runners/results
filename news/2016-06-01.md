Route direction for 2016.....Anti-clockwise.

Kit check at registration

The following kit will be the minimum required.....Water/windproof whole body cover, route map, compass and whistle.

All entry money will be donated to the Border Search & Rescue team therefore the prize list will be minimal!

Full details here - [http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0056&System=Metric&Month=6](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0056&System=Metric&Month=6)
