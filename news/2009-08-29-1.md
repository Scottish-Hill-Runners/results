_From organiser Alex King:_

Runners in this year’s Ochil 2000s had to put up with a strong head wind and some wet conditions underfoot, but the cloud had cleared the hills before the race had started.

The race was won by Alasdair Anthony of Ochil in an impressive 2:48:23 just 39 seconds outside the course record; Kenny Richmond of Bellahouston was second and first vet completing it in 2:49:44.

First lady was Iona Robertson of Bellahouston with a time of 3:06:51. The first lady vet was Susannah MacMillan in a time of 3:37:48 - she was third lady overall.

Both Male and Female team prizes went to Ochils.

Full results – including all the split times - will be posted on the [Ochil Hill Runners web site](http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/) hopefully by Friday.
