Start time will be between 6:30pm and 8pm. The race will be run as a time trial format. Runners will have a chip time and the results will be issued when the last runner has crossed the finish line. 

The decision to use this format was taken by The Race Organiser on behalf of East Ayrshire Leisure who put on the race.

Chip timing will be done by ProTay.

Entry via Entry Central  - [https://www.entrycentral.com/event/116829](https://www.entrycentral.com/event/116829)
