**Get aboard the Cademuir Rollercoaster this Sunday!**

Races for U13s to Seniors

10.00am start for 3km race (U13s and older). 10.30am start time for the 6km race (U17s and older). NB Entry on the day only. More info [here](https://www.moorfootrunners.com/about-5-3):

**JHI Selection race**

The 6km race is also the trial race for Scotland selection for the Junior Home International taking place in Ireland in September. Teams of 4 women and 4 men at U17 and U20 will be selected with first 2 across the line in each category guaranteed a place in the team.

**Junior Hill Running League**

It is also the 7th out of 8 races in the Scottish Athletics Junior Hill League (Sponsored by Crieff Hydro Family of Hotels) and, with 10 bonus points for all finishers, this one could prove decisive in many of the categories.
