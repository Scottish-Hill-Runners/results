title: "Thanks from Run4It"
date: 2011-10-04
excerpt: "A message from Ian White regarding Run 4 It's sponsorship of the SHR championship"
---

Ian White writes:

We were delighted to be able to sponsor the 2011 Scottish Championships series. It is another example of us being able to demonstrate one of our core values of ‘’giving something back to the running community’’. We attended all the events in some capacity or other – getting especially wet at Ben Venue! We get great feedback from runners with many of your comments being fed straight back to the guys at Inov-8 and the likes on gear improvements. Thanks again for your help and support. Hopefully see you all at upcoming races.
