Here are the latest championship standings of 2015 after Durisdeer. Anyone who has run in all four races (and there are quite a few) has already earned their famous SHR mug !

There are already some championships that have been decided (at least for the winner). These are the Women's Over 40 Team, which has been won by Fife AC as no-one can catch them now. James Waldie (Carnethy) has retained his title in the Under 23s (for the fourth and last time!), Gibson Fleming (Westerlands) has won the Over 70s .. probably not for the last time! Finally (but by no means least), Jane Kerridge (Deeside) has retained her title in the Over 60s.

The listings are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2015 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2015 - Female.xls) for women.

or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.
