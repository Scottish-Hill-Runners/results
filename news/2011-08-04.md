title: "Run-4-It Scottish Hill Running Championship after Ben Rinnes"
date: 2011-08-04
excerpt: "After a great race at the fifth championship race at Ben Rinnes many of the championships were decided. Also 66 keen people have, so far, earned valuable SHR mugs. If you've only done 3 races, then Ben Venue in October is your last chance to earn a mug (the prize giving do is afterwards in Aberfoyle)."
---

So after Ben Rinnes, only two individual senior categories have still be be decided. These are the Men's Over 40 championship, in which Mark Harris (Fife) is leading, but favourite must be Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston) and the Women's Over 50, in which defending champion Anita Hamilton (Cosmics) is leading, but the favourite, provided she runs the last race, is Gill Irvine (Highland), who had a superb run at Ben Rinnes. Also both Male and Female Under 23s will be decided at Ben Venue.

Both Male teams have been decided, Shettleston have the Seniors, despite a hiccup at Ben Rinnes, while Carnethy have retained the Over 40s. Carnethy are leading the Senior Women's, but favourites are Ochils and in the Women's Over 40s, it looks like Carnethy will take the title, but Fife could retain a share.
