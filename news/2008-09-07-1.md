---
title: "Hat-trick for Fraser at Braemar, 6 September"
date: 2008-09-07
excerpt: "Scott Fraser (Lothian) wins for the third year running...  '"
---

With thanks to Ally Noble:

Scott Fraser (Lothian) won the Morrone Hill Race at the Braemar Gathering for the third year in succession and came close to breaking the long-standing record. Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven) was second, just one second in front of Colin Donnelly (Lochaber).

Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) must have recovered remarkably quickly from her exploits around Mont Blanc the previous weekend, for she was first woman. Mhairi Brown (Kirkcaldy) was second and Ali Hughes (Aberdeen) third.
