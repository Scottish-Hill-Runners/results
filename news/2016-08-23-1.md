On Sunday 28th August there is an uphill only race for junior hill runners born between 1997 and 2003, up Colsnaur, Menstrie, Clackmannanshire.

The race is the trial for the junior home international in September and counts in the Scottish Athletics Junior League. The U20 boys race will be open to seniors.
