The latest championship listings after the Breweries are now available.

[Here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2021 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2021 - Female.xls) for women.

As it is the 4th out of the five races, quite a few of the categories have been decided, at least for first place, if not for the other podium positions. Carnethy HRC have won the Male Senior; the Male Over 40 and the Female Senior Team categories, while Fife AC have won the Female Over 40s team.  
Murdo McLeod (Westerlands) has won the Over 70s; Jane Oliver (Deeside) and Des Crowe (Penicuik) the Over 60s; Jill Stephen (HBT) and Joe Symonds (Shettleston) the Senior titles. The other titles have not been completely decided but generally have a strong favourite.

Due to the late cancellation of Yetholm Shepherds Show Race - another casualty of COVID19 - the championship is the best 3 from 5, although all but Over 60s and 70s must do at least one Long and one Medium, so if you have a query about not all your points being counted, remember you can only count three scores if you include both a medium and a long (except if you are Over 60 !)

Any queries to [Andy Spenceley](mailto:committee10@scottishhillrunners.uk) SHR Statistician
