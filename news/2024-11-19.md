**SCOTTISH HILL-RUNNERS JOURNAL 2024 - CALL FOR SUBMISSIONS**

Another year, another journal, once again Scottish Hill Runners are looking for submissions for the 2024 Journal!

With all the fun, excitement, competition and general tom-foolery of 2024 no doubt there will be plenty of stories to share.

As usual we will be looking for **ARTICLES, REPORTS** and **POETRY** on hill running, hill races, training for the hills, being in the hills, getting off the hills, hill landscapes, hill people and everything in between. The Journal continues it's open-policy in terms of style and content; we want fact, fiction, poetry (and maybe some lies?) in whatever form you fancy.

If you don't fancy writing, we're also looking for **PHOTOS** (candid, staged, Photoshop, you're choice) and any **ARTWORK** you'd like to share. We've had some great images in the past and it would be good to make the journal as vivid as possible.

The deadline for submission will be Friday 17th January. All content to be sent to [james.callender@live.co.uk](mailto:james.callender@live.co.uk) If you have an idea you'd like to discuss please don't hesitate to get in touch.

We look forward to hearing from you!
