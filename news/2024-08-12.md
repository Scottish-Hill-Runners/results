**Long Classics after 6 races**

Latest scores with hopefully 3 more races still to go as per link.

**Alastair Graves** of **Ochils** leads the men and **Rachel Little** of **Insch TRC** leads the women. Plenty still to play for with 3 races left.

13 have completed 4 races to date, and will be eligible to pick-up the infamous LC keyring which are on offer for anyone completing 4 or more races. So still plenty of time for others to complete the series. Next race up is Ochil 2000s on 24th August.
