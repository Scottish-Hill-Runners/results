Race organiser Paul Emsley writes: _Please note that all 250 places at the Goatfell Race have been booked. Since we are relying on a number of no-shows to bring the number down to a more manageable size, we will NOT be operating a waiting list, NOR allowing transfer of entry from one runner to another. Also, we will NOT be accepting entries on the day._
