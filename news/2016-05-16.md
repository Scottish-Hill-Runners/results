The Gypsy Glen Hill race, the second round of the Scottish Borders Hill Race Series takes place on Wednesday 18 May. Organised by the Moorfooot Runners the event starts in Victoria Park in Peebles at 7pm. It climbs 1,000 feet over 2 ½ miles before turning and taking you back into town. There is a flatter section before the sting in the tail so it is a good challenge for seasoned fell runners and those looking to try a hill race for the first time. The race is kindly sponsored by The Freewheelin' Brewery Company. Registration from the Gytes Leisure Centre from 6pm.

There is still plenty of races and time to get a placing in the Scottish Borders Hill Race Series as the best 5 results from 10 count. There was a good turnout at Chapelgill, near Broughton which was the first race of the series in March.
