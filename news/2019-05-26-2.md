---
title: "Glas Tulaichean Uphill Race Results and Report"
date: 2019-05-26
excerpt: "Wins for Robbie Simpson (Deeside Runners) and Virginie Barrand (Metro Aberdeen) at the Glas Tulaichean Uphill Race on Saturday 25th May."
---

Wonderful for a record field of 61 hardy hill runners to turn out for the Glas Tulaichean Uphill Race despite somewhat dreich conditions. The overall winner was Robbie Simpson of Deeside Runners in a rapid time of 34:15, followed by Chris Richards (Salford Harriers) & Tom Owens (Shettleston). Virginie Barrand of Metro Aberdeen was first female home in 46:17, with Sara Henry (Deeside) & Chantel Clark in 2nd & 3rd places. Carnethy men & Deeside ladies took the team prizes.

Daniel Cunningham won the Glas T Toe Tickle with twin siblings Layla & Tommy not far behind.

Special thanks to all the race officials & other helpers who made it a success, in particular to Angie & Beth Collins for registration & Dave & Lyn Hanlon for timekeeping at the summit. Thanks also to the Dalmunzie Estate team for hosting & supporting the race in style.

Well done to everyone who competed. Look forward to seeing you all again next year.

Steve Matthews (race organiser)
