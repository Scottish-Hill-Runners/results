**SHR safety rules and guidelines – feedback please!**

The revised SHR safety [rules](http://www.shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=SHR%20Generic%20Safety%20Rules%20Issue%201%20010714.pdf) and [guidelines](http://www.shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=SHR%20Safety%20Guidelines%20Issue%202%20%20161014.pdf) have now been in use for a full season and apply to all hill races run in Scotland whether insured through SHR or Scottish Athletics.

We would be grateful for feedback on the rules and guidelines from both _**competitors**_ and _**race organisers**_ . Please contact Dave Scott at [committee4@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:committee4@scottishhillrunners.uk) if you have any comments or suggestions for improvements.
