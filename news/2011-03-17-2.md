The scottishathletics Junior Hill Running League starts on Sunday 27 March with the Lomonds of Fife junior race. The League was introduced several years ago to encourage competitive opportunities for junior hillrunners.

Registration opens at midday, at the Purin hill car park, near East Lomond, with the under 12s race starting at 1 pm (Car-park is one mile up the hill midway off the Falkland road A912).

All races start and finish by the car park, and ascend East Lomond, with routes varying according to age. All routes will be marked and marshalled.

Races are as follows:

Under 12 Males and Females 3.2km;

Under14 Males and Females 4.2km;

Under16 Females 4.2km ;

Under16 Males 6.4km,

Under 19 Females 6.4km;

Under 19 Males 8.6km.

More information on this race is [here](http://www.fifeac.co.uk) . Details of the 2011 League are [here]( http://www.shr.uk.com/Juniors.aspx) .
