The Krunces are on the first Tuesday evening of the month from April to September. The nineteenth Series started a week ago with what was actually the 110th race - and the first one that Alastair Leiper has missed.

Robbie Simpson (Deeside) and Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston) kept the Cosmic masses led by Dan Whitehead and new recruit Simon Peachey at bay. The women's race very much went Cosmics' way, though, Lois Noble just holding off Anita Hamilton.
