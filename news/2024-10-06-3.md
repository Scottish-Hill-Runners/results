The Manor Water Hill Race is next Saturday Oct 12. You can sign up on sientries until Friday for £8 or enter on the day £10 cash.

[https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event\_id=14151](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=14151)

Also.... 

This year we're once again asking you to dig out your spare shoes for Shoe Share [https://shoesharemalawi.org/](https://shoesharemalawi.org/), a charity that supports athletes and their communities in Malawi. Please bring gently worn/nearly new running shoes in good condition, clean(ish) and dry, with laces tied together. It costs £2 to send each pair to Malawi so please add a donation here if you can: [https://donate.justgiving.com/charity/zmcp/donation-amount](https://donate.justgiving.com/charity/zmcp/donation-amount)

Look forward to seeing you.
