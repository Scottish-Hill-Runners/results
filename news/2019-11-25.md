Late news but it was never mentioned on the web site and deserves to be. So, congratulations to Colin Donnelly who became the 2019 World Master Over 60 Champion at the WRMA Masters World Championships held at Puglia, (very) southern Italy at the end of September. Another honour to add to his amazing international career.  
Results [here](http://www.wmra.info/files/results/world-masters/2019-Masters-WCh-2019-Results-Ind_All.pdf) and some more details [here](http://www.wmra.info/news/latest-news-from-the-wmra/759-world-masters-mountain-running-titles-won-on-a-hot-day-in-italy).

Not to mention good old **Geoff Howard** taking gold in the V75s. Well done Geoff!
