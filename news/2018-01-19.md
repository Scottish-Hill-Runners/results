It is with sadness that SHR have heard of the death of Tom Bowie.

  
  

Tom was a regular runner at Scottish hill races and he was one of the first members of the Carnegie club when it formed in 1991. Although he started off on the roads, his real interest was running in the hills.

  
  

Latterly Tom suffered health issues which prevented him from running as much as he would have liked and although he was no longer a Carnegie member, he did still keep up with club matters through his friends.

  
  

Sadly he passed away last Saturday evening. His funeral will take place on Tuesday 30th January at Dunfermline Crematorium, at 2.45 pm.

  
  

Thanks to Val Macaulay and Neil McLure at Carnegie for letting us know
