date: 2009-01-02
excerpt: "Scott Fraser won the Dash in a record time of 15:06. Rowena Scott-Bell was first lady in a new record of 19:47. Luke Foss (aged 12) was first in the Gallop (Fun Run).  '"
---

Scott Fraser won the Dash in a record time of 15:06, finishing just 7 seconds ahead of Alasdair Anthony. Rowena Scott-Bell was first lady in a new record of 19:47, finishing 5 seconds ahead of Claire Gordon.

The Greenmantle Gallop Fun Run attracted 23 smilers, their ages ranging from 4 to "over 21". Luke Foss (12) led them home, followed by Karen Maxwell (13) and Lauren Arbuckle (12).
