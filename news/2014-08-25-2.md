Mark Harris has taken the lead in Long Classics Series after 8 races, closely followed by Dan Gay and Stewart Whitlie, although Al Anthony is poised on 4 races. Diane Baum continues to lead the women's competition, having completed every race so far, however Jasmin Paris could still win the series if she completes the final 2 races.

There are 2 more races in the series, the next counter being Two Breweries on Saturday 27th September.
