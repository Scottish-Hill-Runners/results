That’s three autumn races in a row for me in glorious weather – Merrick, Ben Venue and Meall nan Tarmachan. Fifty four runners finished the race with first place going to Sam Alexander of Westies in 51.33. First female was Jennifer Gane in 1.12.07. Thanks to all the marshals and other volunteers.

Dan Watson (Race Organiser)
