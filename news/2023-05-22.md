The Blackford Highland Games race is on Saturday 27th May. This is the second year of running the race.

It is just under 10km and entry to the games gives entry to the race. Race start will be around 3pm ish (usual Highland Games circa time slot).

The male record is 38.36 and the female record is 43.52
