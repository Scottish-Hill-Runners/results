The championship listings after Arrochar Alps are now available [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2019 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2019 - Female.xls) for women.  
or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.

Very early days, but Mr. and Mrs. Fallas are doing quite well, leading the Senior Men's and Senior Women's respectively! Both male team categories are shaping up once again to be a Shettleston v Carnethy battle - both in the senior and vets they have one win and one second place each There was a lack of female teams at Arrochar, so apart from Westerlands being well placed, it is hard to make any predictions.

Any queries to [Andy Spenceley](mailto:committee10@scottishhillrunners.uk) SHR Statistician
