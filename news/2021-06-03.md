Red Moss 2021

Great to see hill runners doing what we love - out racing in the hills again!

The weather was kind and with a record turnout of 151 runners, both the Mens and Womens course records were broken

Thanks to all the race volunteers at start and finish and marshalls on the hills - and Catriona Bruce of Blastrunning for the very efficient chip timing - results below

Thanks also to the local landowners, farmers and Pentland Hills Regional Park for allowing the race to go ahead

Apologies that under existing restrictions there was no get together in the Grey Horse Inn this year and no formal prize giving.

Something to look forward to next year in June 2022!

Angela Mudge for Scottish Athletics handed out some of the East District champs medals to category winners at the finish - other medals will be posted out

**For the Men,**

Andrew Douglas (InverClyde - West District) set a new mens record of 38:48 , taking 2 mins off the previous record

Eoin Lennon (Carnethy HRC) was 2nd ( and East District winner) equalling Euan Brown's previous record of 40:46 set in 2019

Carnethy Hill racing Club won the Men's team prize with close packing at the front , with top 3 counters finishing 2,3 and 5th

**For the Women,**

Megan Crawford (Fife AC) set a new women's record of 47:39 to win the East District Champs

Kirsty Dickson (Carnethy HRC) was 2nd and also beat the previous record from 2016

Full write up from Angela Mudge on the Scottish Athletics website to follow - including all age category winners and teams
