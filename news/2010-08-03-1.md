title: "The Run-4-It SHR Scottish Championships after Dollar"
date: 2010-08-03
excerpt: "Only one race left, the Glenshee 9 next weekend, so if you still need to do a long race to complete the championship you must run it. Meanwhile many of the championship categories have already been decided..."
---

In the male categories, the only champion not yet decided is in the Over 60s, where Tom Scott has to win and hope Martin Hulme finished not better than 3rd. Even though the first place has been decided in all the other classes, there are however many minor positions still to be decided.

In the female categories, it is a similar situation with only one individual class not decided and that is the Senior women which is between Sarah Bryne, Veronique Oldham, Jackie Higginbottom and Catriona Buchanan. Like the men's, most of the minor placings are still up for grabs. Also the Senior womens team is still very open and is between Carnethy, Cosmic and Ochils.

The listings after the first four races are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Scottish Hill Running Champs 2010.xls) (or available from Championships menu on right of screen, where there are also all time lists).
