The spreadsheet with the final SHR 2019 Bog & Burn placings is now available.

Congratulations to Niall McAlinden for the win ahead of Justin Carter and James Bowden.

The female winner was Ella Peters ahead of Alex Berry and Romy Beard.

Congratulations too to the male and female category winners – Justin Carter (M40) and Claire McArthur (F40) , Julian Warman (M50) and Alison Ridyard (F50) and Gordon McCaffrey and Jane Oliver – M60 and F60 respectively.
