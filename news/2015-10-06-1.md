---
title: "Beinn Resipol Race Results"
date: 2015-10-06
excerpt: "Wins for Shettleston's Tom Owens and Shona Robertson in the inaugural Bein Resipol race in Ardnamurchan."
---

There were 55 runners at the inaugural Beinn Resipol Hill Race in beautiful Ardnamurchan. The weather was great for running. Many said that this was one of the toughest and most interesting routes they'd raced all season. Both boggy and technical.

Wins came for Shettleston Harriers in both the men's and women's races. Tom Owens won the men's race in 01:10:57 and Shona Robertson won the women's race in 01:21:23. There was a superb turnout by runners from Lochaber and Shettleston.

Runners enjoyed post race dinner, ale, whisky tasting put on by the Ardnamurchan distillery and folk music. Over £300 was raised for local mental health and well being charity, Ewen's Room.

Next years race will take place on Saturday the 17th of September. The limit for next years race will be 120 runners. Details will be appearing in a short while on [benresrace.wordpress.com](http://benresrace.wordpress.com/). We are looking forward to seeing you there.

David Houston (Race Organiser)
