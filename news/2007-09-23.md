---
title: "HBT (almost) dominate the Two Breweries, 22 September"
date: 2007-09-23
excerpt: "HBT provided six of the first eight finishers - but neither the overall winner (Colin Donnelly, Lochaber AC) nor the first woman (Lucy Colquhoun, Carnethy).  '"
---

Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) won in 2:47:09, nearly ten minutes ahead of Ray Ward (HBT). Five of the next six were Trotters: Alec Keith (third), Dan Gay (fourth), Billy Minto (fifth), Don Naylor (sixth) and Al Hart (eighth). Ali Hubbard (Unatt.) was seventh.

Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) was first woman by the same sort of margin, followed by Ellen Homewood (Westerlands) and Miranda Grant (HBT).
