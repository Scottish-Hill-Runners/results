---
title: "Beinn Resipol Results and Race Report"
date: 2016-09-13
excerpt: "Wins for Robert Simpson (Westies) and Helen Brown (Mynydd Du)"
---

The weather cleared for Saturday and provided excellent running conditions. The race was won by Westerlands’ Robert Simpson in a time of 1:11:33.

Robert - picturing himself being chased down the hill - managed to finish almost 10 minutes ahead of a clump of 5 runners who ran around 1:21-1:22. Second was Lochaber's John Yells in 1:21:16 and third was Bjorn Verduijn of Shettleston Harriers in 1:21:34.

In the Women's race Helen Brown of Mynydd Du won in a time of 1:36:40. Lyndsey Shaw came second in 1:43:15 and CJ MacPhail came third in her first ever hill race with a time of 1:51:15.

This year, pupils from local schools and members of the community were also running in a fun run around a short loop of the route. This was a brilliant wee addition to the event. We had a field of 41 in the hill race and 35 in the fun run.

After the race, runners enjoyed soup, a buffet and a firkin of Glenfinnan Ale. For runners staying the night there was a folk session and an evening buffet.

The race raised £580 for Ardnamurchan mental health and wellbeing charity Ewen's Room.

Thank you to the Real Food café, Ardnamurchan Distillery and photographer Michael MacGregor for donating prizes. And a big thank you to the Sinclair family of Resipole Holiday Park for providing a brilliant venue!
