---
title: "SHR AGM beckons"
date: 2007-09-19
excerpt: "Proposals for the SHR AGM must be with the Secretary by 29th September..."
---

The Scottish Hill Runners' Annual General Meeting will take place, as usual, in Symington sports club, after the prizegiving for the Tinto Race, on Saturday 10th November.

As required by our constitution, AGM papers and proxy voting forms will be distributed by 13th October, and **proposals for tabling at the AGM, and nominations for officers and committee members must be received by the Secretary by Saturday 29th September**.

So come on, now’s your chance to influence the future direction of SHR and hill running in Scotland!
