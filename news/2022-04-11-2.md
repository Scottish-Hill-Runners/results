---
title: "Newtyle Hill Race - Wed 20th April"
date: 2022-04-11
excerpt: "Entry will be on the night with no pre entry."
---

The Newtyle hill race will be on 20 April from The Field, Brae Street, Dunkeld starting at 7pm.

Event registration from 6pm on the night.

Full body cover, plus hat, gloves, must be carried or worn and compass and whistle with map of the route must be carried.

There is no pre-entry. Price on the night £10.
