### The 2024 edition of the Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay is now open for entries at [SI Entries](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=13912)

**New for 2024:**

*   electronic timing and checkpoint control via SportsIdent
*   capacity increased to 50 teams
*   no limit on teams per club
*   shuttle bus between handover points in Glen Lednock to reduce use of cars
*   Post-race curry, soups and home-baking

More info on the [Strathearn Harriers website](https://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/our-races/simon-wake-comrie-hills-relay)
