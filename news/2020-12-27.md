Long-distance archivist Peter Duggan has listed four new routes from Summer 2020 today, these being Keri Wallace's Glen Coe Corbetts Round, Ian Stewart's Cairngorm Parkrun, Jonny Muir's Pentland Hills Round and the established (but strangely never listed) Lochaber Traverse.

While there are still potentially more to add (some big ones from John Fleetwood!) as well as the 'stop press' times for established routes to be fully incorporated into their pages, we believe most of this busy summer's FKT activity now to be documented on this section of the site.
