Angela Mudge, the former world champion mountain runner, and 5 times British hill running champion, is giving a talk about hill running & hill races in the UK and abroad.

This will take place in Tiso Outdoor Experience on Saturday 17th March at 7pm. Tickets are only £10, and are available from Tiso directly.

Tiso will also remain open that evening, and there is a rumour there will be a wee discount on all purchases as well. Plus of course, the obligatory raffle.

The evening is in aid of the Lomond Mountain Rescue Team vehicle appeal. The fund now stands at £23,500, getting ever closer to our £35,000 target. For more information on the work of the Rescue team, see our website: http://www.lomondmrt.org.uk/

If you are free on the 17th it would be great to see you there, to hear Angela talk.

_Andy Birnie, Bellahouston RoadRunners & Lomond Mountain Rescue Team_
