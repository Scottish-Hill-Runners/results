Brian Marshall (HELP) won Glamaig for the eighth time in succession, with a couple of minutes in hand over Alasdair Anthony (Ochil) and another minute over Alan Smith (Deeside).

Emma O'Shea (Deeside) was the first woman, well clear of Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber) and Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT).
