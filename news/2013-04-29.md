---
title: "Hunters Bog Trot"
date: 2013-04-29
excerpt: "Sarah McCormack takes the record at the bog trot.  '"
---

Good conditions for the race with the April showers holding off and the ground not too boggy. Sarah McCormack took down the women's record by 14 seconds despite the course being lengthened slightly from previous editions. Mens record holder Phil Mowbray's DNF meant the Haries pipped their elders for the mens team prize - no such problem for the HBT Trotettes who took the ladies team. After an entertaining prize giving a healthy band of Trotters made for the Kilderkin pub, and stayed there for the rest of the day.
