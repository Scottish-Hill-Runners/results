Sign up via Entry Central - [Criffel Hill Race - EntryCentral](https://www.entrycentral.com/criffel2025)

Runners are reminded that, as usual, they will be required to carry the following standard safety equipment that will be checked at the start of the race:

Water/Wind proof whole-body cover, Map of the route, Compass.

Entry fee is £10- all profits will go to Moffat Mountain Rescue.
