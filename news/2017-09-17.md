title: "Westies 40th Anniversary Race"
date: 2017-09-17
excerpt: "One off race to celebrate Westerlands CCC 40th Anniversary - Saturday 21st October"
---

Registration will be at the Loch Lomand Arms Hotel,G83 8NY

Registration open at 10:30am

Race start 12

Date: Saturday, 21 October 2017

Race Distance: 11 km

Climb: 1100 m

Runners must be 18 or over. Full body cover, whistle, map & compass must be carried, and will be checked at race registration. Navigational experience and skills will be required.

The route will initially climb Beinn Dubh before running along the ridge to true Beinn Dubh summit. Runners will then descend to mid hill and carry on descending until a checkpoint about half way down the ridge. The route then heads steeply down hill to a checkpoint at a river bend.. Runners are then faced with a sting in the tail back to the summit of Beinn Dubh and a fast decent back down the track they climbed at the start.

A short course will also be available which will be an out and back to checkpoint 1 at the summit of Beinn Dubh.
