Yetholm Hill Race Sun 4th June 2017

All entry money will be donated to the Border Search & Rescue team therefore the prize list will be minimal!

Route will be run anti-clockwise and will be unmarked.

The following kit will be the minimum required, kit check at registration.......water/windproof whole body cover, route map, compass and whistle.

Please note NO DOGS accompanying runners.
