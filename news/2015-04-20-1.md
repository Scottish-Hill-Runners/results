Scottish hill runners were competing all across the UK at the weekend.

Races in Scotland included the Hunters Bog Trot, Normans Law, Beinn Ratha and Beinn Lora.

England hosted SHR insured races in the Lake District (Anniversary Waltz and Teenager With Altitude).

And the first British Championship race of 2015 was held in Wales.
