title: "Craig O Barns Race Results and Report"
date: 2018-12-21
excerpt: "Wins for Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) and Marie Entwistle (Deeside Runners) at the inaugural Craig O Barns Race on Sunday 9th December"
---

Stewart Whitlie Male and Marie Entwistle won the first Craig O Barns hill race above Dunkeld on the 9th December. The new route proved very popular although a few people seemed to go slightly wrong near the end of the race..... follow the red and white tape next time! Please put it in the diary for next year! Results attached.

The kids really enjoyed it too with some fine young juniors leading out their parents and apparentyly giving them quite a run for their money. Fergus Bates was first in the Under 9 race in 7:03 over 1Km with Struan Gatherer first in the Under 15 race in 7:17, quite impressive as this was a mile with a hill in!

All participants received a travel mug and lots of christmas gifts as well as mince pies and tea/coffee at the Royal Dunkeld Hotel for the prizegiving. Special thanks to all the marshalls, our sponsers Dunkeld Deli and Polney sawmill for letting us use the car park. Also a mention to Atholl Estates and Forestry Commision for use of the woods especially as there was a recent outbreak of a tree disease called Phytothora which kills larch trees. Lets hope not too much of our valuable landscape is affected.

Best wishes for Christmas and the New Year

Race Organiser

Adrian Davis
