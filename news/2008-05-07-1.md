---
title: "Lennox and Mudge win at Dumyat"
date: 2008-05-07
excerpt: "Jethro Lennox and Angela Mudge were the winners at Dumyat.  '"
---

Jethro Lennox was first back at Dumyat, followed by Al Anthony and Tom Owens. Troubled by sore legs and possibly still feeling the effects of his Three Peaks run, Jethro did just enough to keep the in-form Anthony at bay. Angela Mudge was first woman, ahead of Elke Schmidt, with Catriona Buchanan in third.

On a sunny evening, there were 260 finishers.
