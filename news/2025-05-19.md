title: "Glas Tulaichean Uphill Race Report and Results"
date: 2025-05-19
excerpt: "Wins for Matthew Knowles and Alice Goodall (both Carnethy) at the Las Tulaichean Uphill Race on Saturday 17th May."
---

Another bright & warm day for this perennially sunny race saw just 34 runners toe the start line for the 670m ascent of Glas Tulaichean.  A light breeze chased the pack up with Carnethy’s Matthew Knowles first to reach the summit finish in 36:57, two minutes clear of 2nd placed Cameron Young (Shettleston) and Tom Cunningham of Corstorphine in 3rd.

First female to cross the tape was Alice Goodall in 47:04 to make the double for Carnethy, followed by Hanna McInally of Strathearn and Ochil’s Alison Smithard.  In glorious conditions most runners lingered for a while on top before the jog back down to the farm for tea and prizes.

Our thanks as ever to Dalmunzie Estate for continuing to support this event & especially to all the volunteers for making it run so smoothly: summit timekeepers, registration & all the folk involved in making the teas, sandwiches & cakes that were demolished afterwards.  And thanks too to all the runners for coming along & rewarding their efforts.

Steve Matthews (Dundee Hawkhill Harriers)
