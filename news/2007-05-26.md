Ian Holmes of Bingley won the [Isle of Jura Fell Race](http://www.jurafellrace.org.uk) in 3:18:31 for a record 5th time. Jura new-comer Lloyd Taggart (Dark Peak) finished 2nd in 3:20:45, followed by Jon Wright (Todmorden) 3rd in 3:39:53.

Angela Mudge (Carnethy) was first woman with her 3rd win at Jura finishing in 4:07:55. 2nd and 3rd women were Hazel Jones (Dallam) in 4:33:42 and Sarah Hammond (Tattenhall) in 4:34:31.
