---
title: "Records tumble at Dumyat, 9 May"
date: 2007-05-10
excerpt: "There was a record turnout by far at Dumyat and two course records as well, set by Iain Donnan (Stirling University) and Angela Mudge (Carnethy).  '"
---

The 208 finishers in 2006 was a record for Dumyat; this year the total was 25% up at 263. The men’s and the women’s course records, both set in 2000, were broken as well: Iain Donnan (Stirling University) took 15 seconds off John Brooks’ time and Angela Mudge (Carnethy) 6 seconds off her own.

Other fine performances included Es Tresidder of Carnethy’s fifth place, just a few days after his incredible [traverse of the Cuillin Ridge](http://www.es-on-ice.co.uk/news/show/17), and Jamie Stevenson of Ochils’ ninth, in the lead-up to the **scottish**athletics’ Junior Championships at Ben Lomond [this weekend](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=2790).

The full results are [here](http://scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0036&RaceYear=2007).
