---
title: "The Symonds brothers are second in the OMM, 27/28 October"
date: 2007-10-28
excerpt: "Andy and Joe Symonds were second to the Bollands in the OMM's Elite event."
---

Andy and Joe Symonds finished second to the Bollands in the OMM's Elite event, having been second behind them at the end of the first day. Alasdair Anthony and Ian Wellock were a respectable thirteenth.

Angela Mudge and Jonathan Whitaker were sixth overall, and the first Mixed pair, in the Long Score event, whilst SHR Convenor Dave Cummins and partner Alec Keith finished third.

It was the battle of the Tullie brothers in the B Class. James, with his partner Duncan Coombs, finished 1st and some 24 minutes ahead of brother Douglas and his partner John Rocke, while another brother, Andrew, finished 8th running with Dad, John Tullie, from Teviotdale ! Four Tullies in the top 8 teams !
