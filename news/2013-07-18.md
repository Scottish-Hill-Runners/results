Please note that the Two Inns is now strictly pre entry only. Entry forms can be obtained via lmacewan@hotmail.co.uk. All entries will be checked to ensure appropriate experience has been obtained before entry approved and kit will be checked on the day. Entrants should ensure that they have the navigational skills required for the remote terrain that the race covers.
