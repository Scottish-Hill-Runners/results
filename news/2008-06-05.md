---
title: "Scottish team for Marco Germanetto Memorial Junior International"
date: 2008-06-05
excerpt: "Scotland is sending a team of six to this International in Italy later in the month."
---

The ninth [Marco Germanetto Memorial Junior International](http://www.atleticasusa.it/en_memorial.asp) takes place in Susa (near Turin) on 22 June. It's restricted to those born in 1991 and 1992. Scotland is sending the following team:

**Junior Men**  
Robert Inglis (Lothian)  
Robbie Simpson (Banchory)  
James Waldie (Cornwall)  
Reserve - Jamie Stevenson (Ochils)

**Junior Women**  
Rachel Armitage (Aberdeen)  
Catriona Buchanan (Central)  
Mhairi Inglis (Lothian)  
Reserve - Deborah Stokoe (Westerlands)
