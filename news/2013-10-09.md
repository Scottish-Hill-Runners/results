_From Adrian Stott_

Just to let you know, or remind you if you allready know that Charlie Ramsay, as part of his ongoing task, to further inform folk about this amazing challenge, will be talking with the help of a superb power point presentation on "The Ramsay Round " on Wednesday 6th November at Run and Become, 20 Queensferry Street in Edinburgh.

If you have seen it already, come and be enthralled again! If you have yet to see it and have any affinity at all with the Mountains of Scotland either walking or running.

Full details and how to sign up [here]( http://run.runandbecome.com/event/edinburgh-charlie-ramsay-061113/)
