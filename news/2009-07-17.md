_From organiser Jason Williamson:_

Dan Whitehead pulled 3 seconds off his 2008 record finishing in 32:01 with Simon Peachey (33:32) and Rob Brookes (34:09) (all Cosmics) chasing behind.

Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) brought the women’s record into the 30s finishing in 36:13 with Anita Hamilton (Cosmics) taking the F50 in 44:34 and Tilly Smith (Deeside) taking the F40 in 48:37.

The Cosmic Summer Series is piloting a junior series this year with support in various numbers at the races. Claire Buchan (Ellon AC) finished in 14:20 and Harris Williamson (NA) finished in 16:05.

These events, large or small, can only happen with the support of our volunteers. A big thanks to all the Marshals , Entry Formers and Time Keepers: Anita Hamilton, Gillian Clunas, Jenny Boyd, Ian Searle, Eugenie Verney, Simon Pearce, Fiona Dahl. Dave Wilkinson, Oscar, Clyde, Harris and Daisy Williamson.

Appreciation goes out to the Forestry Commission and Pittodrie Estate for permission to run the race. The prizes were custom-produced Mither Tap Race 2009 jars of Monymusk Honey thanks to Dave Wilkinson.

The final race in the Summer Series is Cheyne Hill on August 20.
