The Creag Dhubh Hill Race, part of the Newtonmore Highland Games, attracted its usual colourful entry, with visitors from Continental Europe (including Holland, hardly the place to train for the hills) setting off along with the local shinty team and several of the usual suspects.

The men's race was won by Colin Donnelly, followed by Manuel Gorman (who supplied the photos) and Stewart Whitlie. In the women's race Fiona Maxwell won from Dawn Scott and Kirsty Bryan-Jones. In the Junior race Declan Murray was first, Sarah Mackenzie second and Bryony Mullender third.
