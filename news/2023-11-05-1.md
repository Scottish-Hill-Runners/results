Wins for Andrew Douglas (Westies) and Naomi Lang (Carnethy) at the Tinto Hill race on Saturday 4th November.

A new male course record 28:53 by Andrew Douglas smashing the 1995 record 29:58.  Luke Fisher in second place also beat the previous record.
