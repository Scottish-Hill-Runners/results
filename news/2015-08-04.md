The final competitor details for the Two Inns race next Saturday are here: [2 Inns Details](info/2 Inns Race Details 2015.pdf).

In addition, here are some important parking details:

Further info on parking in Glencoe

Additional parking available at the rear of The Glencoe Hotel.  
Please park close and sensibly in order to maximise space. 50 cars approx..

Overflow parking at "Glencoe Composting Site". Turn right (if travelling from the south) in Glencoe towards Kinlochleven. The Compost Site entrance is in a small lay-by immediately opposite a bus stop. Please park close and sensibly, and close to the hedges: 20 cars approx..

There is a paved footpath, and then through the woods along the side of the A82 heading back to the National Trust site.
