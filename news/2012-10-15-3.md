A nice warm and sunny run with chances of showers holding off. Race won by current record holder Stephen Rawlinson in 22 minutes 59 seconds but chased strongly by Angela Mudge who broke the record in 25 minutes 13 seconds. Interestingly, this beats the winning time for the Highland Games race too so still much to talk about on this small but sharp hill. _Stephen Rawlinson_
