_From Andy Birnie_

With the recent wet weather the new forest diversion has become waterlogged so is now closed, I know some folks that recce'd the course were concerned about this section. I've negotiated with the forestry commission, so they have agreed to suspend the forestry works on Saturday to allow us to use the Landrover track up through the forest to the open hill. This will make for a fast & furious race!

Updated course map is [here](http://www.bellahoustonroadrunners.co.uk/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/detailed_map2.pdf). The [weather forecast](http://www.bbc.co.uk/weather/2657816) is also looking stunning for Saturday

So really there is no excuse for more folk to come along & take part in the race!

Hope to see you Saturday, Cheers, Andy
