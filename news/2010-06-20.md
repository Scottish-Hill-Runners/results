---
title: "Record turn-out again for the Seven Hills of Edinburgh, 20 June"
date: 2010-06-20
excerpt: "Matt Bell (Edinburgh AC) and Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) win a warm Seven Hills...  '"
---

There was another record turn-out for the Seven Hills of Edinburgh's Race and Challenge, with runners swarming round the drinks stations on a warm morning, a young piper on Blackford Hill to lift them and at least one vuvuzela taking the Challenge...

Matt Bell (Edinburgh AC), runner-up in 2008, went one better this time, reaching Calton Hill ahead of defending champion David Simpson (Corstorphine) and Carnegie's Andrew Wright, who was third for the second year in succession.

The first three women finished within a minute-and-a-half of one another, Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) beating Joanne Thom (Edinburgh AC) and Amanda Henderson (Portobello).
