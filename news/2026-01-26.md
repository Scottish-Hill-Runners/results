**Met Office App & Website** 

There are currently issues affecting forecast wind speeds and gusts for higher-elevation sites (typically above 500?m) on the Met Office website and app, meaning this data is temporarily unavailable.

  
However, the wind information on the [Mountain Area Forecasts](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__weather.metoffice.gov.uk_specialist-2Dforecasts_mountain&d=DwMFaQ&c=euGZstcaTDllvimEN8b7jXrwqOf-v5A_CdpgnVfiiMM&r=FwHi9-QKH3FQRmG-29QEvW5YwNudAjdatr1mvgkncIJ2_KOHJ_R_9nCVmA7jFhVf&m=MrX6Jtbfk98FMnKp9mlJNa4UhssrgY-ejQThA9c_oHI4ePgzRSW78mvM-Dw1coVI&s=t9RgY3LJ8yO6AFVSFh_UIkZdWXiMoo2ODFxbVJv-GK0&e=) is not affected.

So if you are planning on a high level run please check MWIS or the mountain area forecasts
