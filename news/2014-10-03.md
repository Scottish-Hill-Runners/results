Everything is ready for the Ben Venue race on Saturday, in aid of Lomond Mountain Rescue Team. The forestry commission have asked me to remind runners that there is a car parking charge for all their car parks, and please note that the Ben A’an overflow car park is closed at the moment. There is plenty of parking between Ben Venue & Lock Katrine car parks, but they both cost, so save your pennies & car share wherever possible.

Sunny weather is promised for Saturday – as always for Ben Venue – so come and race my favourite wee hill in splendid Autumn colours.

Thanks

Andy Birnie

Bellahouston Road Runners
