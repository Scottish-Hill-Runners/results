Congratulations to Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) and Lizzie Knox (Anster Haddies) for their wins at the Nebit Downfall race on Wednesday 14th September.

This was the final race in the three race Scottish Downhill series and confirmed Iain and Lizzie as the overall male and female winners.

Full results for the Nebit race are on the SHR results tab.
