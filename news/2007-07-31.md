title: "Message from Archie Bennie"
date: 2007-07-31
excerpt: "Following some comments in the last newsletter concerning the records for The Ben Sheann Hill Race, Archie Bennie sent SHR the following letter to set the record straight, as it were..."
---

In the early spring of 2006, because of wind damage to the trees, the Forestry Commission closed the path up Ben Sheann and the hill race looked to be in some danger. By the beginning of June, however, they announced that a new path had been created and required that this path be used for the race. As an alternative to having no race the Committee agreed to this change, although it caused some controversy. This year (2007) an early request to the Commission to reconsider their decision met with full co-operation and two members of Commission staff walked over the course. This has resulted in the race returning this year to its original route. The change of start/finish venue does not alter the length of the race. Archie Bennie, Secretary, Stuc a’ Chroin 5000 Hill Race
