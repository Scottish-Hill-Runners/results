After a closely fought battle between Mark Harris and Al Anthony throughout the 2013 Long Classics, Al finally came out on top to win the series for a 2nd time. Perennial runner up Stewart Whitlie finished 3rd overall.

Charlotte Morgan completed the final 3 races to win the ladies competition, with Lisa Gamble in 2nd place.

44 runners in total completed the series.  
Jon Gay was the only runner to complete all 8 races.
