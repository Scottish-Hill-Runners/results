Final scores in the 2024 series after Carnethy Skyloop race, are as per link below.

[2024 SHR Long Classics after all nine races](Download.aspx?Item=2024SHRLC0Fa.xlsx)

Congratulations again to the series winners, Andrew Douglas of Westies and Catriona Graves of Highland Hill Runners. Well-deserved winners with both winning 4 races.

**Alastair Graves** of **Ochils** and **Luke Taylor** of **Highland Hill Runners** made up the podium positions in the men’s competition. **Nicky Spinks** of **Dark Peak** and **Michelle Hetherinton** of **Carnethy** filled the podium slots for the women. Very impressive given both in the V50 class.

Veteran class winners for men were **Robin Wilkins** of **Ochils** (V40), **Adam Anderson** of **Hartfell** (V50) & **Stewart Whitlie** of **Carnethy** (V60) and for women, **Rachel Little** of **Insch TRC** (V40), **Ros Baxter** of **Deeside** (V50) & **Hazel Dean** of **Ochils** (V60).

We had an impressive 49 people (37 men & 12 women)  who completed 4 or more races in the series this year, which was 16 more than last year.

As usual, if you spot any issues with scores, please let me know and I’ll do my best to resolve asap.
