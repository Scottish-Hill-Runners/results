---
title: "Meall a'Bhuachaille race cancelled for 2008"
date: 2008-09-16
excerpt: "This year's Meall a'Bhuachaille race, scheduled for 22nd November, has been cancelled due to forestry operations at Glenmore."
---

Race organiser Russell McKechnie has confirmed that this year's race, scheduled for 22nd November cannot go ahead due to forestry operations in the forest above Glenmore Lodge. No alternative race or route will be run. The race will however be back on the 2009 race calendar and this year's cancellation is a one-off only.
