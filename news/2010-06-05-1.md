_From race organiser Jane Milne:_

"This year’s Gargunnock Hill Race was run as part of the Gargunnock Show on Saturday as a poignant tribute to the event’s founder David Hay, who sadly died in January of this year.

The 7.5km race was won in soaring temperatures by Peter Devenport, a Bellahouston Road Runner who lives in Aberfoyle. Peter held off the challenge throughout from Ray Ward (Hunters Bog Trotters) and last year’s winner, Central AC’s Lewis Millar. First lady home was well-known local athlete Hazel Dean (Central AC), just 8 seconds ahead of Amanda Henderson (Portobello). The closely-fought under 20’s race was won by Ross Milne, who lives in Gargunnock and is also a member of Central AC.

David Hay was a popular and highly-regarded member of both the local farming and running communities, so Gargunnock Show’s Hill Race provided the perfect opportunity for his friends from both communities to show their respect and appreciation. David’s daughters, Sarah and Becky, joined other Central AC members in helping to organise the event and his wife, Julia, presented the winner with the David Hay Memorial Trophy."
