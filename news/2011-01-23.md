Shettleston men took the top spot at yesterday’s Devil’s Burdens Relays in a time of 2:22:57 while Hunter’s Bog Trotters lifted the ladies team prize with 2:50:36. Excellent running conditions saw a total of 103 teams finish, some opting for an earlier 9:30 start this year.
