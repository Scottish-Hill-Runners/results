The SHR AGM will be on Monday the 9th of December this year. At 7pm.

The meeting will take place online via a Zoom call.

If you wish to attend, then please email Jill Stephen ( [secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk) ) and Jill will arrange to make the Zoom call link available to you. Similarly, if you have any motions or agenda items that you would like to be included at the meeting then please email Jill.
