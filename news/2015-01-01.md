Well done to all that took part in today’s Alternative Aonach Mor Uphill 2015 race. Sadly we had to use alternative route due to weather conditions and the fact the Gondola was not able to run. We used ¾ of the original route and then returned to the start which seemed to work quite well.

131 took part in what turned out to be miserable conditions for a hill race or just to clear the head from new year celebrations. In the men’s race it was Lochaber’s Nick Sedgwick that took 1st position in a time of 25:53. Well done Nick. 2nd place was Lochaber’s, Tom Smith in 26:05 and 3rd place went to Peter Henry, Deeside Runners, in 26:30.

In the women’s, 1st place went to Lochaber’s Diane Baum in 30:02 In 2nd Place was Lochaber’s Sarah MacKenzie in 31:17and in 3rd place was Christina Rankin, Kilbarchan AAC, in 31:20.

Well done to all that took part and massive thanks to organisers, Marshall’s and timekeepers.
