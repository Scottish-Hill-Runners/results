**Cort-ma Law Race**

A fine evening provided perfect conditions for this year’s race. Andrew Fallas (Carnethy) was first home in 47:45 followed by Sam Alexander of Westerlands in 48:56. Edel Mooney (Ochil) was first woman and 7th overall in 56:28, with Catriona Buchanan (also Ochil) in second place in 57:28. Ochil and Westerlands respectively won the women’s and men’s team competitions.

Huge thanks for to everyone for participating so enthusiastically and to Westerlands colleagues and friends for marshalling and organisation.
