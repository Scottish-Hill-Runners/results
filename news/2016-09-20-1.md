Here are the latest standings with five races now completed - just the Pentlands Skyline left (and many people still need to do this to get their mug !).

So far, Deeside have won the Women's Senior team while Shettleston have won the Men's Senior team and Over 40s team categories. Also for the men, Gareth Bryan-Jones has won the Over 70s; Ewan Paterson the Over 60 and Hamish Battle, the Under 23s. For the women, Veronique Oldham has won the Over 40s and Stephanie Provan the Senior Women's. All other categories have still to be decided.

The listings are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2016 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2016 - Female.xls) for women,  
or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.
