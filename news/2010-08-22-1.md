Claire Gordon was the highest placed of the Scots at the World Long Distance Championships, held at Pikes Peak on Saturday. She finished in an impressive 9th place in a high quality international field, and was followed closely by Fiona Maxwell in 12th - Fiona was also the 1st vet. Paul Raistrick was highest placed of the men, finishing in 23rd. Kenny Richmond was 35th and Simon Peachey 37th. Unfortunately the remaining Scot, Iona Robertson suffered badly with the altitude, but bravely carried on through the sickness to still finish. The women were 3rd team, whilst the men were 4th.
