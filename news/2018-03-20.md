_SHR members will know of the recent sad death of Robin Morris and will be aware of his major contribution to the development of Hill Running in Scotland. In sorting through Robin’s estate his wife Roz has come across a number of meticulously filed documents such as events results and minutes of SHR meetings. Roz would like to find good home, perhaps with someone who is interested in the history of Scottish Hill Running or of a particular hill Race. Anyone who may be interested please contact Martin Hyman [martinhyman@clara.co.uk](mailto:martinhyman@clara.co.uk) or contact Roz directly on 0131 447 8846_ [_roz@rlmorris.co.uk_](mailto:roz@rlmorris.co.uk)
