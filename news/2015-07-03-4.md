In an exciting finish, local man Phil Younger disappointed Owen Bass of Deeside by overhauling him in the last 50m to win the race by a couple of seconds. Third was Andrew Gilmour. First lady was Lisa Blackley.

Looks like there was an epic battle for 6th place between Brian, Angus & Trevor!
