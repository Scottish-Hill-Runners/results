**Penchrise Pen Hill Race**

Sunday 20th September 2020

11:30 am start

Venue: Start/finish parking and registration (10am onwards) at Stobs Camp (GR500094), 4 miles south of Hawick on B6399 (turn off at war memorial near Woodfoot Bridge)

Category BS: Distance 8km, Ascent 280m

Entries on line only via entry central.

There will be no on the day entries

Entrants must be aged 16 or over on the day

Run under SHR rules (full kit may be required depending on weather)

Note: Race and parking all taking place on farmland with livestock by kind permission from local farmers so please do not bring dogs

Race Route at [http://gb.mapometer.com/running/route\_3568219.html](http://gb.mapometer.com/running/route_3568219.html)

Records

M – 33:55 Colin Donnelly (Cambuslang) 2018

F – 35:13 Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) 2014
