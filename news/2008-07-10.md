Race report from Andy Kitchin:

"The mist cleared the summit as the course was marked. 35 souls braved the weather window and enjoyed a dry run for the second edition of this fine little race. Patrick Ryan won the junior race whilst Al Anthony prevailed in the senior race, setting a new record at 24:27. First Lady was Joanne Anderson. First Junior was Tom Ryan. Race organised by and to raise funds for the British Orienteering Squad, many thanks to all the runners."
