With a new sponsor attracting an excellent entry the junior white Hill and senior Black Hill Race went ahead in perfect, if muddy running conditions. In the junior White Hill Race 3 border clubs took the hounors with Thomas Otton (Gala Harriers), James Mercer (Teviotdale) and Joe Agnew (Lauderdale) in the first 3 places with Joe Arres (Gala Harriers) first girl. In the senior event 68 runners fought a keenly contested event with Darin Dougal (Moorfoot), Fergus Johnston (Gala) and Mike McGovern(Moorfoot) in the first 3 places. Ronnie Gallagher (4th) and Jonathan Sharpe (32nd) claimed the local prizes from Lauderdale and JogEarlston. Thanks must go to Ross Allan from Milestone Garden and Leisure and Graeme Sutherland (Lauderdale) and David Young (JogEarlston) for organising an excellent event supported by members of both clubs and Earlston Civic week committee with several hill running virgins enjoying their first hill race.
