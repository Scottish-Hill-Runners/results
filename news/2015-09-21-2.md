---
title: "Cairn William Race Results"
date: 2015-09-21
excerpt: "Wins for James Espie (Deeside) and Moira Davie (Forres) at the Cairn Willam race on Saturday 19th September."
---

Conditions were good and James Espie of Deeside produced a fast time of

46:28, just 10s outside of Dan Whitehead's course record. Metro Aberdeen

runners Tom Brian and Jim Tole took 2nd and 3rd.

The ladies race was won by Moira Davie of Forres Harriers in 57:05 just

pipping Veronique Oldham by 2s who took 2nd place and 1st vet. Diana

MacDonald of Highland a Hill Runners was 3rd overall female.

In the 4.5km junior race Clyde Williamson won in 25:26 while in the 2km U12s

Sam Griffin and Beth Urquart took 1st place boy and girl.
