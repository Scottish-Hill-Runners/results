The PUSH is an event that St Columba’s Hospice is organising. The event takes place on the 10th of September in the Pentland hills and gives participant’s unique access to some areas of the Pentlands that are not usually accessible for walkers. The event is made up of three routes the Gold (26.2 miles) Silver (20 miles) and Bronze (14 miles). Participants are able to walk, hike or run the distances at their own pace or competitively. The Push has the aadvantage of taking place right next to Scotland’s capital city which makes it really accessible for everyone taking part.

The entrance fee ranges from £30, £35 or £40 depending on the distance you do and includes three hot meals, a sports massage after the event and a timing wrist band to break down the timings for each section for participant’s doing the race competitively.

More details on the The Pentland Push website [www.pentlandpush.com](http://pentlandpush.com/)
