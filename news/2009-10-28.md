title: "Jethro Lennox wins the OMM, 25 October"
date: 2009-10-28
excerpt: "Jethro Lennox won the OMM Elite class with his partner (and six times Elite winner), Steve Birkinshaw. Doug Tullie and partner John Rocke won the A class.  '"
---

Jethro Lennox won the OMM Elite class with his partner (and six times Elite winner), Steve Birkinshaw. Not bad for someone running their 3rd marathon in 6 weeks! (after Berlin on the road followed by the World Long Distance championship race - the Kaisermarathon). Bruce Poll and Alan Smith had a great weekend too, finishing 6th, whilst Andrea Priestley and partner Heather Dawe were 27th and first female pair, ahead of all the mixed teams.

But Jethro wasnt the first Scottish class winner to finish, that honour went to Doug Tullie (Edinburgh University), who with partner and fellow international orienteer John Rocke won the A Class in impressive style. The Carnethy pairing of Andy Fallas and Sam Hesling were an excellent 3rd.
