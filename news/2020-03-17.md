---
title: "Hill Racing And COVID-19 Infection"
date: 2020-03-17
excerpt: "Important information for all Scottish Hill Race Organisers and Runners"
---

In view of the latest measures announced by the Government to tackle COVID-19 infection(regarding non-essential travel and social interaction) the SHR must now advise all Race Organisers in Scotland to cancel or postpone their races for the foreseeable future.

Decisions regarding the SHR Championship series will need to be taken and further announcements will follow, but the Cioch Mhor race, the first race in the series has already been postponed.
