Craig a Barns Hill Race Sunday 8 December 2019 parking and registration at Polney Sawmill.

Start Juniors 11.15am. Registration from 10.15 Senior race 12 noon

Just a wee call for help on the 8th December for the Craig a Barns hill race. This is being organised for juniors from 3 upwards, adults no age limits so races for everyone. I will need at least 3 marshalls if that is possible so if anyone can help it would be much appreciated. If you are coming with the kids that would be great if they want to run and maybe you could help on the course.All kids get a wee christmas memento.

Entries Free for kids £8 on SI entries £10 on the day.

Remember to bring warm clothes and jackets. There will be soup and christmas cakes at the Duchess Ann hall in Dunkeld afterwards. There are no changing facilities but the hall is used for food and drinks afterwards as well as the prize giving. Let me know if you are going to make it and can help. I'll also do some baking and soup but once again any donations gratefully received. Free accommodation is available if you want to stay over. Thanks

Adrian
