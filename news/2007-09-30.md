Dan Whitehead (Cosmic HB) led more-or-less all the way to win the Cairn William Hill Race in record time. Graeme Bartlett (Forres H) put in a rare appearance to come in second, nine seconds ahead of his team-mate Kyle Greig, with Jason Williamson (Cosmic HB) a further four seconds behind in fourth. Fifth was Keith Varney (Cosmic HB), back from a fine run in the World Masters in Austria.

Sonia Armitage (Cosmic HB) was first woman, finishing four minutes ahead of Deeside's Ruth MacKenzie and clipping over a minute off Helen Johnson's course record.

Cairn William is the second of the three-race Aberdeenshire Hill Series. The first is [Morven](RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0102&Year=2007), last weekend, and the third [Bennachie](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0105&System=Metric&Month=10), on 7 October.
