We are pleased to be launching a new race .It is a  10 k course in the Scottish Borders with  around 200 m of elevation  starting near Ashkirk and looping around forest trails and moorland tracks and including Drinkstone Hill  Trig.

ORGANISED BY TEVIOTDALE HARRIERS 

SHR event under SHR Rules

Category C Short Race under SHR rules 

Registration opens at  10:45am and closes at 11:30 am 

Cost 10k £7    

There will be no on the day entries
