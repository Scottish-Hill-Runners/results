date: 2019-11-01
excerpt: "Unsure about whether you are eligible to receive your SHR Championship mug this year?"
---

It was great that the number of finishers in the SHR Championship was up significantly on recent years. However there are a number of completers who have not joined this year, and are therefore not eligible for a memento. It would also be great if as a finisher in the Championship you also supported SHR by joining. If you are unsure of your membership status please contact the membership secretary (Gordon Pryde) who will confirm if you are a member or not.
