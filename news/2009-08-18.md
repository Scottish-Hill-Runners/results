---
title: "Colin Donnelly and Roxy Bannerman win the spoils at Helmsdale"
date: 2009-08-18
excerpt: "Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC) and Roxy Bannerman (Highland HR) were the victors in Sat's Creag Bun-Ullidh (Helmsdale Highland Games) hill race. Both won comfortably on a bright, but breezy afternoon."
---

Although well over 1hrs drive north of Inverness, the annual hill race at Helmsdale Highland Games attracted a good field of around 40 runners on Saturday. Some competitors were well travelled indeed, including an Eryri couple on holiday from N Wales.

The approx 6km route makes for a fine short course and weather/underfoot conditions were excellent also. Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) led almost from the off, and came home an easy winner in a little over 26 minutes, with Dave Cummins and Ross Bannerman - both Highland HR - 2nd and 3rd respectively, well spaced out behind. It was a good prize winning day for the Bannerman household, as Roxy was a very comfortable winner in the ladies race.

Summary results of top finishers only are available, and are as follows:

Men

1\. Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC) 26.23

2\. Dave Cummins (Highland HR) 27.56

3\. Ross Bannerman (Highland HR) 28.48

4\. Simon Bath (Highland HR) 30.23

Ladies

1\. Roxy Bannerman (Highland HR) 32.59

2\. Alli Thomas (Highland HR) 33.58

3\. Ruth Parkinson (Eryri) 36.52
