Both medium races have now taken place so only people who are already on the list have the opportunity to claim an SHR mug at the end of the season (except if you're Over 60, when different rules apply).

It's an Ochil double so far in the seniors as Alasdair Anthony is leading the Senior men's and Catriona Buchanan the Senior women's.

As you'd expect all categories are completely open, but one disappointment is that no veteran women teams finished at Dusrideer. Quite a few clubs had two veteran runners, but none had three. this means that only the three clubs that completed a team at Clachnaben can decide the championship. It just shows that even the slowest runners in a club can be vital to make up a team and earn valuable points.

The listings after Durisdeer are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Scottish Hill Running Champs 2012.xls) (or available from Championships menu on right of screen, where there are also all time lists for those interested in the stats).
