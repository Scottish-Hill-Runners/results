Dear Hill Runners

At Venture Trust, we know you love getting into the hills and a bit of competition and perhaps you could consider supporting the Venture Trust's “Bag a Munro” summer campaign. 

[Venture Trust](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/www.venturetrust.org.uk/__;!!O7V3aRRsHkZJLA!B9L1B0dCZiWkPrFsS5NeUgYe3Ylu8XBiHPXhVq_3EFxqe29uz6gvNGjsRIO8wc5zQbXRMJ9WILb8MYA5a7pAlOS7T8o0$) work with people who are furthest from opportunity, struggling with complex life issues. They support their participants through intensive personal development programmes in the outdoors that don't just change lives, but save lives. 

You can see the impact of their work in [Island of Dreams](https://urldefense.com/v3/__https:/drive.google.com/file/d/1IGhhFBnbTeQl709GPYLxKzxQ8IMPbRQ9/view__;!!O7V3aRRsHkZJLA!B9L1B0dCZiWkPrFsS5NeUgYe3Ylu8XBiHPXhVq_3EFxqe29uz6gvNGjsRIO8wc5zQbXRMJ9WILb8MYA5a7pAlMOKeN2r$) which premiered at the Fort William Mountain Festival this year. It's moving stuff.

So this August we want to encourage you to get out there and bag as many Munros as you can for a great cause. Let’s get a bit of competition going between clubs and see who can bag the most Munros  -  And who can raise the most cash to help people who are far less fortunate than us to benefit from being in Scotland's wilder places. 

Venture Trust

Full details on the link below ...Get Bagging!
