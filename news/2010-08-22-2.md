The 13-mile race was won by Chris Scott-Park (Unatt) from Graham Laing (Inverness H) and Simon Axon (HBT). Megan Macrae (HHR) was first woman in seventh overall and M80 Ted Williams (Unatt) 85th.

Matt Sullivan (Shettleston H) finished the 25-miler 10 minutes ahead of Alec Keith (HBT) with Ross Bannerman (HHR) a further 15 minutes behind in third. The first woman was Deeside's Sarah Dunn.
