If anyone can offer to come out to Mull to help out with the Scottish Island Peaks Race (SIPR) it would be very much appreciated. The action is all in Salen, starts early Friday afternoon and running through the night. Usual setting up, taking it down again, kit checking, keeping track of the teams and giving each other a chance for some kip. We also need help putting out and taking in course markers, so if you want a recce in anticipation of doing the race in future years, here’s a good opportunity. Reasonable expenses covered.

If you need to know more then just ask.

Thanks, hope to see you on Mull,
