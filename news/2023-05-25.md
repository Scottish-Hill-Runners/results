Please note the following points from the Race Organiser:

*   The Forestry Commission car park by the gasworks is currently closed. Could competitors please park at Clydebank Crematorium, which is c10 mins walk from the start/finish. Or, better yet, Kilpatrick train station is 5 mins walk from the start line!
*   Due to lambing the route has been changed slightly, runners will now descend directly from the Slacks trig point to the cattle-grid on the road. Please see map below.
*   Depending on the weather, full race kit may be required: No Kit = No Race!
*   Unfortunately there will be no toilets at the start of the race. 
*   Entry is £5, cash only.
