---
title: "Simpson dashes to his third win in a row at El-Brim-Ick, 10 January"
date: 2009-01-10
excerpt: "Robbie Simpson's fine form continues, Veronique Oldham is first woman, and Ewan McWilliam and Frances Wright are the top juniors.  '"
---

Robbie Simpson (Banchory) won his third hill race in a row since Christmas, this time taking the El-Brim-Ick Dash from a record field that included Dan Whitehead (Cosmics), Craig Mitchell (Lochaber) and Simon Peachey (Carnethy). Veronique Oldham (Aberdeen) was the first woman, ahead of Aberdeen University's Moira Davie and Cosmics' Anita Hamilton, who broke the F50 record.

There was also a record turn-out for the junior race, which was won by Ewan McWilliam (U11, Banchory Primary), with Frances Wright (U14, Banchory Academy) the first girl.

Race organiser Ewen Rennie says: "Thanks to Forest Enterprise, Aberdeen City Council and the Agricultural College for access and to Denise Wright, Bob Elder, Fiona McDonald, John Colegrave, Shelley Farrar and Rob Brookes for help on the day. Ground still frozen but temperature just above freezing but strong wind."
