**ROUND 4 OF THE SCOTTISH ATHLETICS JUNIOR HILL RUNNING LEAGUE**

This is also the Scottish Athletics National Championship with medals available to U13s, U15s, U17s and U20s.

And if that wasn't enough of an incentive, there is a bonus of 10 league points for completing this race so the winner scores, 35, 2nd gets 30 etc. Could be crucial when we get to the end of the season.

More info including race route and profile can be found on the SA website: [https://scottishathletics.sportserve.net/pages/event/athletics/athleticsbookingdetails.aspx?id=13582](https://scottishathletics.sportserve.net/pages/event/athletics/athleticsbookingdetails.aspx?id=13582)

_If_ _you are planning to race, the organisers would really appreciate it if you could get your entries in as soon as possible. It's a big help with planning the event if they have an idea in advance of how many to expect in each category._
