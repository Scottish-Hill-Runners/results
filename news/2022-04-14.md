At long last it looks like we're back to a normal championship and so below are the listings after the first race at Clachnaben. Numbers were down on the day, affected by COVID and also clashing with a British Championship race in Northern Ireland, but still a decent turnout.

There should be no surprises as people know where they finished at Clachnaben. There are however, on a trial basis, some new categories, so if I've missed any Over 55s or Over 65s, please let me know.

The teams might be more of interest. Highland HR leading the senior women and Ochil HR the women O/40s, while Carnethy HR, starting where they left off have taken the pole position in both the male team categories.

Listings are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2022 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2022 - Female.xls) for women.
