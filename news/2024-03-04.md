There will be a Highland Boundary Series Championship this year.

This will involve the best 3 results from the 4 races, Birnam, Deuchary, Newtyle and Craig o Barns. There will also be a small prize everyone completing the 4 races. 

Race Entry via Protay

[https://protay.co.uk/birnam24/](https://protay.co.uk/birnam24/)

Seniors: £12 or £15 on the day - please register using the form if you intend to pay on the day.

Juniors: £3 - no surchage for pay the day, however please register

[https://protay.co.uk/newtyle2024/](https://protay.co.uk/newtyle2024/)

£12 or £15 on the day - please register using the form if you intend to pay on the day.

[https://protay.co.uk/deuchary24/](https://protay.co.uk/deuchary24/)

Seniors: £12 or £15 on the day - please register using the form if you intend to pay on the day.

Early start choice for participants who expect to take 2-2.5 hours.
