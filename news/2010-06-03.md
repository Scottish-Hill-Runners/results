date: 2010-06-03
excerpt: "Races this Saturday include Glas Tulaichean - one of only a handful of uphill-only races in the calendar and which doubles as the trial race for the World Masters - and the rescheduled Scolty and Scolty Junior events........."
---

Runners are reminded that Saturday’s uphill race at [Glas Tulaichean](http://shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0054) doubles as the trial race for the World Masters championship in Poland. The first runner to finish in each of the following age categories will be entitled to wear a Scottish vest and shorts in the World event:

Women 40, 45 and 50

  
Men 40, 45, 50, 55 and 60

Athletes are also reminded that to be eligible they must be current members of scottishathletics, and that the World Masters championship is an open event which they must enter themselves.

Please also note that whilst M and W35s can enter the World Masters event itself, they are excluded from this scottishathletics award scheme, after the IAAF's decision that the first band of Masters revert back to 40 from 35. This supersedes earlier selection notices.

Also taking place on Saturday (and not Sunday as in the printed calendar) are the [Scolty](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0049&System=Metric&Month=6) and [Scolty Junior](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0511&System=Metric&Month=6) races, with the junior event not only being a scottishathletics [Junior League](http://shr.uk.com/Juniors.aspx) race, but also a selection race for the upcoming [WMRA Youth Challenge](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0581&System=Metric&Month=6) .

Saturday is a busy day, with other events being the [Loch Lochy Munros](http://shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0572&System=Metric&Month=6) \- fourth race in the SHR Long Classics Series - and the Gargunnock race near Stirling.
