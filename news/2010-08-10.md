---

The fine if somewhat humid weather attracted a good crowd of runners - some regulars, some visitors - to the Creag Dhubh Hill Race (part of Newtonmore's Highland Games).

Hector Haines (EUHH) added to his recent run of good results with clear win from Tom Smith (Lochaber) and Fife AC junior Adam Harris. Laggan's Cathy Slimon was the first woman to finish, ahead of Megan Mowbray (HBT).
