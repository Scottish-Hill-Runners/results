title: "Wellock fastest sinker down Caerketton, 19 September"
date: 2007-09-19
excerpt: "Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) broke the course record and held it for 12 seconds before Ian Wellock (HBT) took it from him. Debbie MacDonald (HBT) was the fastest woman - not counting Helen Rogers (Westerlands), who broke her own record for the junior course.  '"
---

Alasdair Anthony (Ochil) was first away from the summit of Caerketton into the gathering gloom below, followed at 15-second intervals by Ian Wellock (HBT) and 30 others. Anthony took 9 seconds off the record set by Bruce Smith (Carnethy) last year - only to lose his new crown to Wellock, who finished 12 seconds behind him. Steven Fallon (Carnethy) came third overall and first M40, equalling Smith's old record. Debbie MacDonald (HBT) was the first woman, followed by Cali Ingham (Carnethy).

A few minutes earlier Helen Rogers (Westerlands) had taken three seconds of her own record for the junior course. There were eight in this race, from four families.

This is a fun event and everyone won a prize - except, we suspect, the race organiser Mark Johnston (Carnethy).
