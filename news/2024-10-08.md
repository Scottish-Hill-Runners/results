Congratulations to Carnethy HRC senior men’s team who have won this years British Fell & Hill Running Team Championships. The team results were calculated using the team result from the Short and Long Championship events – Blisco Dash and Peris Horseshoe. In the vets team championship Carnethy’s men finished 2nd  in the V40 category and 3rd in the V50 category.
