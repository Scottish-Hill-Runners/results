There is a slight change to the Dumyat Dash route this year with a start in the park to spread the field out before the first single track climb. An extra 600m has also been added to the course to provide a more testing climb.

Graham Gristwood has produced a detailed map and it is available on the Dumyat Dash website here - [http://www.thelawbreaker.moonfruit.com/#/dumyat-dash/4546712790](http://www.thelawbreaker.moonfruit.com/#/dumyat-dash/4546712790)

This is a testing race, especially with uncertain late October Scottish weather, over difficult terrain. Runners should be fully aware of the SHR safety rules and guidelines and be prepared to take guidance from marshals on the course.

[http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Safety.aspx](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Safety.aspx)
