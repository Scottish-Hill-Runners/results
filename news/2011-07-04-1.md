_From Organiser Dave Hanlon:_

Colin Russell of Cosmics outsprinted the Hawk's Paul Leonard (1st Vet) in a thrilling finish to the first White Tops race on the new route. Alison McGill was 1st female in 11th place overall. The Hawks took the team prize and Fife AC ladies, the female team prize.

Numbers were less than expected probably due to the heavy scedule of races in the same week. I got good feedback on the new route though, which is encouraging for the future.
