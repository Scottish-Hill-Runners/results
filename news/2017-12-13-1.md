The Kirk Craigs Christmas Cracker race is on Saturday 23rd December.

Race start is at 11.000am with registration at Centenary Hall, Hamilton Street, Tillicoultry.

The race proceeds will be donated to Play Alloa, a charity who provide play and social opportunities for children and young adults with disabilities and additional support needs across Clackmannanshire and beyond. There will also be a tin for donations at registration.
