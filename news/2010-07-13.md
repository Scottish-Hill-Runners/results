_From Ed Rooney:_

Earlston Civic Week Black Hill race saw a good turnout despite the less than promising weather. In the end conditions were ideal for racing - cool and cloudy, and with a brisk wind waiting for runners at the top of the hill. Graham Murdoch (Gala Harriers) took first, with Georgia Nesbitt (also of Gala) first lady. Additional prizes went to Neil Craig (first runner from the sponsoring club), and Jonathan Sharp (first local). Eleven juniors competed in the White Hill race, which was won by Adam Craig (Lauderdale).
