For any members who have completed the SHR championship series this year, mugs will be available tomorrow at the prize giving. In addition we will be giving away free of charge branded SHR goods (beanies, shoes bags, shirts) on a first come first served basis to members (see

[http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/NewsItems.aspx?NewsItemID=1994](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/NewsItems.aspx?NewsItemID=1994)

for details of what is available). Anyone wanting alcohol or drink other than water is welcome to bring their own for the meal tomorrow!
