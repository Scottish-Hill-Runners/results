Cool breezy weather at Ben Sheann kept the normally ferocious midges largely at bay.

Prasad Prasad and recorded another win, ahead of Jamie Stevenson and Graeme Campbell. Steven Fallon was 1st V40 in 4th place.

Angela Mudge was first lady, thereby essentially retaining her Bog and Burn title, barring an unknown female runner winning the final 4 races, second was Ellen Homewood who moves into 2nd place in the ladies Bog and Burn league.
