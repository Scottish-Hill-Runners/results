**SHR Subscriptions**

As a reminder to all SHR members who may have inadvertently forgotten to pay their annual membership fees for 2017, and wish to retain membership, could you now arrange for payment of these overdue subscriptions? Anyone renewing will still receive a 2016 Journal and any member who finishes the SHR Championship will also receive a memento for completing. As in previous years' any runner who completes the Championship and is not a member of SHR will not be entitled to a memento.

If you are unsure if you have renewed please contact the Membership Secretary (gordonpryde@talktalk.net) and ask for clarification regarding your membership status. The details on how to renew are given on the website at the option; About SHR (Join the SHR).
