Finlay Wild (Lochaber) won the Ben Nevis race (Scottish counter for the British Championship this year) and Victoria Wilkinson (Bingley Harriers) created a new female record for the race. 1hr 43 mins and 1 sec, breaking Pauline Haworth's previous record of 01:43:25 which had stood since 1984.
