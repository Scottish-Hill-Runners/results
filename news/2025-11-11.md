SHR Championship 2026

The six races for the 2026 SHR Championship have been decided upon.

The two longs will be at Ben Chonzie (18th April) and the Two Breweries (26th September). The mediums will be at Durisdeer (30th May) and Meall a'Bhuachaille with a provisional date of 24th October, but note that this is to be confirmed. And the two shorts will be at Glamaig (11th July) and a "one-off Saturday race" at the Law Breaker (20th June).

Ben Chonzie (18th April)

Durisdeer (30th May)

The Law Breaker (20th June)

Glamaig (11th July)

Two Breweries (26th September)

Meall a'Bhuachaille (24th October TBC)
