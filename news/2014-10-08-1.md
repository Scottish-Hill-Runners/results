---
title: "Ben Venue Race Report & Results"
date: 2014-10-08
excerpt: "Al Anthony (Ochils) and Megan Mowbray (HBT) won at Ben Venue on Saturday 4th October"
---

A field of 39 runners basked in the autumn sunshine on Ben Venue on Saturday. It turned out to be a tight race, with Al Anthony (Ochils) finally beating Kenny Richmond (Shettleston), in a time of 1:17, after exchanging the lead several times over the descent.

The race has 4 compulsory checkpoints, with most runners taking the safe route along the ridge, but the more adventurous ones taking a direct line across the corrie. This direct approach helped Al & Kenny hold off third placed Andrew Gilmore, so we’ll see what line Andrew takes next year.

First lady was Megan Mowbray (HBT) in a time of 1:33, and the first team were Carnethy (Andrew & Iain Gilmore, backed up by Alec Erskine).

Nobody got too badly lost, and there were only a few minor scrapes, so everyone was happy. The runners tried to finish all the sandwiches & cakes but failed miserably. And on behalf of the Lomond Mountain Rescue Team, many thanks for the £300 raised on the day.

Andy Birnie
