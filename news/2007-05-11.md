Entries are now open for the re-instated Arrochar Alps race, which traverses the 4 Munros of Bens Vorlich, Vane, Ime and Narnain. The race was last held back in 1992, albeit on a shortened course due to bad weather.

Please enter online at [www.entrycentral.com](http://www.entrycentral.com/index.php?raceID=100193). There is a limit of 200 runners.
