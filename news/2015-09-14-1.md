Two Breweries Race Ceilidh - Dancing at Broughton village hall 8-11pm on Saturday 26th September 2015  
  
We are having a band and some food for runners, helpers etc. after the race prize- giving, to dance the night, and my responsibilities, away.  
  
Plan is for beer and soup as normal, do the prize giving, then have some more beer and pizza before dancing to a ceilidh trio from 8-11pm. It would be good if folks could help with the tidy up afterwards too?  
  
I'll provide some food, beer and wine, but the more copious among you (Dick, Mannie etc...) might want to supplement supplies.  
  
No charge - just a load of friends having a good time.  
  
  
Peter Baxter

(Race Organiser)
