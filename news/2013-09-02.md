The inaugural downhill series got under way last Wednesday night with a rare dead heat between Luke Arnott of Westies and Chris Longstaff of Carnethy. First lady home was Eilidh Wardlaw of HBT, who now has the honour of hosting the second race in the series, the Nebit Downfall, this Wednesday night.
