**Marco Germanetto Junior International Hill Race**, Italy

Jessica Martin was third in the girls' race, and so was the Scottish team. The boys finished fourth equal, with Matthew Gillespie their first counter in seventh.

**Lairig Ghru Race**

Another day, another 'long' record! Paul Raistrick (Highland HR) took getting on for three minutes off the mark set by Don Naylor last year, and in doing so won the race by over half-an-hour (surely one of the biggest margins of the year). Andrew Brierley (Fife AC) was runner-up (as he was last year) with his club-mate John Kay third. Helen Johnson (Deeside) was first lady, followed by Gala's Rachel Fagan.
