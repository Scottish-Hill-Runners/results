_From Alec Keith_

This biennial 2 day mountain marathon was held around Glen Dye, on the (very) heathery hills of Clachnaben and Mount Battock. Organised by Grampian Orienteers, the event has a brief orienteering twist each day to create grief for the hillrunners. hopefully running in deep heather caused the orienteers a reciprocal degree of pain.

The event was open to pairs and solos; the organisers transported all overnight kit to the midway camp at Charr bothy where there was a marquee, hot water, food, beer and conviviality; the weather was as perfect as one could hope for in November, crisp sunshine alternating with a crisp overnight frost. The A course was convincingly won by Murray Strain, with Andy Kitchin 2nd, Alec Keith 3rd and Tessa Hill 1st Lady; the B was won by.Tim and Jack Gomersall, Ciara Largey was 2nd (and 1st Lady) and Robert Hickling was 3rd; Malcolm Rudge won the C with Mark Stockton 2nd, Sam Gomersall 3rd and Sarah Dunn 1st Lady.

More details and results can be found [here.](http://grampoc.com/2012/11/17/grampian-mountain-challenge-2012-glen-dye-results/#comment-108 )
