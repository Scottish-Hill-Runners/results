Race date for Fyrish Hill Race will be on Saturday 28th May.

This is different from the date in the printed calendar which is down as Sat 11 June. The date has been changed in order to co-incide with the Evanton Gala Day.

Registration and race start/finish at Evanton Football Pitch.

Mixture of forest roads and steep hillside to Fyrish Monument with a fast descent mainly on forest singletrack back to football pitch. The distance is 13km with 540m ascent. Category BM.

Race co-incides with Evanton Gala Day and catering will be provided by RNLI. Various stalls and activities for kids being held at the football pitch from 1pm.

There will also be a junior race of approximately 5km.

Entry on the day - £5 adults and £1 for kids.
