After four races, the contenders for the various categories are looking clearer but it is only in two categories that the leader is now unbeatable, these being the Senior Women's Team, which Westerlands have completely dominated and have now won, and the Male Over 60s which Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) has won in the minimum number of races.

But more importantly, with four races taken place, anyone who has completed all four will have already earned a valuable SHR mug !

The 2024 tables after four races are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2024 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2024 - Female.xls) for women.
