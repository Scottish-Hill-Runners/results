The latest championship listings after Ben Rinnes, the first long race of the series, are now available. With four races having taken place of all three distances, quite a few people have already earned a valuable SHR mug and some of the categories look very decided (some also very open!), but even where the winner has been decided, other podium positions are still up for grabs.

The runners who have managed to come top with the minimum number of races are : Holly Page (Carnethy) who has won both the Senior Women and the Under 23 Women; Andy Fallas (Carnethy), the Over 40 Men; Colin Donnelly (Cambuslang), both the Over 55 Men and the Over 60 Men and Murdo MacLeod (Westerlands) has taken the Over 70 Men. None of the other categories, including the teams, are 100% decided, even if some have very strong favourites.
