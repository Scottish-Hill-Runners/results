A good time was had by all at the SHR do and prizegiving in Crianlarich over the weekend. An excellent prizegiving, expertly stage-managed by the inimitable Ronnie Gallagher, kicked off the Saturday night in style. Many congratulations to all the worthy prizewinners who got their just recognition for fine performances throughout the season on the hills. (No thanks to those who forgot to return their trophies, however, please dont forget next time).

There followed a very enjoyable meal in the youth hostel and a foot-stomping ceilidh in the village hall where much talent was on display (in the case of those wearing kilts - Des and John, you know it was you - perhaps a little too much talent!)

Most folk managed to survive the night and get out on the hills next day in glorious weather, a fitting way to round off a great weekend. Many thanks to the combined talents of Ronnie, Louise, Jamie and Dave for making it happen.
