---
title: "Whitehead retains Ben Aigan title, 17 June"
date: 2009-06-19
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) wins for the second year in a row...  '"
---

Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) was a clear winner of the Ben Aigan race for the second year in a row. He was followed by Colin Green (Moray RR), Jason Williamson (Cosmics) and then the first M40, Alan Reid (Peterhead).

Lydia Davis (Moray RR) took the women's first prize with Anni Johnstone (Forres H) second.
