We are very sorry to inform you that the Crags path in Callander remains  
closed, so we are unable to organise our June race.  
  
We will keep you informed regarding the next one.  
  
In the meantime can we suggest you check out the Skidaddle website for  
details of races in the local area., including a delightful though  
challenging 10k this Saturday (9th May) taking in the lower crags path.
