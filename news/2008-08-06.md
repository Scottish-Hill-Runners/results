---
title: "Donnelly pips Marshall at Creag Dhubh, 2 August"
date: 2008-08-06
excerpt: "Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) wins his third race in eight days...  '"
---

Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) beat Brian Marshall (HELP) by eight seconds to win his third race at a Highland Games in eight days. Stanley Charles (Clwydian) was third.

The women's race was an even closer-run affair, Claire Gordon (HBT) winning in the same time as second-placed Val Houston (Westies), with Carnethy's Jacqui Higginbottom just ten seconds behind them in third.
