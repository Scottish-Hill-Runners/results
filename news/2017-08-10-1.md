Strathpeffer Games Race – SHR Championship Counter

Registration Details

The Strathpeffer Games Race is on Saturday 12th August.

Entries are being taken in the cricket pavilion from 11.30. Race start 1.30. The entry fees are the admission fees to the games. There are no separate entry fees for the hill race.

If anyone wants to do any track races after the race and are not members of the SGA they have to take out a day membership.

This does NOT apply to the hill race.

The tug o war has been thrown open this year so if any clubs want to put in a team they could do so.
