**Get a Grip Downhill Series**

Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) wrestled the title from his brother Andrew by victory in the first two races of the series, the Nebit & Bishop downhills. Iain unfortunately twisted his ankle in the final race, the Caerketton Doonhiller, where Andrew secured the victory and second overall in the series. Gary Fraser (Ochils), a real downhill specialist, was dominant in the vets and David Elder (Lomonds) showing great promise in finishing first junior and 5 th overall. Steven Fallon (Carnethy) was first V50, with the evergreen Davie Duncan (Ochils) first V60, though Brian Brennan (Westerlands) may be snapping at his heels in the not too distant future!

Fiona Kelsall (Ochils) managed to defend her title by pipping Lizzie Knox (Anster Haddies) on points after the final race. Lizzie had been heading the series after two wins but Fiona’s Caerketton win was just enough to clinch it in a dramatic finale. Lizzie’s sister Rosie secured third in the series.

The overall series winners will receive their prizes at the SHR lunch after the Dumyat Dash race. Well done to all those who completed the series.

Mark Johnston
