This year we are trying a new format for the SHR Championship prize giving and “do”. Numbers have been falling the last year or two so we thought we would try something different.

We are laying on a lunchtime meal at the Dumyat Centre, Menstrie, immediately after the Dumyat Dash race on Saturday 31st October. This will be followed by an after lunch speaker and the prize giving. Tickets available in due course, but please put this date in your diary!!!
