---
title: "Highland Hill Runners clean up at Fyrish, 28 June"
date: 2008-06-28
excerpt: "Highland Hill Runners take most of the prizes.  '"
---

Highland Hill Runners filled the first three places, in the vests of Graeme Campbell followed by Henry Blake and Ross Bannerman. They also took 1-2 in the women's race, with Mari Todd eighth overall and Roxy Bannerman eleventh.

David Gallie (Inverness H) was fourth overall and first M40; Angela Phillip (Forfar RR) was third woman and Julie Wilson (Inverness H) the first F40.
