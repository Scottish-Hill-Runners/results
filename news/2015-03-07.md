For safety I have changed the route slightly and the finish will be at the National Trust Visitor Centre in Glencoe. This means that runners avoid the A82 crossing and the last section is a lot more pleasant.

I am also planning to bus runners round to the start and am just booking buses and finalising prices. The entry fee will have to increase to cover this and still allow a donation to both Glencoe and Oban Mountain Rescue Teams who provide the marshals on the hill. I don't expect that it will be more than £16.

Race entry is not open yet so the old entry forms won't be accepted. I know folk are keen to get their Championship entries sorted so I will endeavor to get the SIEntries set up by the end of March. Entries will be via SIEntries and limited to 120.

Wishing everyone an excellent 2015 racing season.

Linda
