Four Scots - Alex Hendry (Central AC), Scott McDonald (also Central AC), Robbie Simpson (Deeside R) and Joe Symonds (HBT) - have been selected for the GB&NI team for next month's inaugural World Mountain Running Championships in Italy. (These Championships replace the World Trophy, for which the home countries each entered a team.)

There's more information [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=4443).

Congratulations to all four!
