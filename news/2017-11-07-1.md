Deeside Runners Night Hill Race Series:

Cnoc Dubh: Thursday December 14th 2017: Riverside Cottage Café, Cambus O’May.  
  
Dess Woods: Friday January 26th 2018: Deeside Activity Park, Dess, Aboyne.  
  
Thursday February 22nd 2018: Potarch Inn, Kincardine O’Neil.  
  
Each race is about 8km long with 200m of climbing. They start at 7.30pm and cost £2 to enter. Please register in advance at [http://www.deesiderunners.com/night-race-series/](http://www.deesiderunners.com/night-race-series/) where there is lots more information.
