Conditions were good for the Craig O Barns race from Polney Loch, Dunkeld on 6 October. The winner was Ralph McInally in a time of 28 minutes 17 seconds.

The first lady was Anna Crawford in 32 minutes and 54 seconds. The junior under 11 race was won by Arran Davis in a time of 7 minutes 17 seconds with Finlay McInally in 8 minutes 24 seconds. A few items were left over at the finish. Please contact me to retrieve them please.
