**Isle of Jura Fell Race**

Saturday 25th May saw 251 runners start the 42nd Isle of Jura Fell Race. After a cloudy start, the sun came out and the views from the Paps were spectacular. Congratulations to overall winners Jack Wright (Ambleside AC, 3:18:58) and Catriona Graves (Highland Hill Runners, 4:01:08). Team prizes went to Ambleside AC (Jack Wright, Tom Simpson and Will Barton) and Westerlands CCC (Ella Foreman, Ellen Downs and Ella Peters). 

A huge thank you to all those who helped make the race a success by giving us their time, advice, enthusiasm and prizes.
