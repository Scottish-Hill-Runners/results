The next SHR Championship race is on Saturday 25th June at the Eildon Hills Race, Melrose. The race will start at 2:30pm. Registration and number collection will be upstairs in the Melrose Rugby Club from 1:00pm.

Entry is via Entry Central here - [http://www.entrycentral.com/Eildon\_Hill\_Race](http://www.entrycentral.com/Eildon_Hill_Race)

Championship details after the two races at Clachnaben and Jura are available on the spreadsheets here - [http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/NewsItems.aspx?NewsItemID=2056](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/NewsItems.aspx?NewsItemID=2056)
