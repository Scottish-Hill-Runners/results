The Balmedie Beach Race is back in the SHR Calendar.  It's not a traditional hill race but does involve running up and down multiple sand dunes. It's part of the Cosmic/Deeside summer series.

  
Balmedie Beach Race

Thursday 15 June 2023

Time: 19:00

Venue: Balmedie Country Park

Entry Fee: £2

Distance: 5.5km
