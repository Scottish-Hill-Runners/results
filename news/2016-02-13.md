---
title: "Wins for Prasad Prasad and Charlotte Morgan at Carnethy"
date: 2016-02-13
excerpt: "Carnethy 5 Results – Saturday 13th February"
---

Prasad Prasad (Squadra Porcini) was the winner at a snowy Carnethy 5 in a time of 54.08 just ahead of Sam Hesling (HHR) and Tom Addison (Helm Hill).

The ladies race was won by Charlotte Morgan (Carnethy) in 01:01:30 with Sarah McCormack (Ambleside) and Kirsten Strain (Auld Reekie Orienteering Society) in 2nd and 3rd.

Full results are on the SHR website under Results > 2016 > Carnethy 5
