From Lochaber AC:

Fantastic mild and calm weather brought a fantastic record number of participants to this excellent New Years Day race. The route follows the mountain bike downhill track at Nevis Range from BOTTOM TO TOP ! Dan Whitehead from Cosmics won in a really fast time of 23:36, 36 seconds clear of Murray Strain from HBT. Third place went to Lochaber's Tom Smith in 25:18. First male veteran over 40 was Tim Brand of Lochaber and Graham Brooks was 1st Male vet over 50 even without his shorts!

In the ladies' race Clare Whitehead from Cosmics won in a fantastic time of 27:26. Clare Gordon from HBT was second in 28:24 and Miranda Grant third in 30:51. First Female vet over 40 was Nix Forster from Lochaber and Elma Murdoch also Lochaber was first Female Veteran over 50.

Many many thanks to all the helpers especially Andy Rogers and Clare Rumgay, and a big thanks to all the folk out on the hill cheering on the competitors. Thanks also to Nevisport, Blacks and Off Beat Bikes for donating lots of prizes.
