title: "Blackford Highland Games - Trail Race - Saturday 28th May"
date: 2022-05-25
excerpt: "New trail race to help Blackford Higland Games celebrate their 150th games."
---

Blackford Highland Games are celebrating their 150th games and this year there is a new trail race.

It starts at 3pm and is 8km (70% trail / 30% road).

Entry to the games field allows race entry but it you complete the race you get a partial refund of your entry fee.

It's the usual Highland Games format with a lap of the games filed at the start and finish so the crowd can get a good look at the incredibly athletic specimens taking part in the race.
