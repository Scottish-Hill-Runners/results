---
title: "Northern pair the Kings of Fife, 29 September"
date: 2007-10-03
excerpt: "Alan Smith (Deeside R) and Paul Raistrick (Highland HR) won the Five Hills of Fife Race. Kate Fitzpatrick and Jennifer McMaster (both Fife AC) won the women's race, breaking the course record in the process.  '"
---

Conditions were misty when the runners set off on the Lomonds, but the sun was out by the time they reached Norman's Law by way of Bishop Hill and Largo Law. Alan Smith (Deeside R) and Paul Raistrick (Highland HR) led from the start to win by three minutes from Carnegie's Sandy Bennet and Richie Cunningham, one of whom had had a nasty tumble on East Lomond.

Kate Fitzpatrick and Jennifer McMaster (both Fife AC) won the women's race in record time, while Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT) and Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) were the first mixed team.
