**Dumyat Hill Race 2015  
**Date: Wednesday 06 May 2015  
Time: 7pm  
Venue: Gannochy Sports Centre, Stirling University  
Entry Fee: £6.50  
Online Booking: [www.q-buster.co.uk](http://www.q-buster.co.uk/)  
Distance: 8km  
Climb: 380m  
Male Record: Murray Strain (32minutes and 23seconds) 2012  
Female Record: Angela Mudge (36minutes and 36seconds) 2007  
Website: [www.stir.ac.uk/dumyat/](http://www.stir.ac.uk/dumyat/)  
Notes: As there will be new timing equipment for this year’s race we ask that all runners report to the Gannochy Sports centre between 5.30pm and 6.30pm on the day of the race to collect numbers and timing chips.

Any Questions please do not hesitate to contact either Gail Burchill on: [gail.burchill@stir.ac.uk](mailto:gail.burchill@stir.ac.uk) or myself: [rhona.hendry@stir.ac.uk](mailto:rhona.hendry@stir.ac.uk)
