Results for the trial race at Aberfoyle on Sunday 30th August for the Junior Home International in Hill Running. The trial will be the basis of selection for the Junior Home International which will be held at Betws-y-Coed in Snowdonia on Saturday 19 September. Selections will be announced by Scottish Athletics in the coming week.
