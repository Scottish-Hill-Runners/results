John Coyle says:

"I’m struggling to get enough hill marshals for the Isle of Mull Fell Race on Saturday (21 July). Can you help?

I'll meet you off the 10.15am (9.30am from Oban) ferry and take you to the start and get you back for the return 7pm or even the 5pm boat. The start (12.30pm) is on the stoney shores of Loch Na Keal and heads round some magical hills to the Ben More ridge, then back to the Don Quixote tent where the wild camping is amazing."
