---
title: "Wins for Tom Owens and Clare Whitehead at Morven"
date: 2015-09-27
excerpt: "SHR Championship Race Results"
---

The final SHR Championship race of 2015 was won by the overall winner Tom Owens (Shettleston) in a new record time of 43.22. Carnethy HRC’s Andy Fallas and Iain Whiteside were 2nd and 3rd.

Clare Whitehead (Deeside) was first lady with Shona Robertson (Shettleston) and Stephanie Provan (Deeside) 2nd and 3rd.

Full Championship results will appear on the SHR website in due course.
