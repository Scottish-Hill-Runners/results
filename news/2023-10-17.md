**Articles**

We are looking for articles on hill running, hill races, training for the hills, fun in the hills, fun off the hills, adventures far afield or close to home; prose or poetry, fact or fiction - all styles, plots and viewpoints welcome. 

**Short-Cuts**

Don't think you're able to write a whole article? Well never fear, following the success of the format in the last Journal we are once again looking for **Short Cuts**, 200-300 word mini-articles on a similar theme.  

Taking the opportunity to reflect on the past year, we're looking for people to write on the theme **'Getting Started’.** 

**Images**

Don't want to write anything? No bother – the SHR Journal is always looking for photos (or paintings, or Photoshops, or illustrations) to be added. Send us your snaps!

**Deadline**

The deadline for submissions is **14th January 2024.** 

If you would like to make or discuss a submission please get in touch with [james.callender@live.co.uk](mailto:james.callender@live.co.uk)

The Journal will be available to order once published - free for members of Scottish Hill Runners.

We look forward to hearing from you!
