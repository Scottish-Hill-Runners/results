Scottish Hill Runners Annual “do” and prize giving

Saturday 1st October 2011 at The Forth Inn, Aberfoyle

This year the do will take place after the Ben Venue hill race, the final counter in the Run 4 It SHR Championship, on Saturday 1st October.

The do will take place at the [Forth Inn](http://www.forthinn.com) , Aberfoyle, in their superb “Gathering” function room. Prize giving for the Run4 It SHR Championship will take place at around 6.30pm. Run 4 It will again be awarding a range of superb trophies to category winners in this year’s Senior Championship. Prize giving will be followed by the dinner at around 7.30pm. There will be a three course meal from the Inn’s award winning kitchen, to include soup and crusty bread, a range of casseroles, a vegetarian option, couscous, potatoes, salad, plus desserts. Drinks will be available to purchase from the bar, including a selection of real ales.

Music will be provided by the amazing [Yoohoo Ceilidh Band](http://www.yoohooceilidh.co.uk/band), who play lively, upbeat Scottish music. The band is based in the Scottish Borders but play regularly all over Scotland, the UK and abroad. The line-up of fiddle, keyboard and drums provides an energetic, contemporary mix that will have everyone dancing!

Bed and breakfast will be available at the nearby [Dounans](http://www.soec.org.uk/Pages/Dounans.asp) Centre, half a mile away, for both Friday and Saturday nights or Saturday night only. Accommodation is in self-contained timber cabins with bunk beds. Anyone staying here will have access to the facilities during the afternoon after the race. There is ample opportunity for outdoor activities on the Sunday for those so inclined, whether mountain biking, running, or (Going Ape next door!).

The cost for the do is £20 and the accommodation at Dounans £22.50 a night (B&B). Please complete a booking form as soon as possible to be sure of your place!

Further details from Angela at angela.mudge@btinternet.com or Dave at dcbscott@tiscali.co.uk.
