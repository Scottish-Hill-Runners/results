From Race Organiser Dougie Milligan:

"High pressure again gave Screel fine weather: sunshine and no wind. With ground conditions being perfect everybody enjoyed their day’s racing.

As the 46 runners started Marcus Scotney, a local lad running for Shettleston, dominated the race all the way until the final half-mile on forest road, when Greenock Glenpark Harrier Andrew McCall sped past him to win by nine seconds. In third place and only 29 seconds off the winner was junior runner Jamie Stevenson from Ochil, also beating his father who finished fifth and first vet 40. Mark Whitelaw, Annan DAC, showed good form by finishing fourth, only one second behind Jamie. The vet 50 class saw some close racing with Moray runner Martin Flynn taking the honours in eighth place. The vet 60s was dominated by race organiser Dougie Milligan, who had a very good race finishing twelfth overall. Carnethy's Joan Wilson, vet 40, won the female race by a good margin from Hunters Bog Trotters’ Janice Smith."
