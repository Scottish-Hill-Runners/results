_From organiser Andy Kitchin_

It was records all round at Turnhouse on Wednesday; record temperature, record sunshine, a course record for Hector Haines in 24:46 (by 12 seconds) and women’s record for Sarah MacCormack with 28:50 (by 1:39). Janice Smith dns after gashing her knee whilst warming up. Lots of people missed out on spot prizes by not being there. Jamies Hardie won the prize for initiative (by suggesting his own name).

Thanks to all runners for turning out and apologies for the confusion over start times.
