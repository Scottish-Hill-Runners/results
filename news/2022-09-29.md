Manor Water Hill Race

Venue: Woodhouse Farm, Manor, Peebles (Grid ref NT 212 378)

(signs from A72, one mile west of Peebles)

Saturday 8th October 

Start: 1 pm

Registration from 12 pm 

Note there are some changes to the start location and the registration field location (see the website [http://carnethy.com/carnethy-organised-races/manor-water-hill-race/](http://carnethy.com/carnethy-organised-races/manor-water-hill-race/) for details).

The route is slightly shorter than previous years (see route start location change on website) 9.3 miles, 2130 feet (new records this year!)

Parking is at registration (at the Sheepdog Trial field) which is ~1 mile from the start so be prepared to make your way there on foot in time for the start.

Entry fee is £7. Cash only. No pre-entry. Profits from the race are donated to the Sheepdog Trial. Please bring exact change or if you have a £10 note consider making an extra £3 donation to the Sheepdog Trial.

No Junior or younger junior race this year.
