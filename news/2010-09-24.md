---
title: "Entries for UKA Fell and Hill Running Relay Championships on 17 October still being accepted"
date: 2010-09-24
excerpt: "The deadline for entries has been extended to 10 October..."
---

If you thought your Club had missed its chance of entering the 2010 UK Athletics Fell and Hill Running Relay Championships taking place on Sunday 17 October over the Lomond Hills in Fife - it hasn't (yet).

Fife AC has extended the deadline for entries to 10 October (or until the limit is reached, whichever comes first).

The event will be based in Falkland and full details are on the [Fife AC website](http://www.fifeac.co.uk).
