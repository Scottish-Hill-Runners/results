The winners of the uphill-only Aonach Mor Mountain Race, which also formed the inaugural Senior Home International, were Orlando Edwards and Victoria Wilkinson of England (pictured). Tracey Brindley and Clare Whitehead both had great runs to secure silver and bronze for Scotland in the womens race. Completing a clean sweep for England amongst the men, James McMullan took silver and Jon Brown the bronze. Scotland took the gold medals in the womens team competition - with Nicola Meekin in 5th and Fiona Maxwell in 6th bringing home the team. England were 1st mens team, with Scotland in 2nd.

The Scottish team were led home by their youngest member, Robbie Simpson, who won the U23 competition and was a fine 5th in the race. Prasad Prasad was only 5 seconds behind in 6th, Bobby Quinn was 10th, Sam Hesling 12th on his international debut, Andy Fallas 16th and Kenny Richmond 18th. Catriona Buchanan was 2nd amongst the female Under 23s, and Christina Rankin was 5th.
