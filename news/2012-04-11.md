_From organiser Stuart Simpson..._

Alan Smith rounded off another one of his hill running orgies by doing the unforgiveable and beating the organiser to win this years race. Impressive given he had done Clachnaben the day before and Normans Law earlier on. First Lady, Emma Oshea and a big thanks to Emma for the easter eggs that she appeared with for the prize giving. I intend to get my revenge on Alan for this in the manner that members of the Clan Macleod of Lewis are accustomed to, by doing a raid to the mainland to burn his house down and steal his reindeer
