The Scottish team for the international Marco Germanetto Memorial race, to be staged in Susa (Italy) on June 24th, has been selected. The team is:

Jessica Martin (Kilbarchan AAC)

Christina Rankin (Kilbarchan AAC)

Catriona Buchanan (Central AC)

Matthew Gillespie (Central AC)

Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven AC)

Robbie Cammack (Moray Road Runners)

Congratulations to you all and best wishes for a successful competition.
