Since the printing of the hill race calendar the highland games committee have collectively decided to increase the admission charges to £8 adult, £4 concession, to bring them more in line with other comparable events in the area. However,the hill race prize fund has been substantially increased and will be well spread over all the age categories with a new “local” category for runners permanently resident within a 25 mile radius of Dufftown and bonus prizes for the first three men and women.

Runners are again reminded that in the interests of long term vegetation recovery the track/footpath on Ben Rinnes must be followed at all times. There also exists a 2 hour 30 minute retirement time limit at the road crossing/water station on the return from Ben Rinnes.

Registration, changing and showers will be at the football club as usual opposite the games arena with additional toilets on the field.

From Graeme Bartlett, organiser/highland games representative
