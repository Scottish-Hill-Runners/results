---
title: "Simpson sets new record at Meall a'Bhuachaille, 30 October"
date: 2010-10-30
excerpt: "Robbie Simpson broke the course record in winning the Meall a'Bhuachaille Race...  '"
---

Robbie Simpson broke the course record in winning the Meall a'Bhuachaille Race by five minutes from Dan Whitehead, Craig Mattocks and Alec Keith. Clare Whitehead had an even bigger winning margin amongst the women, beating Veronique Oldham by over six minutes.
