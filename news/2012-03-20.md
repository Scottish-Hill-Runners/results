The first ever “Glencoe marathon” has been announced, starting in Glencoe and finishing in Glen Nevis, covering 26.2 miles and with 1600m of ascent. Although billed as a trail race, the serious amount of ascent may make it of interest to hill runners. It is claimed to be one of the toughest marathons in the UK, and entry is limited to 100 runners.

Participants will raise money for Project Northern Lights, an innovative youth development initiative helping to give disadvantaged young people in Scotland the chance to build their confidence and employable skills through taking part in adventure activities.

For more information on the event and details on how to enter, log onto the official [website](http://www.glencoemarathon.com)
