_From Glen McWilliams_

Unfortunately We have had to make the decision to cancel the longer route due to heavy snow. A decision to run the shorter route (up cheviot mountain & back ), will be made on the saturday morning. However, an event will be hosted as We have a revised lower level route consisting of 5 miles as a back up plan.
