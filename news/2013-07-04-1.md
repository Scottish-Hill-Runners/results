Brian Bonnyman of Westerlands continued his recent run of form when he successfully won this race for the second year in a row. Brian built up a substantial lead on the sharp climb out of the village which was enough to see him win comfortably in 33:11, well over a minute ahead of Iain Anderson with Adrian Davis coming third in just under 36 minutes. Lisa Blackley was the first lady and it was good to see Tom Bowie making a return to hill running after his recent health problems.
