_From Race Organiser Cameron Scott_

Could all entrants please note that the kit list for this year's Run And Become Tinto Hill Race has been updated to the following:-

Waterproof jacket (with taped seams and attached hood), Waterproof Overtrousers, Hat, Gloves, Map and Compass (suitable for navigating the course) and a Whistle. This is the minimum kit requirement and must be carried during the race.

All entrants to date have been emailed today with these changes and anyone who has entered but cannot meet these more specific requirements will be refunded their entry fee if they wish. The race details on SiEntries and Carnethy web site have also been updated to reflect these changes.
