---

A Shettleston first, second and third at Beinn Dubh hill race on Wednesday, with Harry Pulham claiming first place in 33.59. The first three all came home in under 36 minutes which usually wins the race.

Naomi Lang, Carnethy Hill Racing Club broke the women’s record by more than two and half minutes to finish in 38.22
