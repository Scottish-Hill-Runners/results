45 runners from 19 different clubs competed at the Dumyat Junior Races (and Scottish Junior League 1st race) on Sunday 10th April. Weather conditions very favourable to racing and the courses were well received and the finishers all seemed quite happy - possibly due to the sweets and juice on offer at the finish and the Easter egg prizes!
