Delivery of Women’s Navigation Courses Following our 2024 survey on women’s participation in hill racing, Scottish Hill Runners (SHR) is seeking to fund three one-day women’s navigation courses to be delivered in Autumn 2026. Courses are planned for: ? North of Scotland ? East of Scotland ? West of Scotland Each course will be a full-day practical navigation course with up to 12 participants. Subject to demand, SHR may be able to fund additional courses at a later date. We are seeking experienced hill navigation instructors who would be interested in delivering one or all of these courses. Expressions of Interest should include: ? A CV or equivalent summary of relevant experience and qualifications e.g. Mountain Leadership or Coaching qualification ? Details of where you would propose to run the course ? Your rate for course preparation and delivery How to apply Please send expressions of interest to secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk Deadline: end April 2026
