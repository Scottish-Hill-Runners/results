Tinto Hill race will be on Saturday 2nd November (not 9th which is shown in the SHR printed calendar)

Race registration and parking have also changed in order to avoid the chaos at the start, i.e. Symington Football Club is no longer being used. Full details [here](http://carnethy.com/carnethy-organised-races/tinto/).
