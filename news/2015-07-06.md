Jon Ascroft (Carnethy) smashed the Ramsay round record at the weekend by well over an hour, running an incredible 16hr 59mins. For Jon this completes the treble of Bob Graham (18hr 47min), Paddy Buckley (23hr 34min) and Ramsay rounds - not to mention a 12hr 41min Tranter Round !
