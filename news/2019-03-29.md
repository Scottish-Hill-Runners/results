**BEN LOMOND JUNIOR RACES**

**Rowardennan**

**Saturday, 6 April 2019**

**VENUE**

Car Park at road end, Rowardennan, Loch Lomond. [Location Map](http://www.streetmap.co.uk/map.srf?x=236010&y=698707&z=115&sv=236010,698707&st=4&ar=y&mapp=map.srf&searchp=ids.srf&dn=736&ax=236010&ay=698707&lm=0)

Please note that this car park costs and can fill up early. Please car share if possible.

**REGISTRATION**

Entries on the day only.

Will be in the NE corner of the car park, near the visitor centre.

Open from 11am. Entry for each race will close 15mins before the start.

Entry fee is £2. (Fun run is free).

**COURSES**

**12noon** : **FUN RUN**. For all ages, under 8s must be accompanied. 1.7km, 80m climb.

**12.20pm** : **U15** (born 2005 or 2006). 2.6km, 160m climb. (n.b. younger athletes who are over 12 on the day can run in this race, but will not count for the league. Both sexes).

**12.50pm** : **U17** (born 2003 or 2004) 4.3km, 240m climb.

**1.30pm: U20** (born 2000 - 2002). 6.3km, 380m climb .

Course terrain is a mixture of well made-up tourist path, smaller paths and a section which descends through the woods, which can be muddy and wet.

**JUNIOR LEAGUE**

These races form part of the scottishathletics 2019 Junior Hill Running League. The League categories are U15, U17 and U20, male and female (age groups as above).
