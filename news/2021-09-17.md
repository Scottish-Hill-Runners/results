---
title: "Lilliesleaf 10K - New race for the SHR Calendar"
date: 2021-09-17
excerpt: "The Lilliesleaf 10k Run will be in the Scottish Borders on Sunday 17th October"
---

The Lilliesleaf 10k is an annual event and attracts keen runners from across the Borders. It is an off road, hilly run through fields and tracks of the beautiful Riddell Estate with views of the Eildon and Cheviots Hills. The distance this year is 10.6k with 210m ascent. 

Registration is preferably through Eventbrite althouth it is possible to enter on the day. 

[https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/lilliesleaf-10k-run-scottish-borders-tickets-165670622291](https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/lilliesleaf-10k-run-scottish-borders-tickets-165670622291)

Registration/race numbers in the Lilliesleaf village hall from 10am

Race starts on the Leaf Green at 11am

Toilets/post race refreshments in the village hall

Dogs welcome on the course but MUST be on lead

Medals created by local glass artist Inge Panneels. 

All proceeds from the race will go to Harris Trust ([www.harris.trust.org](http://www.harris.trust.org/))
