The inaugural Ben Gullipen Hill Race was won by Andrew Gilmore (Carnethy) just ahead of Gregor Stewart (Westies). Ryan Stewart (Dundee Hawkhill) was 3 rd. The female winner was Gillian Stokes from Stirling Tri Club.
