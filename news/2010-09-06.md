Catriona Buchanan had an excellent race at the World Championships in Kamnik (Slovenia), finishing in 7th place amongst the junior women and leading the UK team to the bronze medals. In the junior men, Alex Hendry ran a solid race to finish 31st, helping the UK team to 6th place. Prasad Prasad, the only other Scot in the UK team and making his debut in this event, finished a very creditable 61st (and 3rd UK athlete) in a senior men’s race dominated by Africans. Ex Edinburgh University athlete Yujiro Iida, racing for Japan, was 45th.
