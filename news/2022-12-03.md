Only 2 weeks until Kirk Craigs Christmas Cracker.

Here are some FAQs:

Q. How can I enter?

A. On the day. Just turn up.

Q. How much is it?

A. £7 (minimum). All proceeds go to local charities....so don't be shy. The more you can give, the more people benefit.

Q. When & where?

A. Centenary Hall, Tillicoultry. 17 Dec. Registration from 9.30am. Race start 11am above Tilly Glen.

Q. What should I wear?

A. You need to wear or carry full waterproof body cover, a hat, gloves and have a compass. No exceptions.

Q. What shoes should I wear?

A. Hill/fell running shoes or very aggressive trail shoes. You'll need them.

Q. Will there be pies?

A. Yes, there will be pies.

Q. Will there be pies?

A. YES!!!
