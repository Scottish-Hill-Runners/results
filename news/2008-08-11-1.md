title: "Symonds again! - Glenshee 9, 10 August"
date: 2008-08-11
excerpt: "Andy Symonds (Carnegie H) wins for the second time in two days; Claire Gordon (HBT) follows third up Cairngorm with first round Glenshee.  '"
---

The inaugural Glenshee 9 Race attracted over 70 runners, of whom 59 completed the 33km/1800m course. Andy Symonds (Carnegie H) followed up his record-breaking effort on Largo Law the day before with another win, this time by two-and-a-half minutes from Carnethy's Stewart Whitlie. Alec Keith (HBT) must have given his lead in the Long Classics Series a bit of a boost by finishing third, and fourth-placed Dave Cummins (Shettleston) must also now be near the top of that particular table.

Claire Gordon (HBT) improved her third place in Saturday's Cairngorm Mountain Run by two, coming in over four minutes ahead of Scuz Wingrove (Cosmics) with Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) a similar time behind in third.
