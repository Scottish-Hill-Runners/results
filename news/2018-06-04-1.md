title: "Yetholm Hill Race Results and Report"
date: 2018-06-04
excerpt: "Wins for Tom Owens (Shettleston) and Steph Provan (Deeside) at the SHR championship at Yetholm on Sunday 3rd June"
---

With the Border hills burnt dry and like concrete underfoot the local farmers rain dance finally produced results! Overnight rain with occasional showers and mist during the race made for ideal running conditions, albeit slippery underfoot as race winner Tom Owens of Shettleston Harriers discovered crossing the final stile….it would appear that he can travel at speed sitting down as well. The ladies race was won by Stephanie Provan (Deeside Runners) after an exciting tussle with Heather Anderson (Fife AC) with a mere 5secs separating them at the finish.

Thanks to all the landowners and marshalls for helping us put on the event and as hosts we at Norham Running Club were delighted to be able to donate the total entry money of £700 to the Border Search & Rescue Unit who as always provided safety cover on the day.

Les Turnbull
