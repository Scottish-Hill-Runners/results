---
title: "FRA Relays, 13 October: Bronze for Bellahouston Ladies"
date: 2007-10-14
excerpt: "Bellahouston ladies finished 3rd in the FRA Relays at Forest of Bowland.  '"
---

We were originally told by our representative at the FRA Relays that no Scottish team had won medals this year, although Bellahouston's Ladies and Carnethy's Male Vets had come close. However, this had to be updated as Bellahouston Ladies took 3rd place. Carnethy's Male Vets were the first Scottish team to finish: 19th overall, and 4th Vets team.

Bellahouston passed Carnethy Ladies near the end of the 5 hour race and beat them by just 2 mins, but thought they had finished 4th, however race leaders, Bingley, went AWOL on the last leg and eventually finished a long way behind both Bellahouston and Carnethy which meant Bellahouston took third (behind Dark Peak and Ilkey).

Borrowdale won the Open Race from Dark Peak, but Dark Peak took the Over 40s with Carnethy just holding off Kendal for 4th place.
