---
title: "Cort-ma Law – Kit Requirements"
date: 2018-06-04
excerpt: "The Cort-ma-Law race is on this Wednesday (6th June) at 7.30 Remember to take your full body kit with you. You will be required to take it."
---

Reminder for all runners that, as usual, you will be required to carry the following standard safety equipment:

• water and wind-proof whole body cover, plus other body cover appropriate for the weather conditions  
• a map of the route, a compass and a whistle.

Your kit will be checked at the start of the race.
