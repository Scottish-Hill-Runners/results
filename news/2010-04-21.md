On a beautiful sunny evening, 63 runners toed the line at Kaim Hill Race to kick start the 2010 Bog and Burn Series.

Glasgow Uni romped home clear winners in the men and women's competition. Matthew Gillespie won the race overall by 2½ minutes from Alan Smith. Whilst Jessica Martin, also representing Glasgow Uni, was first lady with a minute's lead on Ruth Fraser-Moochie (unattached).

Alan Smith finished 2nd overall, just ahead of local Ayr Seaforth runner Ducrot Mayeul.
