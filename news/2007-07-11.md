The team is:

**Men**

Jethro Lennox (Shettleston), Simon Peachey (Carnethy), Joe Symonds (Kendal) and Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy)

**Women**

Sonia Armitage (Cosmics), Jill Mykura (Carnethy) and Dawn Scott (Lochaber)
