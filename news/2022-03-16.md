**Intercounties Senior Hill Running Championship - 7th May, Llanberis**

 Up to 5 male and 5 female senior athletes for each of the 3 regions (North, East and West) can represent their district at Intercounties. Athletes must be born in 2003 or before.

If 5 athletes are selected then 1 must be U23 on the day of the race.

If you are interested in selection or have any questions please contact your district rep
