---
title: "Mitchell and Meekin triumph in the Half Nevis Race, 4 July"
date: 2010-07-05
excerpt: "Craig Mitchell (Lochaber) and Nicola Meekin (Oban) are the first man and woman respectively...  '"
---

Craig Mitchell (Lochaber) finished 40 seconds ahead of Dave Eiser (Ochils) to win the Half Nevis Race, with Bruce Poll (Lochaber) a further 20 seconds adrift in third. Ross Bannerman (Highland) was fourth and Alan Smith (Deeside) - who'd won Melantee the day before - was fifth.

Nicola Meekin (Oban) was first woman, three minutes clear of Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber). Lyndsey Shaw (Lochaber) followed up her first place at Melantee by coming third.
