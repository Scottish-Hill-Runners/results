---
title: "Bronze for Scots men in WMRA Long Distance Challenge, 10 October"
date: 2009-10-11
excerpt: "Jethro Lennox was the highest placed Scot at the Kaisermarathon WMRA Long Distance Challenge, finishing in 10th position. The Scotland men's team finished in third place, and the women's team came fourth.  '"
---

The Kaisermarathon in Austria's Tirol doubled up as this year's WMRA Long Distance Challenge.

Last year's Challenge winner, Jethro Lennox, finished tenth and led Scotland's men to the team bronze medal, behind Kenya and England. Alasdair Anthony was a minute behind Lennox in twelfth, with Kenny Richmond 16th, Simon Peachey 20th and Paul Raistrick 23rd.

The women's team came fourth, with Claire Gordon 13th, Iona Robertson just behind her in 14th, Andrea Priestley 22nd and Megan Mowbray 24th.
