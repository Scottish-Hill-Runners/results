**SHR Committee positions: Website administrator and Insurance co-ordinator needed.**

The SHR committee are looking to fill two positions on the committee to take on the roles of website administrator and insurance co-ordinator.

**Website Administrator:**        

\- SHR Web site administration

\- Website news item posts – based on any requests/details given by SHR committee members or race organisers

\- Loading of data resources that can be linked to news items and web pages.

\- Results – conversion of results into suitable .csv format for load to the SHR website. And setting appropriate news items to go with them.

\- Race detail amendments – changing details when new course records created, adding additional details, entry links, and anything else that the RO wants to inform people on

\- Race maps – set up of any race maps and loading them against the appropriate race

\- Race organisers – maintenance of RO details

\- Championship updates – updates of SHR, British champs and Bog and Burn series

\- Calendar – update and maintenance of online calendar

\- SHR printed calendar – annual compilation of SHR printed calendar

**Insurance co-ordinator:**        

 - Deal with RO requests for race insurance cover

 - Ensure races meet SHR safety requirements

 - Update RO’s of any changes to safety guidelines

 - Liaise with insurance company with any questions/issues

 - Insurance policy renewal

If you are interested in either of these positions then contact the SHR secretary: [secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk)
