Just a quick update on the Newtyle hill race on Wednesday night. There will be junior races as well for 6-15 year olds, actually 2 categories, 6-10 and 11-15.

We also want to open registration a bit earlier to allow for extra numbers, so registration opens at 5.30 at the Field, Dunkeld.
