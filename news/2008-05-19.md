Angela Mudge (Carnethy) made it two wins out of two in the British championships so far this season with a comfortable win at Moel Eilio on Saturday. In another dominant display following her Mournes victory, she came home almost 4 minutes ahead of her nearest rivals, Olivia Walwyn (Altrincham) and Natalie White (Bingley).

The men in the small Scottish raiding party had more modest results. Perhaps still feeling the effects of their sensational 1-2 in the recent 3 Peaks Race, Shettleston teammates Jethro Lennox and Tom Owens were 6th and 16th respectively, behind surprise winner Nick Swinburn of Northumberland FR. Dougie Milligan (Solway) was very pleased with his 5th place in the Vet 60 class behind Dave Spedding (Keswick) who finished a mere 2 seconds ahead of Rossendale's Ken Taylor.

Full results, race report and photos are [here](http://www.eryriharriers.org.uk/mountain.html) .
