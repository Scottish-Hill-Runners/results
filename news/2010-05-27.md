---
title: "Big turn-out for Kinnoull, 19 May"
date: 2010-05-27
excerpt: "Mike Carroll (Perth RR) and Emma O'Shea (Deeside) win the main prizes...  '"
---

There was a larger-than-usual turnout of 160 runners for the race. Mike Carroll (Perth RR) was first, ahead of previous winners Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) and Don Naylor (HBT).

Emma O'Shea (Deeside) continued with her winning ways in the ladies' race. Claire Douglas (Perth RR) was second and Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy) third, in the same time as Isla Campbell (Central).
