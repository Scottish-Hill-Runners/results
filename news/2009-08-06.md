The Ben Kenneth Hill Race (or should that be duathlon?) was won by Steven Fallon (Carnethy) in 36:21, with Debbie MacDonald of HBT the first lady.

_Steven Fallon says:_

Fine conditions, with a swim across a channel through seaweed (yuck!), finished off with a run back along Lochboisdale main street with hundreds of spectators, all cheering and sounding their car-horns. Tom Smith from Lochaber AC had worked his way back up to second place, but wimped out of the swim and ended up eighth. Second and third places were taken by local runners who'd done the course before.
