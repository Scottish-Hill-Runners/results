Alan Smith leads the [Bog and Burn Series](http://www.shr.uk.com/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-003) after the first 5 races, with Ochils runners Gary Fraser and John Stevenson in 2nd and 3rd places. Westies trio Arnis Saltums, Jamie Provan and Robert Kravcovs are the only other runners to complete the first 5 races.

Val Houston currently leads the women's competition from Sarah Adam (both Westerlands).
