A great day, fantastic weather but the rain over the past wee while made the ground very wet and soft underfoot.

Chris tomlin made a good start and stayed clear throughout the race whilst Jason Williamson and Stuart Smith were doing battle throughout but with Stuart pulling clear on the downhill return taking second ahead of Jason.

Veronique Oldham tried to beat her shared course record (with Ruth MacKenzie who couldn’t make the race this year but unfortunately) but the soft conditions thwarted her efforts by 8 seconds. A good effort by Liz Delany and Corri Black seeing the second and third Ladies home.

Cosmics dominated the race with all top three finishers in the men and ladies categories.

Thanks to all who helped and of course the runners.

Simon Beeson
