p> It was a glorious day for the 2nd Cairngorm Hill & Trail race with a total of 39 runners racing in almost too hot conditions in the Glenmore Forest Park.

There were impressive overall male and female wins from Johannes Felter (BASOC) and Lisa Allan (Cairngorm Runners) in the challenging and technical 21km route while Gavin Whiteside (HHR) and Anna Heftner (unattached) stormed round the faster 11km route for first male and female.

Participants enjoyed post race refreshment at Glenmore Lodge. The race will take place on Sunday the 2nd of October in 2016.
