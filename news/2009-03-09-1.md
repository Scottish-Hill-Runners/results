From organiser Adrian Davis:

A record entry and excellent field with 61 were greeted by a clear sunny day. The leading group of four shot off and remained well ahead of the rest. Prasad Prasad, last year's winner, showed everyone a clean pair of heels although Brian Marshall, Robbie Simpson and Andy Symonds chased him valiantly. Prasad was just clear at the top and extended his lead on the swift descent, followed by Andy.

The vets' race hotted up on the descent with Des Crowe having had an excellent climb, passing Adam Ward half way up and pulling away. Catriona Buchanan showed her downhill skills overtaking quite a few of the senior men on the tricky descent - these youngsters have no fear!

At the finish Prasad was clear from Andy in second and Robbie in third with Brian first and Des holding off Adrian Davis for first vet40 in fifth place. Catriona held first place comfortably with Joan WIlson coming through strongly for second and first female vet 40.

Andy Symonds kept the overall three-race Series lead from Robbie Simpson and Alison Pollard held on to hers. The soup and cakes at the end went down very well and a few beers later that night made it an excellent day.
