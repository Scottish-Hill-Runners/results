For the first time ever the course was run in reverse which added a lot of interest and much debate about whether it made legs harder or easier; this added to the low cloud and drizzle to make some teams take interesting diversions! Another change was the 2nd/3rd leg changeover which was moved from Scotlandwell to Kinnesswood, involving a much steeper descent and then climb to White Craigs.

Carnethy men had a convincing victory from Ochils with Bellahouston RR third, while Carnethy Women won the ladies race from HBT. The first vets team was actually Carnethy Over 50s, who finished in front of all the Over 40 teams, the first of which was also Carnethy.
