---
title: "Andrew Symonds in record form on Largo Law, 9 August"
date: 2008-08-11
excerpt: "Andrew Symonds (Carnegie H) and Ali Hughes (Metro Aberdeen) win at Largo Law...  '"
---

Andrew Symonds (Carnegie H) lopped a minute off the record set last year by Fife AC's Chris Russell for the re-routed Largo Law Race. He finished a couple of minutes ahead of Lewis Millar (Central AC) with Paul Matthews (Metro Aberdeen) a further minute-and-a-half behind in third.

Metro Aberdeen took top prize in the women's race, thanks to Ali Hughes, whilst Jocelyn Scott and Hilary Ritchie (both Fife AC) were second and third.
