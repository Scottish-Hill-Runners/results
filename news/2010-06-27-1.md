Mike McGovern (Moorfoots) won the Eildon 2 Hills race by a mere two seconds from Teviotdale's Keith Murray.

Georgia Nesbitt (Gala) produced a stunning performance to come fourth, only two seconds behind Paul Faulkner (Carnethy).
