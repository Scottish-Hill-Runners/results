The second of the Krunce Series of 1st Tuesday of the month races was held on Tuesday 2nd May  and there were wins for Alasdair Couper (East Sutherland AC) and Elaine Ormand (Cosmics).

Full results on the SHR results tab.

Dry conditions but a cold, east wind prevailed.

Many thanks to Aberdeen City Council and Craibstone Golf Course for land use permissions.

And special thanks to Brimmond Café for use of the facilities for registration and for the sales of snacks and amazing soup.

The time volunteers give is amazing and thanks to them all for their dedication.

The Krunce Series is a shared delivery amongst three local clubs – Cosmics, Insch TRC and Garioch Road Runners.
