---
title: "Simpson takes to the Seven Hills of Edinburgh, 21 June"
date: 2009-06-22
excerpt: "David Simpson wins the thirtieth running of Edinburgh's Seven Hills...  '"
---

David Simpson won the thirtieth running of the Seven Hills of Edinburgh Race by nearly three minutes from Corstorphine's Tom Ferrington. Andrew Wright (Carnegie) was third, Grant Wilkie (Corstorphine) fourth and Brian Marshall (HELP) fifth. Amelia Lloyd was the first woman, followed by yet another Corstorphiner, Susan Johnston, and then Harmeny's Caroline Dunn.

The Challenge event went to Graham Nash.
