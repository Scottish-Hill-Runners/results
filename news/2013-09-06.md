We are saddened to hear of the sudden death of hill running stalwart John Blair-Fish who collapsed whilst out cycling. Speaking on the Carnethy Website Gordon Cameron said

_"We are very saddened to report that John Blair-Fish died suddenly on Wednesday 4th September 2013. He was out on a CTC ride and collapsed and died near West Linton._

_The Beeftub race this Saturday, which he founded in 1992, will be dedicated to his memory and called "The John Blair-Fish Memorial Devil's Beeftub Race._

_Black ribbons will be provided for club members to wear at both the Beeftub and the Ben Nevis race on Saturday. At 62 his life was relatively short but consider the width and depth of that life!_

_He will be sadly missed and our thoughts go out to his family and friends._

_Gordon Cameron - President Carnethy HRC_
