**Organising club(s) sought for 2021 British Fell/Hill Relay Championships**

Scotland is next due to host the British Fell & Hill Relay Championships in October 2021. The scottishathletics Hill Running Commission are looking to hear from Scottish clubs interested in hosting the event.

The event was last held in Scotland in 2016, where host club Westerlands CCC welcomed 115 teams of six runners from all over the UK to the village of Luss on the shores of Loch Lomond.

The event comprises four legs, of which two are single legs and two paired legs. Scottish clubs have a strong history of success in the event, in particular Hunters Bog Trotters women’s team who have won the event five times in the past nine years.

Any club (or group of clubs) wishing to express an interest in hosting the 2021 event should contact the Commission Secretary Mark Johnston (email [mark\_johnston@gmx.co.uk](mailto:mark_johnston@gmx.co.uk)) by Sunday 14th April. Further details relating to the organisation of the event (an organisers guide) can also be obtained from Mark on request. It would be helpful, though not essential at this stage, to have a proposal or proposals in terms of venues.
