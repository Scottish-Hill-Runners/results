SHR Championship 2026

There is a slight change to the 2026 SHR Championship with Meall a'Bhuachaille no longer hosting a championship race in 2026. It has had to move dates so the second of the medium races and the final race in the series will now be the Run Of The Mill Race at Alva.                    

So, for the Championship the two longs will be at Ben Chonzie (18th April) and the Two Breweries (26th September).

The two shorts will be at Glamaig (11th July) and a "one-off Saturday race" at the Law Breaker (20th June).

The mediums will be at Durisdeer (30th May) and Run Of The Mill with a provisional date of 24th October, but note that this is to be confirmed. Full details  for ROTM will follow.

Ben Chonzie (18th April)

Durisdeer (30th May)

The Law Breaker (20th June)

Glamaig (11th July)

Two Breweries (26th September)

Run Of The Mill (24th October TBC)
