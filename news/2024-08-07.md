**Strathearn Harriers Junior Hill Race - Sunday 1 September at Laggan Park in Comrie at 2.30pm**

Strathearn Harriers are hosting their annual Junior Hill Race. The race is a perfect introduction to hill running for any young runners seeking a new challenge. There will be two different age categories. U9 which will run 1 mile with all under six to be accompanied by and adult. 9 years and over will run 1.5 miles with a few more hills.

Chocolate and juice for all taking part with prizes for the first three back in their category. The race will start at 2.30pm. Registration will close 15 minutes before the start. So please be there in plenty of time.

Entry fee : free but donations are welcome!
