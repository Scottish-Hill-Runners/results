James Waldie (Carnethy) won the Dollar Hill race on Saturday 4th July ahead of George Foster (Dundee Hawkhill) and Fergus Johnstone (Gala).

The female race was won by Sophie Horrocks (Edinburgh Uni H&H) ahead of Ciara Largey (Lochaber) and Sarah Wiseman (Ochils).

The Team prizes were won by Carnethy (male) and Ochil (female).
