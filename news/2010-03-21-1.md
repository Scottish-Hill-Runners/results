_From the now ex-organisers Peter and Elsie Baxter:_

Another excellent race, following the SHR Championship race the day before at Chapelgill. The weather was kind, with the odd spot of rain. A 100% kit check was implemented. Biggest problem was a lack of maps and the odd missing compass.

The course was not too muddy and all seemed to enjoy themselves. Congratulations to Adrian, Alan and Jacqui, excellent performances all round, showing the youngsters a clean pair of heels.

Thanks to John, Ranjit and the others from Annan ADC at the finish for the help and support.

This race is well-supported by Moffat Mountain Rescue. We're indebted to them for being on the hill over so many years, providing a level of comfort to the organisers on the many occasions when conditions were poor.

We have been organising this race for over a decade and it is time for someone else to take over. Ranjit Thomas will take over as organiser with support from the club and ourselves (A very big thanks from us!). We would also like to thank the Abbey Inn for allowing use of their premises, Hope Vere Anderson the landowner who has been an excellent host and the runners, without whom we might have had a few longer Sunday lie-ins!
