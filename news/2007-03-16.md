---
title: "Knockdhu International and 'Raid'"
date: 2007-03-16
excerpt: "Scotland plans to send five teams to the upcoming Knockdhu International in Northern Ireland."
---

The Knockdhu International Hill Races will be held near Larne in Northern Ireland on Saturday 28 April. Scotland plans to send up to five teams (Veteran Men, Senior Men and Women, Under 20 Men and Women). If you fancy running but aren't likely to be selected for one of the teams you should consider joining the "Raid".  
  
Further details, including a booking form for the Raid, are on the scottishathletics [website](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk).
