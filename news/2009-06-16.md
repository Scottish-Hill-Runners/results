There was an excellent 1-2-3 for Scottish runners in the junior mens race in these trials for the UK team to go to the European Championships in Austria next month. Scott Macdonald (Central) was the winner, followed by Alex Hendry (also Central) and Robbie Simpson (Deeside). In the senior mens race, Prasad Prasad (Clydesdale) was 6th. Jessica Martin (Kilbarchan) was 7th amongst the junior women, followed by Catriona Buchanan in 11th.

Teams for the European Championships have now been announced - and many congratulations to Scott Macdonald, Alex Hendry and Robbie Simpson who are included.
