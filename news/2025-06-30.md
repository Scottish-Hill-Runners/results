In the White Tops Race on Friday 27th June, the winners were Ruan Van Rensberg (Fife AC) and Anwen Darlington (Dundee Road Runners).

On Saturday 28th, Felix Wilson (Carnethy) and Nicky Spinks (Dark Peak) won at Arrochar Alps

And on Sunday 29th, the winners at the Lairig Ghru race were Kris Jones (Dundee Hawkhill) and Emily Mcnicol (Law & District AAC)
