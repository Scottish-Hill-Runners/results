Maddy Moss Hill Race is on 13th July at 7:30pm. All runners must ensure they wear or carry full waterproof body cover, hat & gloves. Map of the race route, compass and whistle also essential. No kit -no race.

Runners should be capable of completing the race within 1hr 45mins, and reaching the top of The Law in comfortably under an hour. A cut-off time may be in force at the top of The Law and if so will be stated at race registration. Runners MUST adhere to instructions from marshals. Failure to do so puts runners and marshals at risk and will result in disqualification from the race.

This is a race over elevated hill terrain and all runners must be capable of managing themselves over the course. You cannot rely on others to support or guide you.

If you are unsure about any aspects of race safety please refer to the Safety page of Scottish Hill Runners website: [SHR Mandatory Safety Rules for Competitors](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Safety.aspx)
