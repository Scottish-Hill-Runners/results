---
title: "Marshall and Wardlaw triumph at Chapelgill"
date: 2008-03-16
excerpt: "Brian Marshall was first at Chapelgill in 20.18, and Eilidh Wardlaw was first woman in 28.35."
---

Brian Marshall was first at Chapelgill in 20.18, ahead of the two Smiths - Tom and Alan (who was also first Vet). Eilidh Wardlaw was first woman in 28.35.

First junior to finish was Alastair Robertson of Lothian, 22nd in an excellent time of 29.21 and even more impressive that he did the "Congrie Conga" as well! Next place behind him was teammate Rachael McIlwraith, who was first junior female (and 2nd overall) at her first attempt on the race, another very good performance after winning the junior race at Bishophill.
