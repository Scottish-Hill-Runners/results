title: "Another win for Al Anthony: Morrone Hill Race, 5 September"
date: 2009-09-09
excerpt: "Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) and Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) make their mark at the Braemar Highland Gathering...  '"
---

Alasdair Anthony (Ochils) showed he is in fine form as the Commonwealth Championships approach by winning the Morrone Hill Race at the Braemar Highland Gathering. Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) was second and Ben Livesey (RAF Kinloss) third.

Clare Whitehead (Cosmics), who will also be representing Scotland in Commonwealth Championships, had a comfortable victory in the women's race, ahead of Mhairi Brown (Edinburgh) and Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT).

There were around 80 starters but only 57 recorded finishers - those arriving back in the arena after 45 minutes are not allowed to complete the race due to the busy games schedule and hence are not listed...
