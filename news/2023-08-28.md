Latest scores after Ochil 2000s, 7th race in the 2023 series, as per link below.

**Steve Halsall** of **Shettleston Harriers** continues to lead in the men’s category and **Ella Peters** of **Westerlands** continues to lead in the women’s category.

With only Two Breweries to go, now that Pentland Skyline has been cancelled, the men’s category looks like a 2-horse race between Steve and Tom Day. The women’s category is very close and think could still be won by any of the following 5 : Ella Peters, Angela Mudge, Sarah Hodgson, CJ Macphail and Ella Foreman.

19 people have now completed 4 or more races in the series and **Nicky Spinks** of **Dark Peak** has managed to complete all 7 races.

Stewart Whitlie
