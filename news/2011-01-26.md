The annual 'Training and Inspiration evening' for the West Highland Way Race (and other ultra events) organised by _Run and Become_ takes place in Edinburgh on Wednesday 9 February. It starts at 7pm and finishes around 9.30pm. The venue is St Bride's Community Centre, 10 Orwell Terrace, Dalry Road, Edinburgh EH11 2DY.
