Jim Haworth (Galloway Harriers) won at the Merrick Hill Race on Sunday 21st September in a new record time of 01:10:58. 2nd place went to Owen O’Neill (Westerlands) with Colin Glencorse (Kilmarnock Harriers) in 3rd.

Lorna Mahoney (Milburn Harriers) was first female in 01:27:32
