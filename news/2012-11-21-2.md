Next year's Highlander will take place over the weekend of 27/28 April. The location hasn't been announced yet, but is due to be a bit more "central" - for the Highlands - than in the past, south of Inverness, which will hopefully tempt a few more of you out of your shells. So if you were inspired by the coverage of the 2012 edition of the event on the Adventure Show - or even if you weren't - then get yourselves organised and get your entry in. Remember, there can be only one. Details and entries [here](http://www.handsonevents.co.uk/?page_id=13)

_Alec Keith_
