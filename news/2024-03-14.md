**Entries**: The waitlist is now active. Due to parking restrictions, we are limited in the number of participants. Entrant swaps are not permitted. No refunds will be issued, as proceeds will go to Arrochar Mountain Rescue.

**Car Parking**: We have secured a limited number of spaces with Ardgarten Hotel and Forest Holidays Ardgarten. Car sharing will be mandatory. You will be allocated a parking zone; full details will be released closer to the event date. All vehicles assigned to the hotel car park must be moved by 4 p.m.

**Public Transport:**  
Bus 926 & 976 - Ardgarten Visitor Center.  
Train station: Arrochar & Tarbet 4.1 miles to hotel.
