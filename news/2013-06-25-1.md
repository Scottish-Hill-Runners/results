The weather forecast for the race was rain and strong winds but Lochaber once again produced a warm sunny day (ok it did chuck it down in the morning) for the runners, giving panoramic views once on top of eagle crag.

Alan Smith (Deeside) once again showed age is not necessary a handicap as he overcame a strong challenge from Stephen Burns(Lochaber) and Jon Gay(Lochaber) to win the race in 01:02:12, Stephen finished in 01:03:59 and Jon just behind in 01:04:14. Diane Baum(Lochaber) was the first lady in a time of 01:14:29 finishing in 8th place overall, the second lady was Lou Roberts(Ambleside) 01:16:19 and third placed was Claire Pearson(Wharfdale Harriers) 01.17.39.

Category winners were MV40 Stephen Burns 2nd overall, MV50 Alan Smith winner, MV60 Ray Murdoch (Lochaber) and MV 70 Walter Ryder (Morpeth Harriers). Category winners for the ladies were FV40 Lou Roberts 2nd lady and FV 50 Aileen Kitson (Lochaber).

A mention also for Jimmy Jardine, another of the 3 score year and 10 brigade to finish. Thanks to All that helped on the day and race organizer Ian Adams.
