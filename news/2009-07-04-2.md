date: 2009-07-04
excerpt: "Joe Symonds wins the Dollar Hill Race in the third-fastest time ever...  '"
---

_From organiser Alex King:_

A sunny day for Dollar hill race if not a bit hot – the entry was larger than expected at 95 including four from Holland who were in Scotland on holiday.

First back was Joe Symonds of HBT in an impressive time of 1:13:10 the third fastest time ever at Dollar. He was closely followed by Prasad Prasad of Clydesdale Harriers in a time of 1:13:50 with Alasdair Anthony of Ochil a couple of minutes behind in 1:16:42. The first Super Vet home was Martin Laing of Fife AC in 9th place and a time of 1:29:01 followed closely by the second super vet Peter Simpson of Carnegie 6 seconds later. Chris Upson of Westies was the first vet in 12th place in 1:29:55. First Over 60 was Mike Walsh of Kendal in 1:33:38 – 18th overall.

As for the Women, Andrea Priestley of Ochil was first in 1:31:29 followed by Alayne Finlay of Ochil in 1:42:10. First Super vet was Isobel Burnet of Carnegie in 2:01:40.

Both Male and Female team prizes went to Ochil – well it was a home race.

Thanks to Dollar Academy for the loan of the facilities and to [Williams Brothers Brewery](http://www.fraoch.com/williamsbros.htm) of Alloa for the Prizes.
