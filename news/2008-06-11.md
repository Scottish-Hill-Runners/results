This news from Mark Johnston:

"Entries are now open for the Cairngorm Mountain Run, a new Alpine-style uphill only running event. The event takes place on Saturday 9th August and incorporates the trial races for the Scottish Team for the World Mountain Running Trophy, but please note you don't have to be super-speedy to take part, just come along and enjoy the run! Please visit the [event website](http://www.cairngormmountainrun.co.uk) where you can download an entry form.

Also read the event details and rules, and view the course via the slide show (the weather will be like that on the day!).

We are also looking for helpers for the event to do tasks such as marshalling, start/finish, sweeper and course marking. If you'd like to volunteer then drop us an email."

If you have any further queries about the event or would like to volunteer to help, please contact: [cairngormmountainrun@live.co.uk](mailto:cairngormmountainrun@live.co.uk)
