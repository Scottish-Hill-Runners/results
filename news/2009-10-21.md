In difficult conditions, with the mist right down for the later legs, a lot of teams in the hard fought British Relays ran into navigational problems. The first three teams to finish were all disqualified for missing a checkpoint on the last leg, leaving Borrowdale to take to the top of the podium. There was a good turnout of Scottish teams, with Bellahouston emerging as first Scottish club with an excellent 11th place. Edinburgh University were next in 23rd, and then it was Fife in 36th.

There are also some photos and a race report on the [Carnethy website](http://www.carnethy.com/#October)
