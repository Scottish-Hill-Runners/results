---

Clachnaben was the first of this year's SHR Championship races, and also the one-off **scottish**athletics Championship. David Armitage summarises what happened:

"Clachnaben was won by Andrew Symonds (Carnegie) who pulled away from Tom Owens (Shettleston) in the latter part of the race. Brian Marshall (HELP) took the bronze medal and Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) was first M40. Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) was first woman followed by Iona Robertson (Bellahouston) and Sonia Armitage (Cosmics) who was also first F40. The cool sunny weather was well-suited to running and was enjoyed by a field of 161 entrants."

Thanks to race sponsors [Deeside Brewery](http://hillside-brewery.com/).

The second SHR Championship race is [Screel](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0021) on Saturday 18 April.
