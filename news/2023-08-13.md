The Cademuir Rollercoasater 6k and 3k races were on Saturday 12th August.

Rowan Taylor (Lasswade) and Lizzie Wellsted (Colchester Harriers) were the winners in the 6k race. 

Full results on the SHR results tab

In the 3k race, the winners were Josh Daunt (Edinburgh AC) and Holly Simpson (Giffnock North AC).

Holly's winning time in the 3k was a course record by 3 seconds.
