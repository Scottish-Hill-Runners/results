---
title: "El-Brim-ick Hill Race - Postponed"
date: 2022-01-05
excerpt: "The El-Brim-Ick race scheduled for Saturday 8th Jan has been postponed."
---

Unfortunatley, following Storm Arwen, the Forestry Commission are unable to issue permits at the moment as they still need to fully assess their locations to ensure they are safe for the general public.

This has resulted in the cancellation of the El-Brim-ick race scheduled for Saturday 8th Jan.

If the race can be rescheduled later in the year, it will be announced here.
