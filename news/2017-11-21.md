The Montane Tor des Géants is acknowledged to be one of the toughest trail races in the world.

Unlike several races of this distance that are run as stage races, this is a non-stop 330km (205mile) circuit in the Alps that involves 24,000 metres of climb. Top Scottish ultra runner and GB 24 hour international Debbie Martin-Consani will be at Run and Become in Edinburgh on Wednesday 29th November at 6.30pm to talk about her experiences of the event.

Details and sign ups at [Run and Become](https://www.runandbecome.com/event/edinburgh-montane-tour-des-geants)
