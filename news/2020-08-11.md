Scottish Athletics Hill Running Championship

Venue - Comrie Croft

12km 800m up 150m descent - uphill only course.

Organiser angela.mudge@btinternet.com

**pre-entry only** from 9th September via the Scottish Athletics website.

Entry Fee £10

Event will be run under current COVID19 guidelines.

Time 10.30 onwards - start in waves, runners will be notified of their start time prior to the event.
