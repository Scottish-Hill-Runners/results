The championship listings after Creag Dubh are now available. [Here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2019 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2019 - Female.xls) for women or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.

As this is the fourth race and there has been at least one at each distance, those who have completed all four have already earned their SHR Mug and there are quite a few of these people. Well done !

Also a few people have so dominated their category and have done the four races that they have already won the championship. These being Veronique Oldham (Cosmics) in O/50, Jocelyn Scott (Fife) in O/60, Alexander Chepelin (Carnethy) in U/23 and Mairi Gilmour (Westerlands) in U/23. However, some of the other categories look very open.

Any queries to [Andy Spenceley](mailto:committee10@scottishhillrunners.uk) SHR Statistician
