Finishing on Ben Klibreck this afternoon for an overall time of 31 days, 10 hours and 27 minutes, Jamie Aarons has not just toppled Donnie Campbell's hugely-admired 2020 outright record for the fastest self-propelled Munro round but shattered the previous female record. See Dan Bailey's very prompt [UKC/UKH report](https://www.ukclimbing.com/news/2023/06/jamie_aarons_sets_new_munro_round_speed_record-73379) for further details.
