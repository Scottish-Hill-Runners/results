---

Conditions were slightly better for the second leg of Lochaber’s Triple Hirple with a light breeze blowing and the temperature down a couple of degrees. James Espie once again faired best in the hot sunny weather (could it be that the weather is always good on Deeside) with James finishing more than nine minutes ahead of his nearest rival. It seems like he only needs to turn up on Monday evening to make it a hat trick of wins.

Elizabeth Apsley was first lady home again and she also looks like making it a hat trick of wins. Great to see her and her club mates (Thames Hare & Hounds ) competing in the Highlands.

Ian Adams
