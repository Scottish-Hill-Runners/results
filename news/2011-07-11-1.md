---
title: "The Callander Highland Games Hill Race is Back!"
date: 2011-07-11
excerpt: "This race is being run again on 31st July for the first time since it was stopped due to foot and mouth 10 years ago"
---

This race is being run again on 31st July for the first time in 10 years, and the organisers promise a 10km+ route taking in tracks, forests, rivers and spectacular scenery including the iconic Bracklinn Falls Bridge.

More details at www.entrycentral.com
