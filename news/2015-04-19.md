An Teallach Hill Race - A new hill race to raise funds for Dundonnell Mountain Rescue Team. We have at last purchased land in Dingwall on which to build a new base for which we need to raise £160,000. We are hoping to make this an annual event.

Sat July 11th 1.00pm

DMRT An Teallach Hill Race

Dundonnell Mountain Rescue Team Base NH094 879

Category AM 10.6km 1062m

Entry £10 (all proceeds to DMRT new base fund)

Raymond Wilby 01349 862014

[raymondwilby@yahoo.co.uk](mailto:raymondwilby@yahoo.co.uk)

Full waterproof body cover, hat, gloves, whistle, compass and map must be carried.

The race starts at the team base in Dundonnell and follows the rough hill path to the trig pillar on Bidein a' Ghlass Tholl and back down the same way.
