---
title: "SHR 2015 Calendar & Journal"
date: 2015-02-13
excerpt: "Available at Carnethy 5"
---

All SHR members will be able to pick up copies of the 2015 printed SHR Calendar and the Journal in the hall at Beeslack school, Penicuik at registration for the Carnethy 5 race on Saturday 14th February.

There are also brand new SHR car window stickers.

And finally, if you haven’t yet collected your 2014 SHR Championship mug, Ronnie Gallagher will have them in the hall also.
