---
title: "Bishop Hill Race - SHR Calendars & Journals available"
date: 2022-03-03
excerpt: "You will be able to pick up your copy of the SHR 2022 calendar and journal at the Bishop Hill Race on Sunday 6th March. And join SHR if you have not already done so."
---

At the entries for the Bishop Hill Race on Sunday you will be able to pick up  your printed Journal and Calendar if you have joined SHR. As the Hall is not available this year these will be available either from the boot of the car that is being  used for registration or from the gazebo that will be used for that purpose. On collection you should sign to confirm receipt so it would be good if you were to bring a pen.  If anyone who has not joined SHR and wants to do so, if you bring a completed application you will also be able to join SHR at Bishop Hill.
