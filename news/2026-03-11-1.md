Another year, another journal, once again Scottish Hill Runners are looking for submissions for the 2025/26 Journal! As usual we will be looking for ARTICLES, REPORTS and POETRY on hill running, hill races, training for the hills, being in the hills, getting off the hills, hill landscapes, hill people and everything in between. The Journal continues it's open-policy in terms of style and content; we want fact, fiction, poetry (and maybe some lies?) in whatever form you fancy. If you don't fancy writing, we're also looking for PHOTOS (candid, staged, Photoshop, you're choice) and any ARTWORK you'd like to share. We've had some great images in the past and it would be good to make the journal as vivid as possible. The deadline for submission will be Sunday 31st May. All submissions to be sent to committee4@scottishhillrunners.uk If you have an idea you'd like to discuss please don't hesitate to get in touch. We look forward to hearing from you!
