---
title: "Brits win team bronzes in Europeans, 12 July"
date: 2009-07-17
excerpt: "The GB Junior Men's team of Scott McDonald, Alex Hendry and Robbie Simpson win bronze at the European Championships..."
---

Great Britain won three team bronzes at the European Mountain Running Championships which were held in Austria on 12 July - for the Junior Women, Senior Women and Junior Men.

The counters for the Junior Men were all Scots: Scott MacDonald (Central) was a fine fifth, with Alex Hendry (Central) thirteenth and Robbie Sinpson (Deeside) just behind him in fourteenth. Congratulations to all three!

The full results are available at the [race web site](http://www.emrc2009telfes.org/index_en.html)
