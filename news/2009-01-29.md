Here's an important message from Edel Mooney:

"Many thanks to those who have expressed an interest in the Raid to the Le Moleson uphill race in Switzerland. Following on from a generous offer from the race organisers (see below) I will need to confirm numbers **as soon as possible**. There is no limit on numbers so if you are interested, but haven’t contacted me thus far, please do so using the contact details below.

I have got further information on costs since the Raid was previously advertised:

*   The return flights which were then £20 are now advertised at £88.
*   The organisers have offered 3 night accommodation for good class international runners but they need to know NOW so we can get priority over other teams.

In view of the above, and since transfer from the airport to the venue will cost about £80, any international-standard runner who pays me a deposit of £50 by 10 February will be guaranteed a flight out on 18 June, returning 21 June, with transfer, accommodation, food and race entry, for a total of £150. The balance of £100 is to be paid on or before departure. "

You can contact Edel at 86 Currievale Drive, Currie, EH14 5RW or 07768-826 772 or Edelmooney75@hotmail.com.
