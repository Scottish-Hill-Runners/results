If you are entitled to a 2022 SHR Championship mug we will try and get it to you as soon as possible.

With the unlikelihood of races such as the Devils Burdens and the Carnethy 5 going ahead in the early part of the year, distribution won't be as quick as we would hope.

But I have the mugs and will try to get them to you as soon as possible. The first 2023 championship race is at Birnam at the start of April and hopefully I will be at a couple of races before then too.

Where possible we will distribute the mugs through a club contact.

Remember, you must be a paid up member of SHR and to have completed the requisite races to get your mug.

Email me to arrange pick up if you can. Or to let me know what races you might be at.
