The Lochaber AC Triple Hirlple concluded on Monday evening. Three races in three days. All the full results are on the SHR results tab.

Wins for Lochaber's Callum Fraser and Sarah MacDonald at Meall an t-Suidhe on Saturday 29th June.

Robin Downie (Lochaber) and Helen Leigh (Higland Hill Runners) were the winners on Sunday's Half Nevis race.

And Ewan Dowd (Unattached) and Sarah MacDonald (Lochaber) won at Monday evening's Cow Hill Race.
