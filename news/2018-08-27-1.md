**Scottish Athletics junior hill league 2018 final standings** .

"With the trial race at Aberfoyle, the Scottish Athletics junior hill league had its final event. Twenty runners completed the the league, many doing more than the required three races to gain a final standings.

The age category winners are:

U20G Rhona Mowat Giffnock N.

U20B no winner

U17G Helen Chong Giffnock N.

U17B Sunny McGrath Devon H.

U15G Isla Hedley Fife AC

U15B Struan Bennet Fife AC

Congratulations to all and many thanks to all supporters of the league. Run For it (Dundee) are offering the prizes; these will be sent out by post as soon as possible. Please contact Mark Harris if there is a mistake in the results."
