---
title: "SHR Long Classics 2018 - Wins for Andy Fallas and Jill Stephen"
date: 2018-10-23
excerpt: "Andy Fallas (Carnethy) and Jill Stephen (HBT) have repeated their 2017 success and won the Long Classics again in 2018"
---

With the final race in the series at the Pentland Skyline Andy Fallas and Jill Stephen replicated their 2017 wins and are confirmed as the winners of the 2018 Scottish Long Classics series. And Andy has won with maximum points. 52 runners completed the series.
