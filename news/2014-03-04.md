Manny Gorman of Westerlands CCC has now published his long awaited "The Corbett Round". It tells the epic story of his 70 day record setting jouney around the 219 Corbetts of Scotland in 2009, with all the laughs, tears and weather along the way. It is available directly from Manny or Brenda at any (random) races they attend this season for £15, or from www.mannygorman.co.uk for £17.50, including p&p
