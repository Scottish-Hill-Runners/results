---
title: "British/FRA Relay Championships, Clwydian Hills, North Wales"
date: 2008-10-20
excerpt: "Edinburgh University first Scottish club at 'FRAs' in a superb 6th place!  '"
---

Edinburgh University put together a very consistent performance to finish 6th at the FRA Relays in good weather on the fast running Clwydian Hills. The race was won by Bingley. At the end of leg 3 the other Scottish clubs taking part Bellahouston, Shettleston and Carnethy were all within 3 or 4 places, around 20th, but a last leg for Shettleston by Jethro Lennox saw then pull clear of their rivals, but still well behind the youngsters of the University! Shettleston ended up in 13th, with Bellahouston 20th and Carnethy 29th. Bellahouston B team were 88th.

Carnethy and Fife were the only female teams from Scotland with Carnethy finishing 5th and Fife 13th.
