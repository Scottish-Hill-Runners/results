**Senior Race**

There are only nine of the 175 places left, so you'd best enter NOW if you want to run. You have to enter on-line, [here](http://www.racentries.com/event/details/25-ben-lomond-hill-race). The race starts at 1.00pm.

**Junior Races and Fun Run**

The Fun Run, open to all and with a nominal entry fee, starts at 11.30am.

The Junior Races incorporate the **scottish**athletics Junior Championships for 2008. They are also a Trial Race for the Marco Germanetto Junior International on 22 June and the first of the three **scottish**athletics Junior League Races. The U14s start at 11.50am, the U16s at 12noon and the U19s at 12.20pm. Entry for all these races is on the day.

Full details, including a map of the courses, are [here](Download.aspx?Item=BenLomondJunior2008.pdf).
