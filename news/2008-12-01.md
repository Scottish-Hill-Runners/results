From race organiser Tom Smith:

"Beinn Lora hill race was round 4 in the Lochaber AC Winter League, In the usual hill race format it’s a race to the top and back - in this case 340m of tough climb up steep rocky foot paths then out on the heathery hillside for the final slog for the summit before the hurtle back down.

The cold snowy conditions today didn’t hamper Andrew Wright (Carnegie Harriers): he smashed by 44 seconds the course record set by Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC) in 2006. But Wright’s time of 26:48 only just got him top spot as a charging Ruari Watt (Lochaber AC) was only 6 seconds down in 26:54. Third was Ewen Thorburn a very impressive 28:36 for his first hill race!

First female was Eilidh Patterson (Highland Hill Runners) with 34:01 who fought off Fiona McDonnald (Cosmic Hill Bashers) to second place in 35:51. Nix Forster (Lochaber AC) was third in 37:18.

Catergory winners were:  
Male vet 40 John Hepburn (LAC)  
Male vet 50 Graham Brooks (LAC)  
Male vet 60 Ray Murdoch (LAC)  
Female Vet 40 Nix Forster (LAC)  
Female Vet 50 Aileen Kitson(LAC)

Thanks to Forestry Commission, Achnacree Farm and Benderloch Village Hall Committee for their support.

The next round is The Black Water Dam. Good fast running out to the dam and back from Kinlochleven. Race starts at 11 am on Sunday 7th December. Registration from 10am at The Ice Factor . If you intend to race please be equipped with full body cover just in case the weather is bad."

Meanwhile Andrew Wright turns from record breaker to Law Breaker with his [new race in the Ochils](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0569) on 28 December.
