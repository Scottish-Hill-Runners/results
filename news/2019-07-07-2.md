---
title: "Jacob Adkin wins the gold medal at the European Mountain Running Championship"
date: 2019-07-07
excerpt: "Jacob Adkin wins the gold medal at the European Mountain Running Championship in Zermatt, Switzerland"
---

Scottish athlete, Jacob Adkin who currently runs for Keswick AC but who has also represented Moorfoot Runners and Edinburgh Uni H&H won gold today at the European Mountain Running Championships in Zermatt in Switzerland.

Robbie Simpson and Andrew Douglas finished in 7th and 9th respectively to contribute to winning Team gold for GB & NI.
