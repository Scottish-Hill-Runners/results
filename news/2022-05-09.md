Entries are now open for both Junior and Senior Eildon Hill Races on Saturday 25th June. 

Note that for the senior race this year there will be a return  to the original route which will start and finish in the middle of Melrose.  The Junior Race will start nearby at 11.00am. 

And note that the revised start time for the senior race is at 14.30 (registration from 13.00).

Entry Central Junior Race - [Eildon Hill Race Junior (Start Eildon Mains) - EntryCentral.com](https://www.entrycentral.com/EildonJuniorrace)
