From Chris Henty

63 Seniors set out in good conditions on a cool, overcast day at the Manor Water race, and with a tail wind headed up to the summit of The Scrape. At the turn the race was led by David Fulton (HBT) with Brian Marshall (HELP) in 2nd. On the descent the order was reversed, and Brian came home first (and first V40) in 01:11:01, with David 2nd in 01:11:10, and Steve Halsall of Westerlands 3rd in 01:12:02.. The ladies race was won by Morag McCracken of HBT in 01:20:30, 2nd and 1st F40 was Karen Pickles of Pudsey Pacers in 01:28:55, and 3rd was Lisa Gamble of HBT in 01:39:05.

Also worthy of a mention is 15 year old Cameron Scott (Unatt) who ran the whole race after a mix-up at the junior turn, and finishing 17th overall in 01:21:58.
