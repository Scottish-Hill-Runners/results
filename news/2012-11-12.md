It was a bright, slightly chilly day with some good muddy conditions to keep it fun. Jo Symonds retained his title from last year, taking first male in 37.56, 32 seconds off the course record! New ladies winner this year with Karen Lyons in 51.02, with last years ladies winner Roma Shepherd coming close second.

_Rebecca McKenzie_
