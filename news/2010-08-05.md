There was a record entry of 254 for the North Berwick Law race on a glorious if slightly windy night.

The headline results were as follows:

1st Individual – Daniel Gay (HBT), 19.48  
2nd Individual – Donald Naylor (HBT), 20.32  
3rd Individual - Paul Faulkner (Carnethy HR), 20.53  
1st Boy (U15) - Thomas Knox (Anster Haddies), 22.42  
1st Girl (U15) – Lisa McCusker (Unatt), 26.42  
1st Lady - Kate Jenkins (HBT), 23.13  
1st Male Vet - Jonathon Knox (Anster Haddies), 21.19  
1st Female Vet – Janet McWhinnie (Carnethy HR), 27.26  
1st Local - Dave Wright (HBT), 21.59  
1st Team of 3 – HBT (Daniel Gay, Donald Naylor, James Jarvis)
