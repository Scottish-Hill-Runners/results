**2017 race date confirmed as Saturday 03 June with 1.30pm race start**

Registration and race start/finish at Evanton Football Pitch.

Mixture of forest roads and steep hillside to Fyrish Monument with a fast descent mainly on forest singletrack back to football pitch. The distance is 13km with 540m ascent. Category BM. Race co-incides with Evanton Gala Day and catering will be provided by RNLI. Various stalls and activities for kids being held at the football pitch from 1.30pm.

There will be a U17 race of approximately 5km and an U12 race of approximately 2.5km.  
Entry on the day - £5 adults and £3 U17 and £1 U12.
