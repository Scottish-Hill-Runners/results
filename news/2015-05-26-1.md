It's race week for Slioch and the next instalment of the Championship. There are no entries on the day. All competitors will now have received some pre-race information by email, please check your inbox.

There will be a full kit check at registration. Remember to bring your pack - no kit, no race number. We've had full winter conditions on the hill recently, please do not risk disqualification or worse.

Due to the limitations in Kinlochewe, competitors are encouraged to car share wherever possible. You may also 'Park and Ride Your Bike' to registration/start. Secure storage is available for those wishing to leave their bike at registration. Unless otherwise instructed, the car park at Incheril is out of bounds. Please leave sufficient time to get from registration to the start.

For those arriving early and needing breakfast, The Whistle Stop Café is next door to registration. It will open it's doors on race day from 8.30am until 9pm. Tea and coffee will be available at registration.

Remember we want all runners back at the hall after their race, hot food, hot showers and hot prizes!

We look forward to seeing you all on Saturday.

_John Hitchen_

**Race Organiser**
