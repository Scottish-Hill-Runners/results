---
title: "Simpson wins at Cheyne Woods, 21 August"
date: 2008-08-22
excerpt: "Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven) wins comfortably and Briony Curtis (EUHH) breaks the women's record.  '"
---

Robbie Simpson (Banchory Stonehaven) warmed up for the Junior Men's race in next month's World Trophy with a convincing victory in the Cheyne Woods Hill Race - the last one in Cosmics' Summer Series. Jason Williamson (Cosmics) and Alan Smith (Deeside) were second and third.

Briony Curtis (EUHH) won the women's race from Anna Hogg and Fiona Dahl (both Cosmics), setting a new course record in the process.

Race organiser Gary Gutteridge says:

"We had an excellent turnout despite the weather leading up to the race. Quite a few from Stonehaven as well! Thankfully it stopped raining for the whole of the event in the end, although it was very wet underfoot.

Series Results will be issued separately and a little later."
