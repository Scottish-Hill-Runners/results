There was a large field for the first race at Birnam, probably due to being a very central and accessible short race. If the numbers keep up it will be a cracking championship. Not much to say about contenders as it is basically the order you finished at Birnam! A few people have moved up an age group to challenge the holders, or are doing the championship after a long gap (yes, that's you, Colin Donnelly!). The male teams have started with the tradition Carnethy v Shettleston battle, while the female teams have a mixture of clubs doing well.

If anyone isn't in the correct age category, please get in touch. This is particularly relevant in the Under 23 age groups as these weren't recorded at Birnam (only those who are Under 18 were noted).
