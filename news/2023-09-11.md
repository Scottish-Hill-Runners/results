**17th Edition of the Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay, Sunday September 3rd**

39 teams descended on Laggan Park, Comrie for a brilliant day racing in the hills. Well done to all the teams and a huge thank you to all the marshals, timekeepers and burger-flippers who made it such an enjoyable event.

The dust is settling on the finances of the day but it does look like we've raised over £1000 for NHS Tayside Health Fund to support research into pancreatic and other upper GI cancers - so thanks to everyone for supporting the raffle and making donations.

Full results are now available here: [https://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/.../race-results...](https://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/our-races/simon-wake-comrie-hills-relay/race-results-and-record-times?fbclid=IwAR1B7LnR5kklTh0ppRV9g5dW2JeRlZOcZTkzUn5wTEBUyl9qxSgnwJooLLo)
