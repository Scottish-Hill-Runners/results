---
title: "SHR Championship Mugs & Long Classics Prizes"
date: 2018-11-07
excerpt: "The 2018 SHR Championship mugs and Long Classics awards will be available at Tinto Hill Race on Saturday 11th November"
---

Those who are paid up members of SHR and who qualify for a 2018 SHR Championship mug will be able to collect them at the Tinto Hill race on Saturday 11th Nov.

If you are unable to attend Tinto then please make arrangements for a club-mate to pick up your mug for you.

  

Also, the winners of the SHR 2018 Long Classics awards will be able to pick up their prizes at Tinto.
