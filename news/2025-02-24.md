A reminder for all those coming to the Bishop Hill race this Sunday about how tight parking is in the area of Portmoak village hall. Please car share where possible and park in the villages of Kinnesswood or Scotlandwell and jog along to the start. Parking on the main road causes locals to call the Police. Entries on the day are available (full body cover)and a Junior race starts at 1:15pm before the main race.Ages 9 to 15.
