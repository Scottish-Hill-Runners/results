What SHR does is summarised [here](WhatWeDo.aspx). Subscriptions for 2008 from new or renewing members became due on 1 January. The amounts are: £8 Senior, £2 Junior / Student / Unwaged; plus £4 each for second and subsequent senior members living at the same address as the first senior member and opting to receive one copy of SHR's paper publications.

You can pay your subscription by posting a cheque together with a completed membership form to the Membership Secretary (I A Nimmo, 5 Whitehouse Terrace, Edinburgh EH9 2EU). The membership form is available for download either as a [Word document](Download.aspx?Item=RenewalForm2008.doc) or as a [pdf file](Download.aspx?Item=RenewalForm2008.pdf).

Alternatively you can do the whole thing electronically by (a) e-mailing a completed membership form to Scottish Hill Runners; **and** (b) sending your subscription to the SHR’s bank account by on-line banking. The relevant form, together with further instructions, is available for download as a [Rich Text Format file](Download.aspx?Item=RenewalForm2008Web.rtf).

The Race Calendar for 2008 and the Journal for 2007 should be ready for distribution to members by the time of the Carnethy 5 (16 February).
