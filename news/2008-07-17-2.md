It's Scotland's turn in 2008 to host the [Junior Home International and British/Irish Junior Championships](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0412&System=Metric&Month=10). They're going to be held at Bonaly, outside Edinburgh, on 12 October.

Note that you don't need to be a junior to have a run on the day: proceedings start at 9.40am with an Open Race (for over-19s).

The [Scottish Junior Trial Races](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0562&System=Metric&Month=9) on 21 September will be over the same courses. The trial also incorporates an open race - which is the final counter for the [SHR's Under-23 Championship](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-002) - and will have a hill fun run as well.
