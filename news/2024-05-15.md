excerpt: "Unfortunately Yetholm Hill Race (scheduled for Sunday 2nd June) has had to be cancelled for 2024"
---

Unfortunately Yetholm Hill Race (scheduled for Sunday 2nd June) has had to be cancelled for 2024.

But the Yetholm Shepherds Show Race in October will go ahead as planned and the Yetholm Hill Race will be back in 2025.
