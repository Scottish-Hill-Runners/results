Al Anthony's 2:25:51 at Pentland Skyline was enough to clinch the title over long-time series leader Stewart Whitlie, with Brian Marshall 3rd overall. Andrea Preistley took the women's title, with Jacqui Higginbottom moving into 2nd place above Emma O\`Shea in the final race. 39 runners completed the series.
