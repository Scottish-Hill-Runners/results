_From Adrian Stott_

Susie Allison will be giving a short talk on her new book Scottish Trail Running and signing copies at Run and Become, 20 Queensferry Street, Edinburgh next Tuesday evening December 4th at 7pm. We will be selling the book at a small discount on the night

Having just got a copy in our hands now, we are happy to say that it is a little gem of a book and does for Trail Running what Steve Fallon's "Classic Hill Runs" book did for Hill Running .

[Full details here](http://run.runandbecome.com/events/category/edinburgh-running-workshops/)
