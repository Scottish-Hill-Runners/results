---
title: "Dumyat: Students' Championship as well as Bog and Burn Race"
date: 2008-05-05
excerpt: "Dumyat is the second Bog and Burn Race. It also incorporates the Scottish Universities Sport Hill Running Championship."
---

[Dumyat](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0036), which starts at 7pm on Wednesday 7 May, this year incorporates the second running of the Scottish Universities Sport Hill Running Championship. This particular Championship is open to any fully-registered student of a Scottish University. There are more details [here](Download.aspx?Item=SUShrDETAILS08.pdf).

The race is also the second in the [Bog and Burn Series](http://www.shr.uk.com/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-003).
