_From organiser Elaine Stewart:_

Robbie Simpson breaks the record in a time of 19:30. Robbie takes the record from Dan Whitehead which stood since 2000 in a time of 19:46.

With thanks to the Forest Enterprise, Aberdeen City Council and the Agricultural College for access and to Colin & Pauline Larmour, Colin Reid, Ewen Rennie, Anne Stone , Keith Varney, Anita Hamilton, Gillian Clunas and Carolyn Hare for their help on the day. Perfect running conditions, nice sunny day…just had to take a slight detour for a rather large fallen tree.
