Grant Baxter and Gary Fraser report a completion of all 12 Bog and Burn Race routes in under 24 hours over 3rd/4th July.

A spreadsheet of their route is available, including predicted and actual running and driving times. They had 2 friends, Mark Leadbetter and Donald McLean (an old Lommie from many years ago) who drove and supported them throughout the challenge.

The bare statistics are : 12 races, 86.9kms running, 5286m ascent and 273 miles of driving. Their total running time was 13:27:00.

Their chosen route, predicated on finishing at Dumyat (OHR's spiritual hill), was definitely not the shortest driving route possible.
