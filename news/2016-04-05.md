Scolty hill race will this year be the inaugural East District hill race championship.

In order to qualify you have to be a member of Scottish  
Athletics, and of an East District club. You can of course still run in  
the race if you don't qualify for the championships. The race is on  
Sunday the 5th June at 2pm. Details here:  
[http://www.deesiderunners.com/?page\_id=31](http://www.deesiderunners.com/?page_id=31)
