Organiser Keith Murray writes_

There was double success for Moorfoot at the first running of the Penchrise Pen Hill Race with Darin Dougal first home in a time of 35:23 and Julia Connor first lady in 41:30.

Darin was chased home by 19 year old Rory Anderson from the home club Teviotdale who also had Craig Grieve in 3rd place. 2nd lady home was Jane Chalmers (Unattached) and 3rd was Lindsey Knox of Teviotdale. Special mention to Westerlands’ runner Owen O’Neill who despite arriving late, dashing from the car to start over 3 minutes behind and finishing with only one shoe, managed to work his way through the field into 10th place.

_

_Although a low turnout the race proved successful with the weather being kind. Thanks to all who made the effort to come along, some coming a fair distance and hopefully the race will carry on and we will see you all again next year._ [Results](http://shr.uk.com/Download.aspx?Item=Penchrise Results.xls) | [Photos courtesy of Lindsey Knox](http://www.flickr.com/photos/lindseyk2/sets/72157627698602478/)
