Traprain Law and Cairn Table offer alternative challenges to the British/SHR Championship counter at Durisdeer on June 14th.

Note from Keith Burns of Carnethy:

For those not competing at Durisdeer or elsewhere, don’t forget the Carnethy low carbon option of Traprain Law Race at 3pm on Sat 14 June at East Linton. Good express bus service (X6) direct to East Linton. Bring the family along to the Village Gala Day festivities. For competitors, this year features locally rare Hebridean black sheep on the Law. If you’re lame or lazy, we need marshals for hill, river and historic locations like Hailes Castle. Contact Keith at 01620 860257 with any queries.
