Gregor Stewart leads the series by a single point from Alastair Thurlbeck who foolishly failed to win the opening race at Kaim by missing the obvious track to the finish whilst in the lead.

Justin Carter is in 3rd place, with Kenny Richmond the next V40 in 4th place.

Newbie M50 hill runner Micky Anderson is in 5th place overall.

Veteran hill runner Don Reid leads the strong competition in the M60 category.

In the women’s competition, only 2 runners have completed 6 races, with Jane Oliver leading Katie Rademaker.

Ruth Crewe is in a strong position to win the series, if she turns up to Caerketton.
