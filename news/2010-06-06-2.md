It was another emphatic victory for Deeside's Robbie Simpson, who won Scolty by over two minutes from Dan Whitehead, Kyle Greig and 1st V40, Mark Harris. Veronique Oldham of Cosmics had almost as large a winning margin, coming home 1.44 ahead of Carolyn McLeod, with Sonia Armitage in third. All three are vets, with first senior woman being Rachel Armitage in 6th.

Congratulations to the category winners in the Junior races, who were:

M12 Liam Clark

F12 Frances Sealy

M14 Logan Rees

M16 Adam Harris

F16 Halina Rees

M19 Adam Gatens

F19 Alex Campbell
