## Whitetops Hill Race - Friday 28 Jun 2024

 Entry Central Link - [Whitetops Hill Race - EntryCentral](https://www.entrycentral.com/event/125295)

Whitetops is organised by the Dundee Hawkhill Harriers and takes place in the Sidlaw hills starting in the picturesque village of Kirkton of Auchterhouse to the north of Dundee.  The race covers 10.4km with a height gain of 360m. Anyone over the age of 17 can take part and the course will be well marked and marshalled, making it suitable for hill racing beginners.

We have been given the original trophies for this race from Dundee Road Runners, so if you want something nice for your trophy cabinet and see your name along with the likes of Joe Symonds,Dan Whitehead,Oleg Chepelin,Jamie Crowe,Kris Jones, Angela Mudge etc., why not come and try to beat the rest!

Prizes will be awarded for first 3 male and female athletes along with first in each ten year age category(v40,50,60,etc) for male and female categories.  
There will also be team prizes for first male and female team(3 to count).

Prizegiving will be as soon as possible after the final runner finishes. Post race refreshments will be provided in the village hall after the race.

The event is run under Scottish Athletics race license(13410).
