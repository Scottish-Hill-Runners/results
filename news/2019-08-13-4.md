Well that was a wet one!!!

Really pleased with the turnout as it was just running like a river off the hills!!

James Dunn won 2nd year in a row and Jillian Fong, visiting from Vancouver, took the 1st lady prize.

James also claimed the title of King of the Hills running the 3 races of the Scottish Borders Hill Race weekend.

Susan Ridley was also crowned Queen of the Hills.

Sheila Cochrane (race organiser)
