A large field of 210 lined up for the start of the annual North Berwick Law Race.

Hunters Bog Trotters (HBT) dominated the field with prizes throughout the categories. HBT’s Murray Strain flew home in an astonishing 18.52 with Donald Naylor coming in 2nd at 21.09. The Bog Trotters also saw wall to wall success in the Ladies category securing all 3 top spots. Tessa Hill took 1st in 22.37, Eilidh Wardlaw in 2nd with 23.35 and Eilis McKechnie 3rd in 23.43.

A huge thank you to all who make this popular event possible. The race would not be possible without the support from Edinburgh Athletic Club who provide marshals and organisational support. North Berwick Community Council kindly sponsors the event and volunteers were on hand to present medals to the runners. The RNLI also provide the event with use of the Boathouse for registration and prize giving.
