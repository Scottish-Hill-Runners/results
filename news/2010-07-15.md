---
title: "Revised date for Bennachie"
date: 2010-07-15
excerpt: "The Bennachie Hill Race will be held on 10 October and not 3 October as originally advertised..."
---

Garioch Road Runners have had to postpone the Bennachie Hill Race (and the associated junior race) by one week. It will now take place on 10 October. There are further details [here](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0105&System=Metric&Month=10).

Please also note that the web site for Garioch Road Runners is now http://www.gariochroadrunners.com/.
