---
title: "SHR welcome Run4It as sponsors again for the 2011 SHR Championship"
date: 2011-02-27
excerpt: "Scottish Hill Runners are again pleased to welcome '"
---

Scottish Hill Runners are again pleased to welcome [Run4It](http://www.run4it.com) as sponsors of the Scottish Hill Running Championship in 2011, with the first of the 6 race series starting with [Birnam](http://shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0556) on 26 March. Run4It are providing the trophies for the Championship, which judging by last year will again be special. They have six shops throughout Scotland, where SHR calendars are on sale, and offer a 10% discount to SHR members both in-store and online. They are keen to promote hill running, with one initiative being the hosting of dedicated SHR notice boards in-store.

Ian White, Director of Run4It, says ‘’We are delighted to be back sponsoring the Champs Races and delighted to be able to put something back into our sport – looking forward to being involved’’.

David Scott, Convenor of SHR, says “It’s great to have Run4It supporting the Championship and helping to raise the profile of Scottish hill running, and we look forward to working with them over the coming season, with some great races to look forward to”.
