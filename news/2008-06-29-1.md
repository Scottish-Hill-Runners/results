Angela Mudge became British Champion for the 5th time, sealing it with a comfortable win at Blackstone Edge. Angela had previously won the British Championship 4 times from 1997 to 2000.

Angela clinched the 2008 British title with wins (and course records) at all 4 of her counting races at the Mourne Mountains, Moel Elio, Durisdeer and Blackstone Edge.

In a closely fought men's race, Jethro Lennox finished 4th behind his 3 major rivals for the men's championship: Rob Hope, Morgan Donnelly and Rob Jebb.
