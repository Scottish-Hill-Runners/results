There has been a record entry for the Lairig Ghru hill race this year. The race limit, agreed with the 2 estates, was reached in 6 days. This resulted in a waiting list of over 80 and despite the fact that places have been offered to those on the waiting list as cancellations have been received, the list has remained high. Deeside Runners, who organise the race, would like to remind people that there will be strictly no entries on the day - as was stated on their website when the race opened for entries.
