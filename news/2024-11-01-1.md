---

The Deeside Runners night race series starts again in December. There are 4 races of around 8km with as much climb as we can fit in, on "interesting" paths mostly in forests. All are run from a cafe or hotel so the runners have somewhere warm to recover and eat cake. The cost is only £1 per race and the prizes are terrible (a chocolate bunny if you are lucky) but this year we have a sponsor! Out & Back Store in Aberdeen and Norwegian head torch brand Moonlight Mountain Gear are giving a head torch as a prize for the male and female outright winners of the series, which is based on combined time over the 4 races. They are also giving up to 20 entrants at each race the opportunity to try out one of these head torches for free at every race apart from the first. Full details are here:  [Night Race Series – Deeside Runners](https://www.deesiderunners.com/races/night-race-series/)
