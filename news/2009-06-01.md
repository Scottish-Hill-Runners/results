Graham Arthur (Ilkley Harriers) has taken an early lead in the SHR Long Classics Series after the first 3 races, being the only runner to have completed the first 3 races.

Brian Marshall is the highest placed runner on 2 races, followed by Lloyd Taggart and Stewart Whitlie.

Angela Mudge is the highest placed female, followed by Clare Whitehead and Jackie Lee.

The next race in the Long Classics Series is the new Loch Lochy Munros event on 13th June.
