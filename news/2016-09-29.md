The full results for the SHR 2016 Bog & Burn series are now on the website.

Available under the 2016 link here - [http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-003](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-003)

Congratulations to the 49 runners who completed the series by running in six or more races.

And particular congratulations to the winners.

Joe Symonds won the overall, just edging out James Espie, James Waldie and Sam Alexander in the final race at Caerketton. They finished 2nd, 3 rd and 4th respectively.

Other male winners were Kenny Richmond, Steven Fallon, Gordon McCaffrey and Ian McManus in the V40, V50, V60 and V70 categories.

The overall ladies winner was Jill Stephen.

Val Houston, Fiona Comrie and Michelle Hetherington were 2nd, 3rd and 4th
