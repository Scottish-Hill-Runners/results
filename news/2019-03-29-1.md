date: 2019-03-29
excerpt: "Details of registration etc for the Birnam & Deuchary Hill races on Saturday & Sunday"
---

Races are entry on the day

Birnam race - Registration at Birnam Arts Centre from 11am Saturday 30th March

Deuchary race -Registration at Birnam Guest House from 10am Sunday 31sh March

Competitors will require map, compass, whistle, waterproof top and bottoms, hat and gloves to be carried or worn. There is no official water or food station on the course so bring your own.
