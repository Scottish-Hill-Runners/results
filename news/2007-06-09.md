**Traprain Law**, 9 June

It was good to see Brian Marshall (HELP) back on his local hill apparently fully recovered from twisting his ankle on Ben Lomond four weeks ago. He kept the Carnethy pack at bay to win in 38:54. Carnethies filled seven of the next dozen places, with Jill Mykura (thirteenth overall) first woman in 45:20.

**Devil O'The Highlands Footrace**, 9 June

The course record-holders John Kennedy and Helen Johnson won again, Helen coming in fifth of the 49 finishers.

**Crans Montana**, 10 June

There was a strong Scottish contingent out in Switzerland for this top quality international uphill only race, and they put in some excellent results, led home by the in-form Joe Symonds who finished 6th (1st Brit) in the mens and Claire Gordon who was 12th (4th Brit) amongst the women. There's a report on the [**scottish**athletics web site](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=2855). Results are [here](http://services.datasport.com/2007/lauf/sierremontana).
