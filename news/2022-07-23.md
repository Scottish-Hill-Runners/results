Have you heard about the 2 day KONG MOUNTAIN MARATHON on the Isle of Arran on the 27th/28th August 2022 in Scotland. Avoid the extra £10 price increase by the 1st of August and join the Kong team for this inaugural race on this beautiful Scottish island. There are 3 linear courses and 2 score courses for different abilities. Run and navigate with your kit through the beautiful North of the island to the secret mid camp location on the Saturday and back to Brodick on Sunday where a healthy lunch and prize giving awaits you. Experience the friendly atmosphere during the race. One not to miss! For information and entries [www.kongmountainmarathon.org.uk](http://www.kongmountainmarathon.org.uk/)
