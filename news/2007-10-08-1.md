title: "Whitehead and MacKenzie add Bennachie to their string of titles, 7 October"
date: 2007-10-08
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead (Cosmic HB) won for the sixth time this year and Ruth MacKenzie (Deeside R) for the third. Ruth MacKenzie also won the Aberdeenshire Hill Race Series, along with Jason Williamson (Cosmic HB).  '"
---

Bennachie, the third and final race in the Aberdeenshire Hill Series, attracted an entry of just over one hundred. Dan Whitehead (Cosmic HB) led them home, with Craig Mitchell (Lochaber AC) and Ross Arbuckle (Keith and District AC) behind him. Ruth MacKenzie (Deeside R) was first woman; Donna Gray (Garioch RR) and Morag McCracket (HBT) were second and third.

The Series was completed by fourteen runners, the winners being Jason Williamson (Cosmic HB) and Ruth MacKenzie.
