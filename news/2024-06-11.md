---
title: "Isla leads the charge at the Junior Interdistricts with double gold"
date: 2024-06-11
excerpt: "A total of 27 Scots made the long trip South to Rossendale in Lancs on Sunday for the UK Intercounties and 7 medals (3 team and 4 individual) represents a very good return for the Scottish contingent. Nearly 500 young runners from as far afield as Exeter, Kent and Inverness took part in some typically grim Lancashire weather."
---

A dominant performance from Isla Paterson of Gala Harriers saw her take the UK Intercounties title by over 2 minutes and lead Scotland East to team gold in the Under 19 Women’s race with Strathearn’s Tasha Myles backing her up in 12th place. It was the standout result from a hugely successful day on the Lancashire hills.

In the Under 19 men’s race, Rowan Taylor of Lasswade finished in an impressive 3rd place behind two GB Internationals while Silver for Dan McGuire of Harmeny for the under 17 men, backed up by Central’s Joel Gillan in 13th and Dan’s Harmeny team-mate, James Wotherspoon, in 16th secured a team bronze for Scotland East.

It was team bronze too for Scotland East’s U15 boys as Seb Darlow of Gala, Jack Foley of Moorfoots and Harmeny’s Rory Beattie finished in 7th, 10th and 17th respectively.

Big thanks to @CrieffHydro for their ongoing support of Scottish Athletics Junior Hill Running and also @ArnoldClark and @StruansPerth Rentals who provided the minibuses for the trip South!
