_From organiser Ranjit Thomas:_

Despite bad weather Brian Marshall (HELP) finished with a very respectable time or 56.56. Tom Smith (Lochaber) and Adam Anderson (Carnethy) were a close second and third.

Charlotte Morgan, smiling through her considerable back pain, was first female at 72.42 followed by Shirley Singh and Joan Wilson (Carnethy).

All competitors finished with a healthy covering of mud and blood and seemed to relish what was 11.5 km of rock and bog, enjoyed thoroughly in the presence of rain, wind, fog, poor visibility and treacherous conditions underfoot.

The Mountain Rescue, as always, did a tremendous job supporting the event.
