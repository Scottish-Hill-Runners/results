While we sometimes wait months for anything new, it seems that everyone's out doing great things in a burst of post-lockdown activity! Since this unfortunately coincides with a difficult time for our Long-Distance archivist (Peter Duggan), it may take a wee while to catch up with documenting all the updates and new rounds, but please rest assured we will get there and everything will be properly reported in due course. To give an idea of what's coming, we've been sent the following in the past two weeks alone:

*   Keri Wallace's new Glen Coe Corbetts Round
*   Finlay Wild's Mamores and Tranter's Round times
*   Katie Henderson's Cairngorm 4000s time
*   Stuart Paterson's Ochils 500 time
*   Ian Stewart's epic new Cairngorm Parkrun
*   Ally Beaven's Cairngorm Big 6 time
*   John Fleetwood's new Loch Shiel Round

Please feel free to keep sending news of further achievements because we're always interested, but understand that it may be a few weeks before we can catch up completely.

Thanks, Peter
