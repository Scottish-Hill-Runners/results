Fyrish 3 Monuments Hill Race, Saturday 19 May, 1pm – 13km – 620m ascent

New route. Registration and race start/finish at Evanton Football Pitch from 11:30. Race starts 1pm.

Mixture of forest roads and steep hillside running through the lower 2 Fyrish monuments before reaching the main Fyrish Monument on the summit. Descent is by the same route back to the football pitch. The distance is 13km with 620m ascent. There will be a U18 race of approximately 6km to the lower monument and an U12 race of approximately 2.5km. Entry on the day - £5 adults and £3 U18 and £1 U12.
