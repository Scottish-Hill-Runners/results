title: "Criffel Race - Deferred. New date to be confirmed"
date: 2020-03-12
excerpt: "The Criffel Hill Race scheduled for Sunday 15th March will not go ahead after risk assessment takes account the disproportionate medical, paramedical and mountain rescue support to the event"
---

Race Deferred. New date TBC.

The race organiser does not think this race should go ahead under the current circumstances. The risk assessment takes into account the disproportionate medical, paramedical and mountain rescue support to the event, and the effect on the local health/emergency services if they require to be isolated.
