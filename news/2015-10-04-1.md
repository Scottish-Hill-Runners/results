Here are the final championship standings of 2015. It looks like 93 people have earned SHR mugs - but remember you have to be a member to receive a mug - if you are not the membership secretary will soon be chasing you !

The champions are:  
Seniors - Tom Owens (Shettleston) and Jasmin Paris (Carnethy);  
Over 40s - Shona Robertson (Shettleston) and Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy);  
Over 50s - Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) and Valerie Houston (Westerlands);  
Over 60s - Jane Kerridge (Deeside) and Ewan Paterson (Moray RR);  
Over 70s - Gibson Fleming (Westerlands);Under 23s - James Waldie (Carnethy);  
Senior Teams - Carnethy (Male) & Carnethy (Female);  
Over 40 Teams - Fife (Female) & Carnethy (Male).

Out of this list, the only new champions are Shona Robertson and Gibson Fleming. All the others have either won before (often many times) or are defending champions. For Stewart Whitlie, it is the 6th time he has won the Over 40s, with the last time being in 2009, and the 4th time he has won the Over 50s! It is also the 4th time that James Waldie and Ewan Paterson have won their categories.
