Congratulations to Jill Stephen who has the ladies competition in control, but the situation in the men’s competition is more complex. Current leaders are Sam Alexander (Westies) and James Espie (Deeside Runners). Both can improve on their scores with good results at Caerketton.

And Joe Symonds (Shettleston) and James Waldie (Carnethy) have just raced five of the required six races so far. Good results for them next week would put them in with a chance of winning also…………….

Full details here - [http://chris-upson.com/bogandburn/2016/BogBurn2016\_r11.htm](http://chris-upson.com/bogandburn/2016/BogBurn2016_r11.htm)

Caerketton will be at Hillend car-park next Wednesday with the race starting at 7.30.

And Bog & Burn Series prize-giving will be in the Steading pub afterwards.
