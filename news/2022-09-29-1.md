This year's SHR do and prize giving will take place after the FRAs on the 15th of October!!

**Where:** At the Grassmarket Community hall, 86 Candlemaker row, Edinburgh, EH1 2QA

**When:** Saturday the 15th of October 7:30pm - 12:00am

**How:**  Tickets are free to all SHR members, just email Jill at: [secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk) to secure your place, as we have limited numbers.

Non-members can also attend for a small fee of £5 a ticket. Payment is to: 'Scottish Hill runners' Sort code: 80-11-10 Account number: 00598107 and again please email [secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:secretary@scottishhillrunners.uk) to secure your place!

**Other important information:** Please note there is no meal this year, but the hall is very central, with lots of places to grab some food beforehand.

Also, if you won a trophy last year could you please organise to return it to a committee member before the do. Thanks!

Looking forward to seeing you all there!
