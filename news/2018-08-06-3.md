---
title: "Glenshee 9 race results and report"
date: 2018-08-06
excerpt: "Wins for Carnethy's Andy Fallas & Helen Bonsor at the Glenshee 9 race on Sunday 5th August"
---

Dry and mostly clear conditions for the biennial Glenshee9 race which takes in the 9 Munro summits around Glenshee ski area. 109 runners out of 132 starters completed the course with a strong showing from Carnethy hill runners.

Andrew Fallas overhauled Eoin Lennon to win in a time of 3:19:14 adding his name to the trophy for a second time and improving on his 2011 win time. Liam Braby came in 3rd to make it a clean sweep on the podium for Carnethy.

Helen Bonsor led the ladies home with Anwen Darlington in second and Sally Fawcett in third.

David Kerr led the MV40 with local runner Alan Sealy winning the MV50 class. Don Reid led the MV60. Fiona Zeiner was the first FV40 and in a strong FV50 category it was Michelle Hetherington who took the honours.

Thanks goes to the 20+ members and supporters of Braemar Mountain Rescue Team who provided race cover and to Glenshee Ski Centre for hosting.

Ali Hubbard (race organiser)
