With the Statistician returning from a long trip to the Himalayas we are now able give you the latest SHR Scottish Champs listing. Despite three races in three weeks spread from the north-east to the south of Scotland, fields have been large and an impressive number have done all three races. One thing you might notice is that for the first time we have a female Over 60 listed !
