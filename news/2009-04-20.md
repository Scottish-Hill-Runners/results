From race organiser Tom Barr:

Saturday saw fine weather for the 2009 running of the Hunter’s Bog Trot, and despite clashes with other races this busy weekend, a good number turned out for the Holyrood Park-based event. Host club HBT dominated throughout the field, winning both the men’s and women's races. First male was HBT’s Joe Symonds (28:10), followed by team-mate Phil Mowbray (29:32) with Bellahouston’s Mark Johnston in third (29:59). Claire Gordon of HBT was first woman home (33:25) followed by team-mate Jane Hart (43:32) with Penicuik’s Sarah Blake in third place. First male veteran was Westerland’s Chris Upson and first female V40 was Heather McIntosh of Garscube Harriers. The men’s team prize went to HBT with Edinburgh University as runners up. HBT also took the women’s team prize with Penicuik in second place.

HBT club secretary Robin Thomas presented an array of weird and wonderful prizes with usual idiosyncratic aplomb, and the runners dispersed, presumably to rehydrate… trotter-style.
