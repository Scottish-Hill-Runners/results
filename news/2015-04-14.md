---
title: "Screel Hill Race Results"
date: 2015-04-14
excerpt: "James Espie (Deeside) and Kirsten Leslie (Dalbeattie RC) won at Screel on Saturday 11th April"
---

James Espie (Deeside) won with a time of 31:54 ahead of Matt Sullivan (Shettlestone) and James Haworth (Galloway Harriers).

The ladies race was won by Kirsten Leslie (Dalbeattie RC) in 38:37 ahead of Jo Zakrzewski (Dumfries RC) and Shirley Singh (U/A).
