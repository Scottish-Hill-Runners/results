---
title: "Scottish Juniors Impress At Junior Home International"
date: 2015-09-21
excerpt: "Three individual medals for Laura Stark, Emily Nicholson and Ross Gollan as well as good team performances by Scotland."
---

Scotland’s junior hill runners had a superb day out in the sun at the British and Irish **Junior Mountain Running Championships** in Betws-y-Coed on Saturday.

The event incorporated a Junior Home International team competition and there were three individual medals for **Laura Stark**, **Emily Nicholson** and**Ross Gollan** as well as good team performances by Scotland.

Full report on Scottish Athletics website here - [http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/scots-impress-at-junior-home-international/](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/scots-impress-at-junior-home-international/)
