I hope you all enjoyed the first of this years Championship races at The Cobbler. We want to start with a huge apology to all our amazing winners from 2023\* for not managing to hold an event to present your trophies, and properly celebrating your successes. We did try to get a belated ceilidh together in Glasgow but recognise this wasn’t ideal for everyone.

Feedback we have received points to organising an event aligned with a Championship race near the end of the calendar year, where we know many of you will be traveling, staying over and up for a bit of a social - Meall ‘a Bhuachaille and Resipole stand out as memorable events.

We will therefore plan that into next year’s champs’ races and make sure we get as many of you along as possible. For this year, 2024, we are in discussion with the Race Director at Tinto to use that popular event for this year’s awards…watch this space. 

The SHR Committee

\*for a full list of the winners from 2023 please see the SHR 2023 Journal
