A great night was had by all who made it to Grandtully! The prize giving was held in the excellent Village Hall. Ian White of Run 4 It presented the Run 4 It SHR Senior Championship trophies – many thanks to them for again supporting the series. After the meal at the Grandtully lodge, plenty of energy was expended in dancing to the Hoochie Koochie ceilidh band back in the hall!
