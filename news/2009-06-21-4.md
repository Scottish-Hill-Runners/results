title: "Ward wins the Lairig Ghru, 20 June"
date: 2009-06-21
excerpt: "Ray Ward (HBT), Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston) and Robert Brookes (Cosmics) finish first, second and third...  '"
---

Seventy runners completed the Lairig Ghru race, led by Ray Ward (HBT). Matthew Sullivan (Shettleston) was second and Robert Brookes (Cosmics) third. Ali Highes (Metro) was the first woman.
