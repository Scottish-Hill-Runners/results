---
title: "Robin Morris and the North Berwick Law Race 1st Aug 2018"
date: 2018-07-26
excerpt: "A new trophy, to mark Robin Morris's contribution to Scottish Hill Running, has been donated to the North Berwick Law Race and will be presented to the winner."
---

Robin Morris, the first secretary of the SHRA, well known hill-runner and race organiser, died in February of this year. To mark his contribution to hill-running in Scotland, and in particular his association with the North Berwick Law race(that he won several times), Douglas Scott, a friend from their schooldays together, has donated a new trophy to be awarded to the race winner. Roz Morris will present the trophy and Douglas Scott will speak about Robin at the prize-giving.

The race starts at 7.30pm from the harbour, North Berwick on Wednesday 1 st August .
