---
title: "Fifth WMRA Grand Prix Race, Austria, 10 August"
date: 2008-08-12
excerpt: "Edinburgh University student Yujiro Iida finishes sixteenth in the WMRA Grand Prix Race in Austria.  '"
---

Edinburgh University student (and SHR member) Yujiro Iida ran in the fifth WMRA Grand Prix Race of the year, at Ebensee in Austria, on 10 August. He finished a very creditable sixteenth out of 129. He puts his success down to the opportunities he's had in Scotland to compete in a variety of hill races of top-class quality.
