A very successful if slightly damp inagural double race weekend was held on the Isle of Eigg. Gareth Green was quickest overall seconds ahead of Adrian Davis. First Lady was Louise Crilly. Winners were presented with Eigg shaped cakes and other local produce provided other spot prizes. The only serious injury was picked up at the Saturday night Celidh. I am hoping that this will now develop into a 'Small Isles' Hill Series next year, incorporating Eigg , Rum and Knoydart

Many thanks to all helpers and participants.

_Laurie Anderson Race Organiser._
