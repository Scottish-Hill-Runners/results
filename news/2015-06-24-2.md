---
title: "Eagle Crag Race Report"
date: 2015-06-24
excerpt: "Report from Race Organiser"
---

On Saturday the 20th June 2015 there was the Lochaber Athletics Club Eagles Crag Race.

Another good turnout of 29 runners took on the short but tough climb up and over the Eagles Crag down to the West Highland Way and along it before heading back up and over the hill to the finish.

Overall winner was Callum Fraser in 01:02:27, pushed all the way by Jon Gay in 01:03:46. Steve Macdonald was 3rd with another fine run in 01:04:01.

First lady home was Lina Mardall in 01:17:37, with Nix Foster 2nd in 01:21:20 and Lucy Prins 3rd , in 01:30:28.

Alan Smith was 1st male vet 50 in 01:05:02 , with Ali Kennedy 2nd in 01:12:07.

Well done to everyone who took part on a overcast day and a big thanks to our band of volunteers who marshalled and officiated - Jim , Cammy , Ellen, Ernie, John, Hughie and Graham.
