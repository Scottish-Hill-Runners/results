_From Race Organiser Cameron Scott_

HBT's Joe Symonds stormed home ahead of Tom Owens and Iain MacCorquodale to take the honours at this year's Run And Become Tinto Hill Race. Carnethy's Charlotte Morgan took the ladies' prize. Fortunately the rain didn't become too heavy and there were plenty of smiling faces back at the hall for soup and prizegiving

I'd like to thanks the small army of volunteers from Carnethy and Tinto Hill Runners who help make this race happen, and Adrian at Run And Become for ongoing support. Please see Carnethy web site for searchable results and picture galleries
