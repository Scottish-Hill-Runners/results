---
title: "Penchrise Pen Hill Race - Sunday 21st September"
date: 2014-09-22
excerpt: "Wins and new course records for Brian Marshall & Charlotte Morgan at Penchrise Pen Hill Race."
---

Perfect conditions saw new records from Brian Marshall and Charlotte Morgan in this years Penchrise Pen Hill Race. Brian edged out local Rory Anderson by 5 seconds to win in 34:54 as they both went under the previous best time.

Charlotte was not far behind in 3rd regaining the ladies record in style, beating the previous best by almost 3 minutes and only 3 seconds outside the previous male record in 35:13.

Thanks to all who came along to compete and help.
