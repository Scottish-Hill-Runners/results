To celebrate the 30th running of the Two Breweries race, there will be a "Seriously Good Venison" BBQ (with veggie option), immediately following the race to help soak up the free Broughton Ale.  
  
The Venison is provided by Vicki (Venison), Mike the Marshall's daughter, who recently took over Fletchers venison.  
  
There will not be Ceilidh this year, but hopefully with a bit of good weather we can enjoy the post race food and banter.  
  
Entries are open on Entrycentral and by post.  
  
We hope to see you there.  
  
Peter and Elsie
