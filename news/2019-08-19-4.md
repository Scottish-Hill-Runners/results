title: "Lomonds Of Fife Race Results & Report"
date: 2019-08-19
excerpt: "Wins for Liam Braby (Carnethy) and Heather Anderson (Fife AC) at the Lomonds Of Fife Hill Race on Sunday 18th August."
---

Another very squally day greeted the 47th running of the Lomond's of Fife race. A late decision was made to avoid the famous steep slope where there were numerous injuries last year, no one complained! After the gun a group of 5 immediately sped off and put a distance between themselves and the chasing pack, this was extended when the Harris father and son combo missed a turn and ended up extending the run by a number of kilometres. In between driving rain and bright sun, the field scaled East and West Lomond followed by a run round the 'Bunnet Stane' then a huge descending traverse back to the forest and back to the start. The front 3 were now battling for a win eventually Liam Braby (Carnethy) prevailed with just 38 secs between the first three. First woman home was Heather Anderson (Fife) finishing 9th overall. Big thanks to all Marshal's especially those on hill tops who had hailstones in August.

Laurie Anderson (Race Organiser)
