---
title: "Seven Hills fall to Peachey and Mykura, 17 June"
date: 2007-06-17
excerpt: "Simon Peachey and Jill Mykura made it a double win for Carnethy.  '"
---

Last weekend Brian Marshall (HELP) beat Simon Peachey (Carnethy) in the relatively short Traprain Law Race; this weekend Peachey turned the tables on Marshall over the longer Seven Hills course, finishing more than two-and-a-half minutes ahead of him in 1:44:19, with Jamie Thin (HBT) third.

Jill Mykura (Carnethy), eighth overall, continued on her winning way; behind her Miranda Grant (HBT) overhauled Dhavala Stott (Sri Chinmoy) to be second woman.
