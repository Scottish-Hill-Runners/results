title: "Tracey Brindley wins OBB's Aonach Mor Uphill outright, 23 July"
date: 2010-07-27
excerpt: "Tracey Brindley (Ochils) wins in a new women's record; Mike Bozzard (Lochaber) is second and first man with Mark Johnston (Bellahouston) third...  '"
---

In her first hill race since October 2005 Tracey Brindley (Ochils) won [Off Beat Bikes'](http://www.offbeatbikes.co.uk/) Aonach Mor Uphill outright, taking three seconds off the record she set in 2002. Mike Bozzard (Lochaber) was second (and first man) 17 seconds behind her, with Mark Johnston (Bellahouston) a further four seconds adrift in third. Emma O'Shea (Deeside) finished fifth overall but had to settle for being second woman.

_Corina MacDonald of Off Beat Bikes says:_

We were very pleased with the turn out especially for a Friday night race. Unfortunately, unbeknown to me at the time of working out the prizes, there were two sets of duplicate numbers which led to the errors in my ‘prize giving’. (I was very worried about this as I know that runners take great pride and interest in their times etc!)

I look forward to next year's Off Beat Bikes Aonach Mhor Uphill Race and hope that everyone enjoyed the event!
