The annual end of season SHR prize giving and social 'do' is to be held this year at Badaguish Outdoor Centre. It will take place over the weekend of 30th Oct - 1st Nov, coinciding with the last Championship race of the season at [Meall a Bhuachaille](http://shr.uk.com.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0110&System=Metric&Month=10), just down the road !

The 2007 'do' was also staged at Badaguish and it proved to be an ideal venue, with first class accommodation and facilites, and making for an excellent base for a great weekend away amongst the hills.

The Committee currently however are looking for an individual (or individuals ?) to act as chief organiser(s) for the event. Support and assistance (eg with arranging the catering, costings etc) will be available from Dave Cummins and other members of the Committee. Given that the accommodation is already booked, the role of chief organiser really shouldn't be that arduous or time consuming !

Anyone interested should contact Dave in the first instance(dave.cummins3@btinternet.com). Any offer of help will be very warmly received !
