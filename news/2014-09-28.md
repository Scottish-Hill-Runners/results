An excellent championship race with a great win for Andrew Fallas and Jasmin Paris, both of Carnethy. Carnethy also took both male and female team prizes.

  

This race was always going to be emotional and the presentation of the Westies trophy in memory of Elsie Baxter (Dr Elspeth W. Scott) brought a moment of reflection at the end of the prizegiving.

  
  

I'd like to thank all those who contributed to a memorable race, all the marshalls and helpers, the landowners and Moffat Mountain Rescue.

  
  

The race was run under the new SHR rules and no issues were noted. Well done to SHR for such sensible guidance.

  
  

I will manage the race next year but would like an individual/team to take over from me in 2016. Dont all rush....

  
  

Peter Baxter
