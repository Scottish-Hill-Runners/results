**scottish**athletics **Junior Championships**

The Junior Championship races also counted as the first of this year's three Junior League races and as a trial for the WMRA International Youth Challenge (to be held in Germany on 27 June). They were organised by Don Reid together with Martin Hyman and were held before the senior race. The races were relatively well-attended and the winners were:

U14 girls : Mhairi Mc Aleer  
U16 girls : Alice Haining  
U19 girls : Catriona Buchanan  
U14 boys : Robert Patterson  
U16 boys : Adam Gaitens  
U19 boys : Alan Cherry

The second Junior League and Trial race is at Scolty on 7 June.

**Fun Run**

_Martin Hyman reports:_

"Continuous rain made the course slippery, but it did not seem to dim the smiles of all who took part. Remarkably Logan Rees broke the course record, with the first time under 8 minutes for the 1600m course with 80m of climbing. Everyone else ran well. Our most loyal customers, Struan and Ailsa Bennet, are coming on, literally and metaphorically, in leaps and bounds. The times of their first runs, 2 years ago, were 34.15 and 23.27 respectively.

Many thanks again to all who helped. Come back next year and bring your grandma."
