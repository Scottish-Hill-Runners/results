Due to very wet weather over the preceding few days (or weeks), the rivers near Shenavall and Larachantivore were uncrossable, so the course was shortened to a more or less out and back route from Poolewe.

The length was a bit over 19 miles, and the going was ... wet.

For those runners doing this event for the first time, rest assured that the congestion on the path from the walkers was exceptional, even if always good-natured, and we do not normally have the pleasure of seeing everyone who is in front of us coming back the other way. Especially, in this case, Murray Strain and Rob Sinclair, who you wondered if they were even in the same race!

The results for both the 13-mile and the longer race are on the Results menu, above.
