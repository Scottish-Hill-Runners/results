---
title: "Cheyne Hill concludes Cosmics' Summer Series, 21 August"
date: 2007-08-24
excerpt: "This was the fourth and final race in the Series. Oleg Chepelin (EUH&H) and Lois Noble (Cosmics) both set new course records.  '"
---

Cheyne Wood was the fourth and final race in the Cosmics' Summer Series, the others being Durris Mast, Balmedie Beach and Mither Tap.

Oleg Chepelin (EUH&H) won in a new record of 26:46, two minutes clear of Steven McGregor (MacStrong) and a bunch of Cosmics. The women's race was dominated by Cosmics, Lois Noble setting a new best of 37:15 with Marjon Van de Pol close behind her.
