The first race in the Junior League is on Saturday 22nd April. Note that the start time has changed - it is now 11:00am

If you are able to help Malcolm Patterson on the day that would be great - he is still looking for a few helpers.

All the details here - 

**DUMYAT JUNIOR RACES**

**Saturday, 22 April 2023**

**VENUE**  **(Dumyat, near Stirling)**

**Car Park:**     [**https://goo.gl/maps/UrdtHUiq3cGReoxd7**](https://goo.gl/maps/UrdtHUiq3cGReoxd7)

Approach roads are narrow, best from Bridge of Allan via Sheriffmuir Rd. If coming down the A9 from the north you can turn left at Greenloaning and come over the moor.

Please note that this car park is on the left when going up Sheriffmuir Road, and is about 1km beyond the (Forestry) Pendreich Car Park.

Please car share if possible and arrive in good time. 

**REGISTRATION**

Entries on the day only.   Will be in white campervan in the car park.

Open from 10.15am. Entry for each race closes 15mins before the start.  Entry fee is £2.  (Free for U8s).

**START**

An offroad route will be marked to take you 400m (5mins) from the car park down to the start.

**COURSES**

**11am**:     **Under 8** years old on the day. All runners accompanied please.   1.1km, 40m climb.

**11.15**:      **U11**  (born 2013 or 2014 or 2015 up to 22/4).                                  2.1km, 80m climb. 

**11.35**:      **U13**  (born 2011 or 2012)  _and_

                 **U15**  (born 2009 or 2010)       both classes run same course:          3km, 105m climb

 **12.00:     U17** (born 2007 or 2008)  _and_

                 **U20**  (born 2004, 2005 or 2006).   both classes run same course:  5.2km, 240m climb .

Course terrain is a mixture of well made-up tourist path, smaller paths and short sections across the moor which could be muddy and wet.  Studs or else trail shoes with a good grip are recommended.   Races all start at 185m and are on exposed moorland. Please bring full body cover, that is: waterproof top & bottom, hat and gloves. Be prepared to carry them. In event of bad weather courses may be changed and shortened.

**JUNIOR LEAGUE**

Some of these races form part of the scottishathletics 2023 Junior Hill Running League. The League categories are U15, U17 and U20, male and female (age groups as above).
