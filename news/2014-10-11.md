---
title: "Manor Water Results"
date: 2014-10-11
excerpt: "Wins For Graham Nash & Joanne Thom at Manor Water"
---

Graham Nash (Carnethy) won the Manor Water race in 1:15:04 ahead of Darin Dougal (Moorfoots) and Iain Gilmore (Carnethy).

Joanne Thom (Edinburgh AC) was first female in 01:24:02 with Eilis McKechanie (HBT) and Sarah Adam (Westerlands) in 2nd and 3rd place respectively.

  

Graham Nash takes a valuable overnight lead into the Man Or Mouse Competion ahead of Sunday's Pentland Skyline but last year's winner Jon Gay (Lochaber) is handily placed just over four minutes behind............
