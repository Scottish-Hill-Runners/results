Great weekend of racing coming up at Kincraig and Kingussie with the Suidhe Hill Race on Saturday 27th September at Kincraig. 15.00 start time

And the Creag Bheag race at Kingussie on Sunday 28th September - 12.00 midday start time.

Suidhe is the final junior Scottish championship counter and double points!!

Creag Bheag will see the introduction of the new Peter Morris Cup, for 1st Lady.
