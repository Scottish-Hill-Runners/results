---

The Balmedie Beach Bash, the second race in Cosmics' Summer Series, has been described as "a hill race without any hills". Hilly or not, the terrain suited Dan Whitehead (Cosmics): he finished half-a-minute ahead of Jason Williamson (also Cosmics) with Scott Young third. Briony Curtis (Edinburgh University) beat Fiona Dahl (Cosmics) to the women's prize.

Whitehead now has two first places in the four-race Series (best three results to count) and Williamson two seconds.
