**Braemar Highland Games**, 1 September

Scott Fraser (Lothian RC), who concentrates more on orienteering than hill running these days, retained the trophy for the hill race and in doing so beat several members of the Scottish team for the World Mountain Running Trophy in a fortnight's time. Jethro Lennox (Shettleston H) was second, Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) third and Grant Stewart (Edinburgh AC) a little way further back. Fourth-placed Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC) was first vet and Mary Wilkinson (Bingley H) the first woman.

[Photos on Cosmics' Flickr site](http://www.flickr.com/photos/41761208@N00/sets/72157601825679381/)

**Junior Home International Trials, Bonaly**, 2 September

Turnout for the Trials-cum-Junior League was disappointingly-thin, although full marks go to James Waldie (Cornwall AC) for travelling all the way from Penzance just to compete in the U16 Boys' race.
