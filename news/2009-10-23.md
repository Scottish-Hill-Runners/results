On Saturday 31st October, [Run4It](http://www.run4it.com/) are officially opening their [Inov-8](http://www.inov-8.com) Off-Road Running Centre at the [Glasgow Outdoor Experience](http://www.run4it.com/shops/glasgow-outdoor-experience/).

Run4It have teamed up with Inov-8, the British off-road running specialists, to offer Scotland’s biggest in-store range of trail and off-road running shoes, packs and accessories.

Inov-8 are offering free socks with every shoe purchase on the day, and are offering a prize draw to win shoes and accessories.

Run4It will have representatives from Inov-8 on hand to discuss the products, and members of Team Inov-8 present including [Jethro Lennox](http://team.inov-8.eu/2008/08/jethro-lennox.html) for those who would like to join for a run on the day.

For more details please contact Run 4 It on (0141) 5595450 or email [glasgow@run-4-it.com](mailto:glasgow@run-4-it.com)
