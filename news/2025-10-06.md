So Storm Amy did her worst and scuppered the Morven Hill Race and our end of year awards and Ceilidh on Saturday.  
It was a really tough decision for the organisers given conditions were different in different parts of the country, but as always safety has to come first and this is something race organisers continually have to grapple with.  
We are looking at a couple of options in order to complete this year’s championships and end of year awards and social.  
We will be back in touch as soon as that decision is finalised and if we can’t go ahead with the Ceilidh will look to refund those that have bought tickets.  
Thanks for your patience and understanding  
  
SHR Committee
