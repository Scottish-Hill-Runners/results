The final race in Bog & Burn series is Caerketton Hill race this Wednesday evening 14th August.

To make things easier on night, it will be appreciated if people can enter in advance via links below please:

[Entry Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd4qkMnqsnClfvxUPKbnWq299Hmx0aURLknHilqFcSigfQTEg/viewform) 

[Junior Entry](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdElGjHXB4YF69hclDXS7ReW0SbCv-ZRqriWPUCR6ZhFlgTlA/viewform)

Just bring along entry fee which is £5 for seniors, £1 for juniors.

For info, the development work at hillend is still ongoing, so no car park at the bottom as yet. Limited Car parking available on main road or at the top car park, so please aim to car-share if you can or come via  public transport, cycle or on foot.
