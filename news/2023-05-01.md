**Registration to collect race numbers, will be open from 5pm in the car park at Eden Mill Café carpark.**

**Registration will close at 6:40pm. Folk will need to allow approx. 15 minutes to get to the new startline.**

**Prize giving will take place at the finishline**

Note the slight change to the race route this year. And to the start/finish line. Revised map available here. And the distance and ascent has reduced slightly.
