Thirty-nine runners took part in Saturday’s Lochaber Athletics club Eagle Crag hill race and conditions were perfect at the start of race. Lochaber’s Steven Trail was the victor in the men’s race followed by Steven Burns and Tiago Cruz (Gau Bajouca ). This trio had a battle for pole position all the way round the tough course , with the sun and the temperature rising making it even tougher.

Helen Gertig U/A, was first lady home with a great run (come on Helen time to join a club). Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber) was second and Nix Forster third with their usual consistent runs.

Thank you to all the time keepers and marshals - Jo, Corrina, Pamela, Cammy, Graham, Keith and John.
