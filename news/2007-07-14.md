From Clare Rumgay:

"Despite a busy calendar, 31 competitors turned out for the Off Beat Bikes Aonach Mor Uphill. The midges helped the runners off to a fast start and soon the relentless climb began. Colin Donnelly looked comfortable at the finish at the top station, winning in 23:53. John Hepburn, another veteran, was second in 25:25, with Keith’s Stephen Gill next in 25:30. Ken Whyte put in another impressive climb, finishing first vet 50 and fourth overall in 27:12. Coming back from injury, Raymond Murdoch (2nd male vet 50) completed in a personal best of 32:58.

Oban’s Nicola Meekin was a worthy ladies winner, finishing 5th overall in 28:25, with Dawn Scott second in 29:56 and Lyndsey Shaw third in 32:43. First lady over 40 was Linda MacEwan who was fourth lady overall in 32:43.

With the gondola off, we were then treated to some valuable descent practice on the downhill mountain bike course, followed by a prizegiving in the ladies’ toilets……….I kid you not – anything to escape the evil midge…….."
