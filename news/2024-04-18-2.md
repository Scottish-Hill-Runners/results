Another fantastic evening over in North Ayrshire for the Kaim Hill Race at Fairlie. The sun once again made its usual appearance for North Ayrshire Athletics Club and 120 finishers, the first race in the bog and burn series.

Overall winner,on his birthday, in a time of 27:15 was Kirean Cooper of Carnethy HRC and first lady Ella Peters of Westerlands CCC in a time of 35:30

Alasdair McCallum (Race Organiser)
