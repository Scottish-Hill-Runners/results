**Fyrish 3 tops route change**

Unfortunately the Fyrish 3 tops Hill Race route this weekend has had to be changed due to a new plantation and a significant number of windblown trees on the top section of the old course. It will no longer go to the 2 tops behind Fyrish Monument and the monument will be the highest top. The distance is 13km with 540m ascent. Category BM.

There will also be a junior race of approximately 5km.

Details of the route/map available on the Scottish Hill Racing website here - [http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0300](http://www.scottishhillracing.co.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0300)
