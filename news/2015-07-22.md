The entry list for the Two Inns on August 8th 2015 has been finalised. We will be in touch with those on the reserve list over the next few day to let them know if they have a place. We will also be sending all runners an email containing race information.

This will contain important information with regard to rules and changes to the finish of the race. It is important that all runners read this. KEY CHANGES - REGISTER AT THE NATIONAL TRUST VISITOR CENTRE GLENCOE, BUS ALL RUNNERS TO START, FINISH AT NATIONAL TRUST VISITOR CENTRE GLENCOE.

I will not accept any entries on the day so if you are thinking of this do not turn up expecting to sweet talk me into letting you run. IT WILL NOT HAPPEN.

... To all those taking part, here's to good weather and a brilliant race for all.

Linda
