**Greenmantle Dash 2023 Race Report**

After a break of two years the Greenmantle Dash was on again and 109 runners and 17 junior runners from near and far enjoyed the short race packed with a bit of everything in bright sunshine and freezing temperatures.

Well done to all the runners for braving the wall, the ice-cold river and the boggy field. Congratulations to Finn Lydon from Carnethy (15:57) and Morag McClelland from HBT (19:45) for their impressive wins. Special mention also to Sam Stephens from Doss AC (23:18) for the win of the Michael Greens trophy for his impressive downhill skills – having a great time dressed up as a banana.

Many thanks to all helpers from Penicuik Harriers and to local supporters!

Results are provisional only, please contact Penicuik Harriers if you spot any errors.
