title: "Newtyle Hill Race"
date: 2015-04-20
excerpt: "Race report and results for Newtyle Hill Race on Wednesday 15th April"
---

The Newtyle Hill race enjoyed a beautiful fine clear evening in ideal conditions as 43 runners set off through Craigmad wlood outside Dunkeld.  
Brian Bonnyman was seen clearly in the lead going up the final climb to Newtyle ahead of Iain Gilmore and Owen O'Neil and that is how it stayed at the finish. Brian set a new record in 31.14 so impressive running over a minute ahead.  
  
Judith Turner was a clear winner of the ladies race in a new record of 37.24. Second lady was Hilary Ritchie and third Lisa Blackley. The Dunkeld and Birnam Growers Association allowed us to use The Field and the marshalls and young lassies who helped at the end did a great job.... thank you.

Adrian Davis

(Race Organiser)
