---
title: "Scots pack well in the Alps, 24 May"
date: 2008-05-27
excerpt: "Alasdair Anthony, Grant Stewart and Joe Symonds finished in the top ten of the WMRA Grand Prix Race at Saillon in Switzerland.  '"
---

There was a strong Scottish contingent at the second WMRA Grand Prix Race of the year, at Saillon in Switzerland.

Alasdair Anthony, Grant Srewart and Joe Symonds were eighth, ninth and tenth respectively in the men's race, which was won by Jonathan Wyatt (New Zealand). Matt Bell (19th), Kenny Richmond (23rd) and Euan Jardine (34th) were also well up in the field of 145.

In the women's race Helen Stuart was a creditable 17th.
