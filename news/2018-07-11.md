The championship listings after Glamaig are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2018 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2018 - Female.xls) for women.  
or can be accessed via the Championship menu to the right, where there are also all time lists.

So far there have been two short races and one medium race, so everything is still to play for as a lot will depend on who manages to run well in the long races. Having said that some people are dominating their category, eg Alasdair Anthony in Over 40, Veronique Oldham Over 50s and Jocelyn Scott Over 60s, so will be favourites. Carnethy are leading all four team contests (along with Westerlands in Senior men), but all these are still very open and could go any way.

Any queries to [Andy Spenceley](mailto:committee10@scottishhillrunners.uk) SHR Statistician
