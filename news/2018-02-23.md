On 22 February 2018, Helen Rennard became the first woman to complete a winter Tranter's Round, recording 23 hours 29 mins 7 secs anticlockwise in proper winter conditions and mostly solo with support towards the end from Kevin Woods.

The winter Tranter is still a huge, underrated/overlooked prize now the Ramsay's been done, so congratulations to Helen for this significant achievement!
