The latest championship listings after Eildon, the only short race of the 2021 championship, are now available (just in time before the next championship race at the Two Breweries!).

[Here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2021 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2021 - Female.xls) for women.

Remember, due to the late cancellation of Yetholm Shepherds Show Race - another casualty of COVID19 - the championship will be best 3 from 5, although all but Over 60s and 70s must do at least one Long and one Medium.
