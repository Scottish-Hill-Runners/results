excerpt: "David Wilby (Highland Hill Runners) won the 25 mile race and the 13 mile race was won by Steven Worsley (Inverness Harriers)"
---

The 25 mile race was won by David Wilby ahead of Ross Bannerman in 2nd and Douglas Bartholomew in 3rd.

In the ladies race, Mary Kiely was 1st with Katy Boocock and Jess Whistance 2nd & 3rd respectively.

[Results](RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0452&Year=2015)

Steven Worsley was the winner in the 13 mile race ahead of Geoff Simpson and Ryan MacLean in 2nd & 3rd.

First lady was Kathleen Steventon with Geraldien Filos 2nd and Jane Patterson 3rd.
