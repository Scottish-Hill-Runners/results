Jon Gay and Diane Baum lead the Long Classics Series after 5 races, having both completed all races so far. They are closely followed by Mark Harris on 4 races, and Dan Gay and Stewart Whitlie, both on 3 races. There are 5 more races in the series, the next counter being Ben Rinnes on Saturday 26th July.
