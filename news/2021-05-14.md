excerpt: "The EntryCentral link for online entry to the Durisdeer race at 12.00 on Saturday 12th June is available."
---

Can entrants please note that in order to comply with Covid regs, entries are online via the attached link. If unable to use the online entry please email the organiser with the usual entry details and be added manually. Parking, registration and post-race food will be in the field on the left once through the village. Although food will be available following the race, this will be basic compared to previous years (bring a flask).

The Church hall we usually use will not be available this year, as such facilities will also be basic. Everyone is well versed in Covid prevention requirements, please be prepared to exercise common sense in communal areas. There will be a briefing at 11:45, all runners are required to attend. We look forward to a return to racing and to welcoming you to Durisdeer.

Adam
