The weather forecast for high winds on Saturday there is a possibility that the race route will be shortened - which is and out to the top of Kirk Craigs, and back with a slight deviation on the descent to avoid congestion. This will be marked and marshalled. Regardless of the route, it is imperative that ALL runners must abide by the full kit requirements stipulated. There will be a kit check before the race. Please remember the race entry fee (£10) is cash only on the day. There will also be a raffle for an exclusive bottle of single malt whisky after the race and tickets can be bought at registration (£2 a ticket), so please bring some extra cash. Grant Baxter
