The annual 'Training and Inspiration evening' for the West Highland Way Race (and other ultra events) organised by _Run and Become_ takes place in Edinburgh on Wednesday 20 January. It starts at 7pm and finishes at 9.45pm. The venue is St Bride's Community Centre, 10 Orwell Terrace, Dalry Road, Edinburgh EH11 2DY.

Full details (including directions and information on how to reserve yourself a place) are [here](Download.aspx?Item=WHW_Evening_20100120.pdf).
