---
title: "Cioch Mhor - Senior Hill Race Cacelled"
date: 2023-08-22
excerpt: "The senior race at Cioch Mhor this year has been cancelled"
---

Regrettably we have taken the decision to not run the senior race this year, but look forward to returning in 2024. The junior race will go ahead as usual on Saturday 23rd September, with registration from 11:00 to 11:45 in the Dingwall leisure centre car park, and the race starting at 12:10 in the usual place behind Tulloch Castle.
