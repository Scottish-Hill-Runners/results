Hot off the press is the great news that Scotland won the overall team competition in today's Junior Home International. Particularly impressive was a 1.2.3.4 domination in the Boys U19 race.

There was an individual Scottish win by Scott Macdonald in the U19B - followed home six seconds later by Matthew Gillespie, with Alex Henry and Robbie Simpson completing the Scottish whitewash (or should that be blue and whitewash?). In the non-international races there were wins for Alice Haining (U14G) and Max Aitcheson (U14B). The U19 girls managed a 2.3.4 (Mhairi Inglis, Sarah Inglis and Jessica Martin) and won the team, but were denied individual glory by England's Hannah Bateson.
