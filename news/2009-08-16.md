Brian Marshall had a straightforward win at the Lomonds of Fife in bright and breezy conditions. Jon Ascroft finished 2nd, with Manny Gorman in hot pursuit in 3rd position.

Rachel Fagan of Gala was first lady, with Ellen Homewood 2nd and Jocelyn Scott 3rd.
