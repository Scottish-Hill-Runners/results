Wednesday's North Berwick Law race caught the evening sunshine - in contrast to last year, when the Lothian Monsoon forced its cancellation.

The headline results are:  
1st Individual - Dave Wright, HBT - 20.35  
2nd Individual - Stuart MacLeod EAC - 21.10  
3rd Individual - Bruce Smith Carnethy - 21.39  
1st Boy (u15) - Cameron Law Dunbar - 25.07  
1st Girl (u15) - Rachael Grieve Anster Haddies  
1st Lady - Kate Jenkins HBT - 23.15  
1st Male Vet - Jonathon Knox Anster Haddies - 21.58  
1st Lady Vet - Megan Mowbrey HBT - 23.31  
1st Team of 3 - Carnethy - Bruce Smith, Shane Bouchier, Mike Rodden  
1st N.B. Local - John Smart Unattached - 22.48
