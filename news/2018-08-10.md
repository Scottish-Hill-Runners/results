The spreadsheet with the final SHR 2018 Bog & Burn placings after Caerketton on Wednesday is now available.

Congratulations to Gregor Stewart for the win by just one point over Alastair Thurlbeck.

The female winner was Ruth Crewe ahead of Jane Oliver and Katie Rademaker.

Congratulations too to the male category winners – Justin Carter (M40), Micky Anderson (M50) and Don Reid (M60).

And special congratulations to the two Ochils’ runners who completed all twelve races – Stuart Brodie and Davie Duncan.
