Andrew Fallas (Carnethy) has moved into the lead with 2 races to go in the SHR Long Classics Series after winning 2 of the last 3 races (Glenshee 9 and Ochils 2000). With best 5 results to count there is still everything to play for and several people could yet top the table. Eilidh Raistrick (Highland) is leading the women, but could be caught by in form Jasmin Paris (Carnethy).
