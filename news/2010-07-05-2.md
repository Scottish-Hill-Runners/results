title: "European team bronzes for Simpson and Hendry: Bulgaria, 4 July"
date: 2010-07-05
excerpt: "The UK's Junior Men's team is third and Catriona Buchanan (Central) is the first UK Junior Woman to finish..."
---

Robbie Simpson (Deeside) and Alex Hendry (Central) were in the UK Junior Men's team that finished third in the European Mountain Running Championships. Simpson was sixth overall and Hendry sixteenth.

Catriona Buchanan (Central) was the leading Brit in the Junior Women's race, coming home in sixth.

There's a fuller report on the **scottish**athletics' web site [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=4962).
