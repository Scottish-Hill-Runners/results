Prasad Prasad took the Tinto race firmly in his grasp and, on a fine day to be out on the hill, came home a clear winner in a record size field. Al Anthony was 2nd and Will Horsley 3rd, whilst Kenny Richmond in 4th was also 1st Vet. Claire Gordon was first woman, ahead of junior Scout Adkin and Emma Birnie, with Shirley Singh finishing as 1st Vet.
