Relays In The Ochils – 7th March 2026

Keep the Date – Details to follow

Runners from Ochil Hill Runners are going to be organising a relay race in 2026. It will be a three leg race with teams of four runners (two solo legs and one paired leg) in the hills above Alva in the Ochils. The legs will be run simultaneously with staggered start times.

All the details of entry and routes will follow but for now note the date of Saturday 7th March 2026 and start thinking about getting organised into teams of four.
