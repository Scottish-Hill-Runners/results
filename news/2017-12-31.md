title: "Callander Crags Race"
date: 2017-12-31
excerpt: "The Callander Crags Race will be on Saturday 6th January."
---

The race will start at 1:00pm on Saturday 6th January 2018.

Registration will be from 12:00 at the bottom of Tulipan Crescent. (FK17

8AR)

Please park at the Station road car park and just walk out to the bottom end of Tulipan. Registration at the red VW Campervan as usual.

The race is strictly for fun but ,as usual, there are some fabulous prizes and the joy of taking part.

Children are welcome , as long as there is an adult with them who can keep up.
