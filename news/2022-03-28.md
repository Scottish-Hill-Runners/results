**DUMYAT JUNIOR RACES**

**Sunday, 10 April 2022**

**VENUE**   **(Dumyat, near Stirling)**

Registration/Start:  Dumyat Car Park    [Location Map](http://www.streetmap.co.uk/map?x=281332&y=698020&z=115&sv=281332,698020&st=4&ar=y&mapp=map&searchp=ids&dn=775&ax=281332&ay=698020&lm=0)   [https://goo.gl/maps/YidjCYsXAAMNhm5v6](https://goo.gl/maps/YidjCYsXAAMNhm5v6)     

Approach roads are narrow, best from Bridge of Allan (or from north) via Sherrifmuir Rd

Please note that this car park is very small.  You are advised to park at the lower Pendreich Car Park from where you can follow a path up to registration. Please car share if at all possible and arrive in good time.

**REGISTRATION**

Entries on the day only.   Will be in campervan in the Dumyat car park.

Open from 9.30am. Entry for each race  closes 10mins before the start.  Entry fee is £2.  (Free for U9s).

**COURSES**

**10am**:     **Under 8** years old on the day. All runners accompanied please.   1km, 55m climb.

**10.15**:      **U11**  (born 2012 or 2013 or 2014 up to 10/4).                               2km, 100m climb. 

**10.35**:      **U13**  (born 2010 or 2011 )  _and_

                 **U15**  (born 2008 or 2009)       both classes run same course:      3km, 125m climb

 **11.00:     U17** (born 2006 or 2007)  _and_

                 **U20**  (born 2003, 2004 or 2005).   both classes run same course:  5km, 235m climb .

Course terrain is a mixture of well made-up tourist path, smaller paths and short sections across the moor which could be muddy and wet.  Studs or else trail shoes with a good grip are recommended.   Races all start at 185m and are on exposed moorland. Please bring full body cover, waterproof top & bottom, hat and gloves. Be prepared to carry them. In event of bad weather courses may be changed and shortened.
