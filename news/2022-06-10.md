**Long Classics Series Update after 3 races**

After a gap of 2 years, you will be pleased to know Scottish Long Classics series is back and up and running in 2022!

Same format as in previous years, with best 4 scores to count. Details of series under Championships link.

One minor change to scoring this year, is that scoring for women will be based on time of 1st woman in each race rather than time of 1st main.

After first 3 races, leaders are **Andy Fallas** of Carnethy HRC and **Chloe Lumsdon** of Ochils.

7 men and 2 women have completed all 3 races so far.

Next race up in series is Arrochar Alps on 25th June.
