**Scottish Hill Runners Annual “do” and prize giving !**

_Saturday 26th October 2019 at Badaguish Outdoor Centre, Aviemore_

This year our annual do and prize giving will take place after the Meall a’ Bhuachaille race, the last event in the SHR championship. The venue will be the hall at Badaguish, also used for race registration earlier in the day.

Prize giving for the SHR Championship will take place at around 6.30pm. We will again be awarding a range of trophies to category winners in this year’s Senior Championship.

Prize giving will be followed by the dinner at around 7.30pm. There will be a three course meal with a variety of choices including veggie. There will be dancing afterwards to the Darloch ceilidh band.

There is accommodation available at Badaguish and locally.

The cost for the do is just £20. Please book as soon as possible to be sure of your place!

Further details from Dave Scott at [dcbscott@tiscali.co.uk](mailto:dcbscott@tiscali.co.uk)

If you wish to book a place contact Angela Mudge at [angela.mudge@btinternet.com](mailto:angela.mudge@btinternet.com%20a) to book your place and transfer money to SHR account ………….. with nameDO.
