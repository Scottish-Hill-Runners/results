The organiser has asked us to remind runners that, as usual, runners will be required to carry the following standard safety equipment:

• water and wind-proof whole body cover, plus other body cover appropriate for the weather conditions  
• a map of the route, a compass and a whistle.  
Your kit will be checked at the start of the race.
