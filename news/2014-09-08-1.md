Due to an admin error, Iain & Andrew Gilmore were placed the wrong way round in the Bishop Downhill results. Many thanks to Andrew for pointing out that Iain was in fact the race winner and the series results have now been updated. So it’s all to play for at the last race in the series, the Caerketton Doonhiller on Wednesday evening!
