Nine athletes have been selected to represent Scotland at this year's World Mountain Running Association (WMRA) World Long Distance Mountain Running Challenge, including defending champion Jethro Lennox. The Challenge will take place over the Kaisermarathon course in Soll, Austria on Saturday October 10th.

The race is of marathon distance, with around 40% on tarmac and the rest on trails and paths, starting at an altitude of 700m and finishing at 1800m, with approximately 1800m of climb in total.

The Scottish team is:

Men

Alasdair Anthony (Ochil)

Jethro Lennox (Shettleston)

Simon Peachey (Cosmic)

Paul Raistrick (Highland Hill Runners)

Kenny Richmond (Bellahouston Road Runners)

Women

Claire Gordon (HBT)

Megan Mowbray (HBT)

Andrea Priestley (Ochil)

Iona Robertson (Bellahouston Road Runners)

Event details and the full start list (including a certain Jonathan Wyatt) can be viewed [here](http://www.tourdetirol.com/english/tour_de_tirol/tour_de_tirol0/kaisermarathon0.html)
