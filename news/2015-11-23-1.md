---
title: "Druim Fada Chase - Race Report"
date: 2015-11-23
excerpt: "Report from the 2nd race in the Lochaber Winter Series."
---

Lochaber Athletic club had another great turnout for their second winter league race with 71 runners taking part in the Drumfada chase.

Blue skies and bright sunshine made conditions perfect for the race. It was still cold when out of the sunshine and today's winner Stephen Burns was feeling a bit cooler than most after competing for Great Britain in Australia just two weeks ago with temperatures in the high thirties.

Second place went to Michael Bossard , third to Craig Mattocks. Fourth place and first junior was Steven Burns jnr and second junior Bruce Maclean, 27th overall.

First lady was Diane Baum ,second Kate Jenkins and third Amanda Blackhall.

A big thank you to all that helped out today - Jim, Ellen, Pamela, Kate, Jo, Graham, Ernie and Cammy.

The next race in the series is at Nevis Range Sunday 29th November.

Jim Hamilton
