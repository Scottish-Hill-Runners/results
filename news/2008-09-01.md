Tom Owens (Shettleston), partnered by Andy Symonds (Carnegie) , and running as the Saab Salomon team, finished in an impressive 2nd place in the five-stage Trans Rockies Race. After just over 11 hours of running, they finished 4 minutes behind the winners, Max King and Erik Skaggs of the Nike team.

[Results](http://transrockies.com/transrockiesrun/news/?cat=9)

Meanwhile, slightly closer to home, Lucy Colquhoun (Carnethy) was a magnificent 12th, and first lady, in the half Tour de Mont Blanc (Courmayeur-Champex-Chamonix). She was a mere 2 hours ahead of the next lady, clocking 14.33 for the 86km course.

[Results](http://www.utmb.livetrail.net/tetecourse.php?course=ccc)
