---
title: "Caerketton just got higher"
date: 2012-07-26
excerpt: "Due to work at the Hillend Ski slope the Caerketton Race will now start and finish at the lower car park......"
---

Race Organiser Stewart Whitlie writes:

_"As some of you may be aware the ski-slope at Hill-end is being extended and will soon cover the area previously used for the start and finish of the Caerketton hill race route. The work has already started and the area is now fenced off. So this year, the route will have to be modified. The plan is now to start close to the bottom car park and the race route will now be as per map on this site. This will effectively be up and down the Caerketton downhill race route for those that may be familiar with that route._

_The junior race will start at the same place as Senior race and turn just before the gate. Registration will be at the bottom car-park. Please note that car-parking at the bottom car-park is very limited, so please either park at the top car-park or in the car parking spaces between hill-end and the by-pass._
