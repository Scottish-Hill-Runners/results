Sign up here - [Cort-ma Law Hill Race - Westerlands CCC](https://westerlandsccc.co.uk/club-races/cort-ma-law-race/)

The Cort-ma Law Race that had to be postponed from June because of roadworks will go ahead on Wednesday 9 August.  Details including pre-entry arrangements will follow.   Note that the race will start at 7 pm rather than its usual time of 7.30 pm.

**Race Director**: Del Young on behalf of [Westerlands CCC](https://westerlandsccc.co.uk/)

Race Start: Crow Road car park, Lennoxtown

W3W: [https://w3w.co/completed.sourced.onion](https://w3w.co/completed.sourced.onion)

///completed.sourced.onion

**Race Kit Requirements:** 

Limited entry on the day.

Please share transport if at all possible.

Runners are reminded that, as usual, you will be required to carry the following standard safety equipment.

Water and wind-proof whole body cover plus other body cover appropriate for the weather conditions:  
A map of the route, a compass and a whistle  
Your kit will be checked at the start of the race.
