_Andy Kitchin writes.._

Dear Sir, I wish to make a complaint......

Running the 2 Breweries I was pretty dismayed and somewhat disgusted to be picking up empty energy gel packets dropped by runners ahead of me. I picked up 4 packets in a spell where I was running around 20th position. I had a wee grumble about this afterwards in the village hall and thought about having a rant at the prizegiving, however I didn't.

It seems like I should have since someone has now commented to me that they saw 10 disgarded gel packets during the Pentland Skyline, whilst running at around 60th position. Firstly any real Hill Runner should be eating Jelly Babies, energy gels are for gullible road runners ;-) Jelly Babies are much nicer and much cheaper.

Also what kind of arrogance makes people think its OK to disgard their litter in the hills? What kind of lazyness makes it too difficult to put the empty packet securely back in your bum-bag ? Maybe people don't want all that sticky goo on their precious super-light, high-tech, expensive gear, but as I say Jelly Babies are the answer.

Anyhow - Can people please stop behaving so inconsiderately ? Regards, Andy Kitchin
