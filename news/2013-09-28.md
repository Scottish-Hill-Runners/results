_From Andy Birnie_

The Ben Venue Hill Race goes ahead as planned on Saturday 5th October, and the good news is that the forestry work is complete, so we're back on the previous championship course.

This race, near Aberfoyle, is so accessible for folks in the Central Belt it makes a great day out. The course is a mix of path & open hill, not forgetting the wonderful steep and rough descent.

All proceeds will go to Lomond Mountain Rescue Team who will be out supporting the race. Look forward to seeing you on Saturday!

More info here: http://www.bellahoustonroadrunners.co.uk/events/benvenuechallenge/

And on-line entries here: http://www.entrycentral.com/bella\_ben\_venue\_challenge
