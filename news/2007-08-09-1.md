John Stevenson holds onto a slender lead in the Bog and Burn Championship following a late rally from Steven Fallon, Dan Gay and Manny Gorman.

The ladies competition is all but wrapped up with Angela Mudge 1st, Elke Schmidt 2nd, and Val Houston 3rd.

The final race in the series is Red Moss on August 15th.

[Full Results after 9 races](http://chris-upson.com/bogandburn/2007/BogBurn2007_v9.htm)  |  [Women-only Results after 9 races](http://chris-upson.com/bogandburn/2007/BogBurn2007_v9_women.htm)

Manny Gorman and Tom Smith (Lochaber) both need a win at Red Moss to win the series with 12 points. Day Gay needs 2nd place at Red Moss, and Steven Fallon needs 3rd to get 12 points to win the series.

Davy Duncan and Dougal Ross are both on target for 10 and of 10 races in the Bog and Burn Series, with all 9 races completed so far.
