Excellent results for the elite raiding party of Al Anthony and Jethro Lennox in the final British championship race at Dufton Pike saw them nip and tuck all the way, with Al eventually prevailing to finish 2nd, with Jethro just behind.

It was an excellent result for Anthony but a vital result for Lennox as he secured a silver medal in the British Championship by finishing ahead of his nearest rival Morgan Donnelly. Meanwhile Pudsey's John Heneghan led all the way in the fast and furious 5 miler, leaving these three, and already crowned British champion Rob Hope, in his wake.
