---
title: "Bragg wins The Highland Fling again, 25 April"
date: 2009-04-27
excerpt: "Jez Bragg wins the Highland Fling for the third time in a row...  '"
---

Jez Bragg was second in the Highland Fling when it was first run in 2006 with 17 finishers. He won it in 2007 and 2008, breaking the course record on each occasion. He repeated the feat on Saturday, when the number of finishers (including teams) was 270. Sarah Ridgway was the first solo woman.
