Joe Symonds (Shettleston) won the Arrochar Alps race in 3hrs 21 mins ahead of Al Anthony (Ochils) and Mark Harris (Lomonds) in 2nd & 3 rd respectively.

Louise Burt (Fife) was first lady with Sarah MacKenzie (Lochaber) and Helen Wise (Carnethy) 2nd & 3rd.
