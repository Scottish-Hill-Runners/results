_From Russell Mckechnie_

Records tumbled on a glorious snow dusted Meall a' Bhuachaille. Joe Symonds showed his class and took command of the race from the gun to finish in a blistering time of 62.45 Second Place Brian Marshall was nearly 5 mins behind. Dan Whitehead was third, Craig Mattocks fourth, both of them passing Dave Wilby who took a fall on the down from the last summit. Charlotte Morgan won the ladies race after an exciting finish straight sprint against another runner her record time was 76.39.

Second was Shona Robertson in 79.06, Eilidh Wardlaw third in 82.08. Fourth was Jacqui Higginbottom. First team HBT Joe Symonds, Craig Mattocks and Alex Keith First ladies team HHR Moriana MacDougal, Bethan Nadin and Louise Nadin. A special thanks to all the marshalls who helped and Bob of Badaguish who is retiring soon.

I must mention John Mackenzie a hero of the day, John was diagnosed with a rare form of cancer early on in the year and never thought he would be running again this year. Unexpectedly John turned up and ran while on chemotherapy finishing in a very respectable 82.44. John you are an inspiration to us all!
