Clear cold conditions for a new hill race in Central Scotland.

Al Anthony took the honours at the inaugural Run of the Mill with a clear victory 1:12:22, ahead of Carnethy duo Sam Hesling 1:14:08 and Andy Fallas 1:15:05.

Natalie White was first lady in 1:27:09, followed by Ellie Homewood 1:29:21, and Louise Burt 1:37:57.
