---
title: "Smiths and Harrises to the fore at Killin, 4 August"
date: 2010-08-05
excerpt: "Tom Smith (Lochaber) wins from Mark Harris (Fife AC)...  '"
---

Tom Smith (Lochaber) won the Killin Games Hill Race from Mark Harris (Fife), who was also the first M40. Steven Fallon (Carnethy) kept his nose in front of 16-year old Adam Harris (Fife) to finish third, while Alan Smith (Deeside) was sixth and first M50.

Melanie Nicoll (Tayside Orienteers) and Joanna Schreiber (Girvan) were the first two women home, only 16 seconds apart.
