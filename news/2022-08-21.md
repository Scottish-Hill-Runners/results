title: "Craig Dunain Race Report & Results"
date: 2022-08-21
excerpt: "Wins for Robin Howie and Kirstie Rogan (both Highland Hill Runners) at the Craig Dunain Hill Race on Saturday 20th August."
---

Craig Dunain hill race had not been held since 2019 due to Covid but was resurrected by Highland Hill Runners with a new venue at Great Glen House and a brand-new course. The new course is similar to previous routes in terms of distance and climb, but is now entirely off road and climbs to the summit of Craig Dunain twice. 

There were wins for Robin Howie and Kirstie Rogan (both Highland Hill Runners). 

Age category winners were MV40 Robbie Paterson and FV40 Iona Robertson (both Highland Hill Runners), MV50 Rob Burnett (Inverness Harriers) and FV50 Hilary Cameron (Forres Harriers), MV60 Graeme Ambrose (Inverness Harriers) and FV60 Lee Ross (Highland Hill Runners). 

Thanks go to Nature Scot for use of their car park, and to all the volunteers who marshalled and helped with the race. 

Caroline Marwick
