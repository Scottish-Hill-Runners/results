---
title: "Tinto Hill Race Results"
date: 2015-11-14
excerpt: "Shettleston Harriers’ Joe Symonds and Shona Robertson were the winners at Tinto on Saturday 14th Nov."
---

Joe Symonds (Shettleston) won the Tinto Hill race in 32:22 ahead of Al Anthony (Ochils) and Jethro Lennox (Shettleston) in 2nd & 3 rd.

First female was Shona Robertson (Shettleston) with Kerstin Leslie (Annan & District) and Lorna Mahoney (Westies) 2nd & 3 rd.
