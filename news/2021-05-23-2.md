Congratulations to Andrew Douglas (Inverclyde) and Catriona Graves (Carnethy) for their wins at the SA Hijacked Maddy Moss Race on Saturday 22nd May.

The race was organised on the existing Maddy Moss course by Angela Mudge. Thanks to Angela for excellent organisation and to the team of marshals from the hill running community scattered around the Ochils.
