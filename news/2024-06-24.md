---
title: "Run Of The Mill Race Date Change"
date: 2024-06-24
excerpt: "The final race in the 2024 SHR Championship will be on Sunday 6th October rather than the Saturday of that weekend."
---

Note the change to the date (previously advised as Saturday 5th). The race has been moved to the Sunday to avoid a clash with the Hodgson Brothers Mountain Relay which is historically on the Sundayday but has moved to the Saturday for 2024. It has been moved to enable clubs to double up and earn maximum championship points in the final counter.
