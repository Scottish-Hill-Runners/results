With one race remaining, the Pentland Skyline on 14th Oct, Finlay Wild already has an unassailable in the Scottish Long Classics, thus becoming the 5th different winner in the 5 years since the inception of the series.

In the women's competition, Jasmin Paris cannot be caught now, and therefore becomes the first female to successfully defend their Scottish Long Classics title, in addition to winning the Lakes Long Classics in the same season, which is an impressive achievement.
