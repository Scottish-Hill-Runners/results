---
title: "Whitehead breaks Mither Tap record, 17 July"
date: 2007-07-18
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) won the latest race in the Cosmics' summer series in record time.  '"
---

From Fiona Dahl:

"Attendance was up on last year to 43 runners. Weather was showery and slightly misty on top but a vast improvement on last year! Dan Whitehead set a new record of 34:01, with the first 3 runners inside Jason Williamson's record from last year.

Thanks to Gary Gutteridge and Rob Brooke for marshalling at the summit, the Williamson family and Helen Mackie for marshalling en route, Mike Stone and Fiona Dahl for timing and Chris MacMillan for sweeping. Also special thanks to Dave Wilkinson for donating Monymusk honey as prizes, and to Pittodrie Estate, Forestry Commission for permission to run the event."
