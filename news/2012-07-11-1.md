_From Hugh Buchanan_

The European Mountain Running Championships took place in Pamukkale, Turkey on Saturday 7 July. In the senior men's race, the GB team was led home by Steve Vernon (Stockport) in 11th, supported by Robbie Simpson (Deeside) in 19th and Chris Smith (Thames) in 35th, placing the team in 7th. In the junior men, Anglo-Scot Peter Harrison (Border) was the first Brit home in 14th, in front of team-mates Joe Johnston, Max Wharton and Nathan Jones with that team placing 6th.

The GB women's teams lacked Scottish representation, but the senior team won an outstanding gold medal with all 3 counters in the top 10 - Mary Wilkinson was 4th, Emma Clayton 6th and Katie Walshaw 10th. Sarah McCormack (Moorfoot) had an outstanding result in 8th place as the Irish team's leading finisher. The GB junior women, also with an all-English composition, saw Annabel Mason win the individual gold, supported by Mel Hyder (4th) and Laura Riches (6th), giving them team silver behind Turkey. Louise Hill-Stirling (Ayr Seaforth) led the Irish team home with her 10th place finish.
