title: "Triple Hirple Race Results"
date: 2025-07-10
excerpt: "The results for the Triple Hirple races at Lochaber at the weekend are all available - Meall an t-Suidhe, Half Nevis and Cow Hill"
---

Robin Downie (Lochabler AC) was the winner at both Meall an t-Suidhe and the Half Nevis race. Angela Mudge (Ochils) was the female winner at Meall an t-Suidhe on Saturday and on Sunday at the Half-Ben, Caroline Marwick (Highland HR) won.

On Monday at Cow Hill, Ewan Dowd (Edinburgh Uni) and Sarah Crowsley (DDJC) were the winners.

Full results are on the SHR results tab.
