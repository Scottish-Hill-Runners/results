Rob Jebb (Bingley Harriers) won the BOFRA Alva Games race ahead of Dessie Flanagan (Carnethy) and Alastair Dunn (Helm Hill). The ladies race was won by Sarah Wiseman (Ochils) ahead of Sharon Taylor (Helm Hill) and Hannah Russell (Helm Hill). Results available under the Results tab
