---
title: "SHR Survey – Now closed"
date: 2015-02-08
excerpt: "The 2015 SHR Championship survey has now closed and the results will be advised after the committee meeting at the start March."
---

Thank you to everyone who took the time to complete the survey. There were 269 responses in total.

The committee will consider the findings of the survey at the next committee meeting (start of March) and the results along with the eligibility rules for the 2015 SHR Championship will be published here before the first of the races at Birnam.
