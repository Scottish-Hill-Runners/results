Everyone at NRC is looking forward to hosting the final round of the 2022 SHR championship on Saturday 1st October at the Yetholm Border Shepherds Show.

Start 12.45pm. Seniors & juniors set off together.

Registration from 10.00am.

Entry fee (Seniors) £10 on the day only, this includes entry to the show.

Junior race 11-15yrs….free entry.

Tractor pull free entry!

There will be a full prize list but the prizes themselves may be modest as we will be donating all entry money equally between the Shepherds Show and the Border Search & Rescue Unit who will be providing cover on the day.

\*Please note that in order to ease congestion at the main entrance race registration and parking for runners will be located on the Kirk Yetholm side of the Bowmont, this will be well signed on the day.\*
