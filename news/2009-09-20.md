---
title: "Adam Anderson comes through to win Merrick, 20 September"
date: 2009-09-20
excerpt: "Adam Anderson, fifth to the summit, wins from Stephen Winter and Steven Fallon...  '"
---

Steven Fallon (Carnethy) and Stephen Winter (Shettleston) reached the summit of Merrick together, followed by Colin Brash (Girvan), Russell Anderson (Moorfoots) and then Adam Anderson (Carnethy). But it was Adam Anderson who had the best descent to win by six seconds from Stephen Winter, with Steven Fallon third. Russell Anderson came in fifth to be the first M50 and Douglas Milligan (Solway) in eighth was the first M60.

Shirley Singh (Unattached) won the women's race from Jo Schreiber (Lochaber) and Jean Robson (Annan).
