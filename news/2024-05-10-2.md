# Red Moss Kips Race 

Sign up Link - [Red Moss 2024 (typeform.com)](https://form.typeform.com/to/stt4f1ec)

Wed 5th June 2024, 7.30pm  
Early start at 7.10 pm if you expect to be a long time out on the course  
  
Entry £9 / £11 (non SAL)  
Includes £5 donation to Pentlands Hills Regional Park  
  
£2 discount for those coming by foot/bike/public transport  
Prizegiving will be held in the garden of the Riccarton Inn as soon as possible after the race.  
  
This is a Scottish Athletics permitted event (Licence no 13555) open to runners aged 19 or over on the 31/12/2024  
  
Held in accordance with SHR rules including mandatory kit
