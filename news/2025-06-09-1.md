Plenty of races on offer at the weekend.

At the Two Mamores, there were wins for Robin Downie (Lochaber AC) and Helen Leigh (Inverness Harriers) who also broke the female course record.

The Trotternish Ridge race was won by Luke Taylor (Highland Hill Runners) and Shona Robertson (Shettleston)

And at Gargunnock there were wins for Jacob Atkin (Moorfoot Runners) and Isla Paterson (Gala Harriers)

All these results are on the SHR results tab.
