Andy McIntyre (U/A) finished over a minute ahead of Adam Anderson (Carnethy) with Mayeul Ducrot (Ayr Seaforth) in third. Louise Hill Stirling (Girvan) just beat Toni McIntosh (Ayr Seaforth) to the women's prize; Jo Schreiber (Girvan) was third.
