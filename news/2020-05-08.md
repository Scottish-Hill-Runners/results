**Articles**

We are looking for articles on hill running, hill races, fun in the hills, fun off the hills, training for the hills in lockdown, adventures far afield or close to home; prose or poetry, fact or fiction - all styles, plots and viewpoints welcome.

With access to the hills severely curtailed now's the time to flex those writing muscles and put pen to paper!

**Short-Cuts**

In addition to standard articles, this year we're trying something slightly different and are asking people to submit 'Short-Cuts**'**. These are mini-articles of 200 - 300 words on the same topic.

With everyone in lockdown, we're looking for people to write on the topic of '**What I Miss About Hill Running**'.

If you would like to make or discuss a submission please get in touch with james.callender@live.co.uk

We look forward to hearing from you!

Yours,

James
