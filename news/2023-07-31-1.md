Hartfell Horseshoe Race Report & Results

A big thank you to all those who made today's race a great success, be it marshalling, helping set up, the clearing up at the end, Precision Race Timing Scotland for the chip timing results and of course the landowners for access to the fantastic Moffat Hills.

A mention for runners who raced today in less-than-ideal conditions, it was super to see so many turn up. We raised £374.62 for Moffat Mountain Rescue Team.

First home was Daniel Lavin (Moorfoot Runners) in a time of 01:38:08, second place went to Ian Patterson (Whisky Chasers) running in the MV50 class 01:44:33 Third place was taken by Adam Dickie (Dumfries Running Club) MV40 in 01:46:40.

In the female classes Marie Marshall (Annan and District AC) FV40 was first in a time of 01:57:21, not far behind her was FV50 runner Michelle Hetherington (Carnethy Hill Running Club) in 02:00:25 and in third place was Hartfell Hill Running Club's Claire Ingram  FV40 in 02:06:03, great running by all the V40/V50 lady runners showing that age is just a number.

Kev Plummer (Hartfell HR)
