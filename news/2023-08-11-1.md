The Cademuir Rollercoaster tomorrow Saturday 12 August offers racing opportunities for U13s (born 2011 and 2012) all the way up to Seniors. Entry on the day - Adults £5; U18 £2.

The race will also serve as the selection opportunity for the forthcoming Junior Home International (JHI) on Sunday 10 September, which will take place over the same Cademuir course.

Two races distances:

2pm - 3km; Climb 105m

2.30pm - 6km; Climb 275m

TRAFFIC WARNING DUE TO WORLD MTB CHAMPIONSHIPS AT GLENTRESS - be prepared for some delays. Saturday is the elite races women's starts 11.30am and the men at 3.30pm. Give yourself plenty of time to get there.
