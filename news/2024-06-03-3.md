Pre entry for Arrochar has now closed and due to unusual popularity of the champs year the number of entrants is over capacity. I am using a random ballot process for those who have the required experience and invitations are being sent out over the coming days. Unfortunately some will miss out and I apologise for this. If you can no longer make the race but did put in an entry, please email me and we'll free up the space for someone else. All the best. Ally
