---
title: "SHR Looking For Feedback From Race Organisers"
date: 2018-09-26
excerpt: "A short questionnaire has been sent out to ROs"
---

After last year’s SHR AGM and subsequent discussions, the SHR committee are keen hear the views of race organisers and their race entry systems and arrangements. We would be very grateful if ROs could complete and return the short questionnaire that they should have received from Survey Monkey.

If you are a race organiser and haven't received a survey email, please contact the SHR convenor, Roddy Pugh.
