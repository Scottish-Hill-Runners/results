On the same day (Monday 26 February) that Finlay Wild set a new winter record for Tranter's Round, Uisdean Hawthorn traversed the Cuillin ridge from the summit of Sgurr nan Gillean to Gars-bheinn in the astonishing time of 4 hours 57 mins 7 secs. As with Finlay's round, we'll be acknowledging this in our record pages when we've got some more details.
