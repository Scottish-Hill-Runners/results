---
title: "New Race - Helensburgh AAC's Hel'n Back"
date: 2015-08-10
excerpt: "Helensburgh AAC have a new race on Sunday 13th September."
---

A new, challenging but very scenic race 'From Hel' n Back' is being launched by Helensburgh AAC. 1200ft of elevation over 12km taking in views of Loch Lomond and the Arrochar Alps to be held on 13th Sept. It's not flat but it's worth it! More details are on Entry Central [http://www.entrycentral.com/helnback](http://www.entrycentral.com/helnback)
