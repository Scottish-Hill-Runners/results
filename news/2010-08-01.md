---
title: "Joe Symonds beats brother Andy's Ben Rinnes record, 31 July"
date: 2010-08-01
excerpt: "Joe Symonds (HBT) wins in record time and Claire Gordon makes it a double for HBT...  '"
---

Joe Symonds (HBT) took two-and-a-half minutes off the record his brother Andy set last year, finishing well ahead of Hector Haines (EUHH) and Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy), the first M40.

Claire Gordon (HBT) was the first woman, followed by Carnethy's F40 Jacqui Higginbottom.
