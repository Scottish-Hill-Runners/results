_From David Rogers_

Iain Stewart (Westerlands) won this year’s Cort-ma Law Race in 50 minutes and 44 seconds, more than one and a half minutes clear of the rest of the field. Second and third places went to old-stagers Alan Smith (Deeside) and Adrian Davis (Carnethy). The first lady was Julia Connor (Tinto), for the third year running, with Karen Kealey (Ochil) and Michelle Hetherington (Helensburgh) in second and third places. The men's team prize was won by Westerlands.

Thanks very much to Westerlands colleagues for marshalling and time-keeping.
