New event to be held 7th March  
Entries open Friday 16th January on [ProTay](https://protay.co.uk/ochilhillrelays/)  
Registration: Cochrane Hall, West Stirling Street, Alva, Clackmannanshire, FK12 5BD, commencing at 9.30am. Cochrane Hall is 700 metres from the start of the race so leave sufficient time.  
Entries  
A team manager can register one or multiple teams and complete the payment. Nearer the event date, team managers will receive a separate email with instructions on how to add individual participants to each team.  
Race start: 11:00  
Leg 1 - Starts at 11am  
Leg 2 - Starts at 11.15am  
Leg 3 - Starts at 11.30am (Paired leg)  
Checkpoints are listed on the individual maps.
