Many features of Chris Upson’s old Scottish Hill Racing website are clearly missed by many Scottish hill runners, in particular individual runner information and analysis, and race maps – we think.

Whether or not you have an expertise in database design and website programming, or any other technical gap you can see in our information provision, it would be very helpful if you at least let us know your wish list for features currently missing from the SHR website.

As is already mentioned on the Race Details pages, race maps are now being incorporated into those pages. ROs are now sending in GPX or JPG files of race maps with their race details for 2022, and we have the complete database of maps and everything else from the old SHRacing website. Maps will be processed and incorporated as time allows, with priority for upcoming races.

We want to develop a strategy for exactly what features to incorporate and how, and to prioritise – then to implement these. The strategy would also involve delegation of various one-off and ongoing tasks, as it is all too much for any one person.

If you are interested in anything ranging from an enquiry to devoting time, please contact [web support, SHR](mailto:webmaster@scottishhillrunners.uk).
