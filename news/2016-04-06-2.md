_After many years of service our auditor, Patricia Smith, has decided not to carry on the role. The Committee would like to extend its thanks to Patricia for her help over the years._

_We are now looking for a volunteer to take on the audit of the SHR accounts. This is not an onerous job as the books and records are up to date, in good order and the SHR is in a healthy financial position._

_If you are interested in helping with this task, or would simply like to find out more about what is involved, please contact Ian Hawkins at [treasurer@scottishhillrunners.uk](mailto:treasurer@scottishhillrunners.uk) or on 07786 277530._
