Congratulations to Alexander Chepelin (Edinburgh Uni H&H) and Jill Stephen (HBT) for their wins at Screel on Saturday 13th April. This was the first counter for the 2019 SHR Championship.

Alexander beat the existing male course record and Jill equalled the current female course record.
