Cademuir Rollercoaster Results (including JHI Trials)

Results for the 3.8K, 5.8K and 8K races that were held at Cademuir on Saturday 12th August.

James Connelly (Garscube Harriers) and Anna Hedley (Fife AC) were the winners in the 3.8K race

In the 5.8K race the winners were Robert Sparks (Fife AC) and Lauren Dickson (Lasswade AC).

And Gavin Bryson was the winner in the 8K race which was also open.

The full results for the 8K race are held on the SHR results tab under Cademuir Rollercoaster.
