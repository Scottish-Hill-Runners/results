The annual British Fell & Hill Running Relay Championships are always a highlight of the hill running season. Scottish teams have had success over the years, including 2013 where HBT won the women’s category outright, and Carnethy HRC finished first over 50 team.

The event has been held four times in Scotland; in Wanlockhead in 1993, in Alva in 1999 and 2005 and in Falkland in 2010. The event has grown in popularity over the years, with 174 teams of six runners competing in the 2013 event.

The UKA Mountain Running Advisory Group (MRAG) are seeking a Scottish host for the 2016 event, provisionally to be held on the second last weekend in October, and would ask any club (or combination of clubs) interested in hosting the event to contact Mark Johnston of the MRAG to express an interest (contact details below) by the end of February 2014.
