Three Scots are well placed in the British Championships after two of the four races (Silent Valley and Sedbergh 3 Peaks completed; Dollar and Black Mountains to come).

Robbie Simpson (Deeside) is lying second in the Men's Open with 92 points, behind Rob Hope (Pudsey and Bramley; 97 points) and ahead of Lloyd Taggart (Dark Peak; 89 points). Robbie also has a commanding lead in the Men's Under 23s.

Clare Whitehead (Cosmics) is second in the Women's Open on 56 points, trailing Philippa Maddams (Keswick) by 8 points but one point in front of Lauen Jeska (Todmorden).

Finally in the M50s Ronnie Gallagher (Carnethy; 59 points) has a 10-point advantage over Tom McGaff (Cheshire) and Bernard Grant (Harrogate).

The next race is at [Dollar](RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0071&System=Metric&Month=7) on 31 July.
