Eildon Three Hill Race

Saturday 18th Sept 2021

Starts 1:00pm

Declarations from 12 noon

No entry on the day

Start and finish have been altered to enable social distancing and facilitate any covid19 restrictions. Nearest post code to start Melrose TD6 9HA.

*   Distance 7.15km Total Ascent 510m
*   60th Anniversary Edition 1961-2021
*   SHR Championship Race 2021

Due to the coronavirus crises the race this year will move from its traditional date in June and will be held on18th September 21. The 2021 start/finish and route will also be different to previous years to facilitate social distancing and any potential covid19 restrictions. Nearest post code to start **Melrose** TD6 9HA. The race route will still cross each of the iconic three Eildon Hills. We hope you appreciate the need for these changes and will continue to support the event.

The race will be run Under UK Athletics Rules and is a **scottish**athletics Licensed Event

Please be aware that in light of the coronavirus crisis, the race organisers will need to comply with the most recent government and **Scottish** athletics guidance available on the day of the event for the area in which the event is to take place. This may result in late changes to the event format.
