The double header weekend at Killin was led home by Prasad Prasad who broke the course record for the Meall nan Tarmachan race to beat Al Anthony into second, despite Al also breaking Brian Marshall's 2010 record. MarK Harris, despite winning the vets race had to be content to take fourth place behind yound Adam.

In a close ladies race Catrion Buchanan nipped inside Angela Mudge's record time by a few seconds and in doing so foiled a Prasad double by finishing 14 seconds in front of Elke.

The survivors of Saturday were at it again on Sunday on the Sron a Clachain course used by the Killin highland games with local Stephen Rawlison repeating his summertime victory, holding off Westies vet Manny Gorman. Moorfoot's most travelled runner Julia Connor won the ladies race.
