[Scottish Hill Runners :: Juniors](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/Juniors2.aspx)

Senior full results are on the SHR website results tab and all the results are available on the Gala Harriers website - [Eildon Hill Race 24th June 2023 Full Results | Gala Harriers](https://www.galaharriers.co.uk/news/eildon-hill-race-24th-june-2023-full-results/)

For the Juniors, we're now at the halfway point of the league races and the standings are starting to take shape. However, with double points available at two of the next four races (Cademuir 12 Aug, Creag Bheag 17 Sep), it's still very much all to play for in all categories. And with just 3 races needed to count for the league, it's not too late for others to make a play for the title too.

Next race is at Braemar Junior Highland Games on 22nd July.
