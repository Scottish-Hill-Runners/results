Due to the unfortunate cancellation of the Yetholm Border Shepherds Show Race this year's SHR championship is now the best 3 out of 5 races.  
  
U23 and V60 / 70 athletes can do any 3 of the 5 races.  
Seniors, V40 and V50 must complete a category long and medium to count.

The next championship race is the Glenshee 9 on Sat 7th Aug.

And the dates for the remaining races are :-

Sat 18th Sep - Eildon 3 Hills

Sat 25 Sep - The Two Breweries

Sat 9 Oct - Cioch Mhor
