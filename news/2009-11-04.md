title: "Robbie Simpson wins Eddie Campbell Award"
date: 2009-11-04

---

Robbie Simpson was presented with the Eddie Campbell Memorial Award at the recent scottishathletics dinner. This award is given annually to a junior athlete who has shown excellence combined with sportsmanship in hill races.
