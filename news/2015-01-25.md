At the 2014 AGM, a proposal was made to change the manner in which the current age-categories work and how the prizes are awarded.

The committee have concluded that as there is divided opinion on what is a fair and proper set of rules, we should canvass opinion from the runners who compete in the races in Scotland.

Consequently, an Online Survey has been set up with a few questions that will allow us to get a view from across the hill running community.

If you were an SHR member in 2014 or a new member in 2015, you will receive an email shortly that will contain a link to the Online Survey.

This poll is not limited to SHR members, so if you don't receive an email with a link, then contact the SHR secretary (Neil Gilmore) at [neilgilmore@blueyonder.co.uk](mailto: neilgilmore@blueyonder.co.uk) or [scottishhillrunners@yahoo.co.uk](mailto:scottishhillrunners@yahoo.co.uk) and I will arrange to email you a note of the link.

Please take the time to complete the survey.
