---
title: "Whitehead in the clear at Scolty, 1 June"
date: 2008-06-01
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead and Sonia Armitage (both Cosmics) took the main honours at the Scolty Hill Race. The junior races were part of the **scottish**athletics Junior League.  '"
---

Dan Whitehead (Comics) won the senior race by over two minutes from Bob Wiseman (Ochil) with Des Crowe (Dundee HH) third and first vet. Sonia Armitage (Cosmics) won the women's race from Carolyn Mcleod (Unatt) and Ruth Mackenzie (Deeside R).

There were several junior races, which were a trial for the Marco Germanetto International as well as part of the **scottish**athletics Junior League. Jamie Crowe (Dundee HH) just beat Ben Stevenson (Ochil HR) in the U14B, whilst James Waldie (Cornwall AC) must have thought his long journey worth-while after a narrow win over Jamie Stevenson (Ochil HR) in the U16B.
