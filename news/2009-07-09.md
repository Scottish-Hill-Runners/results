_From organiser Andy Kitchin:_

A cold wet evening tempted a record field of 59 out into everyone’s favourite cold wet field, you know the one I mean....

This nice little route starts at the end of the Old Kirk Road, goes up over Fala Knowe, indulges the organiser's penchant for heather and bog by heading north down the hill to a wall corner before heading direct to the top of Carnethy then down to the Scald Law col and down the track to finish.

It was on the climb, through more heather, that Al Anthony took a slower route than others. He won though – but no record. No record for Claire Gordon either as first woman.

Robbie Barr (Giffnock North) won the junior race to the top of Fala Knowe and back.

Thanks to all competitors, cheerful even with the rain showers. Proceeds go to the British Orienteering young athletes support fund, thank you.
