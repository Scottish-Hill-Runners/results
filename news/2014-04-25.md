_From Mark Harris_

Wins for Ben Huwkins (Ronhill Cambuslang) in a very fast time of 29.24 and Judith Turner (unattached) in 36.48. And there was some superb running from Fife AC juniors Logan and Tristan Rees to take second and third places. Mary Lye (Carnethy) and Hilary Ritchie were second and third in the women's race. Many thanks for all helpers on the day and runners for coming!
