**SHR 2025 Long Classics - after 5 races**

Great to see a few new long races on the calendar this year plus an old classic, Glen Rosa, back on the go after a 13 year absence. A great race and welcome return to the calendar.

Latest scores after 5 races are as per link. Luke Taylor of Highland Hill runners is leading the way in the men’s scores ahead of his club mate Andrew Gilmore with Felix Wilson of Carnethy in 3rd spot. Shona Robertson of Shettlestone currently leads in the women’s scores with Bethan Nadin of Highland Hill Runners and Zoe Barber of Shettlestone making up top 3 positions. With 12 long races counting this year, just a reminder it will be best 5 scores to count in the series. Next race up in the series is another classic, Arrochar Alps on 28th June.

Just a reminder if you spot any issues with scores please reach out to myself. Thanks.

Stewart Whitlie
