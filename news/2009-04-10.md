The Will's Bothy Relay, scheduled for 31 May, has been cancelled. Here's a bit of background, from Relay organiser Martin Hyman:

Fifteen years ago, friends of the late Will Ramsbotham started to create Will’s Bothy from a ruined cottage in the Borders hills near Riccarton Junction. The cottage is owned by the Forestry Commission, and the Commission gave the Mountain Bothies Association permission for the project. Three years ago the MBA decided that the task was too onerous for them, so they invited the “Friends” to resume responsibility for it. The Forestry Commission seemed supportive so the ‘Friends’ set about establishing a Charitable Trust to facilitate fund-raising. The Will's Bothy Relay was to contribute to the Trust.

Unfortunately the Forestry Commission has still to complete the necessary formal agreement with the ‘Friends’, who have therefore reluctantly decided to cancel the Relay.
