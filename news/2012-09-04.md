It was another fine day's hill racing at Menstrie on Sunday 2 September. The races include the selection races for the Junior and Senior Home International Teams, with those international races being held in Cumbria on 22 September.

There were 6 races in all ranging from the 1.1km Fun Run to the 8.7km open senior race. The races were all "uphill-only" this year, with the Finish for the longer courses near the top of Colsnaur. The organiser (hugh.buchanan@iname.com) has 2 jackets which were left in the Scout Hall afterwards.
