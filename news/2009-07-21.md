Following last week's Maddy Moss race, Kenny Richmond continues to lead the Bog and Burn Series from Oleg Chepelin and Andrew Wright, although Murray Strain now has 2 wins with 2 races remaining.

In the women's competition, Val Houston is currently in the lead with Iona Robertson 2nd, and either Emma Birnie or Ellen Homewood in 3rd place depending on how the points are added up!

The final 2 races in the series are both in the Pentlands with Turnhouse on 22nd July and Caerketton on 12th August.

**Prize Giving**  
After Caerketton there will be a Bog and Burn series prize giving at The Steading just below the Hillend Ski Centre. The new conservatory in The Steading has been booked and runners will be entitled to soup & roll.
