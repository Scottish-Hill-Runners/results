Entries are now being taken for the Two Breweries race on 24 September, which is pre-entry only. You can enter by post or online, and you can also order your tickets for the not-to-be-missed evening Ceilidh at the same time! For postal entries download an entry form from the [race website](http://twobreweries.org.uk); for online entries go to [EntryCentral](http://www.entrycentral.com).
