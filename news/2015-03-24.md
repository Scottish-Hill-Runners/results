---
title: "Criffel Hill Race Results, Sunday 22nd March"
date: 2015-03-24
excerpt: "2nd win of the weekend for Andrew Gilmore. Jane Jackson (Carnethy) won the ladies race."
---

The Criffel Hill Race on Sunday 22nd March was won by Andrew Gilmore (Carnethy) in a time of 56:56 ahead of Jim Haworth (Galloway Harriers). Javier Romo (U/A) was third. This was Andrew’s second win of the weekend after finishing first at Chapelgill on Saturday.

The ladies race was won by Jane Jackson (Carnethy) in 01:18:35 in front of Shirley Singh (U/A) and Joan Wilson (Lomonds).

Thanks to the race organiser, Ranjit Thomas and the Moffat Mountain Rescue team who provided cover on the hill.
