11 in total for this years event, however due to what can only be described as normal Harris weather the RO took the decision to move the race to The Borderlands and 'Luithaid 3 Peaks Race' was born. A 8km/583 route with that starts in Harris and has a high point in Lewis. Hard fought race between 3 well known local celebrities with Sean Zeihm Stephen of Scaladale showing himself to be the strongest descender over the 1500ft Criffelesque descent from the Luithaid. A worthy winner is Sean, as manager of the Scaladale Outdoor Centre, 'Up the Clisham' would probably have never happened in the first instance without his support and enthusiasm. First lady, Melanie Nicoll from Lomond Hill Runners. Melanie the sun does sometimes shine in Harris, it just not shining this year as it's on strike at present up here over the terms and conditions of it's new shining contract. A thanks to the volunteers from Hebrides Mountain Resuce for Marshalling(so they should it was their idea in the first instance, before things got a little more complicated!)
