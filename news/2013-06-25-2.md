_From Laurie Anderson_

Falkland is short and brutal and this year the course had been lubricated by a short shower shortly before the start. The race itself was a tale of brothers to the fore, with the Rees brothers first and second the Gilmour brothers were third and fourth ! Very refreshing to see so many youngsters showing a clean pair of heels to the older generations. Charlotte Morgan continued her recent form by winning the female prize. Next year we hope to return to the traditional start at the town fountain.
