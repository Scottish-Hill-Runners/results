The shortened bad weather route was used for Slioch on Saturday. David Wilby (Highland Hill Runners) was first, ahead of Luke Taylor (HHR) and Mark Harris (Anster Haddies).

1st Lady was Jill Stephen (HBT) with Andrea Priestley (Ochils) and Moira Davie (Forres) in 2nd and 3rd.
