The trials for the UK teams for the [World Championships](http://www.wmrc2009.org/home/) were held at Keswick on 16 August. The races were also the trials for the Scottish teams for the [Commonwealth Championships](http://www.cumbriacommonwealthchampionships.org/) , which are to be held in Keswick in the middle of next month.

Robbie Simpson (Deeside R) had the outstanding result of the day as far as the Scots were concerned, comfortably winning the Junior Men's race, and earning himself an automatic place in the GB team for the World Championships in three weeks time. Alex Hendry (Central AC) was third and Jamie Stevenson (Ochil HR) fourth. In the Junior Women's race Jessica Martin (Kilbarchan AAC) was seventh and Scout Adkin (Moorfoot R) tenth.

Clare Whitehead (Cosmic HB) and Susan Ridley (Edinburgh AC) were the best-placed Scots among the Senior Women, finishing seventh and eleventh respectively. Joe Symonds (Kendal AC) was an impressive fourth in the Senior Men's race, followed by Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) in tenth - both taking some notable scalps. Joe's result saw him selected for the World Championships.
