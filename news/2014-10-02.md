Stewart Whitlie and Alasdair Anthony are tied on equal points in the lead with one race remaining, the Pentland Skyline on Sunday 12th October. Diane Baum continues to lead the women's competition, having completed all 9 races so far, however could be pipped at the final race if Jasmin Paris decides to run.
