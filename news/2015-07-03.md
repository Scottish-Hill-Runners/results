title: "Earlston Black Hill Results"
date: 2015-07-03
excerpt: "Darrell Hastie and Anya Campbell (both Gala Harriers) won at the Earlston Black Hill Race on Saturday 27th June."
---

A new evening start time of 7pm on Saturday 27th June saw 65 runners line up for the Earlston Black Hill Race, part of the Earlston Civic Week.

Darrell Hastie (Gala Harriers) was first with Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) 2nd and Iain Stewart (Westies) 3rd.

The female winner was Anya Campbell (Gala Harriers) ahead of Louise Mitchell and Catherine Dollives (both Cani Sports Edinburgh) in 2nd and 3 rd respectively.
