The Bog and Burn Grand Finale will take place at Cairn Table, starting 7pm on Wednesday 22nd August.

Although Niall McAlinden is leading the series, he could still be challenged for the title by John Stevenson, Don Naylor or Fergus Johnston. The women's competition is extremely close between Julia Connor and Neah Evans, and could go either way depending on results on the night.

Soup and sandwiches will be served at Muirkirk Caravan Park immediately after the race.
