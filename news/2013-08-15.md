Due to road re surfacing in Alford area on the A944 best to avoid this approach to Rhynie. If approaching from south/Aberdeen stay on A96 turn off to Oyne/Insch onto B9002 follow signs to Rhynie

map: http://www.aberdeenshire.gov.uk/roads/traffic/pdf/a944tillyfourie.pdf

road closure: http://www.aberdeenshire.gov.uk/roads/traffic/closures.asp

Looks like approach from south on A97 is not affected if you are coming from the Braemar approach
