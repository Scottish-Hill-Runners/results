_From Neil Craig_

Starting and finishing from the main field, this opening event of the 2013 Oxton Games on Saturday, 27th July was run in the pleasant Scottish Borders sunshine on a well marked and marshalled 5km course with a strong field led home in a time of 16'49" by Andrew Wright of Ochil Hill Runners to take the winners trophy and £30 first place prize money. Second overall and first veteran was Colin Donnely of Lochaber (17'14") and Anne Lillico of Lauderdale Limpers (29'10") was first lady home. Other places were filled by Darrel Hastie of Kelso followed by James Bowden of HBT. Thanks of the organisers to all those who entered; race sponsor Border Events; and the many others who contributed to the success of the event.
