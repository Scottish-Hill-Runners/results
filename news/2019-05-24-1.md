# Tap O’Noth Hill Race

[In conjunction with Rhynie Gala the Tap O’Noth Hill Race will be held on Saturday the 17th August 2019.

Pre-entry can be made online through the link below. Although entries on the day will be accepted it would make our lives easier if pre-entry was completed. Please note that pre-entry does not cover payment…this will be required on the day.

](https://inschtrailrunningclub.co.uk/2017/07/10/tap-onoth-hill-race/ "Permalink to Tap O’Noth Hill Race")

[](https://inschtrailrunningclub.co.uk/2017/07/10/tap-onoth-hill-race/ "Permalink to Tap O’Noth Hill Race")**[Webscorer – Tap O’Noth](https://www.webscorer.com/register?raceid=175648)**

Entry will be £5 with all proceeds going to Rhynie Gala. The race will commence at 2pm with entries closing at 1.30pm.

The race will start at the playing fields in the centre of Rhynie. Ample parking can be found on the streets around Rhynie with only a short walk to the start/registration.

The race route is a 7.9km out and back with 370m elevation gain.
