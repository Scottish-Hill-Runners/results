After the long wait from Meall an t-Suidhe in July (and an even longer wait from the start of the season, back in April) to Meall a'Bhuachaille at the end of October, all has now been sorted and the 2009 championship is over. 74 runners earned one of the very collectable SHR Championsip Mugs this year. Congratulations to the title winners:

Women: Clare Whitehead (Cosmics)

Men: Tom Owens (Shettleston)

Women O/40 : Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy)

Men O/40 : Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy)

Women O/50 : Anita Hamilton (Cosmics)

Men O/50 : Martin Laing (Fife)

Women O/60: Phyllis Lemoncello (Fife)

Men O/60 : Douglas Milligan (Solway)

Women U/23 : Neah Evans (u/a) & Ann Lloyd-Evans (Fife)

Men U/23 : Donald Evans (Westerlands)

Men's Team : Carnethy

Women's Team : Cosmics

Men's Over 40 Team : Carnethy

Women's Over 40 Team : Fife

Jacqui and Douglas both retained their championships. Interestingly, the men's team has been won by only two clubs in the last 16 years, Carnethy and Shettleston, and they were the top two this year. Fife are the only club to have won the Women's Over 40 team in its three year's existence, while Cosmics women's team win was their first since 1996.
