Everything is in hand for the SHR “do” in Killin after the Meall nan Tarmachan race on Saturday evening, but today is your last chance to book if you want to come! There will be a buffet meal (see menu) and a bar serving St Andrews Ale, wine, spirits etc.

Prize giving for the 2014 Scottish Hill Running Championship will be at 1815 in the McLaren Hall and is open to all. The meal will be at 1900 followed by the Ceilidh.

Please email Dave Scott (dcbscott@tiscali.co.uk) to book.
