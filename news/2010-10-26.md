_From Hugh Buchanan:_

Catriona Buchanan (Central/Ochil) and Robbie Simpson (Deeside) won the Under 23 women's and men's titles at the 53rd Trofeo Vanoni at Morbegno in Northern Italy at the weekend. Morbegno is twinned with Llanberis in North Wales and the Italians invite the winners of the Snowdon Mountian Race to take part in their race each year.

The women's course was over 5km with 300m climb, and Buchanan's time of 24.06 gave her 7th place overall in a closely-packed finish. Pippa Maddams (Keswick) was the leading Briton in 2nd place in 23.22.

The men's race is run as a 3-leg relay, with each leg following the same route of 7km with 500m climb. The invited British team was made up of Rob Baker, Ian Holmes (Bingley), and Simpson on the 3rd leg in 31.35, giving him 13th place overall as well as the leading Under 23 time just 2 seconds clear of Italian Luca Cagnati who had run on the first leg.

Full details and results are [here](http://www.gscsimorbegno.altervista.org/Trofeo%20Vanoni.htm#class).
