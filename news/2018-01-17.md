**Exciting news from this year's Peebles Outdoor Film Festival (26-28 Jan) at the town's Eastgate Theatre. This year’s festival includes a session on Sat 27 Jan (entitled Going the Distance), which sees a programme of short films that celebrate female runners and other athletes, followed by an afternoon with three ultra and distance running stars – Jasmin Paris, Lucy Colquhoun and Susan Ridley. Hosted by nutritionist Ruth McKean, the trio will reveal all about their running lives. Also look out for a special run with Lucy Colquhoun the same morning, plus an appearance on Sun 28 Jan by endurance athlete Jenny Tough who will present on some of her many world firsts, including a recent solo, unsupported run across Morocco’s Atlas Mountains. Full details in the festival programme here, [https://eastgatearts.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/POFF\_2018.pdf](https://eastgatearts.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/POFF_2018.pdf)**
