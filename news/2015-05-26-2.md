Jasmin Paris takes an outstanding new ladies' record home, cutting one minute fifty seconds off Angela Mudge's 2008 record.

Finlay Wild clear of the field - improved a few minutes on 2014 in spite of slower conditions.

Alasdair Anthony was awarded the John Dare shield for an outstanding performance in both the Scottish Island Peaks Race and the Jura race, coming first in the SIPR with Es Tresidder (Jura winner in 2011) and second in the Jura a week later.
