**Angus Munro Hill Race**, 14 July

Just over 100 lined up for the long hard challenge round Glen Clova, this year counting towards both the SHR and the British Championships. Fortunately the clouds lifted from the summit of the northern plateau soon after the start of the race, so navigation wasn't a real problem. Stopping the English men was as expected an insuperablele one and they took the bulk of the top spots - though Ian Wellock (HBT), Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) and Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) all came home in the top ten.

Jill Mykura (Carnethy) had Janet McIver (Dark Peak) on her heels for the whole of the second half of the course and just managed to maintain her advantage to win the women's race.

**David Shepherd Memorial Glamaig Hill Race**, 14 July

Brian Marshall (HELP) must have had an unimpeded view of every rock on Glamaig, for he has now won the race for the last five years in succession. Andrew Shepherd (Carnethy) has run 19 of the 20 races.
