**Krunce 2022 update**:

Unfortunately the 2022 Aberdeenshire Krunce series continues to be cancelled until further notice.

Three local running clubs (Cosmics, Garioch Road Runners and Insch Trail Runners) in partnership continue to work with Forestry Land Scotland to establish when the existing or a new route can be found, which does include alternative options in the shire. All 3 clubs have also been exploring non FLS options, but with no success so far. Difficulties around access permissions, fallen trees and adequate parking have hampered our efforts. When the series resumes dates will be posted on the SHR web site.
