---
title: "Highlander Mountain Marathon, 31 March to 1 April"
date: 2007-04-19
excerpt: "The Highlander Mountain Marathon is to be shown on TV as part of BBC2's _The Adventure Show_ on 29 April (6.10pm)."
---

The inaugural Highlander Mountain Marathon took place over the weekend of 31 March - 1 April in Strathconon. Competitors enjoyed 2 days of perfect weather, a variety of interesting courses, and the comfort of a catered marquee on Saturday night. The A class was won in an impressive 7 hours 55 minutes by Tim Higginbottom and Chris Near, ahead of second placed Carl Pryce and Jason Williamson.

Full results, pictures and more details can be found [here](http://www.highlandermountainmarathon.org.uk). A report is also available on our Forum [here](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/Forum/viewtopic.php?t=22).

The event was filmed for the Adventure Show and the programme is to be broadcast on BBC2 on Sunday 29 April at 6.10pm.”
