Enter via Entry Central - [Lilliesleaf 10K Run for Harris Trust - EntryCentral](https://www.entrycentral.com/HarrisTrust-Lilliesleaf10k)

The Lilliesleaf 10k Run is an off road, hilly route through the fields and tracks of the beautiful Riddell Estate with scenic views of the Eildon and Cheviot Hills.

**This annual fundraising event is organised by the charity Harris Trust.**

It is a family friendly event and all ages are welcome although under 16s must have a responsible adult present. Dogs are welcome but must be on a lead. There will be walkers along the route

The race has a limit of 150 runners and will be filled on a first come first served basis.

Entry is a 'pay what you can' donation to make the event as inclusive as possible. 

Prizes will be awarded to 1st, 2nd and 3rd: M/F, V50 M/F, U16 M/F, Runners with dogs. 

Held under Scottish Hill Runner rules

**Registration -** Opens at 10am - Currie Memorial Hall, Main Street, Lilliesleaf TD6 9HX

**Race briefing (10.50am) / Start / Finish** at the Leaf Green (along from Currie Memorial Village Hall)

**Post Race** **Refreshments and Prizegiving** at the Currie Memorial Hall - the village hall volunteers will be providing free delicious homemade soup and bread!
