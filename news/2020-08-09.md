**Caerketton Hill Race – Virtual Challenge**

Caerketton Hill race had previously been scheduled to take place this coming Wednesday, 12th August but sadly will not be happening as a result of Covid-19.

However, Carnethy are organising a virtual challenge event w/c 10 th August. You basically just need to time yourself over the route anytime between Monday 10th August and Sunday 17th August, email time to race organiser Stewart Whitlie, who will collate and publish the results. Non-Carnethy runners welcome to take part.

Further details are on the Carnethy web site: [www.carnethy.com](http://www.carnethy.com)
