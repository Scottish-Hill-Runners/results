The two young super stars Robbie Simpson and Catriona Buchanan have won the 2 race from 3 Under 23 championship, by winning the first two races. The interest now is in seeing whether they can do the same in the Senior Championship ! In the Men's Prasad Prasad is unbeaten with Robbie second in both races, while Catriona is currently leading the women's but Fiona Maxwell beat her in the first race.

Categories that look very open include the Over 60s men, the Over 40s men and the Over 40s women (all 3 have difference winners and a mixture of other placings). Holder, Anita Hamilton, is looking favourite in the Over 50s women, while Ronnie Gallagher is also unbeaten in the Over 50s men.

Despite a 3rd place in the Women's team at Ben Lomond, Carnethy is leading all four team categories, but still could easily change, particularly in the women once other clubs have finished more races. though they do look more dominent in the Mens.
