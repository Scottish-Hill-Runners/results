This excellent new book, "Classic Hill Runs and Races in Scotland", by [Steven Fallon](http://www.stevenfallon.co.uk) of Carnethy has now been published. The 192-page guide gives details of a selection of the best hill races and hill runs from across Scotland, complete with route maps and some great photos. It would make an excellent present for that mad-keen hillrunner in your life! Normal retail price is £10. You can order your copy from [Amazon](http://www.amazon.co.uk/Classic-Hill-Runs-Races-Scotland/dp/1907025065/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1259343508&sr=1-1) or [Cordee](http://cordee.co.uk/CTJ029.php) or get a copy from the Run and Become shop in Edinburgh.

You can read more about Steven on his own [website](http://www.stevenfallon.co.uk).

An unnamed SHR contact raves (and mixes metaphors) on the cover: "This is the book to quench the thirst of anyone who loves to run on the hills. Essential reading for all abilities".
