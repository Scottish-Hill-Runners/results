We have the new SHR calendars and journals just delivered from the printers. Please renew your SHR membership and collect your copies of the calendar and journal at the hall in Penicuik at Carnethy 5 registration on Saturday.

If you are unable to be at the C5 tomorrow then it would be great if you could arrange for one of your clubmates to pick up your copies for you. This will save us a little bit of cost in posting them out.

  

SHR are very grateful to Carnethy HRC for allowing us a table at the C5 registration to distribute the calendars and journals. It presents a great opportunity for runners to renew their memberships or join for the first time.

  

Remember, SHR is composed of people just like you - we're all volunteers who provide the services that SHR supports through our own time. It helps enormously to get memberships from as many people as possible who share our enjoyment of running and racing in the Scottish hills and want to see Scottish Hill Runners continue.

So if you are a lapsed member or have never been a member, please consider joining for 2019 either in person at the C5 on Saturday or via the online membership link -

[http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/HowToJoin.aspx](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/HowToJoin.aspx)
