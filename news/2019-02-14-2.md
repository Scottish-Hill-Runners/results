date: 2019-02-14

With entries now open for Screel, 14th April, which is the first counter in the SHR Championship Series, remember it is also the Scottish Athletics West District Championship race.

Cioch Mhor, 23rd March, is the SA North District Championship race and later in the year there is the East District Championship race at Broughton, 24th August.
