Message from the organisers:

“The Endurancelife Real Relay is an exciting, spontaneous attempt by runners from all over the UK and Ireland to follow the entire route of the Olympic Torch. Running 24/7, the real relay is divided in to stages of approximately 10 miles length. Each stage may be run by an individual runner or by a team. Runners may choose their exact route and may run off road or via quiet back lanes. The only requirement is that the baton must pass through any location where the torch was carried (on foot as opposed to by bus).

The real relay has already run over 1500 miles and is raising money for a great children’s charity – CHICKS. Hundreds of runners have already taken part and are now hoping to see the baton passed successfully around NI and on to Dublin. It really will be a great achievement to get the baton through to London in time for the Olympic games. It is a great way for grassroot runners to get actively involved in the spirit of the Olympics.  
Please help! Please spread the word to your running friends. Please sign up for a stage.
