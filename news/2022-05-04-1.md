---

Good news! After a few years absent we are pleased to announce that Dunnydeer Hill race is back. This will be held on Sunday 5th June at 2pm with the race starting and finishing at the Insch Hospital.

Pre-entry for the race is preferred and makes our job easier, this can be completed online through the link below however we will also accept entries on the day.  Please note that pre-entry does not cover payment so no financial loss if you enter but cant make the race.

[**Webscorer – Dunnydeer**](https://www.webscorer.com/register?raceid=275066)

Entry will be £5 for the main race.  Registration for payment and number collection will open at 12.30pm and will close at 1.45pm (look out for the ITRC tent).  The race will commence at 2.00pm.
