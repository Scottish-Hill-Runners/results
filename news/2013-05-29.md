title: "Cort ma Law Race - reminder of kit requirements"
date: 2013-05-29
excerpt: "A reminder that you will need to wear or carry full kit for the Cort Ma Law race from the race organiser..."
---

David Rogers, organiser of the Cort ma law race has asked us to remind runners that it is _"an AM race and that therefore normal safety rules apply, i.e. people should carry windproof body cover, other body cover appropriate for the conditions, a map of the route, a compass and a whistle.

Every year a few people – sometimes experienced hill-runners - turn up without the necessary gear and though so far we have always so far managed to beg, borrow or steal kit from others for them there is always the risk that we will have to refuse entries.
