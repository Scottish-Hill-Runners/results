---

Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) won the Ochil 2000’s race on Saturday 22nd August in 02:59:01 ahead of Westies runners Gwyn Bellamy and Steve Halsall in 2nd & 3rd.

First lady was Andrea Priestly (Ochils) with Louise Burt (Fife AC) and Michelle Hetherington (Helensburgh) 2nd & 3rd.

Considerable tree planation work and the consequent rough ground and new deer fence made the run to Dumyat from Blairdennon even harder than usual. Split times for the check-points are on the Ochil Hill Runners website here - [http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/](http://www.ochilhillrunners.org.uk/)
