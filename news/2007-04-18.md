Reigning British Champion Natalie White (Bingley), on holiday from her native Yorkshire, showed all the ladies, and many a man, a clean pair of Walshes as she finished 11th overall in this race. Karen Macleod (Inverness) was 2nd lady and 1st lady veteran.

Colin Donnelly (Lochaber) was first man and first veteran, coming home half a minute ahead of Kyle Greig of Forres.

The results are now posted [here](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0023&RaceYear=2007).
