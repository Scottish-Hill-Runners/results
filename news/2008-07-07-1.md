---
title: "Marshall succeeds where Federer fails: Glamaig, 5 July"
date: 2008-07-07
excerpt: "Brian Marshall (HELP) wins 6-in-a-row; Lois Noble takes the women's prize.  '"
---

Unlike Roger Federer at Wimbledon, Brian Marshall (HELP) succeeded in winning the Glamaig Hill Race for an incredible 6th year in a row.

Brian reached the summit in around 37 minutes, just ahead of Alec Keith (HBT), and then charged down the scree back to the Sligachan Hotel in one-third of that time. Alec couldn't match Brian on the descent, but finished 2nd overall and first M40, ahead of Kenny Riddle (Highland HR) and Steven Fallon (Carnethy).

Lois Noble (Cosmic HB) was 1st woman (and first F40), whilst Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber AC) finished 2nd and Elaine Rose (Pennine FR) 3rd.
