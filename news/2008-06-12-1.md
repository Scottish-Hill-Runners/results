At the halfway stage, Bob Wiseman (Ochil HR) is the new leader of the Bog and Burn Series, following his 2nd place at Cort-ma Law. Bob is in a strong position to win the series, although there are still 5 races left for the likes of Jethro Lennox or Kenny Richmond to clinch the title.

Val Houston (Westerlands) is currently leading the women's league, although Angela Mudge is strongly placed to defend her title with 3 wins out of 3 already.

The next race in the series is the midge hell horror show of Ben Sheann on June 18th.
