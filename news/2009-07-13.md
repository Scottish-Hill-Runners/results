title: "Prasad takes Meall An t-Suidhe, 11 July"
date: 2009-07-13
excerpt: "Prasad Prasad was a clear winner in this short (and very steep!) SHR Championship counter, with Emma Birnie the winner of the ladies race.  '"
---

Prasad Prasad (Clydesdale) was a clear winner of this short (and very steep!) SHR Championship counter in a fast time of 29:31. Al Anthony (Ochils) was 2nd, with Brian Marshall (HELP) 3rd. Dave Rodgers (Lochaber) was 4th overall and first V40.

The women's race was extremely close with 18 seconds covering the first 4 women. Emma Birnie (Bellahouston) took the race in 41:01, just ahead of Veronique Oldham (Cosmics) 41:07, with Ruth Mackenzie (Deeside) 3rd in 41:10 and local girl Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber) 4th in 41:19.
