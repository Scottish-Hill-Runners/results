Newtyle hill race 

19th April at 7pm 

from "The Field", 

Brae Street, 

Dunkeld.

Entry on the night, £10 per entry. 

Full body cover, plus hat, gloves, must be carried or worn and compass and whistle with map of the route must be carried.  

Parking is in Dunkeld at the main car park and in the town. Please walk or run up to registration in the Field from 6pm.

There will be a small prize-giving in The Field afterwards.
