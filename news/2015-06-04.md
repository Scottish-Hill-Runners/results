**Symonds smashes record at Cort-ma Law**

110 runners turned out in near perfect weather to traverse the bogs of Cort-ma Law on Wednesday.

Joe Symonds (Shettleston) took 33 seconds off Robbie Simpson's record at Cort-ma Law to finish in 47 minutes and 4 seconds. Ben Stevenson (Ochil Hill Runners) was second in 50:59, followed by Iain Gilmore of Carnethy in 52:30.

Ruth Fraser-Moodie (unattached) was first woman in 59:41, followed by Madeline Robinson (Helensburgh) in 61:27 and Jill Stephen (HBT) in 63:56.

Male and female team prizes were won by Shettleston and Westerlands respectively.

Brian Marshall is believed to have set a new record for a barefoot runner, but he may have to be banned for ever, as feet are part of the body - unless he was carrying his footwear with the rest of his full body cover.
