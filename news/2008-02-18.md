Inevitably, just as soon as you've got your grubby paws on our magnificent new SHR Calendar, with all its 141 race details checked and double checked, there is going to be a change!

And so it is.... Cioch Mhor will now be held on Sunday, April 13th. (rather than April 19th as in the printed calendar, or as is still showing on the HHR website). All other details remain the same. The big plus is that you'll be able to run the in the SHR Championship Race at Knockfarrel on the Saturday (April 12th) and then move next door for Cioch Mhor on the Sunday.

It was great to see so many of you at the SHR stall at Carnethy on Saturday. Welcome to new members and welcome back to renewers. If you werent able to get to Carnethy your Calendar will be posted out to you shortly, as long as you have paid your membership fee. So if you havent yet renewed/joined then get all the details [here](http://shr.uk.com.95.seekdotnet.com/HowToJoin.aspx)
