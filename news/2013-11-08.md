As notified to our members previously the Annual General Meeting of Scottish Hill Runners takes place in the hall at Symington after the prize giving has concluded for the Tinto race.

Please make an effort to attend where you can see what we get up to and contribute to any debates that arise. Members, potential members and ex-members are all welcome.
