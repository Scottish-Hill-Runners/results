The rescheduled Morven Hill Race saw strong performances across all categories, with Elena Heger (Shettleston Harriers) and Matt Knowles (Carnethy) taking top honours in the main event.  
[Results click here](https://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0102&RaceYear=2025)  
  
In the Half Morven, victory went to Alfie Perrin (Insh Trail Running Club).  
[Half Morven Results](https://protay.co.uk/morven-2025/results.php)
