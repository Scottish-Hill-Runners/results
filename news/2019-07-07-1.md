---
title: "Es Tresidder breaks Ramsay Round Record"
date: 2019-07-07
excerpt: "Lochaber AC runner, Es Tresidder has broken the Ramsay Round record this weekend."
---

Es broke Jasmin Paris's Ramsey Round record, which she set in 2016 by just over a minute. Es ran 16.12.32 .  

Jasmin was one of a team who supported him and ran with him on the last leg pushing him on to break it!
