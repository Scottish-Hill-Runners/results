title: "Kilpatricks Hill Race - Wednesday 31st May 7.30pm"
date: 2017-05-28
excerpt: "Reminder of kit requirements and parking instructions"
---

“Hope to see a good turnout at the Kilpatricks Hill Race on Wednesday. If everyone could please take note of the following:

Parking – The Kilpatricks are a popular spot, parking is very limited and the local police are increasingly strict with illegally parked cars. Please make every effort to share lifts or use public transport; Old Kilpatrick train station is right beside registration.

Kit Check – There will be kit checks on the night. Please come prepared with map, compass, hat, gloves and full-body waterproof cover. No Kit = No Race.

See you all at the race!”
