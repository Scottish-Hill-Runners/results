The Long Classics series saw an interesting twist at yesterday's Two Breweries with a dead heat between Mark Harris and Stewart Whitlie leapfrogging them both above Andy Fallas, and Jasmin Paris taking the lead in the women's competition ahead of Eilidh Raistrick.

With one race remaining, the Pentland Skyline on 9th October, the men's title is still wide open between Andy Fallas, Mark Harris and Stewart Whitlie. In the women's competition, Jasmin Paris now looks uncatchable in 1st place, with Eilidh Raistrick strongly placed in 2nd, however the 3rd place in the women's race is still up for grabs between Gill Irvine, Alayne Finlay, Julia Connor and Jacqui Higginbottom.
