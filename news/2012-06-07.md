_From organiser Dave Hanlon_

This year saw numbers back to a respectable 41 after last year's disappointing 14. We also had around 10 kids taking part in the "Glas T toe tickle" fun run, which for 2 year-old Maisie Gorman was no mean feat. Manny did need to do a bit of encouraging though!! Kyle Greig took the honours for Forres with Paul Faulkner not far behind. Chloe Cox took the women's race in an impressive 6th place overall. Davie Weir running now for Forres was 4th in a new V50 record and Gordon Watson from Dundee Hawks also set a new V60 record. Portobello took the men's team prize. The conditions helped with little breeze and cool temperatures for the most part. It was inspiring to see Jamie Andrew complete the race and run all the way back down on his blades.
