On Sunday 14th 2016, Finlay Wild and Tim Gomersall traversed the Cuillin ridge from Sgurr nan Gillean to Gars-bheinn in winter conditions in 6 hours 14 minutes and 17 seconds. While we can't be 100% certain this is a record because we've never recorded one before, we're treating it as one (now added to the SHR Long Distance Records pages) and would be surprised if it's not! So congratulations to Finlay and Tim for this startling achievement!
