**BROUGHTON JUNIOR HILL RACES**

**SCOTTISHATHLETICS JUNIOR HILL RUNNING CHAMPIONSHIPS 2026**

**Saturday 16th May 2026**

**Incorporating the International Youth Cup 2026 Selection Race (for Athletes born in 2009/2010) U12/U14/U16/U18 counter race in the scottishathletics Junior Hill Running League 2026**

**RACE DETAILS**

There are four races which will take place. The schedule for the races is:

**12.00pm**: Under 12 (born 2015/16) Race plus Fun Run: 1.2km, 80m ascent/descent

**12.15pm**: Under 14 (born 2013/14) Race: 3.1km, 130m ascent/descent

**12.45pm**: Under 16 (born 2011/12) Race: 5.3km, 310m ascent/descent

**1.00pm**: Under 18 (born 2009/10) Race: 5.3km, 310m ascent/descent (Youth Cup Trial)

All races start and finish close to the Shepherd’s Cottage (Grid Ref 120374).
