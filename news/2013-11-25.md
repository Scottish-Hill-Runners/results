We have had a great response and are expecting 50 for what promises to be a very lively “do”! Places for the meal are now taken, but feel free to come to the prize giving and ceilidh in the village hall. The hall is a short walk from the lodge. The proposed schedule is:

1830-1930 prize giving in Grandtully Village Hall (PH9 0PL)

1930-2100 meal in [The Grandtully](http://www.thegrandtully.com/index.php) lodge/bunkhouse

2100-2330 ceilidh in the Village Hall with the Hoochie Coochie Ceilidh Band

The menu choices for the meal will be:

**_Starter_**

Lentil soup and roll

Chicken liver pate with oatcakes and caramelised onion chutney

**_Main course_**

Venison stew with new potatoes and veg

Thai green chicken curry and basmati rice

Vege pasta bake

**_Dessert_**

Toffee and vanilla cheesecake with cream

Sticky toffee pudding with ice cream

**_NB_** _If you have a preferred main course, please let me know your preference asap so I can inform the caterer._

Drinks will be available from the bar for the meal, but for the prize giving and ceilidh in the hall please bring your own booze (and preferred drinking utensil!). A limited stock of bottled beer may be available from the lodge bar, but to be sure bring your own!!  

For those staying at the lodge, the rooms will be open from 4pm. Angela will let you know who is in which room! Bed linen is provided but please bring your own towels.
