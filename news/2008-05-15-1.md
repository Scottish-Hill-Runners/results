Just a reminder that the dates of these races have been changed since the printed SHR calendar came out:

Kinnoull Hill Race is on Wednesday, 21st May, and is the 3rd counter in the [Bog & Burn](Championship.aspx?ChampionshipID=C-003) series.

Red Moss Revolution is on 25th June (not 21st May as in the printed calendar), and is the 7th counter in the Bog & Burn series.
