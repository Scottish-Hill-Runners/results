date: 2015-04-05
excerpt: "The Cioch Mhor races were held on Saturday 4th April"
---

The Cioch Mhor race on Saturday 4th April was won by Gordon Lennox (Forres Harriers) ahead of David Wilby and Ross Campbell (both Highland Hill Runners).

The winner of the ladies race was Helen Gertig (Inverness Harriers) with Caroline Marwick (Inverness Harriers) and Jacqui Higginbottom (Carnethy) in 2 nd and 3rd.

Stuart Kirk of Caithness AAC won the Junior Race.
