Wins for Joseph Wright and Clare Whitehead (both Deeside Runners) at the Creag Choinnich race on Wednesday 20th June.

Full results on the SHR results tab

Breezy, cool but dry conditions for a good turnout of 48 runners at this years mid summer race on Creag Choinnich.

Peter Henry climbed well to be the first to pass the piper at the summit but was overhauled on the descent by Joseph Wright who won in a time of 18 mins 15 seconds. Andy King gathered the third place.

Clare Whitehead led the ladies home with Lizzie Knox in second place and Marie Entwistle in third place.

Thanks goes to the Braemar Castle team for hosting the race and providing tea and cake in the grand setting of the great hall afterwards. Special mention to all the marshals and to Sam and Calan for piping the racers.
