Great conditions for the 32nd running of the Chapelgill Hill Race. The race was won by Andrew Gilmore (Carnethy) just ahead of Al Anthony (Ochils) and David Fulton (HBT) in 3rd.

The ladies winner was Mary Lye (Carnethy) ahead of 2nd and 3rd Joan Wilson (Lomonds) and Liza Blackley (U/A) respectively.

[Results](RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0019&Year=2015)

The younger runners were out in force too running up Congrie Hill.
