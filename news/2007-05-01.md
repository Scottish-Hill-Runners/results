---
title: "The Highland Fling, 28 April"
date: 2007-05-01
excerpt: "Jez Bragg and Lucy Colquhoun set new records for the Highland Fling race from Milngavie to Tyndrum along the West Highland Way.  '"
---

Jez Bragg and Lucy Colquhoun each beat last year's winning times (and therefore inaugural records) for The Highland Fling by well over an hour. There's more information about the race on its web site [here](http://www.highlandflingrun.co.uk/).

The results are also posted [here](http://scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0518&RaceYear=2007).
