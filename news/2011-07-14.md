Maddy Moss race report

After the horrendous conditions for the race last year, it was a relief to have a warm sunny evening for the event, no doubt encouraging the great turnout of 128 runners in this 9th race of the 2011 Bog and Burn championship.

Alasdair Anthony of Ochils was the clear winner in 50:31, a great time considering it was his first run since completing an impromptu Ramsay Round 10 days ago! Kenny Richmond of Bellahouston was second overall (1st MV) followed closely by Mark Harris of Fife in third (2nd MV). Des Crowe of Shettleston was first MSV (13th overall) and Murdo MacLeod of Westies first M60+ (50th overall).

Christina Rankin of Kilbarchan continued her run of good form in the Bog and Burn series to be first lady home in 60:17, followed by Shona Robertson of Westies (1st FV) and Eilidh Wardlaw of HBT. Westies continued their good haul of the prizes with Pauline McAdam coming 1st FSV and Patricia McLaughlin 1st F60+.

Carnethy won the men’s team prize, but disappeared before they were able to collect their beer!

Many thanks to race organiser John Macewan and all those who helped with marshalling, time keeping and results!
