---
title: "Cairn William Race Results"
date: 2016-09-19
excerpt: "Wins for Peter Henry and Sally Wallis (both Deeside Runners) at the Cairn William race on Saturday 17th September"
---

35 runners set out in near perfect running conditions. There were comfortable wins for Peter Henry and Sally Wallis, both from Deeside Runners. There was hotter competition in the Men's V40 race with positions changing throughout the race. A fast final descent gave Hayden Lorimer the 1st V40 prize and 2nd place overall. First women's V40 was Jane Glen who also took 2nd spot in the overall standings in the women's race. V50 winners were Tim Griffin (Cosmics) and Anita Hamilton (Cosmics).
