Al Anthony (running for Scotland Central) was a superb 2nd at the Winter Hill Inter-Counties race down in Belmont, Lancashire, followed by Grant Stewart (S.East) in 4th. Tom Owens (S.West) was 12th, Simon Peachey (S.East) 16th and Yujiro Iida (S.East) 23rd. Elke Schmidt (S.West) was well up in the ladies race until a twisted ankle forced her to slow and she finished 7th, one place behind Claire Gordon (S.East).
