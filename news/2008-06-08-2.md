---
title: "Debut win for Winter at Merrick"
date: 2008-06-08
excerpt: "Stephen Winter of Shettleston recorded his first race win at the Merrick new race route.  '"
---

Stephen Winter of Shettleston recorded his first race win at Merrick on Sunday, finishing almost 6 minutes ahead of his nearest rival, Mark Whitelaw of Annan. Jo Schreiber was first, and only, lady. Celebrations were somewhat muted due to the disappointing turnout of only 15 runners, who nevertheless enjoyed a good day's racing over the new course, which is longer, but generally more runnable than the old one, and takes a straightforward out and back route to the summit of Merrick.
