The Two Mamores Hill Race - Saturday 10th June 2023

Entry on SiEntries - [SiEntries - Two Mamores Hill Race](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=11532)

A hill race across two Munro summits in the Western Mamores, Glen Nevis; Mullach nan Coirean and Stob Ban.

Approx 13km w/ 1300m of ascent, this will be an unmarked course over steep and rough terrain, with particular care needed on several sections.

Full body waterproof cover, hat, gloves, map, compass and whistle will be required, with no exceptions. As the race will be held early in the season, runners are asked to think carefully about clothing and kit on the day, and extra kit requirements may be enforced.

The race will be held under Scottish Hill Runners rules.
