title: "Mither Tap Race Results & Report"
date: 2019-07-22
excerpt: "Wins for Hamish Wolfe (Carnethy) and Moira Davie (Forres) at the Mither Tap Hill Race on Thursday 18th July."
---

The race started in warm and muggy conditions, but as the runners were approaching the hill-top a lightning storm ensued, necessitating alteration to a lower route off the summit. Conditions underfoot – initially dry, then wet! Thanks to all the marshalls and the Bennachie visitor centre and wardens for allowing us use of the centre.

Matt Brettle (Race Organiser)
