_From organiser Ali Hubbard_

Great turnout for this years Heart of the Park trail race which starts and finishes in Braemar Highland Games Park. Dan Whitehead and Paul Raistrick battled at the front around a course which involved wading thigh deep bogs, a couple of dips in the River Dee and avoiding torrential downpours before Dan finally took the honours in a time of 52 minutes exactly. First lady was Irene Malone who used her familiarity with the course to good effect to win in a time of 1hr 15mins 28secs. Second lady was Kata Tar of HBT. Ian Anderson was first male veteran and Marvin Speece from Butte, Montana in the USA was first MSV, from the imaginatively named Butte Piss and Moan Running Club!
