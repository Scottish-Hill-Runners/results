The 2024 SHR Championship races have been confirmed.  
They won’t be set up in the calendar just yet, but so people can plan ahead these are the six races that will comprise next year’s championship

*   The Cobbler (S)                                 13 April
*   Two Mamores (M)                              8 June
*   Arrochar Alps (L)                             29 June
*   Moffat (L)                                         3 August
*   Tap o’Noth (S)                                 17 August
*   Run Of The Mill (M)                           5 October
