The Lochaber AC Triple Hirple races were on at the weekend 2nd > 4th July

In the Graham Brooks Memorial Meall an t-Suidhe race on Saturday 2nd, the winners were Clyde Williamson (Cosmics) and Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber).

Sunday's winners at the Half Ben were John Yells and Isla MacKay (both Lochaber).

And in the third race on Monday, Callum Fraser and Amy Disher (both Lochaber were the winners.

Overall Triple Hirple winners  for 2022 were Callum Fraser, 2nd, 2nd, 1st, and Amanda Blackhall, 1st, 2nd, 2nd.
