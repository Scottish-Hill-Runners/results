A record number of 124 runners and 19 juniors turned up to this year’s Greenmantle Dash, which was held again with unusually good weather. However, the rain of the previous days had transformed the field into an epic mud bath and it was very impressive to see people running (or attempting to run) through that.

Congratulations to Eddie Narbett from Carnethy (15:39) and Pippa Carcas from Doss AC (19:75) for their wins. The Michael Greens trophy was awarded to Angus McClelland from HBT, who totally smashed the Fun Run with his remarkable hill running skills.

Results are provisional only, especially considering makeshift bib numbers we’re expecting some mistakes. Please contact Penicuik Harriers or the RO ([j-friedrich2@web.de](mailto:j-friedrich2@web.de)) if you spot any errors.
