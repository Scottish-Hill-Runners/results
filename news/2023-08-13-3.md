Latest scores after Glenshee 9, 6th race in the 2023 series, as per link below.

**Steve Halsall** of **Shettleston Harriers** continues to lead in the men’s category and **Ella Peters** of **Westerlands** takes over at top in the women’s category.

As things stand 8 people, have completed 4 or more races in the series. **Nicky Spinks** of **Dark Peak** is only person to have completed all 6 races.

Next race up in series is Ochil 2000s, on 26th August.
