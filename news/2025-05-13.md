Goatfell – travel update Please be advised that, the 9:15 Calmac sailing on Saturday morning from Troon to Brodick is now full, including walk on foot passengers. Participants will receive a link asking whether they are booked onto a ferry. If you haven’t pre-booked, the system will notify the RO automatically. The RO will assess the need to move the race start time back to 13:00pm to accommodate those unable to travel on the 9:15 sailing. The 10:50 sailing still has availability, as does the return sailing to Troon at 17:20.
