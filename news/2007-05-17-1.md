---
title: "HBT to the fore at Kinnoull, 16 May"
date: 2007-05-17
excerpt: "Ray Ward and Don Naylor (HBT) were first and second, whilst Sarah Blake (Penicuik Harriers) just beat Val Houston (Westies) in the race to be the leading woman.  '"
---

Ray Ward and last year's winner Don Naylor made it a 1-2 for HBT at Kinnoull. Last year's runner-up Graeme Campbell (Highland HR) was third. The women's race went to Sarah Blake (Penicuik Harriers), with Val Houston (Westies) recording her second second-place in five days.

Dave Hanlon tells us that Sandy Bennet (Carnegie), who was uncharacteristically last, actually ran the entire race pushing his two kids in a buggy. (Is this a first?) Continuing with the junior theme, 12-year old Jamie Crowe (son of Des) had an astonishing run as second junior (Jamie Stevenson, son of John, was first), finishing 26th only 3 minutes behind his dad. And finally, at the very front Don Naylor lost his substantial lead over Ray Ward by taking a detour half-a-mile from the finish...
