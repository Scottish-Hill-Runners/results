Lars Ottemoller (Carnethy), who is apparently soon moving to a new job in Bergen, retained his individual title at the Falkland Festival Hill Race. He covered the 2.5 mile/1200ft course up and down the East Lomond in 20 minutes 56 seconds. Fife AC junior Robyn Graham placed 29th overall to claim the female prize clocking 29 minutes 25 seconds.
