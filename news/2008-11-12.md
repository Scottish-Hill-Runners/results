Once again, there will be a midwinter hillrunners' training weekend, giving you an ideal opportunity to put those new year's resolutions to improve your performance on the hills into immediate practice!

The weekend, based in Wanlockhead Outdoor Centre, is designed to cater for all endurance athletes wishing to develop their hill running abilities. Junior athletes (over 12) are especially welcome and all sessions are designed to be suitable for their capabilities.

There will be three organised running sessions – a paarlauf (paired relay interval session on a grassy hill), a hill navigation exercise (orienteering course across moorland), and a longer easy run over the moors. All activities are designed to improve fitness in an enjoyable way, and the emphasis will be on informal group activities that are about having fun. In addition to the running, we will have time to discuss aspects of preparation and training for hill running and racing.
