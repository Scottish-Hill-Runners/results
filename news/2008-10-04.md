---
title: "Another good run for Iida in the WMRA Grand Prix, 27 September"
date: 2008-10-04
excerpt: "Yujiro Iida (Edinburgh University and Japan) was seventh in the sixth of this year’s WMRA Grand Prix races.  '"
---

The penultimate round of this year’s WMRA Grand Prix was held in Puchberg (Austria), the men’s and women’s races being won by World Champions Jonathan Wyatt (New Zealand) and Andrea Mayr (Austria) respectively, Edinburgh University’s Yujiro Iida, whose goal is to represent Japan in next year’s World Championship, finished a creditable seventh in a field of 320.
