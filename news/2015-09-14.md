---
title: "Reinigeadall Race Results"
date: 2015-09-14
excerpt: "Wins for Willie MacRury and Peggi MacKellar at the race on Saturday 12th September"
---

A record turnout of 22 to watch local man Willie MacRury knock 2 and half minutes off the mens' record in what were not the best of conditions. Second place for last years winner Duncan McKellar with third to Manny Gorman. First lady, Peggi McKellar. Afterwards some of the runners decamped to Scalpay Village Hall for Manny's presentation on his Corbett round as part of the annual Mountain Festival and it was good to see a fair turnout of non runners come along for it also.

  

  
Stuart Simpson

Race Organiser
