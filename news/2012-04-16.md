The 2012 Junior Hill Running League’s first race took place in bright and dry conditions on East Lomond on 15th April. The event drew a bigger field than last year, with 40+ runners from 13 clubs – special mention to Strathearn Harriers, who brought along a magnificent nine! The new routes took in the summit and south side of East Lomond, along bridlepath, track and open grassland, and there were some fiercely fought contests as runners set out their stall for the season and the next league race at Ben Lomond on May 12th.
