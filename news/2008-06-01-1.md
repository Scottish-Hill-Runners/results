title: "Slioch Horseshoe, 31 May"
date: 2008-06-01
excerpt: "Simon Peachey won the inaugural Slioch Horseshoe Race in 2:02:09, followed by Al Anthony and Alec Keith. First lady was Eilidh Wardlaw in 2:58:50.  '"
---

Slioch put on a warm (if rough) welcome for the 39 runners tackling the inaugural race round its Horseshoe. [Highland Hillwalkers](http://www.spanglefish.com/highlandhillwalkingclub/) turned out in force to encourage them on their way (and coincidentally celebrate with one of their members who was clinching his last Munro).

Alasdair Anthony (Ochil), Brian Marshall (HELP) and Simon Peachey (Carnethy) headed for the main summit together, with Alec Keith (HBT) trying not to overheat in pursuit. Marshall sped away from the others on the descent from Sgurr an Tuill Bhain and was two minutes ahead when he reached Gleann Bianasdail. But somewhere on the run-in disaster struck - twisted ankle we think - he was seen going for the early bath, and he eventually jogged up to the finish in fourth. A delighted Peachey held off Anthony to win in 2:02:09, with Keith doing his Long Classics score no harm at all in third.

Eilidh Wardlaw (HBT) won the women’s race in 2:58:50 from Joanne Anderson (Carnethy) and Roxy Bannerman of hosts Highland HR.
