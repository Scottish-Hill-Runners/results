It was a sunny day and a record turnout of 86 runners for the Devil's Beeftub, fourth counter in this year's SHR championship. Brian Marshall's trademark demon descent secured his victory, ahead of Stewart Whitlie and Colin Donnelly. Brian's time of 19.50 beat John Wilkinson's long standing record by 6 seconds. Dawn Scott was first woman, ahead of Linda McEwan and Louise Burt. Andy McCall was first U23.
