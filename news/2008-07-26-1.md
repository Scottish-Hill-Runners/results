---
title: "Long Series update after Ben Rinnes"
date: 2008-07-26
excerpt: "Alec Keith continues to hang on to top spot in the Scottish Long Series following a 4th placed finish at Ben Rinnes.  '"
---

Alec Keith continues to hang on to top spot in the Long Series following Ben Rinnes, and is the only person to have completed the first 5 races of the series.

Brian Marshall has moved up to 2nd place and David Riach has leapt into 3rd position.

Joanne Anderson of Carnethy is currently the leading woman in the Long Series.
