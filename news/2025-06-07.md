The latest Scottish Championship positions after Yetholm are now available. There has now been one race at each distance, which gives a good idea of who is going to do well. Sasha Chepelin is leading the Senior men and Clara-Jane Irvine the Senior Women's. Only Veronique Oldham (O/55) and Colin Donnelly (O/60 & O/65) have maximum points for all three races in their categories.

The latest championship tables are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2025 - Male.xls) for men and [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Hill Running Championships 2025 - Female.xls) for women.
