---
title: "Triple Hirple Results"
date: 2017-07-05
excerpt: "Race results for Lochaber’s Meall an t-Suidhe, Half Ben and Cow Hill races over the weekend."
---

Finlay Wild dominated the Lochaber Triple Hirple races over the weekend with a hat-trick of wins. The ladies winners for the individual races were Diana MacDonald (HHR) at Meall an t-Suidhe, Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber) at the Half Ben and Caroline Marwick (Inverness Harriers) at the Cow Hill Race.

Full results for all three races are available on the SHR results tab.
