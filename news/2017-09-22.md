---
title: "Reinigeadal Hill Race – Results and Report"
date: 2017-09-22
excerpt: "A win for Michael Diver in the Reinigeadal race, Harris on Saturday 16th September"
---

Pretty much ideal conditions for this year's race which saw a deserved winner in Michael Diver, who as per normal for Michael arrived at the start on his bike all the way from Clydebank. However John from Brighton and Patrick from New York State had travelled further to the truly cosmopolitan world down south known as The Herrach.

Happiest runner was the slowest finisher, local Scout Leader, Robert Emmott who knocked 12 mins of his previous time and can't wait for next years race to go under the 2hrs mark.

Thanks to Daryll, Ranger for The North Harris Trust for his support with this one.

Stuart Simpson (Race Organiser)
