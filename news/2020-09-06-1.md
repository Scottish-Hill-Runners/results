Welcome to the Bog 'n' Burn series for 2020. Unfortunately, as the world struggles to battle COVID-19 we are not able to bring you races in their normal format this year, but we want to offer a chance to get muddy and wet in all the bogs and burns in the surrounding hills so have created a virtual series. We hope you are well and enjoy this event!
