**Junior Eildon Hill Race – 18th September**

Gala Harriers have now added a junior race to accompany the senior Eildon Three Hill Race.  The junior race will start at 11.00 am

There will be a 3km route for the U13 and a longer 4km route for U15 plus. Cost per runner £6.  Start time for both courses 11.00 am.

Enter at [www.galaharriers.co.uk](http://www.galaharriers.co.uk/)

Gala Harriers will operate under Scottish Athletics guidance and will follow any covid restrictions in place on the day of the race
