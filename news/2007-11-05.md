---
title: "SHR Championship mugs ready for collection, 5 November"
date: 2007-11-05
excerpt: "Those who completed this year's SHR Championship can now collect their memento (mug) from Ronnie Gallagher."
---

To the 60-odd people who completed this year's SHR Championship: could you please arrange to collect your memento (mug) from Ronnie Gallagher, or have someone collect it on your behalf? Ronnie would like to hand out as many as possible of the mugs at Tinto (10 November) and Meall a'Buachaille (17 November).

If you need to get in touch with Ronnie to arrange collection at another time, you'll find his contact details in the Calendar and the Committee section of this web site.
