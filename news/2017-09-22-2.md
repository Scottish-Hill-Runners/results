date: 2017-09-22
excerpt: "Renew your membership now for 2017 if you haven't already done so."
---

Of the 93 finishers of this year's championship the membership secretary has identified 11 who do not have a current membership for 2017. If you have completed the championship it may be that you have forgotten to renew for the current year. It would be great then if you were to now renew your membership for this year.

Only SHR members are entitled to receive a memento mug.

If you are unsure of your membership status please contact Gordon Pryde and he tell you your current status.
