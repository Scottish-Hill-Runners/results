SHR have been contacted by a producer working for BBC Newsbeat who is making a film about running.

The producer is hoping to speak to people who run for the therapeutic benefit it brings them, perhaps to help them cope with a particularly challenging period or event in their life. We’re looking for people who are really keen runners, but not necessarily in a competitive sense, and, crucially, under 30 years old.

The BBC will be making a series of short films with the same theme, that they are filming all over the country.

If any runners are interested in participating please get in touch.

The producer is Simon Whitlock ( [simon.whitlock@bbc.co.uk](mailto:simon.whitlock@bbc.co.uk) ) and he will be happy to chat further to anyone who’d like some more info.
