All the leaders stay the same after Durisdeer, but that hides some interesting battles and upsets..such as Westerlands taking both the Men's and Ladies Over 40s teams and Shettleston being beaten in the Senior Men's team !

Highlights include : Jacqui Higginbottom just hanging onto the lead in the Ladies Over 40s after a disasterous Durisdeer, while David Scott pulls clear in the men's Over 50. Douglas Milligan climbing up the Over 60s ranking and is looking good to retain his title, while Dawn Scott stays well clear in the Ladies...but can she hang on ?

Jethro Lennox is in a class of his own in the Senior Men, but Simon Peachey is really running well this year and has now moved into second.
