The Bog and Burn Results have finally been compiled. Alasdair Anthony won the series overall, followed by Adam Gatens. In the women's competition, Emma O'Shea was first, followed by Christina Rankin. Three people completed all 10 races: David Duncan, Graham Kelly and Ian McManus.
