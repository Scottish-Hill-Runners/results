title: "Marshall and Mykura win at Clachnaben, 14 April"
date: 2007-04-16
excerpt: "Brian Marshall (HELP) and Jill Mykura (Carnethy) won at snowy Criffel a month ago, and won again at sunny Clachnaben on Saturday."
---

Brian Marshall (HELP) and Jill Mykura (Carnethy) won at snowy Criffel a month ago, and won again at sunny Clachnaben on Saturday. Marshall's record in the race over the last five years is pretty impressive: 3-2-1-1-1. Race organiser Shelley Farrar reports:

"It was an an absolutely scorching day on South Deeside for the Clachnaben Hill Race. Temperatures must have been well into the 20's with a light breeze on the tops. Times were a little bit slower as a result but we did have a fantastic turnout of 98 runners. First male senior was Brian Marshall (HELP) in 1 hr 27mins 38s, Alec Keith (HBT) was first male vet (3rd overall), and first male SV was Tom Scott (Fife AC). For the women, Jill Mykura (Carnethy) was first in 1hr 40mins 47s, Sonia Armitage (Cosmics) was first female vet (2nd woman) and first female SV was Anita Hamilton (Cosmics). Men's team prize went to Carnethy and women's prize to Cosmics. As well as the photos of the race on the Cosmics' website there's a link for the winners to the Runningshop so they can spend their well-earned prizes."

The full results are [here](http://scottishhillrunners.uk.95.seekdotnet.com/RaceResults.aspx?RaceID=RA-0028&RaceYear=2007), and the the Cosmics' web site is [here](http://www.cosmics.org.uk/).
