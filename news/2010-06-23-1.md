---
title: "Run-4-It SHR Scottish Hill Running Champs after Glen Rosa"
date: 2010-06-23
excerpt: "We are now half way through the championship and have had one race at each distance, so it's a good indication of the way things are likely to go."
---

Prasad Prasad (Clydesdale) suffered his first defeat at Glen Rosa but still leads the championship, while the ladies has had a different winner at each race ... the latest being former champion Jill Mykura (Carnethy), making her comeback, and this has left Sarah Byrne (Lochaber) in the lead.

The listings after the first three races are [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR Scottish Hill Running Champs 2010.xls) (or available from Championships menu on right of screen, where there are also all time lists).
