---
title: "Balmedie Beach Race Report & Results"
date: 2023-06-16
excerpt: "Wins for Jonathan Gamble (Unattached) and Sue Taylor (Cosmics) at the Balmedie Beach race on Thursday 15th June."
---

On a beautiful evening, possibly a bit hot for Aberdonians, 23 runners turned out for a race up and down the big Balmedie dunes.  The first part of the course is flat and fast but then it's into the dunes for some gruelling ups and fun downs.  Despite extra canes on the northern part of the route this year, a number of runners missed a turning and unfortunately missed out the last few dunes. It's a difficult route to mark as there are so many paths in the dunes but I'll take note for next year and try to make it clearer.  The route itself does have minor variations each year due to the nature of the terrain.

Jonathon Gamble was the first runner home in an excellent time of 26.19, shaving a few minutes off last year's time and Sue Taylor F60 was the first lady home who completed the full route. Well done to you both and all the other runners who participated.

Fiona MacDonald (Race Organiser)
