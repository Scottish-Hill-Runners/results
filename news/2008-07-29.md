date: 2008-07-29
excerpt: "Another win for Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC)..."

Sixteen runners set off but, to the organisers' dismay, only fifteen returned. It turned out that the missing person had reached the summit and then, feeling unwell, had gone home.

Main results:

1st Colin Donnelly (Lochaber AC) 26:08  
2nd Tom Smith (Lochaber AC) 28:29  
3rd John Hepburn (Lochaber AC) 28:34  
1st M40 Niall MacBeath (Lochaber AC) 30:41  
1st M50 Harry Mathews (Unatt) 38:51  
1st F (and F40) Ann MacEachan (Unatt) 48:22
