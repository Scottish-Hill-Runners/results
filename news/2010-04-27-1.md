There are only a few places left on the navigation course which we advertised a couple of weeks ago (see below). **There's still time to book yourself a place though.**

"Following interest shown by members, SHR are organising a navigation course for hill runners on the weekend of 15/16th May based in Peebles. The course provider will be qualified mountain instructor, keen hill runner and mountain marathoner, Bruce Poll. The course is intended both for runners who have limited experience of hill running and for experienced hill runners who wish to brush up their skills. **The closing date for applications is 30th April 2010**. Places are limited and acceptance will be based on a first come first served basis.

You can download further details from [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR_NavCourse_Flyer_201004.pdf) and an application form from [here](Download.aspx?Item=SHR_NavCourse_AppForm_201004.pdf).

For those not able to make this first course, SHR hope to organise future courses subject to sufficient interest."
