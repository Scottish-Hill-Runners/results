Just a quick reminder from the race organiser that competitors at Saturday's Eagle Crag race will require full body cover and a whistle. Car sharing is also encouraged, as parking will be very limited.

At the same time, there is a reminder about the Ben Nevis race - if anyone is entered but not intending to run could they please let the organisers know asap, as there is a bulging reserve list full of eager guys and gals desperate to run!
