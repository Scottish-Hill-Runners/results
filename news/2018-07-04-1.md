Lochaber’s Triple Hirple went down to the wire on the Monday evening in Fort William. With James Espie (Deeside) not able to make the Cow Hill race due to work commitments, it was left to Patrick Roddy (Thames Hare & Hounds ) and Steven Traill (Lochaber) to battle it out for first spot. Thirty seconds was all that separated Patrick from Steven at the start of this race, but with Steven finishing in second place and Patrick further back in sixth, it was Steven who came out on top.

Elizabeth Apsley (Thames Hare & Hounds ) was first lady in all the races making it straight forward for the ladies.

Ian Adams
