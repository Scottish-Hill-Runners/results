---
title: "Another double for the Whiteheads: Ben Aigan, 18 June"
date: 2008-06-23
excerpt: "Dan and Clare Whitehead (Cosmic HB) notch up another family double, Clare breaking the women's record in the process.  '"
---

There was a record entry of 37 for the Ben Aigan Race on a pleasant evening. It included only one woman, Clare Whitehead (Cosmic HB), who proceeded to finish tenth overall and broke the four-year-old record by well over a minute.

Husband Dan won the race, followed by Craig Mitchell (Lochaber AC) and Robbie Cammack (Moray RR).
