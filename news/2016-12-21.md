Saturday the 31st December at 12.00.

Register at the red campervan at the end of Tulipan Crescent from 11am as usual and enjoy your last hill race of 2016.

We are really looking forward to seeing you again and hope you can come along and try for the usual glittering array of prizes.

  
Children under 12 are also welcome if they can bring along an adult who can keep up!
