title: "Record field and fast times for West Highland Way, 20 June"
date: 2009-06-21
excerpt: "Scott Bradley (Carnegie) overtakes Dutchman Jan Lantink to win in second-fastest time ever...  '"
---

There was a record turn-out (151 runners) and a close finish to this year's West Highland Way Race. Scott Bradley (Carnegie) finally overtook Jan Lantink (Holland) to win in the second-fastest time ever recorded, with Richie Cunningham (Carnegie) a couple of minutes behind Lantink in third. Sharon Law was the first women.

There's a full report on the **scottish**athletics web site [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=4303).
