---
title: "Dan Whitehead wins Cheyne Hill and Cosmics' Summer Series, 20 August"
date: 2009-08-24
excerpt: "Dan Whitehead beats Simon Peachey (both Cosmics) to clinch Cosmics' Summer Hill Series while Veronique Oldham breaks the ladies' record...  '"
---

Dan Whitehead (Cosmics) won the Cheyne Hill Race by 12 seconds from Simon Peachey (Cosmics) to clinch the Cosmics' Summer Hill Series. At Cheyne Hill Alan Smith (Deeside) was third (first M40) and Iain Anderson (Ubatt) fourth (first M50).

Veronique Oldham (Cosmics), who just missed breaking the Tap O' Noth record the weekend before, made no mistake this time when winning the ladies' race.
