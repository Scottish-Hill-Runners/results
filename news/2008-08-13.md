title: "Tiorgha Mhor, 9 August"
date: 2008-08-13
excerpt: "Tom Smith just got the better of Anesti Davenhill in a windswept Tiorgha Mhor race...."
---

Sounds like the weather was a real challenge for the hardy runners who toed the line at Tiorgha Mhor, judging by this short report from race winner Tom Smith:

"The entire field were transported to the start in a berlingo multispace. Already in the clag and 40mph winds, Stuart Simpson led the way along the winding trail untill the first climb up Tiorgha Beag. Tom Smith and Anesti Davenhill broke away in to the real clag, where a lot of the markers were blown down, and stuck together over the main top of Tiorgha Mhor and descent. Tom just managed to get the better of Anesti on the final 150m, crossing the line in 49.12, with Anesti 8 seconds back. No women ran.

Excellent soup and rolls were served after the race.
