Strathearn Harriers are going ahead with their annual event the Simon Wake Comrie Hills Relay on Sunday 12th September. It is open to teams of 4 & the course covers 42km with 2,110m of climbing. 

 [http://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/our-races/simon-wake-comrie-hills-relay](http://www.strathearnharriers.org.uk/our-races/simon-wake-comrie-hills-relay).

Entries opened last week on Entry Central [https://www.entrycentral.com/Strathearn\_Harriers](https://www.entrycentral.com/Strathearn_Harriers).
