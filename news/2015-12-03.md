If you have entered the pre-selection list for Jura, you are one of about 500 by now.

Many are wondering how the entries will be sorted down to a shortlist at this stage. This will be a pleasant task, until the bullets start flying. Of course not!

The way in which names will be drawn out of the pool, at great length, can be found at [Jura entry list criteria](http://www.artxraykishorn.co.uk/juraaspnet/entry_form.aspx)
