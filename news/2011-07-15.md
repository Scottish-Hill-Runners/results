Robbie Simpson set a new record for the Creag Choinnich hill race in time of 19 minutes and 34 secs. Ray Ward of HBT was second in 20.48 and Dan Whitehead was third in 21.00 Claire Smith of Metro Aberdeen was first lady in a time of 29.45 with Lesley Clark in second and Ann Smith third.
