The Kaim Hill Race, sponsored by [Advertisign Direct](http://www.advertisigndirect.co.uk/), put on a wet west coast evening and heavy conditions for over fifty runners. It was nip-and-tuck between Andrew McCall (Greenock Glenpark) and Alan Smith (Deeside R) on the climb and back down again to the burn, whereupon McCall's younger faster legs took him away to victory for the third year in succession. Colin Glencorse (Unattached) was third, M40 Chris Upson (Westies) fourth and MU20 Nick Emsley (Arran R) fifth.

After several second and third places in the last year Ellen Homewood (Westies) finally bagged a first, finishing well clear of Emma Birnie (Bellahouston RR) and Alice Miller (Cosmic HB).

Thanks in part to the tricky conditions the winning times did not threaten the course records, so the prizes for breaking them are safe for another year.
