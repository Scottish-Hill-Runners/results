The 52nd OMM is on 26th-27th October 2019 at Largs.

  

Event Video link: [https://youtu.be/tG4kEzh5YS0](https://youtu.be/tG4kEzh5YS0)  

  
**A brief overview:**  

This year marks the 52nd OMM, the UK’s biggest mountain race, which is intentionally held when ‘the weather is bad’. In teams of two, the two-day event (26th-27th Oct) challenges teams both physically and mentally - it’s a wild test of navigation, kit selection and mountain skills.
