The 2023 SHR Race Calendars and Journals have been printed and envelopes packed so SHR members can expect to receive them in the post over the course of the next week.

A reminder for anyone yet to join for 2023 that the membership mechanism is via SiEntries now - all details available on the link here - [SiEntries - Scottish Hill Runners](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=10535)

For lapsed members or those who have never joined please consider joining Scottish Hill Runners and supporting the organisation.
