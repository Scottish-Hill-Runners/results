Please note that the race starts at **12noon**. This is the time advertised in the Calendar, but is an hour later than in previous years (to allow those from further afield a more relaxed morning).

There’s more information about the race [here](http://www.scottishhillrunners.uk/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0028), including a link to the entry form. Although the date for postal entries has passed, the form has useful information about car parking, **kit requirements** and the route.
