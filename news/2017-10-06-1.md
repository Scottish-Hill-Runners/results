Glen Etive, just south of Glen Coe, is regarded by many as one of the most beautiful glens in Scotland. It’s surrounded by fantastic hills on both sides, including 16 Munros, with the iconic Buachaille Etive Mor being the most renowned.

Digby Harris completed a round of these 16 munros within 24 hours over the 1st and 2nd of September 2017. Digby’s account is available on a word doc that can be downloaded here.
