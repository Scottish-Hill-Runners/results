---
title: "Symonds and Wilson make their mark in European Trials, 17 June"
date: 2007-06-17
excerpt: "Joe Symonds was second in the Senior Men's event and Lyn Wilson third in the Senior Women's.  '"
---

The trials for the GB team for the European Championships were held in Snowdonia. Joe Symonds (Kendal) was second in the senior men's race, Lyn Wilson (Carnethy) third in the senior women's and Alex Jones (Fife AC) ninth in the junior men's.
