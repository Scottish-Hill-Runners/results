---
title: "Ras y Aran Postponed"
date: 2021-01-22
excerpt: "Ras y Aran (the Welsh counter in the 2021 British Championship) Postponed"
---

Ras y Aran (the Welsh counter in the 2021 British Championship) will not be taking place on 27th March 2021 but, if possible, it will be rescheduled for later in 2021. British Athletics Fell Running are looking into the possibility of the champs series starting later in the year and will provide an update in due course.
