_From Phil Hodgson and Mandy Goth:_

As you know we are the current organisers of the Isle of Jura Fell Race. This is our fifth year, having taken over from Andy Curtis in 2005. We have enjoyed organising the race immensely. Jura is a magical island and it's pretty easy to pull everything together for race day at the end of May, particularly as there's massive support and help from the islanders. But we've got lots of adventures, travels and ambitions to fulfil and would like to hand on the race (it's the thirtieth this year) to someone else in the next year or so.

It would be logical for a Scot to take over organising the race from us. We would hope to get a volunteer to shadow us at this year's race or next year's and to take on the organisation in 2011 or 2012.

If anyone would like to take over from us, or to discuss what is involved, please contact us on 01422844936 or on philATtodharriers.co.uk. Alternatively, if you're coming to this year's race you can have a chat with us on the island. We're staying at Jura House from 23rd - 30th May.
