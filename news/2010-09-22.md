**Bella Ben Venue Challenge**

_From organiser Andy Birnie:_

Summer is almost a distant memory, but the hill race of the year is now less than 2 weeks away - the Bella Ben Venue Challenge!

It's on Saturday 2 October at 1pm, and hopefully, fingers crossed, the weather will allow us to run the full course this year.

And I'm sure you all noticed that Ben Venue has been chosen as an SHR championship race for 2011, so you could look at this as an early recce!

Hope to see you all on the 2nd. Please tell your friends, family & club mates about how good the race was last year - a great course & superb food afterwards - and encourage them along to the first running of the full Ben Venue course!

It's all in aid of Lomond Mountain Rescue and more details can be found [here](http://www.bellahoustonroadrunners.co.uk/benvenuechallenge).

**Run and Become Tinto Hill Race**

Please note that this year the race, which starts at 2pm on Saturday 13 November, is PRE-ENTRY ONLY. You have to enter [here](https://www.sientries.co.uk/event.php?elid=Y&event_id=387), from 9am onwards on Monday 4 October. (closing date is 7 November).

Remember that, even though this is a short race, you must carry or wear full body cover.

You can enter on the day for both the junior race and the Fun Run.

Please also remember that the **AGM of Scottish Hill Runners** will be immediately after the prize-giving for the race, and we hope for a good turn-out of members.
