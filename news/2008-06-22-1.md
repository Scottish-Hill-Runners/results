---
title: "Jardine at home on the Eildons, 21 June"
date: 2008-06-22
excerpt: "Euan Jardine (Gala H) wins his local race.  '"
---

Euan Jardine of Gala Harriers won the Eildon 2 Hills Race from Steve Cairns (HBT) and Dougal Ross (Bellahouston RR). HBT had further success in the women's race with first (Clare Gordon) and third (Eilidh Wardlaw); Hazel Dean (Central AC) was second.

This report from George Meikle:

"This year is the first in 47 years that we have had a runner with a broken ankle going up! The race itself was dominated from the start by Euan Jardine and Steve Cairns, the two staying together until coming off the second hill. Then Euan opened up a gap which wasn't to be closed, with Dougal Ross coming home in 3rd. The Ladies race was dominated by Clare Gordon. The weather was fair, but very windy so times were slow, yet underfoot the conditions were great."
