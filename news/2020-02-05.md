The Carnethy 5 is not far away – the 15th Feb. It is the 50th running of this race and therefore there are several free goodies, T Shirts and also a Ceilidh in Edinburgh.

Olly Stephenson and Jamie Thin, the hosts, would like to get a great atmosphere and dance the lactic acid away. You don’t have to have done the race to come along, and if you did do the race it will be a good way to warm down.

**Ceilidh Tickets & Technical 50th Anniversary Race T-Shirts for sale.**

**Ceilidh Tickets**  
Location: Assembly Roxy – 2 Roxburgh Place, EH8 9SU Edinburgh (near the Pleasance)  
Time: 7pm to 11pm  
£10 per ticket  
Drinks available from the bar (please eat beforehand, no food will be provided)
