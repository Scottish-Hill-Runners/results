Dan Whitehead and Veronique Oldham, both of Cosmics, won the White Tops Race.

_From organiser Gill Hanlon:_

Thirty-six runners took part in the race this year which is less than last year. That meant more cakes all round!

The day had been warm and sunny until fairly heavy rain started at about 4.30pm. However, by 7.30pm the rain had stopped and it was pretty good running conditions. The vegetation at Balkello Woodland has increased in density over the year and after the rain that afternoon, the runners were treated to showers off the foliage as they made their way along the paths, through some fairly dense undergrowth and low-hanging branches.

All 36 returned unscathed and appeared none the worse for their ordeal; some were even smiling! The field were led home for the second year running by Dan Whitehead in a time of 35mins 17secs.

The run is a really nice route with some lovely views. There are no check points but it is marked out to encourage runners of all abilities. Come and give it a go next year... we’d love to see you.
