**Long Classics after 8 races**

Congratulations to **Andrew Douglas** of **Westies** and **Catriona Graves** of **Highland HR** who have won the 2024 long classic series after their wins at Two Breweries race at the weekend.

Well-deserved winners with 100% winning record in the 4 races they completed.

37 have now completed 4 races or more in the series. Final race in series is Carnethy Skyloop on Sunday 13th October.
