---
title: "Symonds leads the way round the Pentlands, 6 October"
date: 2007-10-06
excerpt: "Andy Symonds (Carnegie) won a sunny Pentland Skyline Race with Sula Young (HBT) the first woman.  '"
---

Andy Symonds (Carnegie H) showed he is in fine form just now by following up his record-breaking run over the Lomonds of Fife in August with a convincing win in the sun on the Pentland Skyline. Alasdair Anthony (Ochil HR) and Grant Stewart (Edinburgh AC), after a relatively conservative start together, came through to finish second and third.

First woman was Sula Young (HBT), now back to something approaching fitness, followed by Ellen Homewood (Westies) and Emma O'Shea (Unatt).
