Iain Gilmore left the rest of the field in his wake, finishing a full 11 seconds ahead of last weeks joint winner Luke Arnott, who could at least console himself with 1st MV40. Eilidh Wardlaw not only arranged the race on a fine night for running but took the ladies prize into the bargain.

Luke leads the overall series but with the best 2 from 3 races to count there are several runners who could pip him to the prize. Eilidh's lead in the ladies looks more secure but not as much as Steven Fallon's in the V50.

Results Updated 9/9/13
