A very misty Lomond Hills welcomed a big field of 54 junior hill runners from 20 different Scottish clubs for Round 2 of the Junior Hill League on Sunday. Congratulations to all the runners that completed the tough course and a huge thank you to Roger Rees and his team from Fife AC for, once again, putting on a very friendly and well organised event. 

The full results are now available [https://www.fifeac.org/events/fife-ac-events/lomonds-junior-hill-race/1414-lomonds-junior-hill-race-2023.html](https://www.fifeac.org/events/fife-ac-events/lomonds-junior-hill-race/1414-lomonds-junior-hill-race-2023.html) and current standings in the league after 2 events are here:
