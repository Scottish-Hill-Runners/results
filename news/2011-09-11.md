Everything is now in place for the finale to the 2011 SHR Championship at Ben Venue on 1st October.

With the larger turnout expected, we've moved the start/finish to the main Ben Venue Car Park, and we've secured extra car parking from the Foresty Commission.

To ease the registration rush on the day, postal entries are now being taken by sending an entry form & cheque here:

Ben Venue Entries, 4 Jubilee Gardens, Bearsden, Glasgow G61 2RT.

The bonus of paying by post is a £1 discount!

More info is [here]( http://www.bellahoustonroadrunners.co.uk/events/benvenuechallenge )
