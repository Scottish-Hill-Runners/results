It's just been confirmed that all proceeds from the new Ben Chonzie Race near Comrie will be donated to [Scottish Athletics](https://www.facebook.com/scottishathletics?__cft__[0]=AZVhDGe-wVDWycpa0fH6cTNsEg7_8ScpFmQWu5DrRW2Si1707VZwIFnVVbKrvi0MVt1-qFYYJs-i5zvKRco0wdaDJTE453APvGbMoj7nc4-cBz5zvY3gnSvbwIX5ZCF2ARqDqg77e-Yons6V7g5jXAOFXFSJhbJwcGtXVNgZQlGEPe-n0gO4fouGifK9CZSR3x6-hPm2_GkQxweoJlZ4DI0T&__tn__=-]K-R) for use in their junior hill running programme.

Race entry costs just £10 and includes post race soups and home baking too. At 21km and 1140m's it's also a counter in this year's Scottish Long Classics series. There's also a junior race/fun run happening alongside the main event!

So please spread the word within your clubs and the hill running community more widely. The more folk that run it, the more we raise for our young hill runners!

Big thanks to the Race Organisers, Ralph & Hanna McInally, and all at [Strathearn Harriers](https://www.facebook.com/strathearnharriers?__cft__[0]=AZVhDGe-wVDWycpa0fH6cTNsEg7_8ScpFmQWu5DrRW2Si1707VZwIFnVVbKrvi0MVt1-qFYYJs-i5zvKRco0wdaDJTE453APvGbMoj7nc4-cBz5zvY3gnSvbwIX5ZCF2ARqDqg77e-Yons6V7g5jXAOFXFSJhbJwcGtXVNgZQlGEPe-n0gO4fouGifK9CZSR3x6-hPm2_GkQxweoJlZ4DI0T&__tn__=-]K-R) for their generous support!
