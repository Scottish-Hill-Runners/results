Results from the GWC 25 and 13 mile races at Poolewe on Saturday 12 th August

In winning the 25 miler for the 7th time, Alec Keith lowered the Vet 50 record by exactly 9 minutes. It had been held for 20 years by Ben Preece (Deeside Runners). The lady winner, 24 year old Kathleen Steventon, won at her first attempt at the distance. She had previously won the 13 mile race in 2015.

The 13 mile race was won by Eoin Coull and Elizabeth Williams.
