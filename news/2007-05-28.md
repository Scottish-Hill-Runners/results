---
title: "Hargreaves and Gordon win at Crieff, 23 May"
date: 2007-05-28
excerpt: "John Hargreaves (Carnegie) won the Knock Hill Race at Crieff with Claire Gordon (HBT) the first woman.  '"
---

According to the [Strathearn Harriers web site](http://strathearnharriers.blogspot.com/2007/05/knock-knock.html) the turnout for the race was a bit lower than usual, perhaps due to the Champions League Final and/or an accident on the A85 near Perth.

Be that as it may, John Hargreaves (Carnegie) and Claire Gordon (HBT) both won in fast times. The veterans' prizes went to Ian Anderson (SPSAAC) and Jacqui Higginbottom (Central).
