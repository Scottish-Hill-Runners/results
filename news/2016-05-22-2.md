Jethro Lennex (Shettleston) won Goatfell on Saturday 21st May with Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) in 2nd and Gwyn Bellamy (Westies) in 3 rd.

Christina Rankin (Kilbarchan) was first lady with Amanda Blackhall (Lochaber) and Megan Mowbray (HBT) 2nd & 3rd.

Full results on the SHR website.
