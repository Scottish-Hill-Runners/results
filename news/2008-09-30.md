title: "Scottish team for Junior Home International on 12 October"
date: 2008-09-30
excerpt: "The team has been announced..."

The Scottish teams for the British and Irish Junior Home Internationals are posted [here](http://www.scottishathletics.org.uk/index.php?p=17&itemType=news&itemId=3777).

The event is being held at Bonaly on Sunday 12 October, beginning at 9.40am with an Open race (at 4.5km/270m a suitable warm-up for the [Pentland Skyline](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0107), which starts round the corner at 11am?). Then there are races for U14G/U14B, U16G, U19G/U16B and finally U19B. These are open to any junior of the specified age – see [here](http://www.shr.uk.com/RaceDetails.aspx?RaceID=RA-0412) for full details.
