Blustery and wet conditions made the Appin Show Hill Race pretty challenging but a few hardy runners turned up to take it on. A new race on the calendar and a good fast record was set by Gary MacDonald who won by a significant margin, although Andy Barrington had a great climb to reach the summit first. Sarah McKenzie had a great race to finish in third place and first lady. Well done to those who took part!  
Nicola Meekin
