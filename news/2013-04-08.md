_From Alex Brett_

We were all very fortunate on saturday as weather conditions were just about perfect for the 12th Cioch Mhor hill race, the river level was low and visibility good,apart from that chilly east wind. There was 62 enties in the senior race, including 4 runners from Sweden, who had come over especially to run in the Clachnaben race ,which was cancelled due to snow conditions, and our race numbers increased beacuse of this. Luck was on our side as by 4pm sleet and rain had arrived, on Sunday the gound was covered by approx 10cm of snow!!!!. The underfoot conditions obviously suited Finlay Wild as he set a new course record of 1:05:12 , an improvement of over 3 minutes, he was followed by Gordon Lennox in 1:09:05, and Brian Marshall in 1:10:16.

There were 5 entries in the Junior race, won by Alasdair Couper of East Sutherland, and all enjoyed the course especially seeing the lambs in the fields as they ran past, hope to have more juniors next year
