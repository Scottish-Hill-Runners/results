The 2nd Bog & Burn race in the 2015 series was run at the Kaim Hill Race in Fairlie on Wednesday evening. Jethro Lennox (Shettlestone) won ahead of Gregor Stewart (Westerlands). Iain Gilmore (Carnethy) was third.

In the ladies race, Madeline Robinson (Helensburgh) was first with Michelle Hetherington (Helensburgh) and Margo James (Westerlands) in 2nd and 3rd respectively.
