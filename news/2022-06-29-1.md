Some impressive wins by Tim Morgan and Chloe Lumsdon. And the racing highlight was the joint 2nd place overall who couldn't be separated after 3 1/2 hours of racing.

30 ran and 26 completed the race which is low but as it was my first year organising it I was particularly glad to just get everyone back down safely. Despite changeable weather and 1 rolled ankle I think everyone seemed to have a good day on the hill.

We did have around 50 pre-registered sign ups so I was expecting a higher turnout but the weather in the morning may have put a few folk off as well as a bad crash South of Crianlarich which messed up travel plans for a few folk from the North and East. Also Durisdeer next weekend.
