title: "Whitlie back to his winning ways at the Devil's Beeftub, 12 September"
date: 2009-09-13
excerpt: "Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) beats Des Crowe (Shettleston) and John Hepburn (Lochaber); Kim Threadgall (Edinburgh) is first woman...  '"
---

The sunny weather attracted 27 runners to the often-gloomy hollow of the Devil's Beeftub - of whom only six were aged less than forty, and three were female.

Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) finished half-a-minute clear of Des Crowe (Shettleston) with John Hepburn (Lochaber) and Adam Anderson (Carnethy) not far behind. Kim Threadgall (Edinburgh) was the first woman home, ahead of Alice Miller (Unatt) and Kate Friend (Carnethy).
