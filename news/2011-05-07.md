On a hot and muggy day Paul Faulkner of Carnethy won the Ben Lomond race getting applauded home by several claps of thunder and a nearby lightning storm. Second was Dave Eiser of Ochils and Gala's Fergus Johnston was third.

A dominant win in the ladies race for Sarah McCormack of Moorfoot Runners, with Shona Robertson in second (also first V40) and Jana Jurackova in third.

First veteran was V50 Alan Smith in 7th place, who just pipped U23 Alastair Robertson with Chris Upson two places further back the first V40. The team prize went to Carnethy.
