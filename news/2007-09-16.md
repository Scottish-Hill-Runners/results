---
title: "Marshall wins and Schmidt breaks record at Merrick, 15 September"
date: 2007-09-16
excerpt: "This was the fifth SHR Championship race (the second medium one). Brian Marshall (HELP) won and Elke Schmidt (Bellahouston RR) set a new women's record.  '"
---

From race organiser Nat Taylor:

The race went well with 84 runners in the senior event - more than treble last year's number. The conditions were very slippy underfoot and a bit claggy at the summit. Stewart Whitlie (Carnethy) went slightly off course, despite its being marked all the way with canes and bright orange ribbon...

The descent off the Buchan Hill was a bit tricky as there had been cattle there which had left lots of hoof prints; but Brian Marshall (HELP) took it all in his stride.

Despite the conditions Elke Schmidt (Bellahouston RR) beat the women's record set by Helene Diamantides in 1999.
