Perfect weather combined with a top quality British Championship field meant that both men and women's records were sure to go.

Rob Jebb (Bingley) won the Durisdeer Hill Race in a new record time of 1:21:53, a mere eight seconds ahead of Morgan Donnelly (Borrowdale), with Shettleston's Jethro Lennox in 3rd place in 1:23:26.

In the ladies race, Angela Mudge finished 1st in 1:34:43, knocking 20 minutes off the previous record. Second was Natalie White (Bingley) in 1:37:11, and third was Bellahouston's Elke Schmidt in 1:38:41.
