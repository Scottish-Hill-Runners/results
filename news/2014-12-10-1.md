The Geal-charn Mor Hill Race was unfortunately shortened due to low/sporadic visibility around the corbett summit. Not sure how popular this decision was but 'safety first' and all that!

  

This was the first proper snowfall of the year here in the Cairngorms (often treated like by Armageddon by some) and given the road conditions on sections of the A9 we had a great turnout of 54.

  

The runners, stayed on the main track and ran to the Polson memorial at the top of the Burma Rd then back into Alltnacriche. This shortened the route by about 2km.

  

We hope that next year we can make it to the top!
