**SHR Bog & Burn Championship Update**

Bog and Burn final positions.

A good turnout at Caerketton saw 8 runners get their 6th required race and 53 runners completed the series, well done to all. This is up on 44 from last year so hoping for 60 next year!

In the Men's seniors Ryad Chebbout took the win ahead of Alistair Thornton and Will Beresford. In the Female seniors Rosalind Heron won ahead of Holly Eadsforth and Edel Mooney

Other category winners -

M40 Jonathan Turnbull

F40 Edel Mooney

M50 Micky Anderson

F50 Susan Lindsay

M60 Don Reid

F60 Joan Wilson

M70 Pat Fitzpatrick + he took a special prize for being the only person to complete all 13 races
