Tinto Tiptoe and Hill Fun Run

Saturday November 8th 2014

Under UKA Rules. Scottish Athletics Permit.

Directions Fun Run Register & Start (Grid Ref: 963 373) Turn off the Lanark to Abington road A73 at minor cross- roads nearest Tinto Hill. (On your right, if heading south.)

Entry Fee 10p (Pensioners half price.)Under 8 and over 98 must be accompanied.

Start 2.07 Approx.

Course Distance 1.2k. Climb 50m.Run (or walk) up track, round hill fort and back.Note: There are no facilities at the start. The nearest are 3 miles away at Symington.

Prizes None. Certificates juice & Kit Kat to all smiling finishers.

Note The Tiptoe is held in conjunction with the Tinto Hill Race 6.5k / 450m, which starts at 2pm, and the tough junior race 3.75k / 250m starting at 2.05pm.Registration for both these is 3 miles away in Symington village hall off A72 (GR 996 352, OS Landranger sheet 72)
